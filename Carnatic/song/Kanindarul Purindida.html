  Lyrics - English:

PallavikanindaruL purindiDa manam illaiyA kadir kAma kandan en duraiyillaiyA uLam
Anupallavipanitta shaDaiyan shivan pArvaiyinAl vanda panniru kaNNan pArvati bAlan uLam
CaranamteriyAmal nAn sheia pizhai ennavO dinamum avanai ninaindurugum aDimaiyallavO
puriyAmal azhuginrEn puzhu pOla tuDikkinrEn porumaiyai ini sOdhikkAmal pon mayilinil Eri uLam
