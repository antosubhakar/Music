  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhArata puNya bhUmi jaya
Anupallaviyamunai kAvEri gaGgaiyum unpAl imayavar taGgum himAcalam unpAl
Caranam 1munnaip-pazham pugazh SIrvaLam OGgum unnai ninaindiDil pAvam nIGgum
ponni vANi aruL Selvar pugazhnda braghma vishNu SaGkaranum magizhnda
Caranam 2tUya taGgam oLi kunRuvaduNDO sUrya candranoLi maGguvaduNDO
mAya ulagu tavap-pudalvan gAndhi tannAl nI peruvAi sukha SAnti
