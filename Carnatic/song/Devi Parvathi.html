  Lyrics - English:

pallavidEvi pArvati parama pAvani
parAtpara rUpiNi paripAlitOham
anupallavishiva kAmya sundari sankari
guhaganEsha zhrikari Omkari
caraNamlOka janani mahAmAya kAriNi anityamOha nivArini
pApa bhanjjani nJAna sanJani advaita darsini
