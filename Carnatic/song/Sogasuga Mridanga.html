PallavisogasugA mRdanga tALamu jata kUrci ninu
sokka jEyu dhIruD(e)vvaDO
Anupallavinigama Sir(O)rthamu kalgina
nija vAkkulatO svara SuddhamutO (sogasu)
Caranamyati viSrama sad-bhakti
virati drAkshA rasa nava rasa
yuta kRticE bhajiyincu
yukti tyAgarAjuni taramA SrI rAma (sogasu)
