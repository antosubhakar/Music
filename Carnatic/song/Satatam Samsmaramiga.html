  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisatatam samsmarANIha sArasAksha bhavantam syAnandurESvara
Anupallaviati sAndra mOhagADAndhakAra saJcaya hati sArasa himakarAyitam jagadISam
Caranam 1nirupama payO vArinidhi tuGga taGgaugha pariluTanEna param
parisa bhuvitata varamaNi rucivrtE sarasam SvEtAntarIpE
uraga kula nAyaka vara maJcadalOpari pariSAyinamaniSam parama purusham ISam
Caranam 2indivarAsita sundaravapushamAnadaika padamISvaram kudAvadA tAbha mandahAsa ruci
nandita bhUmIndram vrndAraka maharshi vrnda vanditamiha vantAru jana kalpapAda pAyitamahO
Caranam 3visada nAbhIpuTa vilasadabjavAsinAkruSa bhakti bharajUshAbhrSamudA
vidhinApi vihitatrayI ghOsha niSamanEna samuuditam truSi pUrNa karuNam
sudIna jana pAlakam prasamida bhava tApam padmanAbhamaham
