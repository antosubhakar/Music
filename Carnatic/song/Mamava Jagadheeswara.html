PallavimAmava jagadIshvara mAnasAkAnta
Anupallavisama bhuvana sharaNa saka pAda vinata kAma dAna catura kamanIyatarA pAnga
Caranam 1vala sutanAdi dEva vrnda sEvita cAru jalaruha madahara caraNayuga mAm pAhi
su-lalita maNihAra shObhi cAru kandara kalaya mama kushalam kamanIya mOhanAnga
Caranam 2taruNa vidhu phAlaturaga divya vAhana sarasijAstra sundara shva-nikara parivrta
karuNArasa nilaya khalamaNI mallAntaka parilasa mama hrdi pankajadaLa lOcana
Caranam 3nArada mukha munigEya carita vinivArida caraNa natapAtaka
sArasanAbhAnujAsam mOhana nirupama surucira mrduhAsa samsara pOta
