  Lyrics - English:

PallavirAdA nAmIda dayalEdA moraviDaga vina
AnupallavivAdA nIdAnuDa shrI varada vEnkaTEshvara vina
Caranam 1parama puruSa vinumu patita pAvana dInajanAvana
karimorAlakincina shrI harihariyani moraliDivina
Caranam 2satatamu cAlA madini nijammuga namminayila draupadi
mAnamu gAcina shrIpati gati nI vagAvina
Caranam 3tiramuga varamulOsange varaduDa vIvayanucu shrI
hari varada vEnkaTAdri vihArA rArAyani vina
