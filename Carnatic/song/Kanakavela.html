  Lyrics - English:

PallavikanakavEla karuNAlavAla kAtyAyani bAla kaN pArttaruL varashIla
Anupallavianagha bhakta paripAla kallOlaghana vicitra svarajAla gAnalOla
Caranamsakala lOka sharaNya kunjaradAsan sakala rOga nivAraNajanana maraNa sakala
shOka haraNa sarOruha caraNa sharavaNabhavA sharaNam kAttaruL idu taruNam
