jAnakIlOla
caraNam 1I mudu I mukhavu I tanuvina kAnti I billu I bANa I nIti I bhAva
I tamma I sIte I paNDa I bhAgya yAva dEvarige uNTU mUrulOkadoLage
caraNam 2uTTa pItAmbara uDigajje mANikkavu doDDa navaratnada AbharaNa iralu
koTTa nambi tanna bhaktaragellA SrshTiyoLage kANe kausalya rAma
caraNam 3pLisanu ayOdhya paTTaNada puravAsa bEDida ishTArttagaLa
koDuviniyanuta
nODi bhaktarigellA bEDa vara koDuva svAmi purandara viTTalarAyanna
