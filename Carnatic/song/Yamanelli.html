AnupallaviyamanE SrI ramanu sandEha bEDa
Caranam 1nambida vibhIshaNage rAmanAda nambadidda rAvaNanige yamane Ada
Caranam 2nambida arjunanige baNTanAda nambadidda kauravanige kaNTakanAda
