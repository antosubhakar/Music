  Lyrics - English:

PallavibArayya veGkaTaramaNa bhaktara nidhiye
AnupallavitOrO ninnaya daya tOya jAmbakane
Caranam 1vEda gOcara bArO Adi kaccapa bArO mOdasUkara bArO sadayA narasimha bArO
Caranam 2vAmana bhArgava bArO rAmakrshNane bArO prEmada buddhane bArO svAmi kalki nI bArO
Caranam 3aravinda nAbha bArO surara prabhuvE bArO puruhUta vandya bArO purandara viTTalla bArO
