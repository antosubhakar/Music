  Lyrics - English:

PallavinA mor(A)lakimpav(E)mi SrI rAma
AnupallavinI mahimalu vini vini nEn(e)ntO nera nammiti (nA)
Caranam 1oka vana caruD(a)la nADu sahOdara bAdhalu
tALaka moral(i)Da brOcitivi tanaku sugrIvamu kAdA (nA)
Caranam 2oka niSi caruD(a)nna mATal(O)rvaka SaraN(a)nag(A)
Suka vacanamulaku nAdu palukul(a)nni vibhIshaNamA (nA)
Caranam 3pUsalu kUrcina(y)aTuvale pUni bhajincaga
Asalu gala tyAgarAju dAsuD(a)nucu telisi (nA)
