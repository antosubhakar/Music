  Lyrics - English:

PallavirAdhA ramaNa rakSamAm anisham
AnupallavimAdhava nAgAntaka varavAha sAdhita kauava sainya samUha
Caranam 1pANDava pakSa bhakta samrakSa puNDarIkAkSa bhuvanAdhyakSa
paNDita mAnava pAlana lOla khaNDita dAnava jAla sushIla
Caranam 2shankara hitavara shankha cakradhara shankara bhaktava shankara rUpa
pankaja bhava mInAnga janaka hariNAnga ravi nayana sankaTa hara shrI
Caranam 3rAmacandra parandhAma sArvabhauma rAmadAsanuta nirmala nAma
rAmacandrapura vara shrI vEnkaTaramaNa bhAgavata pAlana bhauma
