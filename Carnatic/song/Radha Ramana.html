  Lyrics - English:

pallavirAdhA ramaNa rakshamAm aniSam
anupallavimAdhava nAgAntaka varavAha sAdhita kauava sainya samUha
caraNam 1pANDava paksha bhakta samraksha puNDarIkAksha bhuvanAdhyaksha
paNDita mAnava pAlana lOla khaNDita dAnava jAla suSIla
caraNam 2Sankara hitavara Sankha cakradhara Sankara bhaktava Sankara rUpa
pankaja bhava mInAnga janaka hariNAnga ravi nayana sankaTa hara SrI
caraNam 3rAmacandra parandhAma sArvabhauma rAmadAsanuta nirmala nAma
rAmacandrapura vara SrI vEnkaTaramaNa bhAgavata pAlana bhauma
