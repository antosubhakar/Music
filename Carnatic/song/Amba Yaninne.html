PallaviambA yaninE moraliDa vinarAdA O jagad-
Anupallaviambuja sambhavuDaina Adi parASakti nI padAmbujamula
nera nammina vADugadA SrI brhadambA
Caranam 1nIku SaraNamamma nIvE gati mummAtiki nAkevaru dikku nIvE naLina daLAyatAkshi
Caranam 2kOrina kOrkkelu kona sAgiJcavamma pAripAri Sivuni vEsAri kOriti ipuDu
