  Lyrics - English:

PallavirArA muraLIdhara guru pavana purAlaya krshNa
AnupallavirArA rAdhA dhara madhupAnAlasa mAnasA krshNa
CaranammArAyuta kOmaLa Sarat-tArESa nibhAnana nArAyaNa SrIdhara
SrI lalitAmbikA sOdara gOvardhanOddharaNa nata SrI vardhana caraNa krshNa
