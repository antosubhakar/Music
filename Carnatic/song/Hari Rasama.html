  Lyrics - English:

Pallavihari rasamaa vihaari satu -
sarasOyaM mama Srama saMhaari
Caranam 1dayaa nibhRta tanudhaari saM
SayaatiSaya saMchaari
kayaapyajita vikaari
kriyaa vimukha kRpaaladhaari
Caranam 2paraamRta saMpaadi
sthiraanaMdaaSraedi
