  Audios:

  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrIdhara pAhi dayAkara vEda sugOcara giridhara
AnupallaviyAdava varada parAtpara pAda natAmara sukhakara
CaranambhUsurAdi pAlana para vAsudEva muraLIdhara
bhAsura maNi bhUSaNa nAgAsura hara dAmOdara
