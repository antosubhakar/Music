  Lyrics - English:

pallavihariyE gati sakala carAcaramulaku hariyE gati virinci rudrAdulakaina
(rAgA: bauLi)
caraNam 1muddula bAluDai muraLini cEpaTTi bAla muraLivai nAdamu pUrinci
mullOkamulanu munlanu saitamu muripinci maimuripincina SrI
(rAgA: kAmbhOji)
caraNam 2ayA yugamula dharmamu nilupaga avatAramulanu dAlcina daivamu
hayavAhanuDai kaliyuga manduna alamElu mangA pativai velasina
(rAgA: SuddhadhanyAsi)
caraNam 3bhasmAsurulu nayavancakulu asahana sUrulu palumisu pArulu
paTTi bAdhincu niTTi taruNamuna pAlana sEyuTa kevaru mAkevaru
(rAgA: hamsAnandi)

(ராகா: சுத்ததன்யாசி)
