  Lyrics - English:

Pallavibhaja bhaja mAnasa harim anavaratam bhakta parAyaNam ati shubha caritam
Anupallavishuka sanakAdi munIshvara vinutam jagadi samasta carAcara vitatam
CaranamjahitanadAra sudAdi surAgam svapna svamEshuhi mamatA racitam
pankaja nAbham anatapurEsham pannaga parivrDha vanca vilasitam
