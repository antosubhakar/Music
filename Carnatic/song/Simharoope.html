pallavisimharUpanAda SrI harE hE nAmagirISanE
anupallaviommanadinda nimmanu bhajisalu sammatadindali kAivanenda harE
caraNam 1taraLanu kareya stambavu biriyE tumba ugravanu tOridanu karaLanu
bagedu koraLoLagiTTu taraLana salahida SrI narasimhana
caraNam 2bhaktarella kUDi bahu dUra Odi parama SAntavanu bEDidaru karedu
tan siriyanu doDeyoLu kuLisida parama harushavanu pondida SrI hari
caraNam 3jaya jaya jayavendu huvvanu tandu hari hari hariyendu surarella surise
bhaya nivAraNa bhAgya svarUpanE parama purusha SrI purandara viTTalanE
