  Lyrics - English:

PallavirAjabhOga venitiddarenu virAja vAhanolume illadanaka
AnupallavirAjamAna paricararindenu yAjamAnyadali pUjEgoNdarenu

horage oLage samarasavirali parasukha sAgara siri dEvOddhAra
