PallaviEmani Ne Ni Mahima Delupudunamma
AnupallaviSamajagaamini (amba) Sarasa lochani (Emani)
CaranamHarihara Suramuni Varulaku Nidu
Charanamahima Pogaduta Taramaa
Narudanu Nenu Paa-marudanu Devi
Vara Kanchipuraalaya Vaasini Sri
Paradevate Nidu Kumaarudanammaa
