  Lyrics - English:

PallaviramincuvAr(e)varurA
ragh(U)ttamA ninu vinA
AnupallaviSam(A)di shaD-guNa gaNA
sakala bhuvana janulalO (rami)
CaranamrAma(y)anE su-marmamu
rama(y)anE Sarmamu lasad-
amara varulak(a)bbenO
tyAgarAja sannuta (rami)
