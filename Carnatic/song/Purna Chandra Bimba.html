  Lyrics - English:

pallavipUrNacandrambimba vijaya vadanE kamalAmbikE pAhimAm varadE guruguha janani SrI (pUrNacandrika)
anupallavipuNyajana pUjitE nArAyani jagadambikE (nArAyaNi)
caraNam 1pUrNa phalaprada caraNE sarasvatImanOharE (sarasvatImanOhari)
caraNam 2pushpita SuddhavasantE puNDarIka (Suddhavasanta)
caraNam 3aharniSam prakASamAna hamsadhvani virAjitE (hamsadhvani)
caraNam 4aparNE kuNDalini nAgadhvani sahitE dhvani sahitE sahitE hitE tE (nAgadhvani)
