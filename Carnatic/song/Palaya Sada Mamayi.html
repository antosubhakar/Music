AnupallavinIlAmbOdha ramEcakatara vidhi nIlakaNThanuta dAsa janAmara sAlakalpa SrI padmanAbha SaurE jagadISa murArE
Caranam 1sArasara sarAgAmayahAni dApadAna sama sudha nikAshha
vENugAna lOla vairi paritrAsa sAdhu tama krpArasa
Caranam 2vAraNavara tApa Samana caNadaya vArirASi kalpita nija Sayana
mArakOTi sundara muni hrdaya vihAra Agaripu vAhana
Caranam 3bhUri mudita brndAraka hATakacEla SrIlOla gOpAla
cArubAla candrabhAla nava vanamAla sujanAvana SIla
