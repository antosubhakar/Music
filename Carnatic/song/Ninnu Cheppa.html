  Lyrics - English:

Pallavininnu jeppa kAraNamEmi manasA munu nE jEsina karma miTluNDaga
(ninnu)
Anupallavidhana tanayAdi pAsha baddhuDai ghanamaina pAmaruDai nanduku
(ninnu)
CaranampalumAru durbhASyai tirigi kula hInamaina krtyamul-salputsu
kalalOlaina venkatEshayani dalatsa lEni pApa krtyuDai nanduku
(ninnu)
