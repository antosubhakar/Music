  Lyrics - English:

Pallavi.kadhitha samayEpi hari ahaha na yayau vanammama viphalam idam amalam rUpam api yauvanam yamihE kamiha saraNamsakhI janavachana vaJjithAham
. yadanugamanAya nisi gahanam api sIlithamthEna mama hrudayam idam asamasarakIlitham
. mama maraNam Eva varam athivithadhakEthanAkimithi viSahAmi virahAnalam achEthanA
. mamihahi vidhurayathi madhuramadhuyAminikApi harim anubhavathi krutha sukrutha kAmini
. ahaha kalayAmi valayAdhi maNi bhUSaNamharivirahadahana vahanEna bahudhUSaNam
. kusuma sukumArathanum asamasaralIlayasragapi hrudi hanthimAm athiviSama sIlaya
. ahamiha hi nivasAmi na viganithavEthasAsmarathi madhusUdhanO mAmapi na chEthasA
. haricharaNasaraNa jayadEvakavi bhArathivasathu hrudhi yuvathiriva kOmaLa kalAvathi
