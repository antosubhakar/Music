  Lyrics - English:

Pallavi.kadhitha samayEpi hari ahaha na yayou vanammama viphalam idam amalam rUpam api youvanam yamihE kamiha saraNamsakhI janavachana vanjithAham
. yadanugamanAya nisi gahanam api sIlithamthEna mama hrudayam idam asamasarakIlitham
. mama maraNam Eva varam athivithadhakEthanAkimithi vishahAmi virahAnalam achEthanA
. mamihahi vidhurayathi madhuramadhuyAminikApi harim anubhavathi krutha sukrutha kAmini
. ahaha kalayAmi valayAdhi maNi bhooshaNamharivirahadahana vahanEna bahudhooshaNam
. kusuma sukumArathanum asamasaraleelayasragapi hrudi hanthimAm athivishama seelaya
. ahamiha hi nivasAmi na viganithavEthasAsmarathi madhusUdhanO mAmapi na chEthasA
. haricharaNasaraNa jayadEvakavi bhArathivasathu hrudhi yuvathiriva kOmaLa kalAvathi
