  Lyrics - English:

pallaviadE cUDarE mOhana rUpam padi kOTlugala bhAvaja rUpam
caraNam 1velayaga padAruvEla maguvalanu alamina ghana mOhana rUpam
valacina nanda vrajamu golletala kuluku cUpulaku guriyagu rUpam
caraNam 2indirA vanita neppuDu tana ura mandu nilipina mOhana rUpam
kanduva bhUshati kaugiTi sompula vindulu maragina vEDuka rUpam
caraNam 3tripura satula bOdhinci ramincina apurUpapu mOhana rUpam
kapurula SrI vEnkaTapati yaiyila upamiMcaga rAni unnata rUpam
