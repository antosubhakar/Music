  Lyrics - English:

pallavidEva mAmayi pAhi shaDAnana divyakEki vAhana SUra
anupallavisEvakAkhila tApa nivAraNa SrI hari gItapurAlaya dIpa
caraNam 1tArakAsura dEha vibhanga gabhIratara gamana vijita mAtanga
bhUri karuNArasa rAjatapAnga bhUta carita hrdayAmbuja bhrnga
caraNam 2gangAdharAdi manOjna kumAra khalataravairi samUha vidAra
mangaLa carita payOdhi gabhIra mAra janaka bhAginE yOdhAra
caraNam 3dEva sEnAdhipa manjuLa SIla dInatara janatA pAlanalOla
SrI vijitAnanga mOhana lIla SrI padmanAbha hrdayAnukUla
