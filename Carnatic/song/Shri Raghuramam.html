  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI raghurAmam sItA nAtham Sritajana rakshaNam bhajAmi
AnupallavisArasOd-bhavAdipu dhaisssam sEvyam sakala surakshaNam danujav visikshaNam
Caranam 1nirupama caritamakhila Sruti vEdyam niravadhi saukhyA nandasva rUpam
parama purusham Subhadam rAmacandrapura nilayam SrIdharam sujana manOharam
