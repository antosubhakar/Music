PallavipAhi krshNa vAsudEva dEhi sadgatim mAdhava
AnupallaviEhitArthada su-vibhava drOhi kamsa hara kESava
CaranamkandarAtala pari SObhita bandhura maNi bhUshaNa sata
gandha vAhana suta pUjita sindhu Sayana suguNa bharita
kunda kusuma su-virAjita mandahAsa sura vandita
mandarAdri dhara muninata nandagOpa suta sucarta
