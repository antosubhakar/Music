  Lyrics - English:

PallavivyAghrapurIsha jaya jaya shambhO dEhi mudam mama dEva svayambhO
AnupallavinagarAja sutA nayanAnanda nigamAntanuta pAdAravindA
CaranamaghajAla hara mrga shUladharA maghavAja mukha santuta carita
suguNAlaya shrI lalitA ramaNa jagadIshvara mAm pAhi sadA
