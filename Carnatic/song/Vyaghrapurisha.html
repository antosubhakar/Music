PallavivyAghrapurISa jaya jaya SambhO dEhi mudam mama dEva svayambhO
AnupallavinagarAja sutA nayanAnanda nigamAntanuta pAdAravindA
CaranamaghajAla hara mrga SUladharA maghavAja mukha santuta carita
suguNAlaya SrI lalitA ramaNa jagadISvara mAm pAhi sadA
