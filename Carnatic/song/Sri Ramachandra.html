  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrI rAmacandra pAdAbjE yanamanO madhupAyitam svadharma niratO yastu nisprhO yO dvijOttamaha
(rAgA: shankarAbharaNa)
:
yaha kIrtanAni svIyani bhAva rAgayutAni ca gAyannirantaram bhaktyA prINayAmAsa rAghavam
(rAgA: bhairavi)
:
nArada anugrAhAdyastu lEbhE su-jnAna sampadam yatkIrtana rasAsvAdam AdriyantE budhAssadA
(rAgA: simhEndrmadhyam)
:
sItA lakSmaNa shatrughna bharatAni lasUnubhihi sEvitam rAmamAlOkya janma sAphalyam AptavAnam
(rAgA: khamAs)
:
kIrtikAyEna yO bhAti tyAgarAjO gururmahAn tam stuvImO vayam nityam rAma bhakta shirOmaNim
(rAgA: mOhana)
:
shrI tyAgarAja caritam sadA sarvajana priyam vAsudEvasya krpayA vAsudEvEna varNitam
(rAgA: madhyamAvati)
