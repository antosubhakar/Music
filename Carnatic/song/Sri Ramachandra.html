PallavishrI rAmacandra pAdAbjE yanamanO madhupAyitam svadharma niratO yastu nisprhO yO dvijOttamaha
(rAgA: shankarAbharaNa)
2yaha kIrtanAni svIyani bhAva rAgayutAni ca gAyannirantaram bhaktyA prINayAmAsa rAghavam
(rAgA: bhairavi)
3nArada anugrAhAdyastu lEbhE su-jnAna sampadam yatkIrtana rasAsvAdam AdriyantE budhAssadA
(rAgA: simhEndrmadhyam)
4sItA lakSmaNa shatrughna bharatAni lasUnubhihi sEvitam rAmamAlOkya janma sAphalyam AptavAnam
(rAgA: khamAs)
5kIrtikAyEna yO bhAti tyAgarAjO gururmahAn tam stuvImO vayam nityam rAma bhakta shirOmaNim
(rAgA: mOhana)
6shrI tyAgarAja caritam sadA sarvajana priyam vAsudEvasya krpayA vAsudEvEna varNitam
(rAgA: madhyamAvati)
