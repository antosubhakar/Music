  Lyrics - English:

pallavikalayami hrdi nandaka manIya balam
anupallavijayajasana vinata carana variruhamadyam
caraNam 1yamuna pulina viharinam vraja yauvata sammadakarinam
kOmaLa vandAmadhAriNam bahu gOpa vrnda vrtam amburuhAksham
caraNam 2dEva mauli paritOshaNam vara dEha parilasita bhUshaNam
bhAva harshi mrdu bhAshaNam ripu davAdava mati mEcaka bhAsam
caraNam 3sOma vamSa divya maNDanam vidhu sUmukhamakhila pApa khaNDanam
bhauma dAnava caNDadaNDanam SrI padmanAbha mama lASaya sEvam
