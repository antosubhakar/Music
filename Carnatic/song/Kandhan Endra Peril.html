  Lyrics - English:

pallavikandan enRa peyaril karuNai veLLam nI kANak-kiDaikkumO Idu unakkini
anupallaviantam Adi illA haran attan putra maindan maNi enRu umai kulavum kumarA
caraNampunnakai mukham Arilum varam tEDi vandEn pannagaSayanan marugA murugA guhA
tannaiyE maRandu nAn un pugazh pADiDum unnata varam tArAy uyar guNa sundara
