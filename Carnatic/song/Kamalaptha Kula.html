  Lyrics - English:

Pallavikamal(A)pta kula kalaS(A)bdhi candra
kAva(v)ayya nannu karuNA samudra
AnupallavikamalA kaLatra kausalyA su-putra
kamanIya gAtra kAm(A)ri mitra (kamala)
Caranammunu dAsula brOcinad(e)lla cAla
vini nI caraN(A)SrituD(ai)tin(a)yya
kanikarambuna nAk(a)bhayam(i)vvum(a)yya
vanaja lOcana SrI tyAgarAja nuta (kamala)
