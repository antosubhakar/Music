  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSrI candraSEkhara yatIndram bhajE Sritajana janAnanda kandam dayA sAndram
anupallaviSrI candramauli caraNArpita hrtabjam SrImad kAmakOTi pIThAdhISam
caraNamadvatAmrtAbdi sudhAmayakaram AmyAna sahasra bhadra madhukaram
vidhvanmaNI nikara vinuta guruvAram viSva bOdha prabhA dAna dinakaram
SrI dakshiNAmUrti pratyaksha rUpam natavarada kalpa taru pratApam
sAdara kaTAksha nivAdha bhakta tApam naTarAja tanaya hrunmaNi dIpam
