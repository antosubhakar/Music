  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrI candrashEkhara yatIndram bhajE shritajana janAnanda kandam dayA sAndram
AnupallavishrI candramauli caraNArpita hrtabjam shrImad kAmakOTi pIThAdhIsham
CaranamadvatAmrtAbdi sudhAmayakaram AmyAna sahasra bhadra madhukaram
vidhvanmaNI nikara vinuta guruvAram vishva bOdha prabhA dAna dinakaram
shrI dakSiNAmUrti pratyakSa rUpam natavarada kalpa taru pratApam
sAdara kaTAkSa nivAdha bhakta tApam naTarAja tanaya hrunmaNi dIpam
