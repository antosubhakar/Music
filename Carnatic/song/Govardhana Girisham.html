PallavigOvarddhana girISam smarAmi aniSam gOpikAdi manOharam garvita kamsAdiharam
CaranamgOvinda nAma sAram gajEndra rakshaNa dhIram kavijana hRn mandAram kanakajita suSarIram ravi SaSi nayana vilAsam ramaNIya mukhAbhAsam SivagANAdi viSvAsam SrI guruguha manOllAsam
