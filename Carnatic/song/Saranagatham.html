  Lyrics - English:

PallavisharanAgatham enRu nambivandhu ninthan saranaGgalayum thA!
AnupallaviKarunA katakSam panna vendum!
Thillai KanakasabhayilAdum prabhu vallabo!
CaranamJananAdhi bayaGgal anugAmal undhan sivasAyujya padham thara vendum!
mana nAdhigal ariyen thavam puriyen!
balakriSnan panindhudum thiruvadiyai
