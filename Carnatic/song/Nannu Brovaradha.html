  Lyrics - English:

Pallavinannu brOva rAdA O jagad-ambA
nI daya sEyavE
Anupallavikanna talli nIvE ambA
nA moralanu vina rAdA (nannu)
Caranam 1Adi Sakti mah(E)SvarI kaumArI
AdarimpavE vEvEgamE nIl(A)yat(A)kshI bhavAnI (nannu)
Caranam 2kOmaLa mRdu vANI kalyANI
sOma SEkharuni rANI lalit(A)mbikE varadE (nannu)
Caranam 3SyAma kRshNa sahOdarI OMkArI
SAmbhavI O jananI nAda rUpiNI naLin(A)kshI (nannu)
