  Lyrics - English:

pallavidAkshAyaNi abhayAmbikE
varadAbhayahastE namastE
anupallavidIkshA santushTa mAnasE dINAvanahastasArasE
kAnkshitArtha pradAyini kAma tantra vidhAyini
sAkshirUpa prakASini samasta jagadvilAsini
caraNamsakala nishkaLa svarUpa tEjasE
sakalalOkasrushTikaraNa bhrAjasE
sakala bhakta saMrakshaNa yaSasE
sakala yOgi manOrUpa tatva tapasE prabala guruguhOdayE
pancAnana hrudAlayE
bharata matangAdinutE
bhAratISapUjitE
