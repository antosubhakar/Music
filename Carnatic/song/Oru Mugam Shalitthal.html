Pallavioru mugam shalittAl maruvu SaNmugattil
maRu mugam iranguvatu illaiyO - murugA
Caranam 1oru shevi maRuttAl panniru shevikaLil
oru shevi kETpadumillaiyO - murugA
oru karam aDittAl murugA un panniru karattuL
oru karam aNaippadumillaiyO - murugA
Caranam 2oru mozhi shivanukku uraitta meignAna murugA
nI maRumozhi pEshuvadum illaiyO
Om murugAvena nAn OlamiTTazhaikka
ODi vandu aruL purindavanE
Caranam 3OhO idu enna pArvaiyO murugA idu enna pArvaiyO
un mugam enaippArkka maRuttAl maRu mugam uLadO ?
tiruttaNi maNi viLakkE !
