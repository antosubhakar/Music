  Lyrics - English:

PallaviKaruninchtakide samayamayya
Kari rakshaka na kali dirchchavayya
AnupallaviTaralo ni vanti daivamu ledani
Paramanandamu to bhajiyinchitini
CaranamKamalasanadi sannuta pada
Kamaniya subha syamala gatra
Kamalakalatra kanjadalanetra
Amaradi posha adivenkatesa
