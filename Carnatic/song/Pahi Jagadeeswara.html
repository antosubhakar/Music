  Lyrics - English:

PallavipAhi jagadeeSwara purahara
pAhi jagadeeSwara purahara mAm
pAhi sakala lOkAdhAra dheera
pAhi jagadeeSwara
AnupallavidEhi tava charaNam mama SaraNam mama SaraNam
divaspati sEvitam bhava bhaya haraNam
Caranamparama purusha sakala bhuvanAdhAra
paramAnanda swarupa SambhO
parAtpara pApahara gAnamoortE
muraLee bhakta vaSamkar Sankara
