  Lyrics - English:

PallavikEtum bhajAmyaham kirITinam citra varNam
AnupallavijAta kalaha kAraNam jaiminEyam mahAsuram
bhUta bhavya vartamAna bhOga mOkSa jnAnadam
Caranamsarva shAnti kAraNam sajjana manh-pUraNam
sarva dEsha sancAriNam sacitra vastra dhAriNam
sarva kArya bhAsinam candra sUrya vairiNam
sarva bAdhA praNAshAya sadA namAmyaham mudA
Madhyamakala sahityamcitta dhairya vardhanArtham jIvanE mama raKSaNArtham
baddha nindA mOcanArtham padE padE bhaya nAshanArtham
