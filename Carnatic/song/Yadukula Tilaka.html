  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviyadukula tilaka enduku nirdaya evarunnArurA brOva nI dharalO
AnupallavisadayuDu nIvani satatamu vEDitini caraNa kamalamuna sAdhana jEyumani
Caranamvara muninuta pAda vAJcita phalaprada vAraNArtti bhaJjana sugaNa sudhA- (or phalavarada)
karuDu nIvE gadA giridhara prabhuvanu vara garbhapurivAsa nannAdarimparAdA (or prabhuvanu koNTI)
