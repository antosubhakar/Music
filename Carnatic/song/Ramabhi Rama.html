  Lyrics - English:

PallavirAm(A)bhirAma ramaNIya nAma
sAmaja ripu bhIma sAkEta dhAma (rA)
Caranam 1vanaja lOcana nI valana(y)alasitini
manasuna daya lEdu allADi phalam(E)mi (rA)
Caranam 2manasu celi nIkE marulukonnadi kAni
canuvuna ceyi paTTi mamula rakshimpavu (rA)
Caranam 3kOri kOri ninnu koluvaga nI
dAri vEr(ai)nadi dhAta vrAl(E)mO (rA)
Caranam 4kamanIyamagu pAnpu kAvincitin(a)ndu
ramiyimpaka nannu racca jEsedavu (rA)
Caranam 5dikku nIv(a)ni nEnu dina dinamunu namma
ekkuva takkuval(a)ndu enaseDu guNam(E)mO (rA)
Caranam 6nIkE daya puTTi nIvu brOvavale
rAk(E)ndu mukha tyAgarAja varada SrI (rA)
