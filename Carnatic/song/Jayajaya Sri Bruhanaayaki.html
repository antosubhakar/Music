  Lyrics - English:

Pallavijaya jaya SrI bruhannAyaki
hayagrIvAnuja karuNArasamukhi
(jaya)
Anupallavibhaya nivAraNa SivE bhavAni
dhaya tO nanu brOvavE nAraNI amba
(jaya)
CaranampATalISa hrut kamalasthihE bhava
bAdhalanu pAragrolu dharmavati
nIdu mahima gananAtharamu kADanucu
nAdha alaGkrtamunu svIkarimcu
