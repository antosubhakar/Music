PallaviBAvayE pavamAna vaMdanaM BAvayE
Caranam 1maMdAra tarumUla mAnita vAsaM | suMdara darahAsaM haridAsaM
Caranam 1raGunAtha kIrtana raMjita cittaM | aGahara SuBavRuttaM SamavittaM
Caranam 2AnaMda bAShpAlaMkRuta nEtraM | svAnaMda rasapAtraM pavitraM
