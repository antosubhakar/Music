  Lyrics - English:

Pallavibhuvanatraya sammOhanakara SAstAram bhajEham
(bhuvana)
Anupallavibhavabhaya haraNam bhaktajana santAna saubhAgya varada caraNam
Siva mAdhava tatvaikyava taraNam Sabarigiri kOTi SaraNakaraNam
(bhuvana)
CaranamkuvalAksha kuTilALaka mrgamada tilaka kuNDala Sobhita vadanam
divasakara kOTi kAnti vijita nirupama sukumAra apahanam
nava jalajA bhayakara kusumacara maNIpAtra kArmukhEsu vahanam
Sivara skandAti vAhanArUDha pUrNA pushkalA sahita gamanam
(bhuvana)
