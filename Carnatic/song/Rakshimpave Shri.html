  Lyrics - English:

PallavirakshimpavE SrI rajata nilaya vrshabhadvAjananda mOksha vara dAyaka
AnupallavikukshilO prapaJcamunu nirvahiJcina nIku paksha pATamu gAdurA praNatArthihara nannu
CaranamdakshiNa kailAsamandu dikshitO vasiyuJci kAmAkshi kaliki I jagat sAkshiga uNDi nI vikshiJci
SrI tyAgarAjuni gAviJcuTa (rakshimpu) upEkshitamu ElarA uraga bhUshaNa nannu
