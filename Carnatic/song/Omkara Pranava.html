Anupallavisa~kAEtheka threBuvana shakthe murale gAna svarUpe ||
Muktayi sahityampraLaya jala valaya putalalalo thAElana DhAE vata pathram |
shayane~cu varadhune nABenevadhalena kamala sa~janetha veDhe muKa janetha ||

salelamagne naga Kaga tharu m_rgAdhe sakalamu kalugu nE dhayavalana |
