  Lyrics - English:

PallavinAdri dhIm dhIm tana dhIrana tana dhIrana nadridhIm tana dhIrana tana dhiranA
AnupallavinadridridhIm tomdridridhIm tana dhirana nadridridhIm tomdridridhIm tana dhirana nadridridhIm tadirana dhirana nadridridhIm tomdirdirdhIm tanadhirana nadridridri dhIm tadirana dhiranA nadridAni tomdhridAni drutillAnA tillAnAthillAnA dhiranA thillAnA dhiranA thillAna dhiranA
