  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSivakAmIpatim cintayEham SrI guruguha pUjita padAmbhOruham
caraNamnavakALI jita narttana dhIram nAda bindu kalA rUpa maniSam abhinava purandarAdi sanakAdi sannuta bhAnukOTi sankASam
