pallavitalli ninnu nera namminAnu vinavE
anupallaviella lOkamulak(A)dhAramai(y)unna nA (talli)
caraNam 1Adi Sakti nIvu parAku sEyaku
AdarincuTak(i)di manci samayamu
gadA sarOja bhav(A)cyuta Sambhu nuta
padA nIdu dAs(A)nudAsuDE (talli)
caraNam 2dEvi nIdu sari samAnam(e)var(a)ni
dEva rAja munulu ninnu pogaDaga
nA veta dIrci birAna varAl(o)sagi
nannu brOva nI jAlam(E)lanE (talli)
caraNam 3SyAma krushNa paripAlinI jananI
kAmit(A)rtha pradA pankaja lOcanI
kaumArI rANI purANI parA SaktI
kAma kOTi pITha vAsinI (talli)
