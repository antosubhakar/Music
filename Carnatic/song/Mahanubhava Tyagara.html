  Lyrics - English:

pallavimahAnubhavA tyAgarAjA mahA sad bhakta purusha rAja
anupallavisukhAnubhava SrI rAmacandrA sitAnanda snAna bANa pavitra
caraNamdhanati sarjita yOgi rAja guNAbhi manita tyAgarAja
janAbhi manita gANa rAja anadi Ananda dAsa rAja
