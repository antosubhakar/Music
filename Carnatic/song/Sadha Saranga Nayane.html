  Lyrics - English:

pallavisadA sAranga nayanE SrI sadASivadayitE mAmava
anupallavisudhAkara kalASEkhara kaumudImilita mandahAsE
caraNam 1prEmajita jananAnta rINa bhartrukE kAmAri manOranjani
vAmEtara pArSvapriya vibhavE amara gaNahita kumArasambhavE
caraNam 2kailAsa dhavala dhAmani maNimaya-vallakInAdara savilasanE
tAlalAsya para ramAvANi nanditE vara dEva vanditE
