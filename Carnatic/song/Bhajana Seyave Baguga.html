  Lyrics - English:

Pallavibhajana sEyavE bAguga O manasA
AnupallavisujanAdhAruDaina bhujagashayana shrIdharuni
nijamu delisi nEmamuga nityamu shrI rAmuni
CaranambharatAgrEsaruni bhajana cEsi cEsi
niratamu brahmAnanda parulaina bhaktulaina
taramugAdu manasa tappaka sadAshivanutuni
