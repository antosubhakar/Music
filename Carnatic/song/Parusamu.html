  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviparusamu sOkaka pasiDaunA | puruShOttamuDE buddhiccu gAka ||
Caranam 1Buvi BOgamulaku puTTina dEhamu | vivarapu mOkShamu vedakInA |
ivala sakalamunu yElETi dEvuDu | tavili rakShiMcuTa dharmamu gAka ||
Caranam 2Ganamagu saMsAra kAraNa jIvuDu | tana suj~jAnamu talacInA |
venuka munupa SrI vEMkaTa patiyE | kanugoni mammiTa gAcuTa gAka ||
