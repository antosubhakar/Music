  Lyrics - English:

pallaviparusamu sOkaka pasiDaunA | purushhOttamuDE buddhiccu gAka ||
caraNam 1Buvi BOgamulaku puTTina dEhamu | vivarapu mOkshhamu vedakInA |
ivala sakalamunu yElETi dEvuDu | tavili rakshhincuTa dharmamu gAka ||
caraNam 2nanamagu saMsAra kAraNa jIvuDu | tana suj~jAnamu talacInA |
venuka munupa shrI vEnkaTa patiyE | kanugoni mammiTa gAcuTa gAka ||
