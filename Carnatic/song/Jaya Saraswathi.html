  Lyrics - English:

Pallavijaya sarasvati janani SaraNam jagajjIvana kAraNa rUpiNi
AnupallavidayApUraNE indu vadanE suradanE dAridrya haraNa nipuNa caraNE
Caranamkavijana manO gEhini pAhi mAm gAna mOhini SvEta kamala vAsini
tava padAbjAm vinA tArakO namE sarvair vinutE sakalE namastE
Madhyamakala sahityamSvEta vasanE Subha nAda vINAkarE vEda pustaka japa mAlAdharE varE
SrIdharAdi sura brnda susEvitE Srtajana manassukha dAyikE SrIkarE
