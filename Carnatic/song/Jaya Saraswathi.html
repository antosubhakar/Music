  Lyrics - English:

Pallavijaya sarasvati janani sharaNam jagajjIvana kAraNa rUpiNi
AnupallavidayApUraNE indu vadanE suradanE dAridrya haraNa nipuNa caraNE
Caranamkavijana manO gEhini pAhi mAm gAna mOhini shvEta kamala vAsini
tava padAbjAm vinA tArakO namE sarvair vinutE sakalE namastE
Madhyamakala sahityamshvEta vasanE shubha nAda vINAkarE vEda pustaka japa mAlAdharE varE
shrIdharAdi sura brnda susEvitE shrtajana manassukha dAyikE shrIkarE
