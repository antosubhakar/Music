PallaviEntha punyave , Gopi entha bhagyave,
Yashodhe intha maganana kanene
AnupallaviChinthisadaru doraka cheluva raja gopala,
Bhranthi mathugal allave , Bahu nijave.
Charanam 1Sarasija nabhana summane kandare durithavellavu poudhe,
Sarasa dindali omme savi mathadithare harusha kai kooduvathe YTasodhe.
Charanam 2OOrolage ille nere horeyaranjike dhooru thumbithallave,
Aranya dalli navu aadithadha sukha yariikidaru ondhe ne
Charanam 3Ninna Magana kareya , yenna pranadha doreya , ghannannu Parabrahmame,
Cenna Sri Purandara vittala rayana ninna aane bidalrena kele Yasodhe.
