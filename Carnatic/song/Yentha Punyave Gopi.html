  Lyrics - English:

PallaviEntha punyave , Gopi entha bhagyave,
YaSodhe intha maganana kanene
AnupallaviChinthisadaru doraka cheluva raja gopala,
Bhranthi mathugal allave , Bahu nijave.
Caranam 1sharasija nabhana summane kandare durithavellavu paudhe,
sharasa dindali omme savi mathadithare haruSa kai kUduvathe YTasodhe.
Caranam 2OOrolage ille nere horeyaraJjike dhUru thumbithallave,
Aranya dalli navu Adithadha sukha yariikidaru ondhe ne
Caranam 3Ninna Magana kareya , yenna pranadha doreya , ghannannu Parabrahmame,
Cenna shri Purandara vittala rayana ninna Ane bidalrena kele Yasodhe.
