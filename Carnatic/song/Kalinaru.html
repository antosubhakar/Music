Pallavikali narulaku mahimalu
telipi(y)Emi phalam(a)na lEdA
Anupallaviilanu velayu vara vRshabh(A)dulak-
(a)Tukula ruci teliyu candamugA nI (kali)
CaranamdAra sutulakai dhanamulakai Uru
pErulakai bahu pedda tanamukai
sAreku bhakta vEsamu konu vAriki
tAraka nAma SrI tyAgarAj(A)rcita (kali)
