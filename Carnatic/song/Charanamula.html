PallavicaraNamulE nammiti nI divya caraNamulE nammiti
Caranam 1vAridhi gaTTina vara bhadrAcala varadA varadA varadA nI divya
Caranam 2Adi purusha nannaramara sEyakumayyA ayyA ayyA nI divya
Caranam 3vanamuna rAtini vanitaga jEsina caraNamu caraNamu caraNamu nI divya
Caranam 4pAdAravindamE AdhAramani nEnu paTTiti paTTiti paTTiti nI divya
Caranam 5veyyAru vidhamula kuyyAliJci rAvayyA ayyA ayyA nI divya
Caranam 6bAguga nannElu bhadrAcala rAmadAsuDa dAsuDa dAsuDa nI divya
