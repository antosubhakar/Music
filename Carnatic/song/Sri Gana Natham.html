pallaviSrI gaNa nAthaM bhajAm(y)aham
SrI-karaM cintit(A)rtha phaladam
anupallaviSrI guru guh(A)grajam agra pUjyam
SrI kaNTh(A)tmajam Srita sAmrAjyam (SrI)
