PallavikumbhESvarAya namastE SrI maGgaLAmbAsamEtAya namastE namastE
Anupallavitumburu nArada sannuta caraNAya uttuGga kamanIya vRshabhArOhitAya ambudhi kAvErI tIrasthita sukhakarAya SaGkarAya
CaranamkumAraguruguhapUjitAya umAramaNAya mahESvarAya hEmagiripITha sthitAya carmAmbara SaSi SEkharAya nirmala hRdaya vilasitAya nityaSuddha buddha muktAya nityOtsava niraJjanAya
