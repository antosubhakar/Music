PallavishrIsha padmanAbha pAhi dEva dEva mAm jaya jaya
Caranam 1AgamasAra paramahamsa vihAra bhOgirAja bhOga shayana bhuva mOhana jaya jaya
Caranam 2vAraNatApa vihata vAmana rUpa sArasa sanAdivandya sArasa nEtra jaya jaya
