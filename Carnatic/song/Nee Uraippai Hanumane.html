  Lyrics - English:

pallavinI URaippAy anumAne nAn sonnadhAga
unnai yAr ena vinavidil srI rAghava dhUthan enRu
(srI)
caraNam 1muni orutthan yAsika pin senRu dATakayai
panai pOl vizha aDiththen pagaruvAy I sollai
(dhanyAsi)
caraNam 2koDum kA naDandhu vara kUTAdhenRu nAn solla
naDunginaL vAvenRen nagaiththAL appodhu mella
(kAnada)
caraNam 3mAnai piDitthu en vaSam tArum enRu
nI uraitthadhinAley vanda vinai thAn idhu
(vasantha)
