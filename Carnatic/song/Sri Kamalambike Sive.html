PallaviSrI kamalAmbikE SivE pAhi mAM lalitE
SrI-pati vinutE sitAsitE Siva sahitE
Samasti caranamrAkA candra mukhI rakshita kOla mukhI
ramA vANI sakhI rAja yOga sukhI
Madhyamakala sahityamSAkambhari SAtOdari candra kalA dhari
