PallavivandEham SivE brhadAnandE vandita sura brndE lAlitha skandE
AnupallaviSrIdhara sahajAtE karuNArasa bharitE vEdanuta sucaritE sadASiva sahitE sadA bhaktajana pOshaNA saktE purANa kathana(kadana)kutUlayutE girirAjasutE
CaranamSAsvata kaivalya pradhAna vikhyAtE SrIvidyA tOshitE paramAdvaitayutE
aSva gaja padAti samyuta sainya sahitE atri vasishTAdi mahAmuni pUjitE
aSvatta pippala bilva tulasIvana vAsastE anAdi viSva cakra madhyastE
