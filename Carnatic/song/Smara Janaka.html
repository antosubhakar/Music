  Lyrics - English:

Pallavismara janaka Suba caritAkhila lOkanAyaka mAmava
AnupallavitaruNa sArasadaLa nayana tapanIya rucivasana dhara tanuvinindita nIradAdrta nAradAnata bhUrida
Caranam 1pApa dahana sura vimata pATana prasita SrIpatE jalanidhISayana bhava tApa nASana paTutara
Caranam 2pAvana suguNa nirupama bhAnu SaSi nayana dEvadEva purAri kamalajati vishadati bhavanti ta
Caranam 3pAlitabhuvana samudaya paramapurusha harE phAlaSObhita surabhimrgamada padmanAbha jagannuta
