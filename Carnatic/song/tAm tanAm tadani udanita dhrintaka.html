  Lyrics - English:

PallavitAm tanAm tadani udanita dhrintaka tanOm tamOm tanOm tanOm nAdru dhrutAni
tOm dhru dhrudAna dAni dAni udani tanita tajhOm tadhAm dhaNam tadIm nAdru
tillAna tari taddi kiTa taka jham jham taddhi kiTa taka jham jham
CaranampadmanAbha maNImAlAlaGkrta padmanAbham bhUpAlam vibhum
pariNata muraLI vAdana lOlana niratantam sadA dEhi mudam sukham
