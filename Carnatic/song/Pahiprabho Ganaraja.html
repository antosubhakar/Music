  Lyrics - English:

PallavipAhi prabhO gaNarAjamA mam pAhi prabhO sadA SivAtmaja
AnupallavidEhi sukham mE kuru nirvighnam mOhana muraLIgAna vinOda
CaranamAdi dEva tava sEvaka jana gaNa mOda dAyaka vinAyaka
Adi pUjita mOdaka suma pASAdi dhara vAraNAnana karuNaya
