PallavilakshaNamulu-gala rAmuniki
pradakshiNam(o)narintumu rArE
Anupallavikukshini brahm(A)NDambul(u)nna(v)aTa
vicakshuNuD(a)Ta dIkshA guruD(a)Ta Subha (lakshaNa)
CaranamlakshaNa lakshyamu-gala Srutulaku pratyakshamb(au)naTa
