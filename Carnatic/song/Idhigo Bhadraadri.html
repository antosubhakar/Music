  Lyrics - English:

PallaviidigO bhadrAdri gautami adigO chUDanDi
Caranam 1mudamuto sItA mudita lakSmaNulu
kalasi koluvaga raghupati yunDeDi
Caranam 2chAru svarNa prAkAra gOpura
dvAramulatO sundara mai yunDeDi
Caranam 3anupamAnamai ati sundara mai
danaru chakramu dhaga dhaga meriseDi
Caranam 4kali yugamanduna ila vaikunTamu nalaru chunnadi nayamuga mrokkuDi
Caranam 5ponnala pogaDala pU podarinDlanu
chennu mIgaDanu sriGgAram baDu
Caranam 6shrI karamuga rAmdAsunu
prAkaTa muga brOchE prabhu vAsamu
