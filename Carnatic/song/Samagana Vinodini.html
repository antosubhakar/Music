PallavisAmagAna vinOdini nI nAmamE nA jIvanamu
AnupallavikAma damana manOramA nA manasuna ramincuma kAmada padA ninnu sadA prEmatO namminAnu gadA
CaranamkAmEshvara mUla dhanamA nA manavini amba vinumA kAma janakAdulu bhajincE madhura nAmA
kAma sanjIvaneLaSadhamA hEmaishala puNya balamA tAmasamEmi lalitammA nA mahA bhAgyamA brOva
