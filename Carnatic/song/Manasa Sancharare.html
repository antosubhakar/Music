  Lyrics - English:

pallavimAnasa sancararE brahmaNI mAnasa sancararE
caraNam 1madaSikhi pincchAlankrta cikurE mahanIya kapOla vijitamukurE
caraNam 2SrI ramaNI kuca durga vihArE sEvaka jana mandira mandArE
