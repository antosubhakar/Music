  Lyrics - English:

Pallavimarakata maNi varNa rAma nannu
maravaka nA(y)anna
Anupallaviparama purusha binna brOvumu
dEvara SaraN(a)nukonna (marakata)
Caranamvara bhakta su-parNa vAhana
karuNA rasa pUrNa
dharaNi tanayak(u)nna prEma rasamu
tyAgarAjuk(I)(y)anna (marakata)
