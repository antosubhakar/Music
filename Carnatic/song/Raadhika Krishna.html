  Lyrics - English:

Pallavi. sthanavinihithamapi hAram udhAramsA manuthE krusa thanurathi bhAram rAdhikA kriSNa , rAdhikA virahE thava kEsava
. sarasam asruNamapi malayaja paGkampasyathi viSamiva vapuSi sasaGkam
. svasitha pavanam anupama pariNAhammadhana dhahana miva vahathi sadhAham
. dhisi dhisi kirathi sajala gaNa jAlamnayana naLinam iva vigaLitha nALam
. nayana viSayamapi kisalaya thalpamkalayathi vihitha hudAsana kalpam
. thyajathi na pANi thalEna kapOlambAla sasinam iva sAyam alOlam
. haririthi haririthi japathi sakAmamviraha vihitha maraNEva nikAmam
. srI jayadEvabhaNitham ithi gIthamsukhayathu kEsava padham upanIdham
