  Lyrics - English:

PallaviBadukidenu Badukidenu Bhava Yenage HiGgithu |
Paduanabhana Padadolume Yenagayithu |
Caranam 1Hari thIrtha prasAda jihvege dorakithu |
Hariya NAmamrutha Kivigodagithu |
Hariya dAsaru yennaya bandhugaladaru | Hariya srI mudre yenagabharanavayithu ||
Caranam 2MuktharAdaru yenna nUrentu kuladavaru |
Mukthi Margake yogya nAnAdenu |
AkalaGka shhrI hari bhakuthige mana beledu _rukmini arasa kaivaSanAda yenage ||
Caranam 3Indenna jIvake sakala sAmpadavAyithu |
Mundenna janma sAphalya vayithu thande |
Thande SrI kaginele KeSavarAya | bandenna hrudayadali neleyagi ninda ||
