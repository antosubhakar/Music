PallaviAnanda naTanam AdinAL shakti anbar kaLikkavE parAshakti naTanam AdinAr
AnupallavivAnum bhuviyum vaNangi magizhndiDa jnAna veLiyinil vINai Om Om en
Caranam 1panca bhUtangal muzhanga guNangaL pAindu tALam vazhanga
Anju tozhilum tulanga kAlam adira bhuvigal kulunga
Tanjam tanjam enrul anbar muraiyiDat-tantAm tantAm tAkiTa takadhiM enna
Caranam 2kali arakkargaL vizha mINDum krta yuga dEvar vAzha-poliyunangal shUzha
mangalap- pudiya vAzhkkai ponga shalippillAmal sadA shivanuDan
tadingiNatOM tadingiNatOm taLAngu tarikiTatOm ena
