PallavisadAnandamu sarvESvarA nI padAravindamupai bhakti
Caranam 1nayanAnandamu narulaku suralaku jayamagu hari nI sAkAramu
nayamagu SravaNAnandamu vininanu kriya galigina nI kIrtanamu
Caranam 2celagi andariki jihvAnandamu palumaru gonu nI prasAdamu
nalugaDa dEhAnandamu budhulaku balunIpAda praNAmamulu
Caranam 3dhara paramAnandamu nI dAsyamu garimala SrIvEGkaTavibhuDA
narahari nityAnandamu ninunDaga naraviri jEyu samArAdhanamu
