PallavimA(y)ammA nannu brOv(av)ammA mahA mAyA umA
Anupallavisaty(A)nandA sAnandA nity(A)nandA AnandA amba (mAyammA)
CaranamSyAma kRshNa jananI tAmasam(E)la rAvE dEvI
SyAmaLE nIl(O)tpalE him(A)cala sutE suphalE SivE (mAyammA)

sundari ninnE hRdayamu paTTukoni (mAyammA)
