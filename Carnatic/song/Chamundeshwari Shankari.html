  Lyrics - English:

PallavicAmuNDeSvari SaGkari mAmava sAmagAna priyE saundari
AnupallavikAma janaka sOdari gauri kamanIya gAtri kAtyAyani
Caranamparama bhakta viSvAsini paramESvara svarUpiNi
parama dayAkari bhArgavi parvata nandini bharaNi pOshiNi
