Pallavimahita pravRddha SrImati
guha gaNa pati janani
AnupallavipAhi vadana jita sudhA-karE SrI-karE
pAhi suguNa ratnAkarE (mahita)
Caranam 1dEhi caraNa bhaktim-akhila
dEhini sadA Subha
phaladE hima giri tanayE
vaidEhI-pa sahOdari (mahita)
Caranam 2vAhin(I)Sa sannutE
nav(A)hi bhUsha vallabhE
bhav(A)hi nIla kaNThi simha
vAhini janani (mahita)
Caranam 3pArtha sannuta priyE
pad(A)rthE apuNya dUrE
kAmit(A)rtha phaladE SrI
tapastIrtha pura nivAsini (mahita)
Caranam 4rAja SEkhar(A)tma bhU
virAja rAja sannutE
sarOja daLa nibh(A)kshi tyAga-
rAja bhAgya dAyaki (mahita)
