  Lyrics - English:

PallavisAramaina mATalanda cAlu cAlu rA
AnupallavisArasAksha mImentO santOshamu mElu
Caranam 1sOmuDu daya mAyanEmi sudatiki mahA bhAgya mAyanE kAmakOTi
sundara mA bhAgya miTulAyanE
Caranam 2manavi vinara sAmi ninnu nammiyunnAnu marEmi ghanuDaina
