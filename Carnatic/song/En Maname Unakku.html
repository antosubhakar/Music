  Lyrics - English:

PallaviEn manamE unakkinda bhayam ingedu varumO ikkaliyil namakkena ulaindEn
AnupallavimAn maruvum karattemmAn paravum perumAn guhanirukka namakku urumO apAyam
CaranamjnAnAkara vElan nal nAmanArAyaNi bAlan navarasa gAnAmuda shIla
kavi kunjaradAsanai kaNmaNi pOl kATTiDavE kAttirukkavE
