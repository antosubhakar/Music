  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSAradE pAhimAm sarOruha nibhapradE sArasAksha SrI vAsudEva karuNAnvitE varadE
AnupallavinIrajAsana jAyE nikhila vidyApradE nAradAdi sakala muni vinutE suradE
CaranamcAru vINAdi su-SObhitakarE hIramaNi hAra lasita kandarE
vArNEndra gamanE nata sura nikarE vArijAkshaNa rucira bimbadharE
