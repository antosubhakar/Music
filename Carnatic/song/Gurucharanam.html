  Lyrics - English:

PallavigurucaraNaM bhaja mAnuja gurutarANga nathanaM shrI shankara
AnupallavigurucaraNaM bhaja mAnuja gOviNda yativarya shrI tyAgasAgaraM
CaranamkEraLa dEsha vikasitAmaNiM kEvala advaita prakAshakaM sAradhara saNgItAdi dAyakaM sarasa tara varadadAsa pOSakaM
