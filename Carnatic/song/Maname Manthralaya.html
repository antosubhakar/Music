  Lyrics - English:

PallavimanavE mantralayA (nanna) nelesilli guruvE karunAmayA
Caranam 1tandeyu tAyiyu bandhuvu nInE tamavanu kaLeyuva dIvige nInE kalmaSa toLeva gangA jala nInE kambani oresuva karunALu nInE
Caranam 2karedallige baruva kAmadhEnu kAmitagaLanIva kalpataru manjuLavINa gAnavinOdini nI mahimAnvita mantrAlaya vAsi
Caranam 3narahari padakamala sEvipa bhramara maraLi dharegilida guru rAghavEndra shira bAgi namisuve tava padma charaNage karunadi kAyO guru sArvabhaumA
