  Lyrics - English:

Pallavigata mOh(A)Srita pAl(A)dbhuta sItA ramaNa
Caranam 1bhava sArasa bhava mAnasa bhavan(A)mara vinuta (gata)
Caranam 2bhava tAraka sava pAlana bhavad(A)Sara haraNa (gata)
Caranam 3vinatAja gamana rAghava muni pUjita caraNa (gata)
Caranam 4Sata kOTi carita mAnava mata bhEdaka damana (gata)
Caranam 5kara SObhita Sara pApa timira bhAskara suguNa (gata)
Caranam 6Saraj(A)nana karuNAkara vara vAraNa SaraNa (gata)
Caranam 7nata mAnasa hita kara pAlita tyAgarAja (gata)
