  Lyrics - English:

PallavinAma kIrtanamE gati namakku naTanArAyaNan naLina daLAyata lOcanan divya
AnupallavikAma lObha mOhAdigaL uDaittu kAlam kaN Indanil kAkUttan divya
Caranamvijita karmaGgaLai viraTTi viTTOm nAm avijita karmaGgaLai vAvA enRazhaippOm
mahimai tAGgum mahanIyargaL manamadil viLindu santatam aruL varadadAsan pOTRum
