  Lyrics - English:

pallaviDangurava sArirayya dingarigarellaru
anupallavibhUmanDalake pAnDuranga viTHala paradaivavendu
caraNam 1hariyu mudida hUvu harivANadoLiTTukonDu
harushHadindADi pADi kuNidu kuppaLisuta
caraNam 2oDala jAgaTeya mADi nuDiva nAligeya piDidu
DHam DHam DHaNADHaNArendu hoDedu cappALikuta
caraNam 3intu jagakkella lakSmIkAntanendu
santata purandara viTHala paradaivavendu ||Dangurava|
