  Lyrics - English:

PallavivandE mahESvaram indu kalAdharam vAgISAdi vinuta vaibhavam
Anupallavidanda Suka nikara dAma SObhitagaLam nandanIya sucIndra nagara nAyakam ISam
Caranam 1bhAsita dviguNitOrusita bhAsam vara paraJjAtArtha Tanta krta vAsam purO-
lasita Samyakam pAlitadAsam budha laghu pApa haramati mrdu hAsam carma-
vAsanam bhUta samvrtam vAsava sucikarama sadrSa mahimAnam ambikApraNESam
Caranam 2harihara suta guha parivrtam mataGgAnana pOshakama viratam vrshavara
nija vAhana sEvitam mrdu vacana nanditASEsha surajAtam lOka-
SaraNyam gaGgAdharam SAnta yOginiSEvyam smaraNIya padayugam sAmbam Subhakaram
Caranam 3madana dAhaka bhAlatala nEtram tripura mArutOragam dEvanutipAtram vidhu-
vadanam vAsuki kaTi sUtram kunda radanamalaGkrta rAjata gOtram lOkam
udita rathOtsavam mOhanatama rUpam padanata sadyam SrI padmanAbha tOshiNam

லசித ஷம்யகம் பாலிததாசம் புத லகு பாப ஹரமதி ம்ர்து ஹாசம் சர்ம-
