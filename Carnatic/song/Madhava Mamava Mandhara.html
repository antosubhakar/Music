  Lyrics - English:

pallavimAdhava mAmava mandara nagadhara marakata maNinibha surucira sundara
anupallavikOdhana paripAlana SIla uragOdhara SayanAravinda mayana
caraNamnAda madu vinOda madhusUdana vAdita muraLI mrdu sangIta
yAdava kula pAvana kAraNa mamAdhAra bhavapriyA sahOdara
