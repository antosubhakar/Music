AnupallaviSempon malai Silai vaLaitta divya paramAnanda SambhO SivaSaGkara SrI tANDava naTEsA
Caranam 1gaGgai matiyaravak-kAdirk-kuzhai aSaiya SeGgai mazhuttuDi aSaiya Saiva SaDai aSaiyap-
