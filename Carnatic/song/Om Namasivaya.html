  Lyrics - English:

pallaviOm namaSivAya Sivaguru nandanAya OmkAra vAsAya OjasvinE namaha (Om)
anupallaviSrI nAtha vidyAdi susEvitAya siddhAnta tatvAya SrI SankarAya (Om)
caraNammuktva haraNAya mOksha pradAya mUrkhAdi vandya mukha cidvilAsAya
SOkAdi haraNAya surESvarAya sAnanda abhirAma purESvarAya (Om)
