  Lyrics - English:

Pallavi. maJjuthara kuJjathala kELi sadhanEiha vilasa rathi rabhasa hasitha vadhanE pravisa rAdhE mAdhava samIpamkuru murArE maGgaLa sathAni
. nava bhavatha sOkadhaLa sayana sArEiha vilasa kuchakalasa tharaLahArE
. chala malaya vana pavana surabhisIthEiha vilasa rasavalitha laLitha gIthE
. kusumachaya rachithasuchi vAsa gEhEiha vilasa kusuma sukumAra dhEhE
. madhu tharaLa pika nikara ninadha mukharEiha vilasa dasana ruchi ruchira sikharE
. vidhatha bahu valli nava pallavakhanEiha vilasa pIna kucha kumbajaghanE
. madhumudhitha madhupakula kalitha ravEiha vilasa madhana sara rabhasa bhAvE
. vihitha pdmAvathi sukha samAjEbhaNathi jayadEva kavi rAja rAjE
