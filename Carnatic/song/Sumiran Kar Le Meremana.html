  Lyrics - English:

pallavishumiran karle mere mana,
teri bIti umar hari nAm bina,
caraNam 1KUpa nIr bin dhenu chhIr bin,
dharti megha bina,
jaise taruvar phal bina hIna,
taise prAni hari nAm bina,
caraNam 2Deha naina bin raina chandra bin
mandir dIp nina
jaise pandit ved bhina
taise prAni hari nAm bina
caraNam 3KAm krodh mad lobh nirAdo
chAd de sab sant jana
kahe nAnak Sah sun bhagvanta
ya jag main nahi koi apna

तैसे प्रानि हरि नाम् बिन
