  Lyrics - English:

pallavirAga rasika sirOnmaNi
yoga mOkSa pradambanu
anupallavisAgara Sayanuni rANi
SrIkara prasannavadani
caraNammangaLa mUlI! SrI
vEnkaTapati hrudayamElu
sankIrvANi pada
pankajamunu sEvimpa jEsina
