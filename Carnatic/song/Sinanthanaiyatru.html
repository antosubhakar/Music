  Lyrics - English:

pallavisinandanai yaTRup piriyamum tAn aTRuc ceykaiyaTRu
ninaindadumaTRu, ninaiyA maiyumaTRu, nirccindanAyt
tanandani yEyirundu Ananda nittirai tanguginRa
anandalil enRiruppEn attanE! kayilAyattanE!
