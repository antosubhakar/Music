PallaviGopikaa manoharam bhajeham
govardhana giridharam suravaram muraharam
Samashti charanamTaapasa hridaya vesham ramesam
taapaarthihara vinutham natha chaturam

aparaarjitham ananta vedagosham amaresanuta guruguha santosham
