pallaviakhil(A)ND(E)SvarI durusuga brOvumu
anupallavinikhila tApa hAriNI bhuvilOna
ninu mincina vAr(e)var(u)nnAr(a)mmA (akhila)
caraNam 1mANikya mayamai(y)unna
mandira madhya vAsinI ali
vENI SrI Sambhu nAthuni rANI
varam(I)yavE gIrvANI mA(y)ammA (akhila)
caraNam 2ambhO-ruha sambhava hari Sankara
akhila mun(I)ndra pUjitA ati
gambhIrA dIna rakshaNI
gadA nA moralanu vina lEdA (akhila)
caraNam 3O ambA ninu nammina nApai
inta parAmukham(E)la vinu
SyAma krushNa nutA cinta dIrci
sAmrAjyam(I)yavE vEgamE (akhila)
