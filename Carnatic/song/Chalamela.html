pallavicalamEla jEsEvayya cakkani mA rangayya |
anupallavikala kAlamu nI pAdamulE gatiyani nammi yuNDaga
muktAyi sAhityamkari moraliDa vini, rayamuna veDali, makarini dunimi,
kari-varadu-Dani birudu dharanu velasi, suralu pogaDa galigina
patita pAvanuDa! nA manavi vina ||ca||
caraNamnannu brOva nIku bhAramA
svara sAhityam 1idE bAgani UrakAnu nIvunna ninu nE viDacedanA
svara sAhityam 2nIdu smaraNa gAka vEre eruganu, nIraja nayana, danuja mada haraNa
svara sAhityam 3anDANDamulaku-nadhipati-vani niNDa moraliDaga, vinI-vinaka yuNDa
sari-yagunA? madana janakuNDa, varamosagi anudinamu
svara sAhityam 4A draupadiki valuva sabhalO tarugakanu osagina mahA daya gala ninu
pogaDanu nA taramagunA, siri velayu rangapurini Elina pati,
nAgala rangani komaruNDa, telisi teliyaka nadacina palu naDatalanu
madi talupa valadu, valadu, SaraNu murahari
