  Lyrics - English:

PallaviharinAmamu kaDu nAnaMdakaramu | marugavO marugavO marugavO manasA ||
Caranam 1naLinAkShu SrInAmamu | kalidOShaharamu kaivalyamu |
PalasAramu bahubaMdha mOcanamu | talacavO talacavO manasA ||
Caranam 2nagadharu nAmamu narakaharaNamu | jagadEkahitamu sammatamu |
saguNa nirguNamu sAkShAtkAramu | pogaDavO pogaDavO pogaDavO manasA ||
Caranam 3kaDagi SrIvEMkaTapati nAmamu | oDi oDinE saMpatkaramu |
aDiyAlaM bila nati suKamUlamu | taDavavO taDavavO taDavavO manasA ||
