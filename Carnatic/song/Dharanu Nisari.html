pallavidharanu nI sari daivamu kAnarA raghuvara
caraNam 1SrI-d(A)ja vandana sindhu bandhana
SrI daSaratha nandana Srita hrut-syandana (dha)
caraNam 2pApa vimOcana pankaja lOcana
prAp(a)guTaku yOcanA bhakta pAlana (dha)
caraNam 3sAla vidAraNa sarva lOk(A)vana
pAlaya candr(A)nana patita pAvana (dha)
caraNam 4indirA bhAgyamA ina kul(O)ttamA
candamu kanupimpumA satta mAtramA (dha)
caraNam 5pAd(A)ravindamu bhAvanak(a)ndamu
nI daya dhana brundamu nity(A)nandamu (dha)
caraNam 6vAg-ISa sannuta tyAgarAj(A)rcita
nAga Sayana pAlita nata su-carita (dha)
