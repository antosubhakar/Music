PallaviviLaiyATa itu nEramA murukA-en
vinaiyAlE paDum pADu tanai solla varumpOdu (viLaiyADa)
AnupallavikaLaittEn janmam eDuttu iLaittEn poRuttirundu
uLamAra unai nADi unai pADi varumpOdu (viLaiyADa)
