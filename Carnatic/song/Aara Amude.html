  Lyrics - English:

PallaviArA amutE aracE AnantaveLLap
pErARE inpap perukkE parAparamE. .
Caranam 1ettikkun tAnAki ennitayat tEyURit
tittikkum Anantat tEvE parAparamE. .
Caranam 2anpaip perukkiena tAruyiraik kAkkavanta
inpap perukkE iRaiyE parAparamE. .
Caranam 3pArAyO ennaimukam pArttorukAl enkavalai
tIrAyO vAytiRantu ceppAy parAparamE. .
Caranam 4OyAtO enkavalai uLLEA nanta veLLam
pAyAtO aiyA pakarAy parAparamE. .
Caranam 5eNNAta eNNamellAm eNNieNNi EzhaineJcam
puNNAkac ceytatinip pOtum parAparamE. .
Caranam 6eGkeGkE pArttAlum evvuyirkkum avvuyirAy
aGkaG kiruppatunI anRO parAparamE. .
Caranam 7anparpaNi ceyyaenai ALAkki viTTuviTTAl
inpanilai tAnEvan teyvatum parAparamE..
