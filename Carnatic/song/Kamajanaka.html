  Lyrics - English:

PallavikAma janaka ripugaNa madahara kAmita tAnalOla paripAhi cArumukha
AnupallavibhUmIramAdi nAda bhOgi bhOga shayanAdi mEcaka vibhO munIndratata sEvya padayuga
Caranam 1pATalaja pAdharAnakha bhAnu shashAnka lOcana hATakOpamita
cEla bhUri rucihAra shObhita gaLAmbuja lOcana
Caranam 2mOhana kirITa bhAsita mOdakarAtma bhASaNa dEhalAlasita
divya candana su-dIna lOka paripAla nAticaNa
Caranam 3vAraNa samAnayAna nivArita pApa sancayabhatayIsha mada
nAshakAdi guNa pamanAbha dhrta shankha cakradhara
