  Lyrics - English:

PallaviAnjanEyam sadA bhAvayAmi apramEyam mudA cintayAmi
AnjanAnandam vAnarESam varam pancavaktram surESAdui vandyam
guruguha hitam SAntam sEvita SrI rAmapAdapankajam
sanjIvi parvatakaram mukhAbjam sadA rAmacandram bhajE
