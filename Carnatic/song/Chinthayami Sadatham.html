  Lyrics - English:

pallavichiMtaya sadA vAraNa vadanaM
chittaparASakti sahitamEkadaMtaM
anupallavisatataM mahAmuninuta charaNaM
madhyamakAla sAhityamchiMtitArtha phaladAyaka chidAnaMdaM
chitprakASa kRuti nipuNaM
caraNamanaMtAnaMta suguNa sAMdraM
anAdiSrIguru svarUpadharaM
anityavyavahAra baMdhananivArakaM
AnaMdakara mOkshhapadadAyakaM (mOkshhapradAnanipuNaM)
madhyamakAla sAhityamparatatva pratipAdita praNavasvarUpaM SrI vidyAtmakaM ||
parAdichatvArivAkpratipAditaM siMdhurAmakriyAtihitaM ||
