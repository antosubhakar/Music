pallavipurushOttama mAm pAlaya vAsudEva karuNAkara kamala nayana dEvadEva
anupallavigaruDa dvaja bhArgavIsa sarasija bhava puruhUtAdi mavanditAmita vibhava
caraNambhujagAcala vAsa nirata suguNa bharita gaja pAlaka nASa rahita subadha carita
nija dAsanutA vilasita maNI bhUshita rajanISa mukhAngadayuta daSaratha suta
