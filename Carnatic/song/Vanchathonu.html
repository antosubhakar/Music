PallavivAncatOnu nAvagalu delpavE ambA
AnupallavivAncita phaladAyaki shrI vallI guhuni manasu karaga (vaancatOnu)
CharanammAninI nI pAluDau nAmAnamu kApADi abhimAnamuncavalenu
talli marakatavalli kalimAnavulanu brOva nI samAnamevaru
mArAyuta samAna rUpa harikEsha kumAra rANi vanajanEtri
