  Lyrics - English:

PallavimAmava harE mukunda madhu murahara gOvinda
AnupallavisAma gIta cidAnanda suravinuta padAravinda
CaranamkaruNArasAlavAla kalita kalyANa jAla sharaNAgatAnukUla sakala vishva paripAla
