  Lyrics - English:

PallavipankajAkSa pAhi kinkara mAm shrI ramEsha venkaTEsha hriSIkEsha sarva lOkEsha
AnupallavisankaTa mOcaka harE sajjanArthita murArE pankaja bhava praNuta sarva daitya harE
CaranamvAridhi shayana dEva surakOTi tulya shObha vAritAbha padmanAbha shriSTa kaiTabha
kAma rUpa kambukaNtha kAnada vibhO vaikuNTha kAnana tuLasIdhAma bhUSita kaNTha
