  Lyrics - English:

pallavinArAyaNa pankaja caraNa guNa pArAyaNa karOrE mandabadari
anupallavinArada mAnana vilOka SOkApaha narOttama SankarAcArya karArcita vigraha
parAdi sapta svarUpa parama purusha varakaTihasta haraNa kalusha SrIman
caraNamdaya Sama sukha bhOga mOksha dAyaka kamala kaLatra mamaraka nAyaka
amala jala alakanandAkula vimala vihanga raja vrddapAla
amarESOddava anantamudita nija dvijAvanti rAjni nata vandita
dEhOtsarga dEha kaivalya kara gEha SrIvatsAnkita SrIkara nIla dEha
