pallavininu vinA nA madi(y)endu niluvadE SrI hari hari
anupallavikanulaku nI sogas(e)ntO krammi(y)unnadi ganuka (ninu)
caraNam 1nIdu kathalu vInul(a)ndu niNDi(y)unnadi rAma
SrI-da nI nAmamu nOTa celagi(y)unnadi ganuka (ninu)
caraNam 2nEnu(y)ecaTa jUcinanu nIvai(y)unnadi rAma
bhAnu vamSa tilaka nIdu bhaktuD(a)nucu pEru ganuka (ninu)
caraNam 3kapaTamau mATal(e)lla kamman(ai)nadi rAma nA
tapamu yOga phalamu nIvE tyAgarAja sannuta (ninu)
