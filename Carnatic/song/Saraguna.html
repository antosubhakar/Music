pallavisaraguna gAvumu varaguNa nilavE karimA cala vAhanE sadayE
anupallavikaruNArasa pUrita hrdayE muraLI gAna sudhArasa priyE
muktAyi sAhityambhAvamunanu rAgAlApanamu cEsi layamunu manasunanu nilipi
svaramunu SrutilO nilipi ninu muda monarincitani karuNinci varamosagi
eTTugaDa sAhityam 0nI garimagani nI darini jEritini sadA sAma nigama mOdini
eTTugaDa sAhityam 1kAma madada manigadA dharI
eTTugaDa sAhityam 2rEyi pagalu talacukoni nI karuNagoni sArasa hrdilO mAnaka nilipi
eTTugaDa sAhityam 3kAmitamu phalamuliDi surESvara ihamu phalamula sAra midiyani
viSAla vilOcana nIdu mahimalu taramA teliyadE nanu gAvumika
eTTugaDa sAhityam 4sAdhu sujanavara vitaraNa vila sadgAna candrakaLA bharaNi
sAreku hrdi nilavana valyunadi sakala janulu budhulai managavale
nI daya galigina khalamulu parigiDunu sakala sampadalu tAne galugunu
bhArata bhuvijana gaNamulu nokaTai melagu sukhulai jayamu galugunu
