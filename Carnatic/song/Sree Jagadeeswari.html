  Lyrics - English:

PallavishhrI JagadeSvari Durga matha
shvagatham su svagatham
Caranam 1Raja Rajesvari RajIva nethri
shhiva manoraJjani bhava bhaya bhaJjani
Caranam 2shriSti sthithilaya kArini devi
DuSta ney graha siSta palini
Caranam 3ViSva rUpini viSva dharini
PAlayamam sarva papa nASini.
