en pAzhvinaik-kunnai edirkkum dhIramA ammA
Anupallaviannai unakku shatakOTi maghavundu agadi Ezhai adil en pOl unDO

bhAra pakSam Ora vanjanaiyum unDO nAn en sheivEn
