  Lyrics - English:

Pallavibhaja mAdhavam aniSam vAsudEvam bhujagAdi pati Sayanam dEvadEvam
Anupallaviaja rudra surESa vinuta caraNam gajarAja bali prahlAdAdi SaraNam
CaranammAra janakam ambuja nibha nayanam nAradAdi munijana krta namanam
SUra mArIca rAvana madanam SAradEndu vadanam paribhrta bhuvanam
