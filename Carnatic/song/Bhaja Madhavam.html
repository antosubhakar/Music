  Lyrics - English:

Pallavibhaja mAdhavam anisham vAsudEvam bhujagAdi pati shayanam dEvadEvam
Anupallaviaja rudra surEsha vinuta caraNam gajarAja bali prahlAdAdi sharaNam
CaranammAra janakam ambuja nibha nayanam nAradAdi munijana krta namanam
shUra mArIca rAvana madanam shAradEndu vadanam paribhrta bhuvanam
