  Lyrics - English:

PallaviraghunAthA nannu nayamuga nI rakSincuTakidi samayamurA
Anupallaviagha jAlamella harinci bahu AdaraNa sEyu AdidEva
CaranamkanakAmbaradhara karuNAkara kamala bhavanuta kari moraliDaga
kanikaramutO kAvalEdA shrI vanajAkSa shrInivAsa varada
