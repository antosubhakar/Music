  Lyrics - English:

Pallavitana hitavE tana matamu tana sakhuDE taraNI
Anupallavitanuvala cinadE vanita tanu talacinadE kavita
Caranam 1tananu tAnerugu varakE dvaitamu tananu tAnerigina advaitamu
Caranam 2kAla gamanamE kAla jnAnamunu mUla meruguTE muraLI gAnamu
