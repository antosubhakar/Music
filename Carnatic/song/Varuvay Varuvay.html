  Lyrics - English:

PallavivaruvAy varuvAy varuvAy-kaNNA!
varuvAy varuvAy varuvAy!
Caranam 1uruvAy aRivil oLirvAy-kaNNA!
uyirin namudAyp pozhivAy-kaNNA!
karuvAy ennuL vaLarvAy-kaNNA!
kamalat tiruvO TiNaivAy-kaNNA! (varuvAy)
Caranam 2iNaivAy enadA viyilE-kaNNA!
idayat ridanilE yamarvAy-kaNNA!
kaNaivA yasarar talaigaL-sidaRak
kaDaiyU riyilE paDaiyO TezhuvAy! (varuvAy)
Caranam 3ezhuvAy kaDalmI tinilE-ezhumOr
iravik kaNaiyA uLamI tinilE
tozhuvEn sivanAm ninaiyE-kaNNA!
tuNaiyE amarar tozhum vAnavanE! (varuvAy)
