  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviamba nAdu vinnapamu vini nannAdarincuTa kiti samayamamma (amba)
Anupallaviambuja pANi vANi marakatAngi madhura bhASani
shAmbhavi shashAnka vadanE shiva shankari tribhuvana sundari jaga (damba)
Caranam 1vArAhi vaiSnavi sumangali vAmini nigamAgama sancAriNi
shrI rAjarAjEshvari vimala chimayAnanda dAyini trijaga (damba)
Caranam 2shUlini purANi tritApa shamani sOma sUryAgni lOcani vara
pAlini natajana bhaya vidAriNi praNava panjara nivAsini jaga (damba)
Caranam 3caNDa mahiSAdi dAnava mada vibhanjani niranjani manOnmani
pANDava sahAyuDaina venkaTapati sahOdari trikUTa vAsini (amba)
