PallavirUkalu padi vEl(u)nna cEreDu
nUkalu gati kAni O manasA
AnupallavikOkalu veyy(u)nna
kaTTukonuTak(o)kaTi kAni O manasA (rU)
CaranamUr(E)lina tA paNDuTa mUDu mUra tAvu kAni
nUru bhakshaNamul(a)bbina entO nOTik(a)nta kAni

sArataruni harini tyAgarAja sannutuni maravakE manasA (rU)
