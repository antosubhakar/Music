  Lyrics - English:

pallavipadmini vallabha dEhi pradEhi pApatama hara tapana grharAja
bhAgavatajana hitakara tavapada bhakti sudhArasam dEhi mAm pAhi SrI
anupallavitatvAcamana sarvagyAna SrI vidyOpAsana vivaraNa virOcana
satya dharma paripAlana karNAsAgara vikasita sArasa caraNA
sapta taranga rathAntara gamanA chAyA mukha ramaNa lOka jIvana
caraNammahA padmAtavI pUrva bhAgE prakASa vardhULAkAra
analArgya pAtrAdi kAriNE sahasrAma mAlA dhAriNE
tapinyAdi dvAdaSakalA rUpiNE SrI hari Sankara
brahmmAtmaNE dEva dEva raghuvamSoddhIrNa kAriNE
(madhyamakAlam)
ahankAramada vishankAdi hara navAvaraNa sancAriNE
matangAdinuta parandAma jaya bhava sAgara bhaya nivAriNE SrI
