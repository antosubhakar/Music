  Lyrics - English:

Pallavibhaja mAnasa sarasvatIm Subha caritAm vidhi dayitAm
nija pAda pankaja vinatA Srtajana pOshaNa niratAm
AnupallavisujanEshTa vEda sadanAm nija pANI dhrta varavINAm
dvija rAja sannibha vadanAm gajarAja manda gamanAm
CaranamdEva dEva SrI vAsudEva paramAnugraha bharitAm
bhAvanAti dUra nirupama mahimAtiSaya samanvitAm
bhAvarAga tALAdi su-madhura sangIta rasA svAdhinIm
kaivalya mArga bOdhaka supratibhA visEsha dAyinIm
