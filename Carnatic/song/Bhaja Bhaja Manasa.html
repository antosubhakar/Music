  Lyrics - English:

PallaviBhaja bhaja manasa Raghavendram , guru Raghavendram,
Yati Raghavendram
AnupallaviThungaa nadhi theeram punyasthala vasam ,
Manthralaya kshethram , Brindavna vasam.
CaranamDheena jana Priya gana rasa poornam ,
Prahlada avatharam kinchitha roopam,
Phalguna sangama gunashila namam ,
Mruthika kaliyuga parama oushadham.
