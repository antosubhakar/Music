AnupallavivAsAnisham mama jagadIshvara vairijAla dharacApa vishikhadhara
Caranam 1hEma cAru ruci madahara nirupama cEla hita sajjana samudaya pAla dasha kANTha vinAshana kAmakOTi sundarapata natajana kAma dAyaka kaTAkSa nirIkSaNa himakara sama ruci vadana parilasita

pArijAta suma vAsita kaca bhara sharadAmburuha nEtra yugAmala dinakara suta bhayahara
