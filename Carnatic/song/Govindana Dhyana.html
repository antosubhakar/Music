pallavigOvindana dhyAna balu SubhakaravO
anupallavibhAvisi nODalu bahu janmagaLa pApa haravO
caraNam 1ondAvarti neneyalu bhavabhanda khilavO hindina satkrtake mundina sukrtAnanda balavO
caraNam 2vidha vidha sAdhanakinta idu ghanavO udaramBharaNakke uDuvO vasanakke idu susAdhanavO
