Pallavijaya jaya svAmin jaya jaya
Anupallavijaya jaya jitavairi varga pracaNDa jaya jaya gajamukha jaya vakratuNDa
Caranam 1mUshika vAhana munigaNa vandya tOsha rahita duritAsura brnda
SEsha bhUshaNa Saiva vAridhi candra pOshita pari jana puNyaika kanda
Caranam 2lambOdara dhIra lAvaNya sAra kambu sudhAnidhi karpUra gaura
sAmba sadASiva sadkrti catura sAma vEda gIta sakalAdhara
Caranam 3cakrAdi suragaNa sannuta caraNa SAta kumbha maNi divyabharaNa-
dhikrta ghana vighna timirAvaraNa dhIra nArAyaNa tIrtha sukaruNa
