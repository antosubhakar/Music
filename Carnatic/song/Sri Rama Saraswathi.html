  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSrI ramA sarasvatI sEvitAm SrI lalitAm tvAm bhAvayE
caraNamtArasadruSa nAsAmaNI virAjitAm sampatkarI sEvitAm tArAmantriNyAdi parivrutAm dhIraguruguhavinutAm SivayutAm

  Lyrics - Tamil:
