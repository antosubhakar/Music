  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI dharma SAstAram ArAdhayE SrI SabarISvaram bhUdESvaram vIram
AnupallaviSrIdhara sarva kumAram sukumAram SrIkhara viSvESvaram paramOddhAram
Caranamsakala suguNAkaram lOka SaGkaram sajala jaladha sama SyAmasundaram
saubhAgya sanyakaram sarvadharam saugata kAnana tuLasIdaLa dharam
