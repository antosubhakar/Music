chaelaM tyajatae chaeTI bhavAmi //pa//
Caranam 1chala chala mama saM sadghaTanae kiM
kulisha h_rdaya bahuguNa vibhava
pulakita tanu saMbh_rta vaedanayA
malinaM vahAmi madaM tyajAmi || nAlaM ||
