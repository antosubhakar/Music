Pallavivarasiddhi vinAyakA sumukhA
AnupallavisharaNAgatArthi shamanA vAmanA caraNA vighna haraNA vAraNA
CaranamgaNa parivAra gajamukha dhIra guNa mUlAdhArA kumkuma sharIrA
phaNi rAja hAra bhaktavarA dhIrA harikEsha tanayA sundarA
