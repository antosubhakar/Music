  Lyrics - English:

Pallavitalachina vAru dhanyulayyEru
Anupallavitalachi vEDukoni golichina vAriki
phalamulanosagE lalitAmba ninnu
Caranam 1kOri sannidhini jEri ne pada
vArijamula manasAraga golichi
Caranam 2koJchepu narula noppinpaga ninu madi
nuJchi chAla bhajiJchi niratamu
Caranam 3sura ripula damana kumAra janani
karuNA rasAkSi kaumAri yani sadA
