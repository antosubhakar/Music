PallavivadarasanE shrI guru prabhAvam vAncita phalam dadAti
Anupallavipadara jO vijita shiSya samUhA pApajula hari bhakti varEti
Caranam 1kAkarlAnvaya kala sapayOnitau kaLAdhara sama rUpada rEti
nAga sthita gandharva samAna nAdOdbhava gAna rasa dhUriNEti
Caranam 2bhUsura vara nI rAmabrahma pUrva janma krta bhUri puNyEna
vasudEvAmsha nArada vara yOgi tyAgarAja hakyA AtmajObhUditi
Caranam 3rAma nAma sudhArasa paripUrNa rAja mAnavara rasanAgrEti
rAmacandrapura vara shrI vEnkaTaramaNa bhAgavata shrI guruvarEti
