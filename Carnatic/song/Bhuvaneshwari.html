  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhuvanESvari brhannAyakI nava candra kalAdhari bhArgavI
Anupallavibhava sAgara vimOcani bhavapriya OmkAriNI
CaranamnhavAni SaGkara mOhini dEvAdi pUjita mAnini
kuvalayadaLa nayani kAmini bharaNI vinuta paramahamsini
