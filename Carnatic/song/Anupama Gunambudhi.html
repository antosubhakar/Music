Pallavianupama guN(A)mbudhi(y)ani ninnu nera nammi-
(y)anusarincina vADanaiti
AnupallavimanupakayE(y)unnAvu manu patI vrAsi
mEm(a)nupa mAk(e)varu vinumA daya rAni (anu)
Caranam 1janaka jAmAtavai janakajA mAtavai
janaka jAlamu cAlu cAlunu hari (anupama)
Caranam 2kanaka paTa dhara nannu kana kapaTam(E)la tanu
kanaka paThanamu sEtu kAni pUni (anu)
Caranam 3kalalOna nIvE sakala lOka nAtha
kOkalu lOkuva kan(i)cci kAcinadi vini (anu)
Caranam 4rAja kula kalaS(A)bdhi rAja sura pAla gaja
rAja rakshaka tyAgarAja vinuta (anu)
