  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviamma nannubrOvavE raghurAmuni komma nannu kAvavE
Caranam 1amma nannubrOvu sammati tODa mAyamma vanucu ninnu nemmiti kolicEdan-
Caranam 2kanna talli nIvu kanugoni nA pADu vinnapa monariJci vibhunaku vEga
Caranam 3ullamulOna mI yubhayula neranammi calladanamuna vEDi vEsAriti
Caranam 4calamu mAni bhadrAcala rAmadAsu nalasaTa beTTaga yAdaraNa jEsi
