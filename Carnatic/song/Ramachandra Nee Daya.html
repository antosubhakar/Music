  Lyrics - English:

PallavirAmacandra nI daya rAma(y)Ela rAd(a)ya
AnupallavikAma kOTi sundar(A)kAra dhRta mandara
prEma mIra mundara piluva rAka(y)undurA (rA)
Caranam 1kAnanambu tApamO kaika mIdi kOpamO
nEnu jEyu pApamO nIku Sakti lOpamO (rA)
Caranam 2ADad(a)nna rOsamO ala nAD(u)pAsamO
mEDa lEni vAsamO mEmu jEyu dOsamO (rA)
Caranam 3kallal(ai)na nEyamA kaNTE nIku hEyamA
tallaDilla nyAyamA tyAgarAja gEyamA (rA)
