Silai valaivaa sevagane Si Rama Thalelo.
Caranam 4Devarayum asurarayum thisaikalayum padaithavane
Yaavarum vanthu adi vananga aranga nagar thuyindravane

Ea vari ven chilai valavaa Raghavane thalelo
