  Lyrics - English:

Pallavivaidyana nAnariye bhavarOga vaidya nInE hariyE
AnupallavibhavarOgake bandhu nIne
Caranam 1krshNa nI kai piDidu kapaTadi ushNavAyu jaridu vishNu bhakta
ninna sEve enagittu utkrshTana mADenna kashTa paDisade
Caranam 2hari nimma SaraNavemba rasadali caraNa dyAnavittu gurutara
mAtregaLenage koTTu durita pArugeiva bandhu nInallade
Caranam 3ninna dAsa nAnu duritagaLenna kADuvudEnu ucitavayya ghanna
SrI purandara viTTalarAyane enna duritava harisuvanallade
