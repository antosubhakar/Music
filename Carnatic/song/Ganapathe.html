PallavigaNa patE mahA matE gaurI kumAra mAM pAhi
AnupallaviaNimAdyashTaiSvarya prada guru guha pUjita vara
CaranamsOma sUryAgni nEtra sadASivAnanda putra
vAma dEvAdi bhadra vArija gambhIra gAtra
