PallavirAmam bhajE shyAmama manasA rAmam bhajE shyAmam bhajE vacasA
AnupallavivibhISaNAnjanEya pUrita caraNam vashiSTAdi munigaNa vEdita hrdayam
vashIkrta mayAkarita vESam Etam purEsham sarvEsham
CaranamnIla mEgha shyAmalam nitya dharmacAriNam daNDinam kOdaNDinam
