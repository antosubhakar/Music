  Lyrics - English:

PallavidhiranA tanadhImta dhiranA dhiranA tana dhImta dhiranA
AnupallavinAdrudru tAni tom drudru tAni tanOm tanata
tAkiTa kiTatOm tanakiTa tOm takatOm takatari kiTataka taddhIm takadhIm jhaNutaka dhIm
takajhaNu takadhIm kiTatam taddhit tarikiTatOm takataLAGgu taka Namtari tajjhaNu taka
taLAGgu tatara tAni dhittillAna dhiranAdhiru dhimita kiTataka jhaNuta taNata
Caranamenni vidhamuga vEDukondurA ikha tALa jAlanura dayayunJcara
ninnE namminAnurA vinara suguNa sAndra SrI krshNarAjEndra
JatisvaramdhittaLAGgu taka taLAGgu taka dhittaLAGgu ma dha pa ma ga ma dhittillAna
