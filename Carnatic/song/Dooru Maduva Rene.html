  Lyrics - English:

PallaviDuru Maduvarene Caru Maddiyolage Muru Lokake Muddu Toro Rangayyana
Caranam 1Nandagokuladalli Mande Govugala Munde Kolalnudi Chandadi Baruvana
Caranam 2Kallatanadalli Gollara Mane Hokku Gullu Madadeyavara Galla Kaccidanendu
Caranam 3Kamadindali Ninna Kalavala Tanakagi Shyama Sundara Shri Purandara Vittalana
