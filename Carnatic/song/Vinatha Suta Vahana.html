  Lyrics - English:

PallavivinatA suta vAhana shrI ramaNa
manasAraga sEviJceda rAma
Anupallavininu sAreku jUDani bratuk(E)la
manujulan(E)ceDu jIvanam(E)la (vinatA)
Caranammata bhEdam(a)nE segan(A)rcaga
sammata vAkkulu palkuTa sukhamA
kSitilO sat-saGgati saukhyamu
pAlita tyAgarAj(A)mara pUjita (vinatA)
