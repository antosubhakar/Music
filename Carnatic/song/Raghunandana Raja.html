  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviraghu nandana rAja mOhana
ramiyimpavE nA manasuna
Anupallavinagaj(A)nilaja nArad(A)di hRn-
nALika nivAsuD(ai)na kAni SrI (raghu)
Caranamcittam(a)ndu ninn(u)nci prEmatO
cintincu sad-bhaktulan-
(u)ttam(O)ttamul(a)ncu nA madin-
(u)nci pUjinca lEdA
tattarambu tIrucu kAraNambu nIvE
tALa jAlan(i)ka tyAgarAja nuta (raghu)
