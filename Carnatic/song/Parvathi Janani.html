PallavipArvati janani bhavAni SrI rAja rAjESvari
sarva lOka pAlini mAnini dEvi
nIrAj(A)kshi parama pAvani kAmAkshi
niranjani mAm-ava amba
Caranam 2SrI-kari janani mRDAni SrI rAja rAjESvari
hrIM-kAra rUpiNi hariN(A)kshi dEvi
SrI kAncI pura vAsini kAmAkshi
SrI kAmESvari mAm-ava amba
Caranam 3SAmbhavi janani purANi SrI rAja rAjESvari
Sarvar(I)Sa dhAriNi Sankari dEvi
SyAma kRshNa paripAlini kAmAkshi
SyAmaL(A)mbikE mAm-ava amba
