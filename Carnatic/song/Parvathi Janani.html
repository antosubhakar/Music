  Lyrics - English:

PallavipArvati janani bhavAni shrI rAja rAjEshvari
sarva lOka pAlini mAnini dEvi
nIrAj(A)kSi parama pAvani kAmAkSi
niranjani mAm-ava amba
Caranam 2shrI-kari janani mRDAni shrI rAja rAjEshvari
hrIM-kAra rUpiNi hariN(A)kSi dEvi
shrI kAncI pura vAsini kAmAkSi
shrI kAmEshvari mAm-ava amba
Caranam 3shAmbhavi janani purANi shrI rAja rAjEshvari
sharvar(I)sha dhAriNi shankari dEvi
shyAma kRSNa paripAlini kAmAkSi
shyAmaL(A)mbikE mAm-ava amba
