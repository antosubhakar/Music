Pallavisatatam tAvaka pada sEvanam karavANi sArasanAbha mudA
AnupallavisUtasAyaka cArumUrtE krpayA vitara kuSalamayi vAraNa bhayahara
Caranam 1sura jAlAkhila mauni su-mAnasa kumuda paramOdghATana bahu paTutama vilasita
surucira mrduhAsa SarvarIdha varucE vAraya pApajAtam pArvaNa vidhumukha
Caranam 2kanaka maJjima hara kamanIyatara cEla ghana karuNA bharita kaTAksha nirIkshaNa
sanakAdi muni gEya samsAra dukkhEshumAm mAnita guNavAsa mAkshipa jagannuta
Caranam 3kamalAbAhu lAlita kisalaya pada padma vimala maNI bhUshaNa vanaruha dLa nEtra
mama hrdi vasAniSam madhusUdana SaurE SamalArtti bhaJjana sAdhu jana sEvita
