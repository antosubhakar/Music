  Lyrics - English:

PallaviepuDu krpa galgunO inakula tilaka nIku
AnupallaviepuDaina krIgaNTa jUcitE cAluvani enci ninnE cAla nammiyunna nApai
Caranamtalli taNDriyanna guru deivamu nIvEyani dalaci evvaganaina nannu brOtu vanucunA-
