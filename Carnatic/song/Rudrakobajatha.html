  Lyrics - English:

pallavirudrakOpajAta vIrabhadramASrayE sadA hrudayE
anupallavibhadrakALI ramaNam bhava haraNam bhadra pradAna nipuNa caraNam rudrAksha mAlikAbharaNam kshudrAdi nivAraNam bhakta bharaNam SrI
caraNamvijita vidhi hara harihayam vIrAdhivIra mabhayam rajata parvatASrayam ravi vidhu tEjOmayam gajamukha gaNESa raksham ajavadana daksha Siksham nijarUpa dAna daksham nija guruguha svapaksham
