  Lyrics - English:

PallaviBhajAmahE sree vinAyakam- Hrudi
bhavika dAyakam GananAyakam
AnupallaviAnantapura pUrvApaNa vAsam
Ananta karuNA varunA vAsam
CaranamAghajAnandanam advaya radanam
adurlabha shubha sadanam Gajavadanam
sakala kalArasa lasitA pAngam
sangItArdra hrudanta rangam
Madhyamakala sahityamvAsava vinutam varaguNa bharitam
vArita vighna shatam muni bhajitam
vasudhAkhilajana pAlana niratam
vana tulasI pari vasitam satatam
