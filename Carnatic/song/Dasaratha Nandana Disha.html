  Lyrics - English:

PallavidaSaratha nandana diSa kuSalam mama daSamukha mardhana dIna janAvana
AnupallavikuSa lava janaka kauSika maka rakshaka paSupati kAnuka jana vibhanjana
CaranamvAsavAdi pati vamSa samjAta vAsudEva jagadprANa surakshita
SrI sarasIruha bhava mahAdEva vAsavAdi vinutAdbhuta carita
