  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrIvidyArAjagOpAlam bhajEham SrI rukmiNi satyabhAmAsahita divya dEham
CaranamjIvESa jaganmOhini rUpam nandagOpam SritamandAram dhAtramSasvarUpAvatAram gOvindam induvadanam guruguhanuta mRdu gadanam dEvakInadanam vasudEva hRdaya candanam

  Lyrics - Tamil:
