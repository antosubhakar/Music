  Lyrics - English:

PallaviguruvaruLum tiruvaruLum enakkaruLum gaNapatiyE poruLnirainda kavi amudam enakkaruLvAy!
Anupallavimurugan unai azhaittadumE gajamukhanAi mun ninrAi uravukulA vaLLiyai maNamuDittAi oru noDiyil
Caranamguruparanum tirumAlum tirukayilainAtarum
akila pala munivOrgaL vaNangi nalam peRRAr
shruti layamum shor shuvaiyum isai layamum aLittu
suguNamAga sabaiyOril kurai kaLainda gaurisutA!
