  Lyrics - English:

PallavigAiyE gaNapati jagavaMdana।
SaMkara suvana bhavAnI kE naMdana॥
Caranam 1siddhi sadana gajavadana vinAyaka।
kRpA siMdhu suMdara saba lAyaka॥ gAiyE gaNapati jagavaMdana ॥
Caranam 2mOdaka priya muda maMgala dAtA।
vidyA bAridhi buddhi vidhAtA॥ gAiyE gaNapati jagavaMdana ॥
Caranam 3mAMgata tulasIdAsa kara jOrE।
basahiM rAmasiya mAnasa mOrE॥ gAiyE gaNapati jagavaMdana ॥
Caranam 4gAiyE gaNapati jagavaMdana।
SaMkara suvana bhavAnI kE naMdana॥
