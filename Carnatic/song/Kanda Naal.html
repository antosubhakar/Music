  Lyrics - English:

pallavikaNDa nAL mudalAy kAdal peRugudaDi
kaiyinil vEl piDitta karuNai siva bAlanai (kaNDa nAL)
caraNam 1vaNTicai pATum ezhil vacanta pUnkAvil
anta/vantu cukam tanta kantanai (en) kAntanai (kaNTa nAL)
caraNam 2nIla mayiltanai nencamum maRakkavillai
nEcamuTan kalanta/tanta pAcamum maRaiyavillai
kOla kumaran mana kOyilil iRanki viTTAn
kuRu nakai tanai kATTi naRu malar cUTTi viTTAn (kaNTa nAL)

சரணம் 2நீல மயில்தனை நெஞ்சமும் மறக்கவில்லை
