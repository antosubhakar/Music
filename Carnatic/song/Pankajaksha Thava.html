  Lyrics - English:

PallavipaGkajAksha tava sEvAm bahudAkara vAni
AnupallaviSaGkara valaSAsana mukha sandutOru kIrtE
Caranam 1SrIdhara tava pAdukAm SirasA vahAni sAdhu tAlavrtEna samvIjayAni
Caranam 2sarasam samvAhayAni murahara tava caraNau karavi SvEta patra dharaNam tava SaurE
Caranam 3upaharANi tava samsphuradu ruta rakutukEna upakaraNAni ca hitAni sapadi padmanAbha
