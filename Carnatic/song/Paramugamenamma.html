  Lyrics - English:

PallaviparAmukham-En-ammA pArvati(y)ammA
AnupallaviparAtparI parama pAvanI bhavAnI ambA
pAril nAn unnaiyE nambinEn (parA)
Caranam 1akhilam-engum nirainda jOtiyE ambikaiyE
annaiyE ini nAn tALEn-enac-connEn (parA)
Caranam 2unadu pAdam-inri vEru tuNai(y)uNDO
undan manam-irangavum nAn sollavO (parA)
Caranam 3SyAma kRshNa sOdarI kRpA-karI
SaraNam SaraNam-enru sonnEn tAyE (parA)
