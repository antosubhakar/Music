  Lyrics - English:

Pallavininnu sEvincina janulaku nika E janara bAdhalu galavE
AnupallavikanakAngi shrI rukmiNi hrdayAbja dinamaNE pArthasArathi svAmi
Svara sahityamrArA ninnu sadA (kOriti vinu) kOritini manavi vinumu
sArasa daLAkSa sharaNAgata janAvana krpAnidhivi
Caranambhava sAgara tArakamai yunnadi nI pada sarasa yugamamu
bhuvi mAnava lOkulaku bhukti mukti pradamu (nI padamu)
bhava sannuta nIdu nAma bhajana sadA jEyucunna
kavi janAvana lOla nI sari galadA delpu kumAra pUjita pAdA
