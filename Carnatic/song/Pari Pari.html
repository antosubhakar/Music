  Lyrics - English:

pallavipari pari nI padamE bhAvana
paramadayAvana pAvana
anupallavivaramulu vEDanu ninnu vIDanu
karIndra mukha sumukha nI vADanu
caraNammuraLI mrudanga nATya prabhAkara
saraLa madhura sangIta sudhAkara
mundugA ninnu kolichE vAriki
mundE vindai ninDai nilichE
