Anupallavijaya sakalAmara pOsha jaya sama bhuvanESa
Caranam 1jaya krpA bharitApAGga jaya kandarpasundarAGga jaya mauLI SObhita gaGga jaya daJjALibhaGga
Caranam 2jaya vidhISakranuta jaya pAvana sucarita jaya Sva-gaNa parivrta jaya kAmadAnarata
