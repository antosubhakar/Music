  Lyrics - English:

PallaviparipAhimAm prabhO purushOttamama murArE paripUta venkaTaSailESa daityArE
Anupallaviparama pAvana SIla janakAraka lakshaNa parama sukhAnubhava bhiItaraNa nipuNa
CaranampadmajA pAkArinuta nArAyaNa padma Sara janaka bhakta parAyaNa
madhyamakAla:
padma bhUshadhara pApa viSOshaNa padma nayana madhurAmrta bhAshaNa
padmajA ramaNa sadguNa pOshaNa padmanAbha vana tuLasI bhUshaNa
