  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSrI rAma nI nAmamenta rucirA O rAma nI namamEmi rucirA
anupallavikarirAja prahlAda dharani vibhIshaNula gAcina nI nAmamEmi rucirA
caraNam 1gOvindu nE vELa golutam golutam dEvuni guNamulu dalutam dalutam
caraNam 2vishNu kadalu cevula vindAm vindAm vEre kadalu cevula mandm mandAm
caraNam 3rAmadAsulu mAku sArAm sArAm kAmabAsulu mAku dUram dUram
caraNam 4nArAyaNuni mEmu nammEm nammEm narulaninka mEmu nammam nammam
caraNam 5mAdhava nAmamu maruvAm maruvAm mari yamabAdhaku veruvAm veruvAm
caraNam 6avanija pati sEva manAm manAm mariyoka jolante maunAm maunAm
caraNam 7bhadragiriSuni kandam kandam bhadramuto manamundAm mundAm
