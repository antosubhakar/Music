  Lyrics - English:

PallavimOhana kalyANi rAga vinOdini pAhi mInAkSi sundarEshvari amba
Caranam 1lalita vasanta vihAra vinOdini vana hrdyuti ranjita smita vadani
manasija shatakOTi sama vibhUSaNi mihira candra kara pUjya suranjani
(rAgA: vasantA)
Caranam 2vAgadhIshvari ramAnutAhini vandita bhakta janArtti vidAyini
vanajanAbha kamalOdbhava pUjini vAmadEva manOllAsa lOlini
(rAgA: vAgadhIshvari)
Caranam 3nAgagAndhArinuta pada naLini nigamAntanuta nAma svarUpiNi
nAradAdi muni gAna vinOdini navanIta mrdu svAnta vilAsini
(rAgA: nAgagAndhAri)
Caranam 4ranjani kAma rAga vidAhini rajani caragaNa pUrNa niSUdini
dIna manOhari rAjarAjEshvari mInAkSisuta hrdaya nivAsini
(rAgA: ranjani)
