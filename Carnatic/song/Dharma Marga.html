  Lyrics - English:

pallavidharma mArga pAlasa bhAma dharmO viSatihanta yAtra pApa rahitEshu daNDO vidhiyatE
caraNam 1Eva lOka pAlanE niyuktA yAdapi tE bhajantIha vishamatAm kOnu SaraNyAyatE
caraNam 2Evam abjanAbha dUta nikarOyamudira yannApi punarEva mahA vacO yat sudhAyatE
