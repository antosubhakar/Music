  Lyrics - English:

PallavirAmacandra prabhu tum bina pyArE kaun khabar lE mErI
Caranam 1bAj rahi jinki nagarai mO sadA dharam kI mErI
Caranam 2jAkE caraN kamal kI rajsO tiriyA tan kU phErI
Caranam 3auran kU kacu aur bharOsA hamE bharOsA tErI
Caranam 4padmanAbh prabhu phaNi parashAyi krpA karO kyOn dErI
