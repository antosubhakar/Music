  Lyrics - English:

PallavirAmacandra prabhu tum bina pyArE kaun khabar lE mErI
Charanam 1bAj rahi jinki nagarai mO sadA dharam kI mErI
Charanam 2jAkE caraN kamal kI rajsO tiriyA tan kU phErI
Charanam 3auran kU kacu aur bharOsA hamE bharOsA tErI
Charanam 4padmanAbh prabhu phaNi parashAyi krpA karO kyOn dErI
