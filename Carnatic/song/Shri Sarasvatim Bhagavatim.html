  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrI sarasvatIm bhagavatIm bhajata shrtajana sakalAbhISTa dAyinIm sadA
AnupallavishrI sarOja sambhava dayitAm mahitAm vAsavAdinuta vAsudEva bhaktAm
CaranamsanakAdi muni varENya samstutAm kanakAmbara ratnahAra vibhUSitAm
anupama karuNArasa paripUrNam su-lalita kavitAdi tOSita svAntAm
vanruha nayanAm vAraNa gamanAm nirupama vINAm pustakAkSa mAlA sahitAm
