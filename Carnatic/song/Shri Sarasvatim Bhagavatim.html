  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI sarasvatIm bhagavatIm bhajata Srtajana sakalAbhIshTa dAyinIm sadA
AnupallaviSrI sarOja sambhava dayitAm mahitAm vAsavAdinuta vAsudEva bhaktAm
CaranamsanakAdi muni varENya samstutAm kanakAmbara ratnahAra vibhUshitAm
anupama karuNArasa paripUrNam su-lalita kavitAdi tOshita svAntAm
vanruha nayanAm vAraNa gamanAm nirupama vINAm pustakAksha mAlA sahitAm
