  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSAradE viSAlAkshi suguNanidhE SAntAkAri SrI dharmavati amba
anupallavisarasijanAbha sOdari SrI lalitE sadAnandakari SrI mAyE
caraNamsOma bimba vadanE ambOruha nayanE sOmaSEkhara priyE bharaNI vinutE
sa ni dha pa ma ga ri sadA sangIta rasikE sanmArga darSini suSIE bAlE
