  Lyrics - English:

Pallavibinnahake bAyillavayya annanta aparAdha ennalli iralAgi
Caranam 1SiSu mOha sati mOha janani janakara mOha rasika bhrAtara mOha rAjamOha
paSu mOha bhU mOha bandhuvargada mOha asurAri ninna maretano kAyo hariye
Caranam 2annamada arttamada akhila vaibhavada mada munna prAyada madavu rUpamadavu
tanna satvada madavu dhAtri vaSavAda mada innu tanagidirillavendemba madadinda
Caranam 3ishTu dorakidare mattishTu bEkambAse ashTu dorakidaru mattashTarAse
kashTa bEDembAse kaDu sukhava kAmbAse nashTa jIvanadAse purandara viTTala
