  Lyrics - English:

pallavimOhana vamshI vAdana madhurima
vAhini lO tanuvu pulakitamainadi
anupallavimOhitulai gOpikA samUhamella hariyinchenu hAigolpaga
prANi kOTulella vENu vAdanambu
vInula vinabaDaga vEga dari chErenu
caraNamakaLanka nAdamayamai jagamu pulakincha
amara dAnava yakSa kinnerAdulu mechcha
nagajA manOharuDu tAnDavamu sEyagA
munulella mudamandi vandanamu sEyagA
