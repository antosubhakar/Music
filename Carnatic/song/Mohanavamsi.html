  Lyrics - English:

PallavimOhana vamSee vAdana madhurima
vAhini lO tanuvu pulakitamainadi
AnupallavimOhitulai gOpikA samoohamella hariyinchenu hAigolpaga
prANi kOTulella vENu vAdanambu
veenula vinabaDaga vEga dari chErenu
CaranamakaLanka nAdamayamai jagamu pulakincha
amara dAnava yaksha kinnerAdulu mechcha
nagajA manOharuDu tAnDavamu sEyagA
munulella mudamandi vandanamu sEyagA
