  Lyrics - English:

Pallavimama kulESvaram SAstAram santatam cintayEham Ananda tANDava puravihAram
AnupallaviSamitanata jana duritam nIlAmbara SObhitam amita sukha SrIpradam atulita divya guNAnvitam
Caranamkamala rucira karadhrta kaSAyudha vibhUshitam amara munISvara bhUta vaTuka nAyaka sEvitam
kamanIya nava yauvana sundarAmbA sahitam sumaSara guhAgrajam SrI rAjam harihara sutam
Madhyamakala sahityamrAkA SaSinibha vadanam ravi sOmAnasa nayanam
rayagati turaGga gamanam rakshita samasta bhuvanam
