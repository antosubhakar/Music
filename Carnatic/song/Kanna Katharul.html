  Lyrics - English:

PallavikaNNA kAttaruL mEgha vaNNA kaDaikkaN pArttaruL kamalak-
AnupallaviviNNADarum munivarum vaNaGgi vENDa narakAsura vadam Seiyya viraindu vanda
CaranampAmai vaDivAna bhUmippirATTi tErOTTa asurar kulam azhittavA
cakrapANi ulagelAm maGgaLa dIpA vaLiyoLi vISa aruL purinda
