  Lyrics - English:

PallavinI bhajana gAna rasikula
nEn(e)ndu kAnarA rAma
AnupallaviSrI bhava sarOj(A)san(A)di
SacI manO-ramaNa vandya ilalO (nI)
CaranamsaguNa nirguNapu nija dabbaralanu
shaN-matamula marmam(a)shTa siddhula
vagalu jUpa santasilla kaNTini
var(A)nana tyAgarAja vinuta (nI)
