  Lyrics - English:

PallavibAlagOpAla pAlaya pankaja nayana
AnupallavinIla vArivAha nibha cAru kaLEbara bAla kaLESa tulya phala SObhi tilaka
Caranam 1karNayuga SObhi kanaka kuNDala SaurE karNajOdbhUta mAyA haraNa nipuNa vara
karuNa pAlaka tApita SakaTa sura varNayatE kathamiva vAsudEva lIlayE
Caranam 2vAtarUpa danuja varApa hari krshNa cuta bANa janaka sUdita vrSAsura
SIta kiraNa kulajAta SrtamandAra Sata kumbha vasana sArasanAbha dEva
