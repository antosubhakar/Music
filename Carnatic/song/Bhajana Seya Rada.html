Pallavibhajana sEya rAdA rAma
Anupallaviaja rudr(A)dulaku satatam(A)tma mantramaina rAma (bhajana)
Caranam 1karagu bangAru valva kaTin(e)ntO merayaga
ciru navvulu-gala mukhamunu cintinci cintinci (bhajana)
Caranam 2aruN(A)bh(A)dharamunu surucira dant(A)vaLini
merayu kapOla yugamunu niratamunanu talaci talaci (bhajana)
Caranam 3bAguga mAnasa bhava sAgaramunanu tarimpa
tyAgarAju manavini vini tArakmagu rAma nAma (bhajana)
