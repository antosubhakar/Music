  Lyrics - English:

PallavishailEshvaram bhajarE rE citta shrI varada guruguha mudam vAraNa
CaranamshailAtmajA kAmAkSI ramaNam shiva kAHncIsadanam mRdu gadanam bila dvArAnta madhyasthitam bahukIrttita bhASAramaNanutam
