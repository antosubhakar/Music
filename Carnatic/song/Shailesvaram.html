PallaviSailESvaram bhajarE rE citta SrI varada guruguha mudam vAraNa
CaranamSailAtmajA kAmAkshI ramaNam Siva kAJcIsadanam mRdu gadanam bila dvArAnta madhyasthitam bahukIrttita bhAshAramaNanutam
