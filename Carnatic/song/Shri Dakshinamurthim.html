PallaviSrI dakshiNAmUrtimISam citprakASam praNaumi
AnupallavivEdAntavEdyam nyagrOdhavRkSa mUlavAsinam vallakInAdAnubhava mOdamamRta phEnadyutimandasmitamukhAmbujam
CaranamjananAdi khEda bhaJjanacaturam jagadIndrajAlacamat kRtikaram munijanAdi nikhila samSayaharam mudrAkaram guruguhAkaram anAdyavidyA tamO bhAskaram AcArya SEkharam sundram manOvAgagOcara SaSidharam mauninam madanaharam SaGkaram
