  Lyrics - English:

PallavivirAja turaga rAja rAj(E)Svara
nirAmayuni jEyavE
Anupallavijar(A)di rOga yuta tanuvucE
nar(A)dhamulu(y)Emi sAdhinciri (virAja)
Caranamnirantaramu sakala vishaya duHkha
paramparalacE civukaga madilO
dur-anta kAma madamunu bAdhincaga
dur-matuDE tyAgarAja sannuta (virAja)
