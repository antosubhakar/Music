PallavipAhi mAm harE mahAnubhAva rAghava
Caranam 1pAhi rAma(y)anucu rEyu pagalu koliciti
pAhi rAma nIv(a)nu sampadanu valaciti (pAhi)
Caranam 2pAhi rAma(y)anucu pAri pAri kOriti
pAhi rAma nAma muktA phalamul(E)riti (pAhi)
Caranam 3pAhi rAma(y)anucu bhuvini bAga puTTiti
pAhi rAma(y)anucu gaTTi paTTu paTTiti (pAhi)
Caranam 4pAhi rAma(y)anucu nIdu padamu nammiti
pAhi rAma(y)anucu manasu bAga grammiti (pAhi)
Caranam 5pAhi rAma(y)anucu nIdu padamu pADiti
pAhi rAma(y)anucu parama padamu vEDiti (pAhi)

pAhi tyAgarAja vinuta bhaktuD(ai)tini (pAhi)
