  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviguru krpalEka SrI hari krpa galgunA hari matamE guru matamu kAdEmayya sad-
Anupallaviguru krpalEka bhAskara tanujuniki munu karagadamaina divyAstramu lEmAyE
Caranamguru krpa galgina surapati tanujuni parama purushuDaina SrI vAsudEvuDE
paramAdaramuga pAlincalEdA dorakunEma kalinarulakI bhAgyamu
