  Lyrics - English:

PallavisrInivAca tiruvEGkaTamuTaiyAy
jeya kOvintA mukunta ananta ||
AnupallavitIna caraNyAn enum pukazh koNTAy
tInan enaippOl vEREvan kaNTAy ||
Caranamjakampukazhum Ezhumalai mAyavanE
tirumakaL alarmEl maGkai maNALanE
jakannAtA caGku cakrataranE (nin)
tiruvaTikkapAyam apAyam ayyA ||
