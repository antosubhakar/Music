pallavisujana mAnasa hamsAnandinI sudhA sAgara madhya vAsini amba
anupallaviajasurESa sEvita mAlinI nija sukhadAyaki nIrajalOcani
caraNamvijita vAruNi vidhrta yOgi hrda bhajita pAvana padayugaLE
