  Lyrics - English:

PallaviYen mana thamarai mel ,
Ye thaye un malar thalinai
Vaithu aruluvaye
AnupallaviThunbam thudaithe thUya nilayile,
Inbamana un isaigal padave
CaranamThan mayamakkum thava vazhi ariyen,
Dharmamum thyagamum chirudum theriyen,
Chinmaya rUpini shhiva manonmani,
Varmamillamale vandu udava nIye.
