  Lyrics - English:

pallavinI balamA nAma balamA nijamuga balkavE O rAma
anupallaviSrI bhuja mA talli balamA SIlAtmutau sau-bhakta balamA
caraNamgirirAja jAmAtAdi aja sura rAja kAncana rAja bhayahara
rAja rAvaNa rAja bhIkara tyAgarAjanuta dhAma bhIma
