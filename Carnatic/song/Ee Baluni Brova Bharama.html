  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhAramA I bAluni brOva parAshakti patita pAvani
AnupallavisArasAkSi sakala lOkamula samrakSika lEdA
CaranamghOra bhaya sAgara taraNi gOvinda hrdaya vAsini
mAra dahana citta manOhari mAm pAlaya sadA shankari
