  Lyrics - English:

pallaviAnandamaya manavE O manasA
anupallaviAnandamanavE nityAnanda manavE sadAnanda manavE siddhAnta manavE brahmm-
caraNam 1ajnAnamagu yavivEkamula drunci sujnAna mayamagu sUkshma merunguTE
dEhalOka SAstrAdi vAsa nala sAhasamuna kOsi sangamula taluTE
caraNam 2bhEda buddhini bahu vAdamu sEyaka modakhEdamu landu sama bhAvamuncuTE
vEdAtItulai velayu sadgurulacE nAdamu vinucunu AhlAda monduTE
caraNam 3jnAna svarUpamai kaivalyamai Suddha svAnubhavampaina sAyujya monduTE
rAmacandrapura varulagu vEnkaTaramaNa bhAgavata varaduni vinOdamu
