  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSankara suta pAlaya gaNanAtha SabarI mahIndara taTAvAsarada
anupallaviSankhA bhEda sujana Subha nirata sakalita guNita Sata mahA vinuta
caraNamvighna samUha vipartana nipuNa viSvAkhila kucalASvata caraNa
kanya guNAkshitArcita kadana nivArana kAnana tuLasIdhAma vibhUshaNa
