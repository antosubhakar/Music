  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSrI guru dakshiNAmUrtE namOstutE SrImad umApatE tArakarUpa paSupatE
anupallaviSrI girijA vihAriNE ananta rUpiNE sadguru Sishya rUpa prapanca vyApinE SrIvidyAsvarUpiNE avikAriNE
caraNamyOga paTTAbhirAma mahAmAyA vaNDita tyAgarAjAdi mahAkavi pungava sEvita
yOgAnga suprIta bhavapriya rAgatOshita tyAgEnaikE amrta tvamAnaSuriti Sruti vidita
agOcara mahAtE jOjjvAlAvrta aghOrAdyashTanAma sampUjita amaranuta
