  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI kAJcIpura vAsini Srtajana pAlitE SivE pAhimAm
AnupallaviEkAmra citta rUpiNi Ekadanta shaNmukha janani ajani SrI kAmAkshi
CaranambhaNda daitya prakhaNDitE akhaNDa karuNAmUrtE aNDaja harISa stutE maNDala sthitE
paradEvatE paNDita jananutE nIlakaNTha hrdaya sphUrtE paJca bANa rUpadE prthvI liGgAliGganAGgE
