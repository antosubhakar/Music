  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrI kAncIpura vAsini shrtajana pAlitE shivE pAhimAm
AnupallaviEkAmra citta rUpiNi Ekadanta SaNmukha janani ajani shrI kAmAkSi
CaranambhaNda daitya prakhaNDitE akhaNDa karuNAmUrtE aNDaja harIsha stutE maNDala sthitE
paradEvatE paNDita jananutE nIlakaNTha hrdaya sphUrtE panca bANa rUpadE prthvI lingAlinganAngE
