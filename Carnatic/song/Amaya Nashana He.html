  Lyrics - English:

pallaviAmaya nASana hE madhusUdana mAmava venkaTaSaila nilayana
anupallavisOma samastEda mOhana vadana pAmasAyata kOmala nayana
caraNamnirmaya nIrada SyAma niranjana prEma madhura ramA manOranjana
kAma samAna agarAja pASana sAmada kAnana tuLasI SObhana
