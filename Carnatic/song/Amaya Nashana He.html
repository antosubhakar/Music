  Lyrics - English:

PallaviAmaya nAshana hE madhusUdana mAmava venkaTashaila nilayana
AnupallavisOma samastEda mOhana vadana pAmasAyata kOmala nayana
Caranamnirmaya nIrada shyAma niranjana prEma madhura ramA manOranjana
kAma samAna agarAja pAshana sAmada kAnana tuLasI shObhana
