  Lyrics - English:

Pallavitripurasundari nApai nI dayauncumu aparimitA nandAmrta varSaNi
AnupallaviapAra dayAnidhi nIvEyani nammi aparulu nevarini apEkSimpa lEdE
Caranamgauri nI pAdamu bhaktitO pUjimpa kramamunu teliyani parama pAmaruDE
vArija lOcani vEgamE vacci kOrina varamulu nosagumu lalitamma
