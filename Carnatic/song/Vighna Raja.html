  Lyrics - English:

Pallavivighna rAja vinutara nammiti vishESa phaladAyakuDu nIvEgAdA
Anupallavibhakta mAnasArti bhanjana bhaktAbhISTa phaladAyakA (nI) (nija) sakala
CaranamvAraNamukha ninnu vAramu nammina vArija nI varamulicci brOvumu
vArija nayana sOdari kumAra varadadAsuDu pogaDe (shrI) sakala
