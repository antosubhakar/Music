  Lyrics - English:

pallaviParipUraniye, manonmaniye,
Papa vinasiniye , yen thaye
anupallaviParivodu inda parinai yellam,
Paripalithu idum para shhakthi
caraNamNithya niranjani nirmala Kalyani
NIla mani jaya gIrvani,
shathya jnana Jyothi svarUpini,
shama rasa Veda sunada vinodhini
