  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisolla vallAyO?-kiLiyE!
sollanI vallAyO?-
Anupallavivalla vElmuru kandanai-iGgu
vandu kalandu magizhndu kulAvenRu (solla)
Caranam 1tillai yambalattE-naDanam
seyyum amararpirAn-avan
selvat tirumaganai iGgu vandu
sErndu kalandu magizhndiDu vAyenRu (solla)
Caranam 2allik kuLattarugE-oru nAL
andip pozhudinilE-aGgOr
mullaic ceDiyadanbR-seyta vinai
muTRum maRandiDak kaTRaden nEyenRu (solla)
Caranam 3pAlai vanattiDaiDE-tanaik kaip
paTRi naDakkaiyilE-tan kai
vElin misaiyANai-vaittuc conna
vindai mozhigaLaic cindaisey vAyenRu (solla)
