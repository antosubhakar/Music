  Lyrics - English:

PallavimaracitivO mA ramaNa
Anupallavisura krtya sAdhana mAtramA narasimha rUpamuna nannu
CaranamsurulatO vavi varamA narulapai anAdaramA vara
nArasimha paradaivamA karuNincu dAsa dEvuni
