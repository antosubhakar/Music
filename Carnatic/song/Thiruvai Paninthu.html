  Lyrics - English:

pallavitiruvap paNintu nittam cemmait tozhil purintu,
varuka varuvatenRE-kiLiyE!-makizhvuR RiruppOmaTi!
caraNam 1veTRi ceyaluk kuNTu tiyin niyamamenRu,
kaTRut teLinta pinnum-kiLiyE!-kavalaippaTalAkumO?
caraNam 2tunpa ninaivu kaLum cOrvum payamu mellAm,
anpil azhiyumaTI!-kiLiyE!-anpuk kazhivillai kAN.
caraNam 3nyAyiTRai yeNNi yenRum naTumai nilai payinRu,
Ayira mANTulakil-kiLiyE!-azhivinRi vAzhvO maTI!
caraNam 4tUya perunkanalaic cuppira maNNi yanai
nEyattuTan paNintAl-kiLiyE!-nerunkit tuyar varumO?
