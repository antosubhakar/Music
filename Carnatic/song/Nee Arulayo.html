  Lyrics - English:

PallavinI aruLAyO tAyE! nIlAyadAkshiyE!
Caranam 1nItAnE nikarillAta mAtA!anAtiyE!nILum tamizhicaiyE!
Caranam 2pAtapatuma sEvai tantumE emaiyaruL paripuramcEr caraNiyE!
nitamunaiyE catamenuvOrk karuLuka vicAlAkshiyEvinaitavirAyO?
Caranam 3pAriTamikap pApakarumam pAparaniTap pAlamarum tAyE!
carAcaramatanil karAvenum ulaka turAcaiyil uzhala viTAtuaruLuvAy
mAritAnE enAk kOrinArArumE nAriyE! nalamuRavE
Caranam 4nArAyaNi tirumakaLuTanE pAratiyumE patamatu tozhutiTa
satA inimaiyAna icai uLamatAku nacai peruki OTa icaiaruLuvAy
pAcamatE cUzha viTAmE aruLvAy IcvariyE unataTaikkalamE!
ena nI varavE paripUraNa pairavi nAn nALum unait tutittananE!
