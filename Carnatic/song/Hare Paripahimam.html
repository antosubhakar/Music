  Lyrics - English:

PallaviharE paripAhimAm naraharE karAmbuja dhrta girE shaurE
AnupallavikarIndra bali dhruva varada murArE surEndra vandita pada kamsArE
CaranamshrI vAsudEva yAdava kulOdbhava dEvAdi dEva mahAnubhAva
jaivAtru kAnana jIvEsha kEshava sEnAdrutAkhila dInALi bAndhava
