  Lyrics - English:

PallavisimhA calamuna velasina SrI narasimhA mahima teliya vasamA
AnupallavisimhAsanamuna mA ramaNI maNi ciru navvulatO sirulu kuriyimpa
Caranamvaramulu vEDa narulu vElu vElu varadAyani piluva kalugu mElu
karamulO abhayamiccina cAlu muraLIravamunu niratamugrOlu
