PallaviMandagamane ivanyaare pelamma
Mandaradhara /govinda kaanamma//manda//
Caranam 1Kendalirunakha sasibimba pada padma/
Anduge ittavanyaare pelamma/
Andukaalingana pedeyathulida ditta/
Nandana kandamukunda kaanamma//manda//
Caranam 2Uduge peethaambara /naduge honnudu daara/
Kadaga kankana vittavanyaaramaa/
Madadi kel sakala/lo kangala kukshiyo/
Lodane thorida/jagadodeya kaanamma//manda//
Caranam 3Neerada neela dantheseva bakshadi/ke//
Yurava nittava nyaaramma/
Neerekelu nirjara raada varige/
Prerisiphala vitthu daari kaanamma//manda//
Caranam 4Sankha chakravu /gade padma kai yolagitta/
Lankarisuvaneetha nyaaramma/
Pankajamukhi sree bhudevi yararasanu/
Sankeyillade gopi thanayakaanamma//manda//
Caranam 5KAMBUKANDHARA/karnaalambitha kundala/
Ambujamukha dava nyaare pelamma/
Rambhe kelitha purandara vitala/
Nambida bhaktha kutumbi kaanamma//manda//
