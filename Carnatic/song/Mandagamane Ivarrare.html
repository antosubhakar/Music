pallaviMandagamane ivanyAre pelamma
Mandaradhara /govinda kAnamma//manda//
caraNam 1Kendalirunakha sasibimba pada padma/
Anduge ittavanyAre pelamma/
AndukAlingana pedeyathulida ditta/
Nandana kandamukunda kAnamma//manda//
caraNam 2Uduge pIthAmbara /naduge honnudu dAra/
Kadaga kankana vittavanyAramA/
Madadi kel sakala/lo kangala kukSiyo/
Lodane thorida/jagadodeya kAnamma//manda//
caraNam 3NIrada nIla dantheseva bakSadi/ke//
Yurava nittava nyAramma/
NIrekelu nirjara rAda varige/
Prerisiphala vitthu dAri kAnamma//manda//
caraNam 4shankha chakravu /gade padma kai yolagitta/
LankarisuvanItha nyAramma/
Pankajamukhi srI bhudevi yararasanu/
shankeyillade gopi thanayakAnamma//manda//
caraNam 5KAMBUKANDHARA/karnAlambitha kundala/
Ambujamukha dava nyAre pelamma/
Rambhe kelitha purandara vitala/
Nambida bhaktha kutumbi kAnamma//manda//
