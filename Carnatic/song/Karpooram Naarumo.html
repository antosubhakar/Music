  Lyrics - English:

PallavikaRpUram nARumO kamalap pU nARumO
thiruppavaLac cevvAythAn thiththithirukkumO
maruppisaindha mAyavan tan - vAysuvaiyum nARRamum
viruppuRRE kETkinREn! sol! Azhi veN saGgE
