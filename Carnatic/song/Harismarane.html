  Lyrics - English:

Pallavihari smaraNe mADO nirantara paragatige idu nirdAra
Charanam 1durita gajakke kaNDIravanenisida sharaNAgata rakSaka pAvana nI
Charanam 2smaraNe kaida prahlAdana rakSisida duruLa hiraNyakanudarava sILIda
Charanam 3taruNi draupadi moreyiDalAkSaNa paradinda kSayavitta mahAtmana
Charanam 4andu ajAmiLa kandana kareyalu bandu salahi Anandava tOrida
Charanam 5shrIsha purandara viTTala rAyana shrSTi koDeyana muTTi bhajisi nI
