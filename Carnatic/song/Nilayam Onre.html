Anupallavimalai nadi vaLam Seyu malar kani SOlaiyil mAn paSu puLLinam vAzh paSum kOyilil
Caranam 1pala jAti mataGgaLin kalagamillAmalE pariSuddha sanmArga bhaktaruDanE kUDi
kalaigaLum tozhilgaLum phalan pera vaLarttoru kavalaiyuminRi nAn kaDavuL arattai kAkka
