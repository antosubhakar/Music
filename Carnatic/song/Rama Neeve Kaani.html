  Lyrics - English:

PallavirAma nIvE kAni nannu rakshincu vAr(e)vvarE
AnupallavisOma sUrya lOcana sundara vadana SrI (rAma)
Caranam 1tAta vacana pAla puruhUt(A)d(y)amara
pArijAta saumitritO sItA ramaNitO velasina (rAma)
Caranam 2dhArA dhar(A)bha SarIra bhAvaja
sukumAra sAkEta pura vihAra nann(E)lukOra (rAma)
Caranam 3nAga Sayana muni yAga pAlana bhakta
bhAgadhEya pAvana tyAgarAja sannuta (rAma)
