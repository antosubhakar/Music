PallavigAna mUrtE SrI kRshNa vENu
gAna lOla tri-bhuvana pAla pAhi
AnupallavimAninI maNi SrI rukmiNI
mAnas(A)pahAra mAra janaka divya (gAna)
CaranamnavanIta cOra nanda sat-kiSOra
nara mitra dhIra nara siMha SUra
nava mEgha tEja nagajA sahaja
narak(A)ntak(A)ja nata tyAgarAja (gAna)
