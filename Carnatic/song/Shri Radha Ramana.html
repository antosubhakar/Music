  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrI rAdhA ramaNA jaya pankaja caraNA kSIrAbdi shayanA sArasa daLa nayanA
Caranam 1jaya nanda kumArA taDi navanIta cOrA jaya jagadAdharA shrta jana mandArA
Caranam 2jaya jaya gOpAlA kuvalaya daLa nIlA jaya durjana kAlA jaya sajjana pAlA
