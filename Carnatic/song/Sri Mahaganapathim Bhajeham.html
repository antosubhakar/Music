pallaviSrI mahAgaNapatim bhajEham SivAtmajam shaNmukhAgrajam
anupallaviSrta gaNasEvitam vighna nASakam vara prasAda dAyakam
madhyamakAla sAhityamsadayam kapilamuni varadAyakam guru sEva Saktam hErambam
caraNam 1jnAna mudrAlankrtam mUlAdhAra nivAsinam
caraNam 2gajAraNya vAsinam jyOtirmayam upanishad sAram panca
bhUtAtmakam sindhUrapriyam pancamAtanga mukham
caraNam 3kAmESa nayana hlAdakam nAgalinga vara putram
SrI vidyA cit-prabhAnanda rAja yOgIndra vanditam
