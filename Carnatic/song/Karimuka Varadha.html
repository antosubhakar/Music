  Lyrics - English:

Pallavikarimukha varadA mAm pAlaya
AnupallaviparikIrtita vighna nivAraNa sari sama rahita sankaTa haraNa
Caranamhari surEshAdi vandita pada giri kanyA suta gIta jnAnata
durita dukkha bhaya tApa samhara haritA mOdaka Amra phala priya
