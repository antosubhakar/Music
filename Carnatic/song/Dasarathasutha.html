  Lyrics - English:

PallavidaSaratha suta suragaNanuta daSa dikkyAta rAma
AnupallavikuSa lava tshta kuvalayOdbhEda guNanidhE jayarAma
CaranamdIna jana hitA mAnava pUjita bhAnu kulajAta dhIra subOdha
jAnakI nAtha gAna janArcita dhyAna rUpa jayarAma
