  Lyrics - English:

PallavihAlAsyanAtham smarAmi kOlAhala mInAkshI samEtam
AnupallavimUlAdi navAdhAra nAyakam munijanAdi bhakta vara dAyakam malayadhvajapura maNTapasthitam mahA gaNESa guruguhAdiyutam
CaranamnavanIta kALIpUjitam nandirUpAJjanEyAdi nutam navAdyAshADha mahOtsavam nata surAsura dEva prabhAvam
