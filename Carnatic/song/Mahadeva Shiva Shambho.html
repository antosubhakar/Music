  Lyrics - English:

PallavimahAdEva shiva shambhO mArakOTi sundara prabhO shrI
AnupallavisahasrakOTi jana pAlana sAdhu samrakSaNa pAvana
CaranamsarvEshvara karuNAkara mUrtE sAmagAna priya vEda pUrtE
paramEshvara gangAdhara kIrtE patita pAvana shrI pashupatE
