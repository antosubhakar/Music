pallavipAlayamAm bruhadISvara pAlita bhuvanESvara
anupallavivilasita tanjapurISvara bruhannAyakI manOhara
caraNamdInajanAvana Sankara divAkarAgni SaSi nEtrA dAnava sura sEvita varadAna nipuNa mahESvara gAna vidhyApradESvara mAnitaguruguha sOdara sannu tAnandakara paramESvara Subhakara hara hara
