  Lyrics - English:

Pallavidasharatha rAma gOvinda nanu daya jUDu pAhi mukunda
Anupallavidasha muklha samhAra daraNIjApati rAma shashidhara pUjita shankha cakradhara
Caranam 1mI pAdamulE gati mAku mammElukO svAmi parAku mApAla
galigina shrIpati I proddu kApADi rakSincu kanakAmbaradhara
Caranam 2nArAyaNa vAsudEva ninu nammiti mahAnubhAva garuDa
gamana hari gaja rAja rakSaka parama puruSa bhakta pApa samharaNa
Caranam 3tArakanAma mantramu rAmadAulakella svatantramu iravuga
krpanElumipuDu bhadrAdrini sthiramuga nelakonna sItA manOhara
