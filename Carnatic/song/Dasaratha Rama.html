  Lyrics - English:

PallavidaSaratha rAma gOvinda nanu daya jUDu pAhi mukunda
AnupallavidaSa muklha samhAra daraNIjApati rAma SaSidhara pUjita SaGkha cakradhara
Caranam 1mI pAdamulE gati mAku mammElukO svAmi parAku mApAla
galigina SrIpati I proddu kApADi rakshiJcu kanakAmbaradhara
Caranam 2nArAyaNa vAsudEva ninu nammiti mahAnubhAva garuDa
gamana hari gaja rAja rakshaka parama purusha bhakta pApa samharaNa
Caranam 3tArakanAma mantramu rAmadAulakella svatantramu iravuga
krpanElumipuDu bhadrAdrini sthiramuga nelakonna sItA manOhara
