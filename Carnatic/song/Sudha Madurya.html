  Lyrics - English:

PallavisudhA mAdhurya bhASiNi amba sukha svarUpiNi kalyANi niranjani
AnupallavisadAnanda lahari shankari sakala lOka janani pAhimAm
CaranamkamalAlaya taTa nivAsini kamalE suta (vimalasukrta) phaladAyini
paramEshvari shrI pArvati patita pAvani pApa vimOcani
