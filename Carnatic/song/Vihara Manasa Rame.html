  Lyrics - English:

PallavivihAra mAnasa ramE saccidAnanda ghana shyAmE
Anupallaviahita rAkSasa virAmE mahitanyAbhirAmE
Caranam 1padanata jana bhava khEda vihAraNa lOlE vidhumada hara cAruvadaba parilasuitE
Caranam 2khara mukha vidaLa nAshanalasitE hariNa mada ghana sAra shObhita phalE
Caranam 3purandarAdi vinutE pApa ghana samIrE karuNArasa nilayE sarasIruhanAbhE
