  Lyrics - English:

PallaviBhaktha jana vatsale(hari)
Caranam 1hey ivo vittale...... Bhakthajana vatsale
Karunakallole pandurange!!! ||||
Caranam 2Sajala jalada ghan pithambara paridhaan
yEyi uddharane Keshi Rajeh...... Bhakthajanavatsale||||
Caranam 3Nama mhane thu vishvachi janani
Ksheerabdhi nivasini jagadambe.... Bhakthajanavatsale||||
