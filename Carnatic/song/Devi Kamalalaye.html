  Lyrics - English:

PallavidEvi kamalAlayE tava pAda bhaktim dEhi
AnupallavidEvAsurAdi sampUjitE divyAbharaNa bhUSitE
CaranamshrI vAsudEva hrdaya nivAsini sEvaka janAbhISTa dAyini
bhAvanAtIta mahimAnvitE bhavyAkrti sam-shObhitE
