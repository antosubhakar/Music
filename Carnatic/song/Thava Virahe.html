  Lyrics - English:

pallavi. vahathi malaya samIrE madhanam upanidhAya spudathi kusuma nikarE virahi hrudaya dhaLanAya thava virahE vanamAlI sakhI sIdhadhi rAdhE
. dhahathi sisira mayUkhE maraNam anukarOthi pathathi madhana visikhE vilapathi vikalatharOthi
. dvanathi madhupa samuhE sravaNam apidhadhAthi manasi chalitha virahE nisi nisi rujam upayAthi
. vasathi vipina vidhAnE thyajathi lalitha dhAma lutathi dharaNi sayanE bahu vilapathi thava nAma
. bhaNathi kavi jayadEvE viraha vilasithEna manasi rabhasa vibhavE harirudhayathu sukruthEna
