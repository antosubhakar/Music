PallavirAjarAjESvari girirAja kumAri SrI rAjarAjESvari
AnupallavikESava sOdari sundari gaGgAdhara manOhari ASrta jana karuNAkari akhila lOka janani SaGkari
Caranam 1mAyAmayi mahA mOhini maGgaLa rUpiNi rudrarANi tAyE varadAyini vANi SarvANi purANi dEvi nArAyaNi
Caranam 2AdiyE paramjyOtiyE paramAnanda valli umaiyE nI dayavaruL SeidiDuvAyE nityakalyANi AdarippAyE
Caranam 3nIlakaNThar vAmamEvi ninriraGgu mahESvari bAla guha gaNESanaip-peTra pArvati paramESvari gauri
