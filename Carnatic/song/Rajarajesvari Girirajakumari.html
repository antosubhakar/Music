AnupallavikEshava sOdari sundari gangAdhara manOhari Ashrta jana karuNAkari akhila lOka janani shankari
Caranam 1mAyAmayi mahA mOhini mangaLa rUpiNi rudrarANi tAyE varadAyini vANi sharvANi purANi dEvi nArAyaNi
