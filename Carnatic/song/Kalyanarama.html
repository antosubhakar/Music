  Lyrics - English:

pallavikalyANarAma raghurAma rAma kanaka makuTa marakata maNi lOla hAra daSaratha bAla sItA
anupallavimallikAdi sugandha maya nava mAlikAdi SObhitagaLEna
ulhAsa pariSIlana cAmara ubhaya pArSvEna kuNDala kElana
caraNam 1Agata suravara munigaNa sajjana agaNita janakana ghOshita mangaLa
rAghava raghurAma rAma janakajA ramaNa manOhara sItA
caraNam 2gautama vaSIshTa nArada tumburu kASyapAdi munigaNa vara pUjita
aupavAhya skanda dESa alankrta haima simhAsanasthita sItA
caraNam 3bhAgadEya bahumAna sudhAya ubhatArpita diSi diSi rakshakavara
mEgha vAhanaravAhanAdhinuta EkarAja mahArAja mamarAja
