  Lyrics - English:

PallaviVictory to the Lord of Janaki. Victory to the protector of VibheeSana.
Victory to the Lord with lotus-like feet. Victory to the Lord that dispels all darkness.
Caranam 1sakala lOka nivAsa sAkEta pura vAsa akaLanka nija hAsa abja hAsa abja mukha bhAsa
Caranam 2Suka mauni nutipAtra subha ramya cAritra makara kuNDala vaktra mahanIya gAtra
Caranam 3kamanIya SAntIra koustubhA lankAra kamalAksha raghu vIra kalusha samhAra
Caranam 4samara ripu jaya dhIra sakala guNa gambhIra amala hrutsancAra akhilArti hAra
Caranam 5rUpa varjita mAra rucira sadguNa SUra bhUpa daSaratha kumAra bhUri yAbharaNa hara
Caranam 6pApa sangha vidAra pankti mukha samhAra SrIpatE sukumAra sItA vihAra
P: Victory to the Lord of Janaki. Victory to the protector of VibheeSana.
Victory to the Lord with lotus-like feet. Victory to the Lord that dispels all darkness.
A: Victory to the savior of the three lokas (universe). Victory to the repository of kindness.
Victory to the Lord that bestows boons. Victory to the most eminent one.
: You dwell in all the lokas. You dwell in shaketa (Ayodhya). You are unblemiSed.
You have a cheerful countenance. Your smile resembles the brightness of a lotus.
Caranam 2You are adored by the shage shhuka. You are known for your impeccable history.
You wear Makara Kundalas (ornaments on ears). You have an excellent body.
Caranam 3Koustubha (gem) Sines beautifully on you. You are lotus eyed.
You are the warrior of the _raguhu dynasty. You dispel all sins.
Caranam 4You are the winner of all wars. You are the paragon of virtues.
You live in the hearts of pious people. You destroy all afflictions.
Caranam 5You admire shiva, the destroyer of Manmatha. You are the hero with virtues.
You are the son of Dasaratha. You wear gold ornaments.
Caranam 6You dispel the cluster of sins. You destroyed the ten headed _ravana.
You are the Lord of LakSmi. Your nature is delicate. You move pleasantly with shita.
