  Lyrics - English:

Pallavihanumanai anudinam ninai manamE vidhivinai maraindiDum idu nijamE
(madhuvanthi)
Caranam 1dinam dinam tavarugaL SeyginROm manam guNam mAriyE naDakkinROm
paNam maNam enRE tavikkinROm guNanidhi hanumanai marakkinROm
(bAgESrI)
Caranam 2bhaktikku mudaliDam tandavan sattiya rAmanaik-kavarndavan
bhaktarin uLLattil niraindavan bhaktiyin tilakamAy uyarndavan
(sindhubhairavi)
Caranam 3rAma rAma ena bhajippavan rAman sEvaiyai rasippavan
rAma nAmattai pusippavan rAma pAdattil vasippavan
(rEvati)
