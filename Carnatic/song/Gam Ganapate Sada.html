  Lyrics - English:

Pallavigam gaNapatE sadA namastE
AnupallavigangAdhara suta garga munisuta gangA nadI taTa nivAsarata gambhIra mEghadhaviyuta shubha carita
CaranammurAri pramukhAdi samsEvita parAri catvAri vAgvibhUSita
purAri caraNa kamala sEva nirata surAsurAdi sannuta shrI vidyA tOSita
Madhyamakala sahityamnAdabrahma samjAta naham jIvOham brahmAsmIti pratipadita
nAda nAgasvarAvaLi vibhUSita sOhum bhavaikadantam
