anupallavidara cakradAmbuja dhara bhUmi ramAkAnta saridISa krtAvAsa samudita paritOsha
caraNam 1suramuni bhayahara SONa pallavAdhara parama krpA rasa paripUrNa tarApAnga
muravArida samIra mOhana maNI bhUsha harihaya mukha vibudhAnata pada yuga
caraNam 2kana kOpamitacEla kauravAkula pANDu tanaya tApamOcana tArAdipavadana
manasija janaka SrI madhuvana krta khEla vanajAruNa caraNa vAsudEva dEvESa
caraNam 3kamanIyatara rUpa kalyANa guna pUrNa samarAgada dAnava samudAyaAhikha kEndra
gama pATava nirjita gandha sAmaja rAja vimala kunda radana vEdAntAgama vEdya
