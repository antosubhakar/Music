  Lyrics - English:

PallaviUrakE kalgunA rAmuni bhakti
AnupallavisArekunu samsAramuna jocci
sAram(a)ni(y)encu vAri manasuna (UrakE)
Caranam 1Alu sutulu juTTAlu vara
sadanAlu kAya balAlu kanaka
dhanAlu kala vibhavamula kani asthiramul-
(a)nE bhAgya-SAlulaku gAka (UrakE)
Caranam 2manci vArini poDagAnci santatamu
sEvinci manavin(A)lakinci(y)Adari
sAdhinci sarvamu hari(y)ancu telisi
bhAvinci madini pUjincu vAriki gAka (UrakE)
Caranam 3rAjasa guNa yukta pUjalan(o)narincaka
aja sannuta tyAgarAjuni jihvapai
rAjillu vara mantra rAjamunu
sadA japincu maharAjulaku gAka (UrakE)
