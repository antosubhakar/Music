  Lyrics - English:

PallavimahArAgjni mahAlakSmi mahA tripurasundari
AnupallavimahA rAjarAjESvari mahAbala giri nivAsini
CaranamdEva dEva pUjitE paradEvatE namOstutE bhAva jAri harikESa bhAgadEya paramESvari
