  Lyrics - English:

Pallavitolliyunu ma~r~rAku toTTelane yUgegana | cellubaDi nUgIni SrIraMgaSiSuvu ||
Caranam 1kaliki kAvEri taragala bAhulatalanE | talaga kiTu raMga madhyapu toTTelanu |
palumAru danunUci pADagA nUgIni | cilupAla selavitO SrIraMgaSiSuvu ||
Caranam 2adivO kamalajuni tiruvArAdhanaM banaga | adana gamalaBavAMDamanu toTTelanu |
udadhulu taraMgamula nUcagA nUgIni | cedarani sirulatODa SrIraMgaSiSuvu ||
Caranam 3vEdamulE cErulai velayaMga SeShuDE | pAdukonu toTTelai paragagAnu |
SrIdEvitO gUDi SrIvEMkaTESuDai | sEdadIreDi vADe SrIraMgaSiSuvu ||
