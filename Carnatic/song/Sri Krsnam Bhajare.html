PallaviSrI kRshNam bhajarE rE mAnasa SrI rUpavatI gOpastrIjAram
CaranamcakranivArita bhAskara prakASam candra SEkhara guruguha viSvAsam akrUravanditapadam arjuna prEmAspadam nakrahata danti varadam nata Suka sanaka nAradam
