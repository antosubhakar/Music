  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrI vENugOpAla dEvA nI vAvA
AnupallavikOvE nin maruga guha kumara gurupara SaNmukha kOvE mAvElar ishaikkuighavE
nin aruL perugavE
CharanammA nandagOpa kumArA vara brahmAnanda gItam taravAn anda mukhil vaNNa
mAlE nin nAma paramAnanda kavi kunjaradAsa nidhipOlE
