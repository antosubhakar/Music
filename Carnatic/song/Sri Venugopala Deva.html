  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI vENugOpAla dEvA nI vAvA
AnupallavikOvE nin maruga guha kumara gurupara shaNmukha kOvE mAvElar iSaikkuighavE
nin aruL perugavE
CaranammA nandagOpa kumArA vara brahmAnanda gItam taravAn anda mukhil vaNNa
mAlE nin nAma paramAnanda kavi kuJjaradAsa nidhipOlE
