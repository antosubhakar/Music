  Lyrics - English:

Pallavirangam shrIrangam ranganAthan urangum arangam tiruvarangam
Anupallavishankham cakram Endi ninravan caturmukha brahmanai tAngi ninravan angamellAm dEvar shUzhndavan engum edilum nilattu nirppavan
CaranamnAradar vaNangiDum nArAyaNA nAn marai uraiviDam nArAyaNA
nAmagaL pAmagaL bhUmagal vaNangiDum dEvAdi dEvanE nArAyaNA
