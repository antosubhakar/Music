  Lyrics - English:

PallavivashamA nI atishaya mahima delpa
Anupallavishashimukhi lalitE shankara shaitE vishAla lOcani nicAcara damani
CaranammunigaNambulu munu nini caraNana kanikarinci kAmitamu licci
mudamunarincina dharmavatI muraLIdhara madhusUdana sOdarI
