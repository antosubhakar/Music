PallavisAmba SivAyanavE rAjitagiri
AnupallavisAmbavI manOharA parAtparA krpAkarA SrI
Caranam 1nIvE guru daivambaniyE vELanu sEvimpucu sadA midini Siva
Caranam 2parama dayAnidhi vanucu maruvaka nA hrdayamuna
mahAdEva mahAprabhO sundara nayana suravara dAyaka bhavabhaya haraSiva
Caranam 3sthira madurApuramuna varamulosagu haruni niratamunu dalaci
Caranam 4SrI Subhakara SaSi makuTadhara jaya vijaya tripurahara
Srtajana lOladbhuta guNa SIlA krtanutapAlA patituni lOlA-
mudambala raGga padAbjamulanu padambujErcu paSupatini
gnyAnamudhyAnamu snAnamupAnamu
dAnamumAnamu abhimAnamanucu
kanikaramunacaraNambulukanu
konuSrutulannutula SaraNanucu (sAmba)
Caranam 5sAresAregu nI nAma mantram kOrinAnu nI pAdAmbuja mantram
dAsuDau cinni krshNuniki dikku nIvEyani SokkanAthuni nammukoni
