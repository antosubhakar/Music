  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrIpatE jaya natajana dAsA shrIla venkaTa shikhara nivAsA
AnupallavilObha rahita krpamrta sindhO lOkanAtha carAcara bandhO
Caranampadmabha murArE kEshavA padma sambhavanuta hE sakalOdbhavA
pAlita bhuvana caturdasha dEvA pAvana vana tulasIrada bhAvA
