  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrIpatE jaya natajana dAsA SrIla veGkaTa Sikhara nivAsA
AnupallavilObha rahita krpamrta sindhO lOkanAtha carAcara bandhO
Caranampadmabha murArE kESavA padma sambhavanuta hE sakalOdbhavA
pAlita bhuvana caturdaSa dEvA pAvana vana tulasIrada bhAvA
