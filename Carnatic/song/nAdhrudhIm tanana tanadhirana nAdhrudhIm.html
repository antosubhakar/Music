  Lyrics - English:

PallavinAdhrudhIm tanana tanadhirana nAdhrudhIm nAdhrudhIm nAdhrudhIm dhrukiTa
AnupallavinAdhrudhru dhIm tomdhrudhru dhIm tataratAni sa ga ma ri sa ni dha pA sA
nAdhruta dhIm ga ma ri sa tadingiNatOm sA ma pa dha ma pa gA tadingiNatOm
CaranamvAraNAnana mayUravAhanAdi rAma bANa samayOdanam
caranAdi sura sundari cikura shEkarI krta padAmbujAm
kAraNAdhipati pancaka prAkrti kAraNa prathama mAtarkam
vAraNAta mukha pAraNam manasi bhAvayAmi paradEvatAm
