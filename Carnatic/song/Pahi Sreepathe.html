(pAhi)
AnupallavisAhAsAdhika garvaharAsura vairinuta parama garimAlaya pAvana
(pAhi)
Caranam 1tApasa gaNa hrdaya talimakrta viharaNa gOpa hatadi suta karuNA rasavAsa
pApa ghana samIraNa pATala bimbAdhara tapanIya vibhUshaNa dAsagaNa bhava-
tApa vibhaJjana lOla sadAvara dAnacaNa kurumE kuSalam bhuvanESvara
(pAhi)
Caranam 2SArada SiSi karasama vilasita mukha nIrada sadrSkAntE nigama sannuta
smaramaJjima haraNa surucira tanurucE hariNa mada SObhita taruNEndu niTila
nArada Saunaka namyapada guruvindaradAkhila mauni budhAvana kOmaLa
(pAhi)
Caranam 3bhAnu SSAGka nayana vAraya pApajAtam vanaruhAsana hara valaripu pUjita
kanaka ruci vasana kamanIya tara kIrtE syAnandUrapurAdhISa sarasijanAbha
mauni samAja bhayApaha maJjuhasEndu-rucE guNasAgara sundara
(pAhi)
