  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhAvamulOna bAhayamulandunu gOvindA gOvindA ani koluvavO manasA
(bhAvamu)
Caranam 1hari avatAramulE akhila dEvatalu hari lOni vE brahmANDambulu
hari nAmamulE anni mantramulu hari hari hari hari anavO manasA
(bhAvamu)
Caranam 2viSNuni mahimalE vihita karmamulu viSNuni pogaDeDi vEdambulu
viSNudokkadE vishvAntarAdamuDu viSNuvu viSNuvani vedakavO manasA
(bhAvamu)
Caranam 3acyutuDitadE Adiyu antyamu acyutudE asurAntakuDu
acyutuDu shrI venkatAdri mIdanitE acyutA acyutA sharaNavO manasA
(bhAvamu)
