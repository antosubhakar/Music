  Lyrics - English:

PallavininaikkAda nEramillai murugA ninaindu ninaindu urugi pADinEn
Anupallavininaitta pOdu nI en mun vara ven(d)um ninaittadellAm nIyAga ven(d)um
Caranamagham magizhndu (or) aNaittukkoNDu Adi mayil Eri varum murugayyA iNaittukkoNDe
ennai ETri vaippAi murugA urugippADiDa nI enakkarul purivAi
