PallavinIlOtpalAmbikAyAstava dAsOham SrI
AnupallavinIlAmbarAnujAyAh nirupama padAmbujAyAh nIlakaNThArdha SarIriNyAh nikhilalOka jananyAh
CaranammAyAmALavagauLAdi dESa prakASinyAh mAtaNgavadanEndrAdi pUjita varadAyinyAh mAyAmaya viSvOtpatti sthitilaya kAraNAyAh mAdhuryAmRta dhAra mahAdEvaguruguhapASinyAh
