  Lyrics - English:

Pallavimayil vAhanA vaLLi manamOhanA SaravaNabhava varamaruLvAi vA mA
AnupallavikayilAyam mudal malaigaLilellAm kaLiththu
viLaiyADum panniru kaiyA murugaiyA
CaranampUrNa candiran pOlum arumukhA bhuvanam eGgum nirai mAyavan marugA
