  Lyrics - English:

PallavikAdiruvenu nAnu SrI rAmA kApADuva daiva nInendu tiLidu
Anupallavikambaniya nIru hAgi dAriyali hrdaya huvanu haraDi ninna nODa
Caranammadhura madhura ninna nuDi kELi Adaradinda ninna nAmava hELi
pAdAmbujagaLa pujeya mADi paramAtmA ninna baLiye iralu
