PallavisadASivamupAsmahE Sam mudA cidAnanda rUpam sadAmudA
AnupallavinidAgha dattAtrEya kapila vAma dEva vyAsa SukAdi vanditapadAmbhOja yugaLam varAbhaya pradAna kuSalam bhakta vatsalam
CaranamcarAcarAtmaka prapaJca gEham surAsura sEvita vRshabha vAham murAri prabhRti dEva samUham parASakti sammELana mOham purANa purusham purAntakam SaGkarAbharaNa bhAsamAna dEham nirAmayam nikhila SOkApaham parAtparam paramaguruguhamaham
