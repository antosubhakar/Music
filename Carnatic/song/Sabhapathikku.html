  Lyrics - English:

PallavisabApatikku vERu deyvam samAnamAkumA?
tillai sabApatikku vERu deyvam samAnamAkumA?
AnupallavikirupAnidi ivaraip pOla
kiDaikkumO inda dAraNi tannil
Caranamoru daram sivasidambaram enRu sonnAl pOdumE
paragatikku vERu puNNiyam paNNa vENDumA?
ariya pulaiyar mUvar pAdam aDaindAr enRE purANam
aRindu solla kETTOm gObAla kirushNan pADum tillai
