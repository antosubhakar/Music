  Lyrics - English:

pallavismarasi purA guru vanita vacasa gatamasmAbhirvana mati tarasA
caraNam 1indhana jAla sama nayanArtham niSi timirENa tadA vitatAyAm
sOdham varsham jatamakAlE bhrAntam vidhta karairayE sahasA
caraNam 2uSasi samEtya sa dESika tilakO dhrshTvatamAniha valitAn vipinE
mOda parItOnu graham akarOdati karuNArdra tarENa hi manasA
caraNam 3Evam sarasijanAbhO vhagavAn tamiha kucElam Samsan sarasam
sa punarabhANidEtam vANim dvija manu jitamihirO nijamAhasa
