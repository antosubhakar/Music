  Lyrics - English:

PallavimAlAghinEn vElavA nin mEl
AnupallavinAlAr marai mEl Agama mUlA mati viSAlA SAlAgama mUlA nAn
CaranamkArAr aJjanagat-kuramin mOhA enavE sadA nAn uruga
mAraraJjana mAl marugA vara kavi kuJjaradAsa nin tAL maruva
