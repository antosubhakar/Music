  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrI kAmAkSi mAm pAhi
AnupallaviEkAmrEshvari bhuvanEshvari vAgIshvarI sEvitE paradEvatE
Caranamvitara janani tava padayuga drDha bhaktim ajnAna vipina viSaya vyAghra bhItAya mE patita pAvani
bhakta pAlini paramEshvari suta sEnAni pAshAnkusha cApa bANa dhAriNi shrI nagara vAsini suvAsini
