  Lyrics - English:

PallavisIsa gang anga aradhang gaurI sang paradhara kE var turanga tApa bhanga jag kE
Caranam 1sadA nanha bharA rang bhUSaN kE bhaya bhujangaTe caraNn mrgamatangsang kIjO pag kE
Caranam 2hatAnangkrpApAng dhArE hAth bich kurang bAs kInhO hrdaykamal padmanAbh prabhu kE

  Lyrics - Tamil:
