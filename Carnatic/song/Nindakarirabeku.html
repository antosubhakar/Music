Pallavinindakarira bEkirabEku
Anupallavihandiyiddare kEri hyngge suddhiyo hAnge
Caranam 1andandu mADida pApavemba maladindu hOguvarayya nindakaru
vandisi stutisuva janarellaru namma pondiha puNyvanoyyuvrayya
Caranam 2duSTa janaru I shrSTiyoLiddhare shiSTa janarigella kIrtigaLu
iSTaprda shrI krSNa ninnoLu iSTE varavanu bEDuvenayya
