PallaviramA ramaNa rArA O (ramA)
Caranam 1samAnam(e)varu vinumA nA manavini
tam(A)su pogaDa taram(A)hi patikini (ramA)
Caranam 2budh(A)d(y)avana daSarath(A)rbhaka manO-
rathamb(o)sagu suma rath(A)rha sad-guNa (ramA)
Caranam 3kal(A)rtha bhUsha sakal(A)rtha SaSa dhara
kalA dhara nuta vikal(A)rti samhAra (ramA)
Caranam 4raN(A)dhi SUra SaraN(A)gata tvac-
caraNaM bhava tAraNambu cEsunu (ramA)
Caranam 5mukh(A)bjamunu Sata mukh(A)ri jUpu
samukhAna kolutu durmukh(A)sura haraNa (ramA)
Caranam 6birAna brOvaga rAdA SrImad-aga
rAja dhara tyAgarAja sannuta (ramA)
