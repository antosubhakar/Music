  Lyrics - English:

PallavisannutAGgi SrI cAmuNDESvari SatOdari sAdhu janAvanE sArasa nayanE
Anupallavipunnama candruni bOlumukhi bhUpa nAlvaTi SrI krshNEndra sannutAGgi
CaranambAlE bhava harikESalOlE bhakta pAlE
