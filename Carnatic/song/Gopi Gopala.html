  Lyrics - English:

PallavigOpi gOpAla lAla rAsa maNDalamAhi tAta dEhi dAshuDaGga niratata gahimAhi
dhrima dhrima dhrima dhrima mrdaGga jhana nananana rUpa raGga
dhrkiTa dhrkiTa tAlataGga ukaTata rasa nAhi
rAdhA mukha Sarada canda pUJjata jala sama ghananda
SrI bhraja canda laTaka laTaka harada mukuTa chAhi
tatta tatta sugaragAda sa ri ga ma pa dha nI ghaDADa
aura pradahi prahlAda saGga dampati atikAla
sahita sahita yamuna nIra gaga mrga jaga maga SarIra
dhana nanda kEtu mAra bhali bhali jAya sUradAsa rAsa sugati pArayE
