  Lyrics - English:

PallavigOpi gOpAla lAla rAsa maNDalamAhi tAta dEhi dASuDanga niratata gahimAhi
dhrima dhrima dhrima dhrima mrdanga jhana nananana rUpa ranga
dhrkiTa dhrkiTa tAlatanga ukaTata rasa nAhi
rAdhA mukha sharada canda pUnjata jala sama ghananda
shrI bhraja canda laTaka laTaka harada mukuTa chAhi
tatta tatta sugaragAda sa ri ga ma pa dha nI ghaDADa
aura pradahi prahlAda sanga dampati atikAla
sahita sahita yamuna nIra gaga mrga jaga maga sharIra
dhana nanda kEtu mAra bhali bhali jAya sUradAsa rAsa sugati pArayE
