Caranam 6nA manO-haramu nAdu SRngAramu (pAhi)
Caranam 7nA pAli bhAgyamu nAdu vairAgyamu (pAhi)
Caranam 8nAdu jIvanamu nAdu yauvanamu (pAhi)
