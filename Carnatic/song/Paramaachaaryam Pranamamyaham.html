  Lyrics - English:

PallavipamarAcAryam praNamAmyaham prabhum pASaNDa ripum parakAya pravEsha vibhum
Anupallaviniravadhi sukha dAyakam nIti mArga bOdhakam nitya vastu rUpakam nikhila vandya pAdukam
CaranamkAma rahitam krpAkaram pAmarahitapradam varam kAmakOTi sundaram abhirAma kambu kandaram
sOma sUrya lOcanam abhirAmapura vAsinam svarNa venkaTEsha dIkSitEna shritipada pratiSThitam shrI
Madhyamakala sahityamsubrahmaNya prakAsham sUkSma saccidAnanda cinamudrayA chinna samshayam
aprAmANya prabhEdinam AryA shiva gurvAtmaja mahamityAnandinam shrI shankara
