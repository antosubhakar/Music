  Lyrics - English:

PallavipamarAcAryam praNamAmyaham prabhum pAshaNDa ripum parakAya pravESa vibhum
Anupallaviniravadhi sukha dAyakam nIti mArga bOdhakam nitya vastu rUpakam nikhila vandya pAdukam
CaranamkAma rahitam krpAkaram pAmarahitapradam varam kAmakOTi sundaram abhirAma kambu kandaram
sOma sUrya lOcanam abhirAmapura vAsinam svarNa veGkaTESa dIkshitEna Sritipada pratishThitam SrI
Madhyamakala sahityamsubrahmaNya prakASam sUkshma saccidAnanda cinamudrayA chinna samSayam
aprAmANya prabhEdinam AryA Siva gurvAtmaja mahamityAnandinam SrI SaGkara
