Pallavihariharanai nidam kanivuDan bhajittAl janana maraNa bhayam Edu manamE
(mAND)
AnupallavikaruNAmUrtiyai kArmukhil vaNNanai arumarai pOTRum andivannanai
(shaNmukhapriyA)
Caranam 1karidanaik-kAkka parivuDan vandAn nariyai pariyAkkit-toNDarukk-aruLina

(dhanyAsi)
