  Lyrics - English:

pallavibhajarE mAnasa siddhi vinAyakam sadA
anupallavibhavAnI sutam siddhi dAyakam siddhi sAdhya sEvitam
caraNamvidyOpAsaka vaSyam padmamituram vidyAdhipam
sadyOjAtAdi panca brahmAtmakam anavadyam
madhyamakAla sAhityamvirAga cUDAmaNIm bhakta cintAmaNIm
bhAnucandrikApriyam sAmaja varadm sadayam
