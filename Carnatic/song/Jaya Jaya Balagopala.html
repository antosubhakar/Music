Pallavijaya jaya bAlagOpAla jaya jaya madana gOpAla
jaya jaya vijayagOpAla jaya jaya kalyANa gOpAla
Caranam 1parama purusha jagadISa bhaktajana bhavaya vinASa
nirupama guNa ratnakOSa nitya nirmala kIrti pUritASa
Caranam 2brahmAdi sura vandyapAda bhava bandha hrdaya granthi bhEda
brahmaNya dEva dhrta vEda bala bhadrAvaraja bhakta varada
Caranam 3sura vadhU parigIta vibhava bhakta sukhakara paripUrNa bhAva
nara nArI krta bahu sEva Siva nArAyaNa tIrtha dEva
