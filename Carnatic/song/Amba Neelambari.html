AnupallaviambujAkSi nIdu padAmbujamula neranammiti
ambarIshu modalu ninnu pogaDaka Adaramaina bruhannAyaki
Caranamitaranu nI sari samAnalikalEranucu nE gAcimari mAdhava sOdariyani nI pilacina
