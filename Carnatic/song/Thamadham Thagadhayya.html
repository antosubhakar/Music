  Lyrics - English:

pallavitAmadam tagAdaiyyA
dayAparA tuNai murugayyA (tAmadam)
anupallavitAmasa guNa dInanAna
tamiyEnai ALa taruNamIdaiyyA (tAmadam)
caraNammAlum ayanum kANA mahAdEvanmaindA
mAdu vaLLi dEvamAdu magizh kandA
vElum mayilum ennALum ennaik kAkkavE
vEvEgamAgavE varum varam tArum (tAmadam)
