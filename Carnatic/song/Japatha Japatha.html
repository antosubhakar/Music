PallaviJapatha, japatha hari nama, manuja japatha
Charanam 1Aparimithamho hara manaapyam,
Agama sadhitha mruthyopayam.
Charanam 2Pathithanam api pavana manthram,
Sithi kanda nisa jeevitha thanthram.
Charanam 3Kim bahu thapasa, kim bahu yagai,
Kim bahu yasasa, kim bahu bogai.
Charanam 4Bhakthi yuthaa vaa, bhaya tharala vaa,
Mukthi paraa vaa, bhukthi paraa vaa.
Charanam 5Snane, Pane, Shayane, Gamane,
Dhane, Gane, dharma vidhaane.
Charanam 6Aapadhi, Sampadhi cha akhila samaye,
Sripathi pada manya chinthaya hrudaye.
Charanam 7Jani mruthyu roga rasayana bhootham,
Sanaka mukha akhila san muni geetham.
Charanam 8Sri Ramachyutha ri raman ethi,
Narayana padmanabhethi jayathi.
