PallaviJapatha, japatha hari nama, manuja japatha
Caranam 1Aparimithamho hara manaapyam,
Agama sadhitha mruthyopayam.
Caranam 2Pathithanam api pavana manthram,
Sithi kanda nisa jeevitha thanthram.
Caranam 3Kim bahu thapasa, kim bahu yagai,
Kim bahu yasasa, kim bahu bogai.
Caranam 4Bhakthi yuthaa vaa, bhaya tharala vaa,
Mukthi paraa vaa, bhukthi paraa vaa.
Caranam 5Snane, Pane, Shayane, Gamane,
Dhane, Gane, dharma vidhaane.
Caranam 6Aapadhi, Sampadhi cha akhila samaye,
Sripathi pada manya chinthaya hrudaye.
Caranam 7Jani mruthyu roga rasayana bhootham,
Sanaka mukha akhila san muni geetham.
Caranam 8Sri Ramachyutha ri raman ethi,
Narayana padmanabhethi jayathi.
