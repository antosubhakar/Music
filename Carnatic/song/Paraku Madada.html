PallaviparAku mADade parAmbarisi enna aparAdhangaLa kSamiso
AnupallavidharA ramaNa phaNidharArcita surAdhipati vandita
Caranam 1nararoLage pAmaranu nAniha parake sAdhanavariye shrI hariye
sharaNu hokke ninna caraNa kamalakke kaRuNadhim ninna smaraNe enagittu
Caranam 2japavanariyenu tapavanariyenu upavAsa vratagaLanariye shrI hariye
krpAvalOkanadinda A pApagaLanella apAhatava mADo apAra mahimane
Caranam 3kariya rakSisi draupadiya more lAlisi taraLa kolidu nI porede dayadi
sariyarasa ninna sariyAru kANe karuNisayya shrI purandara viTTala
