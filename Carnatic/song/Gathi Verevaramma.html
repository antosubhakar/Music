  Lyrics - English:

pallavigati vErevarammA kAmAkshyammA
anupallavipatita janAvani parama pAvani idi meragAdu ISvari nAku
caraNamrati kOTi lAvaNya nidhi ninnu sEvincunu Sruti vedAgama
stuta Subha dAyika vidhi hari vandita vimala padayugE
