  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSambhO mahA dEva Sankara girijA ramaNa
AnupallaviSambhO mahA dEva SaraN(A)gata jana rakshaka
ambhO-ruha lOcana pad(A)mbuja bhaktiM dEhi (SambhO)
Caranamparama dayA-kara mRga dhara hara gangA dhara dharaNI
dhara bhUshaNa tyAgarAja vara hRdaya nivESa
sura bRnda kirITa maNi vara nIrAjita pada
gOpura vAsa sundarESa gir(I)Sa parAtpara bhava hara (SambhO)
