  Lyrics - English:

PallavidAsOsmyaham danuja shAsaka dAmOdara dEvA tAvaka
Anupallaviguru mAruta nagara bhASaka paricaraka durita nAshaka
CaranamkamsAntaka vamsha kAyaka kalyANa baladALa dAyaka
kAntAjana kusuma sAyaka kAntAra tuLasI vidhAyaka
