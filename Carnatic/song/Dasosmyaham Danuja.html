  Lyrics - English:

PallavidAsOsmyaham danuja SAsaka dAmOdara dEvA tAvaka
Anupallaviguru mAruta nagara bhAshaka paricaraka durita nASaka
CaranamkamsAntaka vamSa kAyaka kalyANa baladALa dAyaka
kAntAjana kusuma sAyaka kAntAra tuLasI vidhAyaka
