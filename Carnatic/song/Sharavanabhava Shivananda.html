  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSaravaNa bhava SivAnandA nalla taruNamidu karuNai puriguvAy kandA
AnupallavitiruvaruLagelAm Siranda vEl murugA dEvasEnAdhipa Sen kaN mAl marugA
CaranamvEdanai SiraiyiTTa vimala mey bOdhA meypporuL viritta vEdA sAminAthA
mAdavarkkaruL mAri pozhiyum prasAdA vAnavar munivar vaNaGgum porp-pAdA
(madhyamakAlam)
mAdumaik-karumai kumArA mAya dushTharai mAykkum vIrA
nIda mAtiruvOr siGgArA nIlakaNThan adDiyEnuk-kupakArA
