  Lyrics - English:

pallavikoti janmani saphalani santu
gopavadhU jana lochana viSaya
komala madhura damini nirata
anupallavikrIdita sarasa rasa rasa senanga
khelita hari pada padme uttunge
caraNammarakata maya veni yamuna tIra
madhura govardhana malaya samIra
sura gana nuta lata kunja kutIra
Sobhita radha mukha chandra chakora
