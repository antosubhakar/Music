  Lyrics - English:

PallavigIta vAdya naTana nATakapriyA rasikE san-
AnupallavivEda rUpiNi visvAsini nAdasvarUpiNi prakASini
CaranammArakOTi sundarAGki mAyE mAtaGki marakata chAyE
sArasAkshi sarvasAkshi sAdhujana rakshaki kaTAkshi
