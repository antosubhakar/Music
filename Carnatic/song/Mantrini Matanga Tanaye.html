  Lyrics - English:

PallavimantriNi mAtanga tanayE
Anupallaviyantra tantra svatantra shAlini indranutE sakala mantra rUpiNi
Caranambhakta jana hrtkamala vAsini rakta bIjAdya sura vimardini
shakti pore harikEsha bhAmini raktiyuta sangIta mOdini
