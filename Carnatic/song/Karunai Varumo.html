  Lyrics - English:

PallavikaruNai varumO enrEnginEn shivakAma sundari shiridEnum unNdan
AnupallavivaruNan pozhiyum mazhaimukham nOkki vADum sAtaka paravaiyAnEn unran
CaranammaNNil viLaiyADum nAL mudal undan malaradi maranNdaduNDO
viNNOr munivar pugazh pOlagham vaLar en kuladeivamE manamiranNgi enrunadu
