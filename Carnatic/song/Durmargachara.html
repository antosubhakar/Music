  Lyrics - English:

PallavidurmArga car(A)dhamulanu
dora nIv(a)na jAlara
Anupallavidharm(A)tmaka dhana dhAnya
daivamu nIvai(y)uNDaga (dur)
Caranampaluku bOTini sabhalOna

khalulan(e)ccaTa pogaDani SrI-
kara tyAgarAja vinuta (dur)
