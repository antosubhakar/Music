  Lyrics - English:

pallavikarunApAyOnidhE dASarathE mAmava sItApatE kamanIya rUpa nijinta kAma raghukula sAvanbhaumA
anupallaviSaraNAgatAtin haraNAnagha suguNAma ragana tOshaNa
hariNAnka SEkhara vara kOdaNDa khaNDana durINA
caraNamsuravairi rAvana madana vara yOgi mAnasa sadana Saradindu sannibha vadana
paripAlitAkhila bhuvana bharata vibhIshaNa lakshmaNAdi parivrta paramAdbhuta carita
parama purusha vAsudEva paraipUnan kAma pavana tanaya vanditapada
