  Lyrics - English:

Pallavininn(A)Da(n)Ela nIraj(A)ksha
AnupallavikannavAri paini kAka sEyan(E)la (ni)
Caranam 1karmamunaku taginaTlu kAryamulu naDucunu
dharmamunaku taginaTlu daivamu brOcunu (ni)
Caranam 2cittamunaku taginaTlu siddhiyu kalgunu
vittamunaku taginaTlu vEDuka naDucunu (ni)
Caranam 3satya rUpa ninnu sannuti jEsi
tatvamu telisina tyAgarAjuniki (ni)
