  Lyrics - English:

PallavinavamanOhara murAri vanamAla haradhAri nava yauvanAGga maurI sumanOhara hE
AnupallaviramaNIya smita vadana kamanIya jita madanA harE harE yamunA vihArI su-manOhara hE
CaranammuLIdhara gAnalOla surucira gOpIlOlA taruNAruNa padayugaLA
karuNAmaya kambugaLA harE harE ghananIlA su-manOhara hE

  Lyrics - Tamil:

கருணாமய கம்புகளா ஹரே ஹரே கனனீலா சு-மனோஹர ஹே
