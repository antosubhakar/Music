  Lyrics - English:

Pallavikaivannam angu kanden, kal vannam ingu kanden.
ivvannam nigazhndha vannam, ini indha ulahukkellam
uyivannam anri, matror thuyarvannam uruvadhundo?
maivannathu araki pOril, mazhai vannathu annale, un
kaivannam angu kanden, kal vannam ingu kanden.
