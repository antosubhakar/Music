  Lyrics - English:

PallavibirAna varAl(i)cci brOvumu ninu nera nammiti
Anupallavipur(A)ri manO-hAriNI SrI kAmAkshi (birAna)
Caranam 1tAmasamu sEyakE nIvu karuNA nidhi
gAdA parAmukham(i)kan(E)la vinu sarOja mukhi (birAna)
Caranam 2kAmit(A)rtha phala dAyakI dEvI nata kalpa
latikA purANi madhura vANi Sivuniki rANi (birAna)
Caranam 3SyAma kRshNa sOdari gaurI param(E)SvarI
girijA anAtha rakshaNambu salupaga rAvE (birAna)
