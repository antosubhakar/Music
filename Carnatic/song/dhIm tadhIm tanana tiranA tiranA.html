  Lyrics - English:

PallavidhIm tadhIm tanana tiranA tiranA udana tanana tanOm tanana nAdhrutAni
Anupallavitom dhru tAni tom dhrudhirutAni tom tarikiTa taka dhittillAna
tAmkiTa jham tillAnA tAmkiTa jham tAmkiTajham
CaranameGgum nirainda EkamAna nitya maGgaLAkAra SrI mahA tripura
sundari Siva manOhari SaGkari santatam karuNai tandaruLa vENum
JatisvaramtAm tadhimi dha ga ni , dha ma ga jhaNutari dhitlAm sA sa ni dha ni nI tlAm kiTatOm tlAm kiTatOm
