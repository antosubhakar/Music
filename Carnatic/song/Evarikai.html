Pallavievarkai avatAram(e)ttitivO
ipuDaina telupa(v)ayya rAmayya (evarikai)
Anupallaviavaniki ramm(a)ni pilicina maharAj(e)vaDO
vAniki mrokkeda rAma (evarikai)
CaranamvEda varNanIyamau nAmamutO

muni rAja vEshiyau tyAgarAja nuta (evarikai)
