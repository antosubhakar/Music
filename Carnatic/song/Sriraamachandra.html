  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI rAmacandra diSa mama Subhamati mayi kAruNya sAndrA
AnupallavinarAca cApa SObhi nijakara dhIrOdara suguNa nivasana sAramEya vinata suragaNa
Caranam 1rAma yani niSicara nikara virAma nija mukha ruci vihasita sOma
nara vara kulamahita lalAma ravi vamSa vibhUshaNa sAmajAdhipa
samAnayana vara jAnakI ramaNa mauni janAvana
Caranam 2nIla naLa pavanaja mukha kapijAla parivrta Subhatamanija
lIla vitaraNa hrta mada suraSAla kanakOpamitAmbara kAlakAla
vara cApa vibhaJjana kambu kaNTha maNihAra virAjita
Caranam 3kESajita jaladhara SambhuvanESa pada sarasija vinata surESa
hatanata jana ghana bhava pASa apratimAmita vikrama naSitAkhila
malAjita paGkajanAbha cAru sarasIruha lOcana
