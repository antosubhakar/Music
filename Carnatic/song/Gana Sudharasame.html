  Lyrics - English:

PallavigAna sudhArasa pAna niratam
gAnam karOmi santatam sAma
Anupallavishri nAradAmshajam bhAratIya
gAna lakSya lakSaNa sampradAya
samskartAram kruti kartAram
CaranamgAyakajana bhukti mukti pradam
dhyAmi dIna bAndhavam sari
gamapadanI sapta svara rupam
tyAgabrahmam muraLI
