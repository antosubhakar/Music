  Lyrics - English:

Pallavienduku daya rAdurA SrI rAmacandra nIk(enduku)
AnupallavisandaDi(y)ani maracitivO indu lEvO nIk(enduku)
Caranam 1sAreku dur-vishaya sAram(a)nubhavincu
vAri celimi sEya nEraga mEnu
SrI rAma sagam(A)ye jUci jUci
nIraja daLa nayana nirmal(A)paghana nIk(enduku)
Caranam 2tIrani bhava nIradhi AraDi sairimpa
nEraga bhayam(o)ndaga pankaja patra
nIru vidham(a)llADaga iTTi nanu jUci
nIrad(A)bha SarIra nirupama SUra nIk(enduku)
Caranam 3jAg(E)la idi samayamE kAdu jEsitE
E gati paluk(av)ayya SrI rAma
nIvE kAni dari lEd(a)yya dIna SaraNya
tyAgarAja vinuta tAraka carita nIk(enduku)
