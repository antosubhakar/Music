  Lyrics - English:

PallavisadASivam upAsmahE satatam sadASiva sahitam amara vinutam
Anupallavisadguru rUpadharam SaGkaram sadAbhIshTha dAnaparam sarasAGgy-ardhanArISvaram
CaranamaparOksha sugaNa dAyakam arishadvarga mada dhvamSakam agajAtmakam guhajanakam
amartyanagara samrakshakam japa tapAdi nishkAmakaraNa vyaktarUpa pApAkusuma
bhAsuram jagat pratipadya nija rUpam jagadAnandakara trayambakam
