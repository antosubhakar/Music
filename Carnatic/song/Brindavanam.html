  Lyrics - English:

pallavibrndAvanam adhunA manyE sakhi brhadAraNyama ham
sundaratara vamSinAda samsUcita svAnanda bOdhAmrtamaya purusham
caraNam 1unnata SAkAghra parAnanda nirmaya rasapUra phala bhAram
puNyavatAm ati ramaNIyam amarEndra varNita bhUmapadam bhavabhAram
caraNam 2munivara parisEvitam mOha virAma mUlam aSEksha viSEshAtpadam
anikEta nikEtanam akhila SOka haram anudayalaya para tara siddhita phaladam
caraNam 3sakrdidam avalOkitam samyagiha svasuta kaLatrAdhi dhanavirasam
prakaTayati paramahamsa samAkulam amala mAnasa vrtti vilasita sarasam
caraNam 4parihrta ripu vargam parama yOgi paripUrNa bhAv viSEshOdayam
hari parabhAvanayA gOpikA gItam iti phaNitam nArAyaNa tIrtha yatinA
