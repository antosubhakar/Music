  Lyrics - English:

PallaviparipAhimAm shrI dAsharathE para vAsudEva mahAnubhAva
AnupallavidharaNi sutA mAnasa vihAra dharmArtha kAma mOkSAdAra
CaranamsAkEta purAdIsha sarvEsha satya dharma pAlaka svaprakAsha
rAkA sudhAkara nibhAnana rAjIva nayana rakSita bhuvana
