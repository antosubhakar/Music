  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI pArvati paramAdvaita SivE parE tripurasundari
AnupallavinA paritApamu dIrci nanubrocuTa nikhila lOka janani nIve para mantra nivEdini
CaranamparASakti parama kalyANi parAku sEyaka rAdamma sari yevarunnArilalO nIku samAna
deivamu ne gAci SaSimukha lalita SrI krshNa sahOdari sadAnanda jagadamba mahESvari
