  Lyrics - English:

Pallavi. kisalaya sayanathalE kuru kAminicharaNanaLiNa vinivEsamthavapadha pallava vairi parAbhavamidhamanu bhavathu suvEsam kshaNamadhunA nArAyaNamanugatham anubhaja rAdhE
. karakamalEna karOmi charaNamahamAgamadAsi vidhUramkshaNamupakuru sayanOpari mAmivaNoopuram anugathisUram
. vadhana sudhAnidhi gaLitham amruthamivarachaya vachanam anukUlamvirahamivA panayAmi payOdhararOdhakam urasi dhukoolam
. priya parirambaNa rabasa valithamivapuLakitham athidhura vAbammadhurasi kuchakalasam vinivEsayasOshaya manasija thApam
. adhara sudhArasam upanaya bhAminijeevaya mrudhamiva dAsamthvayi vinihitha manasam virahAnaladhagdha vapusham avilAsam
. sasimukhi mukharaya maNirasanAguNamanuguNa kaNda nidhAnamsruthiyugaLE pikaruthavikalE mamasamaya chirAth avasAdham
. mAmapi vipalarushA vikaleekruthamavalOkithum adhunEdhammeelathi lajjithamiva nayanam thavavirama visruja rathi khEdham
. sree jayadEvabhaNithamidham anupadhanikadhitha madhuripu mOdhamjanayathu rasika janEshu manOramarathi rasa bhAva vinOdham
