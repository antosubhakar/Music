. priya parirambaNa rabasa valithamivapuLakitham athidhura vAbammadhurasi kuchakalasam vinivEsayasOshaya manasija thApam
. adhara sudhArasam upanaya bhAminijeevaya mrudhamiva dAsamthvayi vinihitha manasam virahAnaladhagdha vapusham avilAsam
