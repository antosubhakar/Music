  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviparama pAvanA pApa mOcanA bhAgyamiyAdrA karuNAlavAla
AnupallavinirupamAna tEjO nirmalAtma rUpa parama hamsa vara sadAshiva brahmendra
CaranampApajAlamula kOpa tApa ghana pApamOcana shrI raghu nIvE
guharanjana nE pogaDajAla dhuma rUpadhara mIshukrSNa sannuta
