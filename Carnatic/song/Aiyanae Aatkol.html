  Lyrics - English:

PallaviayyanE ATkoL meyyanE enai muttu murugayyanE kaNNi
Anupallavi: sheyyanE nin ishai dikkengum paravattakka vAkkanugrahittuccittu muttu murug-
Caranam 2santamighunda kanda gAnAmudam tA brahmAnandak-kanda shonda murug-
Caranam 3konkulAm kavi kunjradAsan anjcorkkaruL aindaru guru tiru murug-
