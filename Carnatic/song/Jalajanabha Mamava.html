pallavijalajanAbha mAmava SrI nAyaka
anupallavijalada mEcaka dEha jAtarUpa bhUshaNa salila nithiSayana sarasijadaLa nEtra
caraNam 1dharaNIbhara murAdi danuja kula nidhana pari dhrta manuja rUpamala mUrtE
dara tulya manOgya kandhara dInabandhO karuNAlayamE dEva kalaya kuSalamayi
caraNam 2asi tAlakAvrta tarAsya SIta kiraNa hasita nindita muktAhAra dEvESa
lalita pItavasana lambavanya mAlika vasa mama harudi nityam vArijAsana sEvya
caraNam 3makara kuNDala kAnti maNDita su-kapOla Suka nAradAdi samstuta guNajAla
vikaTatara samsAra vipinadaLa Saunya sakala lOka nAyaka sAmajAdhipa pAla
