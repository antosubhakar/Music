Caranam 1dharaNIbhara murAdi danuja kula nidhana pari dhrta manuja rUpamala mUrtE
dara tulya manOjnya kandhara dInabandhO karuNAlayamE dEva kalaya kushalamayi
Caranam 2asi tAlakAvrta tarAsya shIta kiraNa hasita nindita muktAhAra dEvEsha
lalita pItavasana lambavanya mAlika vasa mama harudi nityam vArijAsana sEvya
Caranam 3makara kuNDala kAnti maNDita su-kapOla shuka nAradAdi samstuta guNajAla
vikaTatara samsAra vipinadaLa shaunya sakala lOka nAyaka sAmajAdhipa pAla
