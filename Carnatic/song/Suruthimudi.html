poruma adhilE koNda mukguNa vibAga nilai
karudha ariyA vaJjaga kabada mUdi udal vinaithAnE
kalagamidavE poGgu kuppai mala vAzhvu nijam
enauzhalu mAyan jeniththa guhaiyE uRudhi
karudha suzhamAm indha mattai thanai ALa una dharuLthArAy
oru niyamamE viNda Satsamaya vEdha adi
mudi naduvumAy aNda muttai veLiyAgi uyir
udal uNarvadhAy eGgum uRpanamadhAga amar uLavOnE
udhadharisamAm inba puththamirtha bOga sugam
