  Lyrics - English:

PallaviAdipurIshvaram sadA bhajEham tripura sundarI samEta vara guruguha janakam vandita muni samUham
Caranamvidhi hari pUjita tyAga rAjAHNgam Aditya kOTi prakAsha liHNgam nandi pUjita svayambU liHNgam nAgakavacadharasaikataliHNgam
