pallavidEvakI kanda mukunda
caraNam 1nigamOddhAra navanIta cOra khagapati vAhana jagadOddhAra
caraNam 2Sankha cakradhara SrI gOvinda pankaja lOcana paramAnanda
caraNam 3makara kuNDaladhara mOhana vEsha rukumiNI vallabha pANDava pOsha
