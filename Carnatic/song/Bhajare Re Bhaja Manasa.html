PallavibhajarE rE bhaja mAnasa SrI nArasimham bhava sAgara tArakam bhukti mukti dAyakam
Anupallaviaja rudrAdi vandita padapangkEruham nijabhakta sandOha kalpabUruham
CaranamparamAdbhuta rUpadharam karuNakaram aham sura kinnara bhUsurAdi ripu hiraNyakashipuvaram
parama bhakta prahlAda paramAnandakaram vara vAsudEvam paramESvaram nirantaram
