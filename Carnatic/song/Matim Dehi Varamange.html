Anupallavipatita pAvana ambOruha caraNE paGkajAsanE SaSinibha vadanE pAlita bhuvanE karuNAbhavanE parama purusha vakshasthala sadanE
CaranamdEva ramaNa kalaSa jalanidhi vadana calita divya mAlA vara bhUshaNa dOrvilAsa darSanAdi harisha viSAla padma nayanE dEva mahIruha mAlAsanE divya kalabha yuga krtAbhishEcanE dEva dEva dayitE paramAdbhuta caritE sura muni vara gaNa pUjitE
