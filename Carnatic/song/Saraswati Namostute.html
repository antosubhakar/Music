Pallavisarasvati namOstutE sArasa bhava sammOda sandAyini
Anupallavivara svarAlaya sthitE vAgISvarivardE SrI
Caranamnigama SirOmaNI rUpiNI natajana paripAlini
jagajjanani nIlavENi rAmacandra vinuta vANi
