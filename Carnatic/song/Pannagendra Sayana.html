  Lyrics - English:

Pallavipannageendra-sayana! Sri-Padmanaabha! mudaa Kaama-
-sanna-maanasa maamava saarasaayatha-lochana! - shankaraabharaNam
Caranam 1manda-maaruthopi mama maanasam vivasayathi
kunda-kudmala-radana! kopa-maasu jahi mayi - kaambhOji
Caranam 2kokila-sukaa-di manju-koojithamapi mee hantha!
paakasasana-vinutha! bhaathi khoarathama mayee - neelaambari
Caranam 3maaninee-jana-hasitham maa kuru maam-anyeesa!
soonasaayakasadrusa! sobhanaamga! dayaa-para! - bhairavi
Caranam 4yaaminee-samvesaya twam kaama-mavalokayaami
komalaanga vigalitha-khora-thaapa khsanam tadaa - tODi
Caranam 5paatala-bimba-sadrusa! paavana-vimalaa-dhara!
haatakopama-vasana! haara-sobhitha-kandhara! - shuruTTi
Caranam 6deva-deva! krupayaa mee dehi baahu-peedanam
bhavayaami bhavadeeyaa-paanga-leelaam Ramaavara! - naadanaamakriyaa
Caranam 7modayaami jagadeesa! mohana Kaama-kelishu
saadara-marthayee natha! saamaja-vara-gamana! - bhoopaaLam
