  Lyrics - English:

PallavigOpAla nandana gOkulabAla gOpImanOhara kuJjarAgammana
AnupallavibhUpAla nandana puNyAvatAra SrIpatE mAmava SrGgAra rUpA
CaranamgOvardhanadharA krUra samhArA bhAvita munijana pAlakA vAsudEvA
SivapriyA SiSupAla kAlA SrI harikESapura divyAlaGkArA
