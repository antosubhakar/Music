  Lyrics - English:

PallavimuLLu koneya mEle mUru kereya kaTTi eraDu baridu ondu tumbalilla
Caranam 1tumbalillada karege bandaru mUvaroDDaru ibbaru kuNTaru obbage kAle illa
Caranam 2kAlillada oDDarige koTTaru mUru emmegaLa eraDu baraDu ondakke karuve illa
Caranam 3karuvillada emmege koTTaru mUru honnugaLa eraDu savakalu ondu sallale illa
Caranam 4salladA honnugaLige bandaru mUru nOTagaru ibbaru kuruDaru obbage kaNNE illa
Caranam 5kaNNillada nODagarige koTTaru mUru UrugaLa eraDu hALu Uru ondakke okkale illa
Caranam 6okkalillada Urige bandaru mUvaru kumbAraru ibbaru coNTaru obbage kaiye illa
Caranam 7kaiyillada kumbarAru mADyAru mUru maDakegaLa eraDu oDagu ondakke buDave illa
Caranam 8buDavillada gaDigage hAkidaru akkigaLa eraDu haJjikki ondu bEyalilla
Caranam 9bEyalillada akkige bandaru mUvaru neNTaru ibbaru uNNaru obbage hasive illa
Caranam 10hasivillada neNTarige koTTru mUru ToNebegaLa eraDu sOkavu ondu tAkale illa
Caranam 12innu I arthagaLella purandara viTTala balla anyar-yAru tiLidavarilla
