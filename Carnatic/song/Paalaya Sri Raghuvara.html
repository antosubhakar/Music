  Lyrics - English:

pallavipAlaya SrI raghuvIra su-
krup(A)laya rAja kumAra mAM (pA)
caraNam 1tAr(A)dh(I)Sa vadana rAma
tAr(A)d(I)Sa damana mAM (pA)
caraNam 2hima kara kOTi nibh(A)nga
Ehi makara haraNa nis-sanga mAM (pA)
caraNam 3rAkshasa jana gaNa dUra
makar(A)ksha samara hara SUra mAM (pA)
caraNam 4SrI daSaratha kula SaraNa rAma
SrI-da Saradhi mada haraNa mAM (pA)
caraNam 5ku-manO jana gaNa bhIma rAma
su-manO vAridhi sOma mAM (pA)
caraNam 6jaya vijay(A)gha virAma
ravija yama lAlita nAma mAM (pA)
caraNam 7tyAgarAja nuta caraNa
nity(A)ga rAja dhara suguNa mAM (pA)
