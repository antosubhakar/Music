PallaviSankari ninnE ika cAla nammitini Sankari ninnE ika cAla nammitini
AnupallavipankajAkshi nIdu mahima pogaDuTa nA taramA
pankajAnanE praNatajana Sankari SrI tripurasundari
CaranamkAma janaka vAsudEva sahOdari SyAmaLAngai Siva hrdayOllAsini
