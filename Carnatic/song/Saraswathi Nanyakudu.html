  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisarasvati nannepuDu callaga jUDavamma
Anupallavivara vINA pustakamula vadalaka dhariyiJcina SrI
CaranamnIdu vAkkuvalana nikhila lOkamulaku vEda vEdAntamula nosagi brOcitivi nAdu
manamu nanneppuDu nilacigAdu vanucu nI pAdamulanu nammiti gOpAladAsa pOshiki ikanu
