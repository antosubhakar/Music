PallaviSaurE vitara kuSalamayi mE aniSam sakala jagadadhISa
AnupallavikAruNyAdi guNalaya kanakAmbara sanakAdi nishEvita
Caranam 1pAlita vAraNa parivrDha himakara bhAnunayana rucira lIlakAla
kAlanutAnagha kamalAvara SamalApaha sucarita
Caranam 2kAmita dAyaka madhuhara tanu jitakAma vadana vijita sOma
SyAma dEha harucEvara samarApatadhamarAnta kavi dalaNa
Caranam 3nArada Saunaka mukhanuta sarasijanAbha kaLa vilasita hAra
vArijAyata lOcana vasudhAbharaNa sudhA mrdula vacana
