  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisarasvati namOstutE SAradE vidyApradE
Anupallavikara dhrta vINA pustaka varamaNi mAlAlaGkrta
Caranamnarahari suta vidhi lAlita navamaNi yuta kambhugaLE
sura sEvita pada yugaLE sudhAkara samadhavaLE
