  Lyrics - English:

PallavibudhamAshrayAmi satataM
sura vinutaM candra tArA sutam
Anupallavibudha janairvEditaM bhU-surairmOditaM
Madhyamakala sahityammadhura kavitA pradaM mahanIya sampadam
Caranamkunkuma sama dyutiM guru guha mudAkRtiM
kuja vairiNaM maNi makuTa hAra -
kEyUra kankaNAdi dharaNam
kamanIya-tara mithuna kanyAdhipaM
pustaka karaM napuMsakaM
Madhyamakala sahityamkinkara jana mahitaM kilbiSAdi rahitaM
shankara bhakta hitaM sadAnanda sahitam
