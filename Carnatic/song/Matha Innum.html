  Lyrics - English:

Pallavi: mAtA innum vAtA uyar tiru mayilaiyin nAyakiyE tAyE sakala lOka
Anupallavi: pAdAra vindam aladu vErenda bhAgyamum virumbA tozhumban enniDam jagan
CaranamEzhai endan muraiyIDu unran iru sevigaLil vizhAdaden tIvinaiyO
pAzhAm at-tIvinayai vEruDan kaLainderiya tiruvarut-tiran ilaiyO
tAzha vizhum enai mElum vizhaviDudal dharmamO iduvum varmamO
tALinai caraN ena nambinEn ammA rAmadAsan tozhum kalpaka nAyaki
