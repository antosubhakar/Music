  Lyrics - English:

PallavisvAmin gajamukha vibhO mAmava
AnupallavishyAmaLa varNa sunaya shakti shankara tanaya kOmaLa guNAlaya kumAra sahOdara jaya
CaranamindrAdi vanditapadA shrita mandAra manjuLa sadA muni brndAdi hrdkamala
bhrnga sadrsha sukha mukunda bhAginEya hita kumjara kavi praNIta
