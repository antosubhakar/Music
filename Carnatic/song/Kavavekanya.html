  Lyrics - English:

PallavikAvavE kanyA kumAri
nIvEgativammA sukumArI
AnupallavipAvanamu mukha kamalamu nA
jIvanamu nI nAmagAnamu
CaranamnIvE karmalakella kartavu
nIvE karmala phalamu littuvu
nIdu rUpamE ninDu punnami
nIdu varNanE muraLI gAnamu
