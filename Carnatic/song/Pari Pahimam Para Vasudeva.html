  Lyrics - English:

pallaviparipAhi mAm para vAsudEva karuNAlavAlASrta mandAra
anupallavikarirAja sannuta saccaritra dharaNI sutAvara SrIkara
caraNamvanajAsanAdi sura vandita sanakAdi yOgi janArcita ina vamSa
sudhAmbudhi pUrNa candra ghana sannibhAnga SrI rAmacandra
