  Lyrics - English:

PallaviISvari rAjESvari SaGkari
AnupallaviSASvata sampat-saukhya dAyini SArada candrAnanE suguNe
Caranammati vihInarigella matyIva tAyE patita pAvanE pAlisau umE SatamakhanutE harikESa sahitE
