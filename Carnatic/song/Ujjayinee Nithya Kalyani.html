  Lyrics - English:

PallaviujjayinI nitya kalyANI!
Omcakti Omcakti Omcakti Omcakti (ujjayinI)
Caranam 1ujjaya kAraNa caGkara tEvI
umA sarasvatI srI mAtA sA. (ujjayinI)
Caranam 2vAzhi punaintu mahEcuvara tEvan,
tOzhi pataGkaL paNintu tuNintanam, (ujjayinI)
Caranam 3catya yukattai akatti lirutti,
tiRattai namakkaru Licceyyum uttami. (ujjayinI)
