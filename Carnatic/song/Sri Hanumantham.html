  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSri hanumantam bhajarE chitta
pavana sutam Sri rAma dootam ||Sri || Oh mind, meditate upon Sri Hanumanta the son of Wind God and Sri Rama's messenger.
AnupallaviSri hari bhajanAmruta pAna niratam
sakala pApa dooram sAdhu Seelam||Sri || He is engrossed in chanting the nectar-like name "Sri Rama" perpetually. He is sinless and has noble character.
CaranamsadA Sri rAmanAma
japOtsAham gadA dharam
sarasa muraLee gAna lOlam
Sri rAma karuNA kaTAksha pAtram ||Sri ||
