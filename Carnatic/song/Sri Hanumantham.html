  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSri hanumantam bhajarE chitta
pavana sutam Sri rAma dootam ||Sri
AnupallaviSri hari bhajanAmruta pAna niratam
sakala pApa dooram sAdhu Seelam||Sri
CaranamsadA Sri rAmanAma
japOtsAham gadA dharam
sarasa muraLee gAna lOlam
Sri rAma karuNA kaTAksha pAtram ||Sri
