  Lyrics - English:

Pallavishri hanumantam bhajarE chitta
pavana sutam shri rAma dUtam ||shri
Anupallavishri hari bhajanAmruta pAna niratam
sakala pApa dUram sAdhu shIlam||shri
CaranamsadA shri rAmanAma
japOtsAham gadA dharam
sarasa muraLI gAna lOlam
shri rAma karuNA kaTAkSa pAtram ||shri
