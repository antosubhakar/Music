  Lyrics - English:

PallavisAgara kanyakA ninnu mrokkiti bhAgyadAyikA
AnupallavivEga nannu samayamu vAgIsha janani dikku nIvamma
Caranam 1kamalamukhi sumukhi kara kalita padma kanja nayana suradana
vimala hrdaya vishvamaya kanci varaduni komma pErundEvamma
Caranam 2ghana sama vENi kanakamaya kumbhastani sumanAsikE rasikE
vanaja nayana yugaLE kambugaLE mrgamada tilakAnkita phAlE
Caranam 3svarNamaya shubhra vasanE sakalAbharaNa bhUSitE bhakta
sankaTa shamanE sadAshiva kavi sannuta suguNE nityakalyANi
