  Lyrics - English:

PallavikaruNa(y)ElAg(a)NTE(n)I vidhamE
kalyANa sundara rAma
Anupallaviparam(A)tmuDu jIv(A)tmuDu okaDai
paraguc(u)NDu bhakta par(A)dhInuni (karuNa)
Caranam 1anRtamb(A)DaDu alpula vEDaDu
su-nRpula koluvaDu sUryuni maravaDu (karuNa)
Caranam 2mAmsamu muTTaDu madhuvunu trAgaDu para-
himsala sEyaDu erukanu maravaDu (karuNa)
Caranam 3mUD(I)shaNamula vADaDu jIvan-
muktuDai tirugu mudamunu jUpaDu (karuNa)
Caranam 4vancana sEyaDu varulatO bonkaDu
cancala cittuDai saukhyamu viDuvaDu (karuNa)
Caranam 5sAkshi(y)ani telisi(y)andu lakshyamu viDuvaDu
kanj(A)kshuni tyAgarAja rakshakuD(ai)na vAni (karuNa)
