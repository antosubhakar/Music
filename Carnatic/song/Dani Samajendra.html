  Lyrics - English:

pallavidAni sAmajEndra gAmini tapAmiha kAmini
anupallavimAninI sukhadAni madhumAsa cAru dinAni mAnanIya SrI padmanAbha dhutAni mamatu mOdhAni
caraNamgAna parANihALi kulAni
svara sAhityam 1gADhasAnvayapi kOpi vitapini
svara sAhityam 2gAma laghu sa purApatira krtayAmahaha bahudhA hrdila gati
svara sAhityam 3mApa mApata tAkhilAdhi bharApaha sucarita mAkalaya madhu
rasa madhurima sadrSa vacasamati mrduhasa mayi mayi sarasa mihahi
svara sAhityam 4pAtumayimA sumaSarA Sugapari patana nIrasa dhrata
visAmupadhAnita bAhulatA madhunAsmrtE mahAniha nahi nahi
kima paramavaSata tamAssuradhanIpatIm apAnnidhimrtE viSati
