  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhajanasEya rAdA O manasA SrI rAmuni
Anupallaviaja bhavEndrAdi nutuni sujanAvana lOluni
CaranamparanArI sahOdaruni paripUrNa kAmuni dharaNijA manOharuni
para vAsudEvuni caraNa janAdhAruni sarasIruha nEtruni
niravadhi sukhadAyakuni paramAtmuni bhakta pAluni
