  Lyrics - English:

pallavichorA chathurya lIla gopala
govinda nanda sukumara navanItha chorA (chita chora)
anupallavimArA kalaka nipuNa
vraja jana maNditha dAsOthsava (chatura chita chora)
caraNamgoparaja thanaya buvana mangaLa
kumudahita charaNa jAla
pApaharaNa dinakara manasija
vriSabhAnu nandini thadalola
nIpatharuNi kara dharasAthirUda
nIratha kaNitha kanakasEla
santhApathimira kalakalmaSanatha
parandhAmA paramabhaghavatha (chita chora)
