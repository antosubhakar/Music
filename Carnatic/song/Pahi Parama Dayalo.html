pallavipAhi parama dayALO harE mAm
caraNam 1sundar(A)nana mukunda rAghava
purandar(A)di nuta mandar(A)ga dhara (pAhi)
caraNam 2pankaj(A)pta hariN(A)nka nayana
SrI-d(A)nka suguNa makar(A)nka janaka mAm (pAhi)
caraNam 3krUra mAnava vidAra bhavya kara
nIrad(A)bha su-SarIra mA ramaNa (pAhi)
caraNam 4nir-vikAra guNa sarva rUpa dhara
pUrva dEva mada garva bhanjana (pAhi)
caraNam 5pUrNa rUpa kalaS(A)rNava sthita su-
parNa vAhana su-varNa cEla mAm (pAhi)
caraNam 6raksha mAm-aniSam-akshar(A)mruta
ras(A)ksha nirjara su-paksha cApa dhara (pAhi)
caraNam 7ranga nAyaka Subh(A)nga sUrya kula
pungav(A)ri mada bhanga sarvadA (pAhi)
caraNam 8nAga Sayana bhava rOga nASa krupA
rAgadE varada tyAgarAja nuta (pAhi)
