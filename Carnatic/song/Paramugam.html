  Lyrics - English:

pallaviparAmukhamEla namma parAdhIna patita nApai
anupallavinirAtaravu nIvE jEsitE nirvahimpa taramA talli
caraNammarALajayita mandagamani mahAdEva matirAyita vadani
nirAmayE nIrada SyAmaLE nitya kalyANa guNAlayE
