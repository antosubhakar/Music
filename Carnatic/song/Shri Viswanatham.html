  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI viSvanAtham bhajE SrI viSAlAkshi sanAtham
AnupallavibhUvisrta vArANasi puravara sadanam sundara vadanam
CaranamannapUrNESvari prANESam aga hara gaGgA tIra nivESam
prasannam alaGkrta kASi nagaram SaGkaram haram SaSi kalA dharam
Madhyamakala sahityamsarva jagan maGgaLakara dIksham sakala manuja kula krupA kaTAksham
garvita kAla haram niTilAksham kAla kaNDam ArUDa mahOksham
