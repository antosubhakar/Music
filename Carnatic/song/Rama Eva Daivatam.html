  Lyrics - English:

PallavirAma Eva daivataM raghu kula tilakO mE
Caranam 1vigata kAma mOha madO
vimala hRdaya Subha phaladO (rAma)
Caranam 2jita gautama kRta SApO
nata varada sItApO (rAma)
Caranam 3bhakta citta tApa harO
bhAvuka dAna caturO (rAma)
Caranam 4sura tAraka gaNa candrO
mura narak(E)bha mRg(E)ndrO (rAma)
Caranam 5hari padmaja Sarv(E)SO
harid(a)Sva su-sankASO (rAma)
Caranam 6tyAgarAja hRt-sadanO
nAga rAja vara SayanO (rAma)
