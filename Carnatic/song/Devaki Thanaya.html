  Lyrics - English:

PallavidEvakI tanaya vAsudEva sadaya mAm pAhi
AnupallavidEva jagannAyaka shrI vara nata shubhadAyaka
Caranamuraga shayana garuDagamana sarasijadaLa lOcana parama puruSa patita
pAvana sura munigaNa tOSaNa shaurE harE murArE dUridhArE kamsArE
