PallaviSara Sara samar(ai)ka SUra
Saradhi mada vidAra
Anupallavisura ripu mUla balam(a)nu tUla
girulak(a)nala samamau SrI rAma (Sara)
Caranamtoli jEsina pApa vana kuThAramA
kalan(ai)nanu sEyaga lEni
balu vilunu virici velasina SrI raghu
kula vara brOvumu tyAgarAja nuta (Sara)
