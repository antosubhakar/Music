  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisumanasa vandita sundari mAdhavi
candra sahOdari hEmamayE
Caranam 1munigaNa vandita mOkshapradAyinI
maJjula bhAshiNi vEdanutE sumanasa
Caranam 2paGkaja vAsinI dEva supUjitE
sadguNa varshiNi SantiyutE
Caranam 3jaya jaya hE madhusUdana kAmini
Adilakshmi sadA pAlayamAm sumanasa
