  Lyrics - English:

PallavishUrya PrakaSa nateSa maheSa gauri kanta koti surya
AnupallavishUrya chandragni netra sujana rakSaka poSaka koti surya
Caranamshamagana Priya sarveSa Dharma palaka jadadiSa
Nilakanta nitya sukha dayaka
Mrutyu bhaya hara Chandrasekhar koti surya
