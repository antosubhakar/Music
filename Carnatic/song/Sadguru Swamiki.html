  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisadguru svAmiki sATiyu lEdani santatamu pogaDi smariJcavE O manasA
AnupallavisadguNa SrI sArasAkshuni bhajiJci sadgati pondina sadbhaktuDau SrI tyAgarAja
CaranamvasudalO merayu SrI paJcanadamuna vara nAdAmcuDai avatariJci
rasamugA gIta SAstra marmamulanu rAjillaga manujulaku bOdhiJci
vasudEva tanayuDuagu SrInivAsa vajIruni okka krpaku pAtruDai
yASamunu nirantaramugA jendina yatIndra parama pAvana SrI tyAgarAja
