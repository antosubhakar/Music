  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisapta girISam sadA bhajEham sadamalam SrI sakham SrInivAsam
Anupallavisapta svara vEdyam sanAtanam SyAmaLam SaGkha cakrAGkitam
Madhyamakala sahityamdhanada mitram kaJjAta nEtram taruNa tuLasikA dhAma vilasitam
tarucara SEkhara sEvita caraNam caraNA sakta janAgha SOSONam
Caranamnirupama karuNA nIradhim varam sura kinnara bhUsura sEvitam
parama pAvana caraNAm bhOjam varadam kalyANI varNita guNajAlam
Madhyamakala sahityamapannArtiharam anantam AnandAmrta varshiNam
ati madhyAnta rahitam accutam AdiSEsha SAyinam mukundam
