PallaviADinaiyE kaNNA brndAvanam tanil Ananda rasamaya arpuda naDanam
AnupallaviIdilA azhagiyar gObiyar unaittEDi nADi vandiDa nadikkarai nilavoLiyil
Caranammayil pIli SaTRE koNDai tanil aSaiya navaratna maguDam Siram tanil oLira
magara kuNDalaGgaL iru Sevi ilaGga madimukham tanilE muruval viLaiyADa
maNam kamazh mAlaigaL mArbil aSaindADa mayaGgum andivaNNa Adai iDaiyADa
madhura mOhana kuzhal iSai kUTTa maGgaiyar kaNgaL maiyyal kATTa
malar kamazh SalaGgai jadi layam kUTTa manamarindu aruL Sorindu iNaindu onrAy
