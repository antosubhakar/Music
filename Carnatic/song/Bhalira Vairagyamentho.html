  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhaLi vairAgyambentO bAgai yunnadi cancalamaina nAmanasu nishcalamai yunnadE
Caranam 1ariSaDvargamulu nannaNTakunnavi shrIhari nAma smaraNa jihvanu anuvai yunnadi
Caranam 2gurudhyAnamuna manasukudurai yunnadi cittamiruvadi yAriNTi mIda niravai yunnadi
Caranam 3parama shAntamennaganu bAgai yunnadi mAku paratattvamandE mA buddhi paTTiyunnadi
Caranam 4virasamu pOrulEni vidhamai yunnadi mAku prakruti yeDabAsi mOkSamuna kiravai yunnadi
Caranam 5guri bhadrAdrIshunandE guruvai yunnadi yipuDu aramaralEka rAmadAsuDanadagi yunnadi
