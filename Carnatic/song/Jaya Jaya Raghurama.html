Pallavijaya jaya raghu rAma
nihata nishAchara satya kAma
Anupallavinayana jita kamala nAma dUritamala
mayidyAm kuru mahita taramaNi - maya vibhUshaNa manukulOttama
CaranamsAkEtapura bhUpa pankajanAbha
samaradarshita kOpa
nAka nAyaka tApa nAshanAbhuta rUpa
srikarAkhila sEvya mamaparamAka laya shubhamAshu dinamanu
