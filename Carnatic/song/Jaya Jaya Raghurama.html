  Lyrics - English:

Pallavijaya jaya raghu rAma
nihata niSAchara satya kAma
Anupallavinayana jita kamala nAma dUritamala
mayidyAm kuru mahita taramaNi - maya vibhUSaNa manukulOttama
CaranamsAkEtapura bhUpa paGkajanAbha
samaradarSita kOpa
nAka nAyaka tApa nASanAbhuta rUpa
srikarAkhila sEvya mamaparamAka laya SubhamASu dinamanu
