  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSaraNu siddhi vinAyaka SaraNu vidyA pradAyaka
AnupallaviSaraNu pArvati tanaya mUrutiSaraNu mUshika vAhana
Caranam 1niTila nEtrana dEvi sutane nAgabhUshaNa prIyane taTila tAGkita kOmalAGgane karNa kuNDala dhArane
Caranam 2baTTa muttina padaka hArane bAhu hasta catushTane iTTa tOdugeya hEma kaGkaNa pASa aGkushha dhArane
Caranam 3kukshi maha lambOdarane ikshucApana gelidane pakshi vAhana siri purandara viTTalana nija dAsane
