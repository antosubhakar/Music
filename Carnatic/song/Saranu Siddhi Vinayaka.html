  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSaraNu siddhi vinAyaka SaraNu vidyA pradAyaka
anupallaviSaraNu pArvati tanaya mUrutiSaraNu mUshika vAhana
caraNam 1niTila nEtrana dEvi sutane nAgabhUshaNa prIyane taTila tAnkita kOmalAngane karNa kuNDala dhArane
caraNam 2baTTa muttina padaka hArane bAhu hasta catushTane iTTa tOdugeya hEma kankaNa pASa ankushha dhArane
caraNam 3kukshi maha lambOdarane ikshucApana gelidane pakshi vAhana siri purandara viTTalana nija dAsane
