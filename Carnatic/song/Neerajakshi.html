PallavinIrajAkSi kAmAkSi nIrada cikurE tripurE
AnupallavishAradA ramA nayanE sArasa candrAnanE vArijapAdE varadE tAraya mAm tatvapadE
CaranamgaurI hindOLa dyuti hIramaNimayAbharaNE shauri viriHnci vinuta shiva shaktimaya navAvaraNE
