  Lyrics - English:

PallavipAhimAm shrI varalakSmi kAmAkSi
AnupallavimahiSAsurAdi ripukula vidhvamsini mahA mahima shAlini
mahA gaNapati guruguha janani mahAbalAdri vAsini
CaranamEhi vArijamukhi tribhuvanEshvari dEhi mEmudam shrI vidyAdIshvari
ahikAmuSmika halapradEshvari dEhi mE drDha bhaktim jagadIshvari
parabrahma svarUpiNi vara guNDakriya rAga rAgiNi kalyANi
