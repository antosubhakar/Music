  Lyrics - English:

PallavipAhimAm SrI varalakshmi kAmAkshi
AnupallavimahishAsurAdi ripukula vidhvamsini mahA mahima SAlini
mahA gaNapati guruguha janani mahAbalAdri vAsini
CaranamEhi vArijamukhi tribhuvanESvari dEhi mEmudam SrI vidyAdISvari
ahikAmushmika halapradESvari dEhi mE drDha bhaktim jagadISvari
parabrahma svarUpiNi vara guNDakriya rAga rAgiNi kalyANi
