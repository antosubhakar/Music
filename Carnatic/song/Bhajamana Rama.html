  Lyrics - English:

Pallavibhaj man rAma caran sukhadAyI
jihi caranani sE nikasI sursari SaGkar jaTA sAmAyI
jaTA SaGkarI nAm paryO hai tribhuvan tAran AyI
jin caranan kI caran pAdukA bharat rahyO lab lAyI
sAyI caran gautam rshi nArI parasi param pad pAyI
daNDak van prabhu pAvan kInhA rshiyan trAs miTAyI
sOyI prabhu trilOk kE svAmI kanak mrgA saGg chAyI
kapi sugrIv badhu bhaya vyAkula tin jai chatra phiri
ripu kA anuj vibhIshan nisihcar parsat laGkA pAyI
Siv sanakAdik aru brahmAdik SEsh sahas mukh gAyI
tulsIdAs mArut sut kA prabhu nij mukh karat baDhAyI
