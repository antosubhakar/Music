  Lyrics - English:

pallaviIsha pAhi mAM jagad(Isha)
anupallaviAshara gaNa mada haraNa
bilEshaya bhUSa sapta RS(Isha)
caraNam 1shrI nAtha kar(A)rcita dorakun(A)lpulak(I) darshanam-
(E) nATi tapaH phalamO nI nAmamu dorakE
shrI nArada gAna priya dIn(A)rti nivAraNa param-
(A)nand(A)rNava dEvayAnA-pa janaka sapta RS(Isha)
caraNam 2vyAs(A)rcita pAlita nija dAsa bhU lOka
kailAsamb(a)nu palkulu nijamE sAreku kaNTi
nI sATi evar(a)yya nI sAkSAtkAramuna
vEsaTal(e)lla tolage nEDE janmamu sAphalyamu (Isha)
caraNam 3sAm(A)di nigama sancAra sOm(A)gni taraNi lOcana
kAm(A)di khaNDana sutrAm(A)rcita pAda
hEm(A)cala cApa ninu vinA mar(e)varu muni manO-
dhAma tyAgarAja prEm(A)vatAra jagad(Isha)
