PallaviEla dayarAdO rAmayya nIkEla dayarAdE rAmayya
AnupallaviSrIla mElukai pAlu paDitinani Ela I Dambhamu cAlunika
Caranam 1brahma gUrcE gade ahO parabrahma kAvagade brahma
janaka bhava brahmEndrAdulu brahmANDamu pAlainArata
Caranam 2pApamula ceta rAmayya nEnOpalenu gade SrI
prabhupaye prApu kEka mI prApu kOriti gOpa rUpa ika
Caranam 3talapaga jAlanAnanda bhAshpa jalamulUrE gade nIla nIrada
nibha kOmala rUpa bhadraSaila vAsA rAmadAsuni nelaga
