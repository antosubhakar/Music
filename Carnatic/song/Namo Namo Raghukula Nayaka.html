  Lyrics - English:

PallavinamO namO raghukula nAyaka divijavantya vandya
namO namO Shankara nagajAnuta!
Caranam 1vihita dharma pAlaka! vIra daSharatha rAma!
gagana vAsini tATaka mardanagahana
ahalyA ShApa vimOchana asurakula bhanjana,
sahaja viShvAmitra Shamana rakshaka savana
(namO)
Caranam 2hara kOdaNDa hara sItAn.ganA vallabha
khara dhUshanArI vAli garvApaha!
taraNi tanujAdi tanujara pAlaka
Sharadhi ran.ga kritya saumitri samEta!
(namO)
Caranam 3biruda rAvaNA chirObhEtaka! vibhIshaNA varada!
SirO bhEdaka
sAkEta puravAsa rAghava nirupama
ShrI venkaTa nilaya-nija
sakala puravara vihArA, puNDarIkAsha
(namO)
