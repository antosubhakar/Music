  Lyrics - English:

Pallavievaraina lErA peddalu
ilalOna dInula brOva
Anupallavibhava sAgaramuna carincu
balu gAsi rAmunitO telupa (evaraina)
Caranamkali mAnav(A)dhamula kAryamulu
kAma matsar(A)dula kRtyamulu
cala cittamu lEni vAr(i)puDu
cAla tyAgarAja nutinitO telupa (evaraina)
