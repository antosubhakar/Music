anupallavigangAdhara tanujam SrI vidyA putram guNagaNa bharitam gaNAdhipam gaNita SAstra caturam
caraNamparAdi catvAri vAg-pratipAlitam harAdi SrOttamOpAsitam Ekadantam parA prakrti
