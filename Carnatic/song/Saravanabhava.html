  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSaravaNabhavA samayamidirA saraguna nannu brOvarA
Anupallaviparama purushA paSupatipriyA sura sEvitA sukumArA
Caranamciru nAda nI cintanatOnu mari mari nI mahima balku nAdu
taramu tIrpa taruNamidirA harikESapura Adi nAyakA
