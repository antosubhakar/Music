  Lyrics - English:

PallaviambA saurambA kucakumbA darabimbA O jagad-
Anupallaviambujamukhi dEvi bhavAni aDigina varamu losagi brOvavE jagadambA
Caranam 1rAdi garbhamuna velayu nI manasu rAyikA sEyaku dEvi nI tanayuni
brOvaga iTuluNDina nEniga viDuvananucu madinencumu
Caranam 2karuNa gAcinanduku nE kOrina kAmitArthamu losagavamma
hari sOdari shrI kAtyAyani Adi mUla kAraNambu nIvE
Caranam 3bhajincinavAri kellanu ika varamu losagu pArvati dEvi
gaja carmAmbara taruni komma grakkuna brOvavE cAmuNDyavammA
