PallavieGgu nAn selvEn aiyA – nIr taLLinAl
eGgu nAn selvEn aiyA?
AnupallavitiGkaL veN piJcinai ceJcaTai tAGkiTum
caGkarA ampikai tAy vaLarmEniyA!
CaranamaJcinOr iTarellAm azhiya Or kaiyinAl
apayamE kATTiTum aruTperum aNNalE!
naJcinai uNTumE, vAnuLOr nalamuRa
nATiTum vaLLalE, nAnmaRai nAtanE!
