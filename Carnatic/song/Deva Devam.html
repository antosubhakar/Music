  Lyrics - English:

PallavidEvadEvam bhajE divya parabhAvam rAvaNAsura vairi raNa pungavam
Caranam 1rAjavara shEkharam ravikula sudhAkaram AjAnubAhu nIlAbra kAyam
rAjAri kOdaNDa rAjadIkSA gurum rAjIva lOcanam rAmacandram
Caranam 2pankajAsana vinuta parama nArAyaNam shankarArjita janaka cApadaLanam
lankA visOkSaNam lAlita vibhISaNam venkaTEsham sAdhu vibhuta vinutam
