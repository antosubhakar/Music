  Lyrics - English:

PallavitAvaka namAmi shubhadAni prajApAni
AnupallavidEva dEva patitOpi divyatamAni yAni
kEvala mAsvAdya yAti kEshava paramAmrutam
Caranam 1vacham yamamauLirapi nUnam yatOhi param
vAlmIkiravApa mahimAnam
shri chakOra sudhAkarAshrita kOmalapadajAtA
sAcha krutirapi tadIya jayati bhuvana punanti
Caranam 2alaghu sarva nindya charitEna sadaiva hanta
kulaTAsamsakta hrudayEna
jalajAkSa satvaram ajAmiLa bhusurEnApi
pralApatA yadEkamihakila mukti rAsAdita
Caranam 3bAlachandrashEkharOpi kAmam yanyEva sadA
padmanAbha japati gatakAmam
shailarAja tanubhavayA sakamati vimala ruchA
kailAsachalE mahati kalita vasati ranisham
