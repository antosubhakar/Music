pallavivenkaTESanAtham jagadjAla pAlam vandE SrI
anupallaviSankha cakra varada pANIm angavastra dhArIm
caraNampankajapada padma nityam indradEva vandya caraNam
mangaLa gOvinda smaraNam sAligrAma mAlAbharaNam
saptagiri vAsam sarvalOka SaraNyam sarva rUpa vEsham sarva rOga nivAriNiyam
madhyamakAla sAhityamSubhanana sumanana sucarita sujanana sugauri sEva
