  Lyrics - English:

PallavimAm-ava raghurAma marakata maNi SyAma
Caranam 1pAmara jana bhIma pAlita sutrAma (mAM)
Caranam 2duritambulu pOdu dunuma manasu rAdu (mAM)
Caranam 3kalaS(A)mbudhilOna karuNa karagi pOyenA (mAM)
Caranam 4vinu mari samaramun(A) vidhi Saramu virigenA (mAM)
Caranam 5kala satyamu suguNa kAnanamuna nilicenA (mAM)
Caranam 6divya nar(A)paghana daivatvamu pOyenA (mAM)
Caranam 7rAj(A)dhipa tyAgarAja vinuta bAga (mAM)
