  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviparama pAvani mAmava parvatarAja putrikE ambE
Anupallavisuravara kinnara sannutE shObhana guNajAtE lalitE kara dhrta
pAshAnkusha suma vishikEkSu cApE kAncipura vAsini shrI kAmAkSi
CaranamcaraNa vinata sura gaNapati su-manOgaNE kara sarasija dhrta maNi vINE candra vadanE
paramEshvari sEvaka jana rakSakE sadA praNata phaladE sadA praNata phaladAyikE
bhaNDana khaNDana bhaNDa mahiSa mukha caNDa daitya maNDalE ripudaNDE
