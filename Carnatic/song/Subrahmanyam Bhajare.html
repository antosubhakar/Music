  Lyrics - English:

PallavisubrahmaNyam bhajarE rE mAnasa suj~nAna rUpam suravaradam
AnupallavisubrahmaNyam surAgragaNyam cUrasamhArAdi nipuNam
CaranammArakOTi prakASa gAtam mAradhara priyA susArdhakam
sAradhara sagIta dAyakam varada varadadAsAdi pOshakam
