  Lyrics - English:

PallavimAmavatu SrI sarasvati kAmakOTi pITha nivAsini
AnupallavikOmalakara sarOja vara vINA sImAtIta vara vAgbhUshaNa
CaranamrAjAdhirAja pUjita caraNa rAjIva nayana ramaNIya vadana
Madhyamakala sahityamsujana manOratha pUraNa caturA nijagaLa SObhita maNimaya hArA
aja bhava vandita vAsudEva caraNArpita sakala vEdasArA
