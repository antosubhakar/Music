  Lyrics - English:

PallavigOpAlam Eva daivatam bhajE sadA gOpAlam Eva daivatam
Caranam 1sarva lOka kAraNam satya kAmAdi guNam
Caranam 2bhaktAnugrahAbhi vyakta bhavya mUrti dhAriNam
Caranam 3yAdavaAbdhi candramiha yati jana mOhApaham
Caranam 4kamsAdi vairi virAma kalita divya vigraham
Caranam 5gOpikAnana kumuda gurutar candrOdayam
Caranam 6ghOra samsAra kAraNa vAraNa kEsariNam
Caranam 7manasApi na gaNayAmi mAdhavAdanya
Caranam 8bhavya nArAyaNa tIrtha divyadrgvidhAyinam
