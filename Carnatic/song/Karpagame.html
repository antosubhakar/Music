PallavikarpakamE kaDaikkaN pArAi (tiru mayilai)
Anupallavishirparai yOgiyar sidhhargaL jnaAniyar
Madhyamakala sahityamtiruvudai adiyavar karudum varam
udavum tirumagaLum kalaimagaLum paravu tirumayilaik-
Caranamsattu cidhAnandam adAi sakala uyirkkuyirai avaL nI
tattuvamasyAdi mahA vAkkiya tatpara vastuvum nI
sattuva guNamoDu bhakti sheibavar bhava tApamum pApamum ara immaiyil vara
santAna saubhAgya sampattoDu marumaiyil niradhishaya inbamum tarum
