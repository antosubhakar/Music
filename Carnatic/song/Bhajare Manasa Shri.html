  Lyrics - English:

pallavibhajarE mAnasa SrI praNatArtiharam sadA
anupallaviaja biDaujAdi vanditam Adi madhyAmta rahitam rajata bhUtAsthitam ravikOTi sankASam tam
caraNamcarAcarAtmakam virAja rAjita jaTAdharam parAtparam haram cidambarAdhipam digambaram
parASaktivaram bhakta parAdhInam Sankaram murAri SrI venkaTESvarAdhitam ISvaram
nirAmayam nirvikAram niranjanam nirAkAram nirAlambam nirAdhAram nirantar-AnadAkAram
