  Lyrics - English:

PallavihaNNu bandide janaru haNNu koLLiro
AnupallavihaNNu embo harinAma embo Srutiya nimbeya
Caranam 1manada bhUmiyali puTTi matigaLinda ballavisi tamage tamage tiLida hottu bhakutiyindali
olidu goJjalAda haNNu jnAnavembo paJjarada tiLidu nAlikeye mEle bandu kuLita bALeya
Caranam 2ondu varusha ondu balavu bitti banda rAma candranembo kalpavrksha kadaLi santati
kundadihudu sakala balava alli gone gUDi gOvinda rAma vAsudEvanembo halasina
Caranam 3hariya nAmavannu nitya smarisa balla vipraige suraru munigaLella bAguvarellayya
giriya mElippa alamElumaGga arasanAda purandara viTTalanembo jambunEraLe
