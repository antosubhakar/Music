  Lyrics - English:

PallavisArasa daLa nayana saGkaTa haraNa harE gOvinda mAm pAhi
AnupallavinIrajAsanAdi pUjitAGga SrI ramantaraGga SubhAGga murahara abhaya prada mukunda yAdava kula bhUshaNa mAdhava
CaranammAghamAsOtsavaprabhAva mAnitaguruguha vandita bhava gOkula janapAla gOpikA manOhara gOvarddhanadhara
