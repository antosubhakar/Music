  Lyrics - English:

PallavigangAdhara tripurahara shrI sArangadhara
AnupallavishrngAra shekhara shiva shankara jaTAdhara
bhrngi naTanA vinoda bhrngAraka vandita pAda
Caranam 1sadAshiva vinu tAnanda sambhramachita asura madahara
muni pOSaNa mahAdEva shambhO hara
Caranam 2ahi bhUshana amarAvana aja sannuta badarIvana
duhi nAdi tanaya priya dukha dUra kAlakAla
