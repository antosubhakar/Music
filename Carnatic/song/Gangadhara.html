  Lyrics - English:

pallavigangAdhara tripurahara SrI sArangadhara
anupallaviSrngAra Sekhara Siva Sankara jaTAdhara
bhrngi naTanA vinoda bhrngAraka vandita pAda
caraNam 1sadASiva vinu tAnanda sambhramachita asura madahara
muni pOshaNa mahAdEva SambhO hara
caraNam 2ahi bhUSana amarAvana aja sannuta badarIvana
duhi nAdi tanaya priya dukha dUra kAlakAla
