  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSrI kAmAkshi varadAyaki Srtajana paripAlini
anupallaviOmkAra svarUpiNi kalyANi janani uraga SayanAvana japava madana ripu vinutE bhavataranE natavaraNE Subha caraNE
caraNamsAmagAna lOlE padAbja SIlE SaSAnka vadanE sarasIruha sadanE
kAma rahitE sujanahitE mahitE karadhrta vara Subha kamalE
amaranutE vimalE karuNArasa varabhUshaNI mrdu bhAshiNI
