SaGkarAbharaNa vENi
(nitya)

(nitya)

(nitya)
Caranam 4dikkevaru nI vEgadA dInadayApari amba makkuvatO brOcE SrI maNgaLa nAyaki
(nitya)

madhurApura vAsini mahESvari mInAkshi
