  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisarasvati annaiyE ennNEramum sharaN adaindEn unnaiyE dEvi
Anupallavinara stuti sheiden-nAvaip-pAzh sheidEn nAnmukhan nAvil vITrirukkum dEvi
