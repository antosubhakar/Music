PallavilAli pAvana caraNA lAli aghaharaNA lAli vEGkaTaramaNA lalita kalyANa
Caranam 1vanajAksha mAdhava vAsudEva tanayA sanakAdi muni vandya sAdhujana priyA
inakOTi Sata tEja munikalpa kUja kanakAdri nilaya vEGkaTarAyA
Caranam 2naga mrgakari hiraNyatana vairi karirAja rakshaka kAruNya mUrtI
hari Adi kESava guru apramEya siridhara SEshagiri vara simharAyA
