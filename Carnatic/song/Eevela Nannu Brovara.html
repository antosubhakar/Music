Caranam 1ati viharamucE nI sannidhi kEgitira gati nAku nIvE gadara annayya krSNa
Caranam 2cinna prAyamu nADE kOriyunnAnu kanna taNDri nIvE kadara annayya krSNa
Caranam 3nannAdarincura nA muddu rangayya kannaDa sEyakura annayya krSNa
Caranam 4enni vidhambula sannutincina nAvinnapamu vinarAdA annayya krSNa
Caranam 5pakSamutODa nA kaSTamulanu dIrcci rakSimpa jAlamElarA annayya krSNa
Caranam 6kanakAmbaradhara kAmita phalada nA manavini maravakura annayya krSNa
