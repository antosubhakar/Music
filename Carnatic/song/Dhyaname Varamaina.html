  Lyrics - English:

PallavidhyAnamE varamaina gangA
snAnamE manasA
AnupallavivAna nITa munuga munuga lOni
vancana drOham(a)nu kara pOnA (dhyAna)
Caranampara dhana nArImaNulanu dUri
para nindala para himsala mIri
dharanu velayu SrI rAmuni kOri
tyAgarAju telusukonna rAma (dhyAna)
