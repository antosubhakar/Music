  Lyrics - English:

Pallavishankari sadAnanda lahari pAhi shivAnandakari shrI girijA kumAri
Anupallavisharadindu vadanE su-manOhari shankara guhamOda shrI vidyArUpiNi
shakambari shatOdari shApa vimOcanakari shyAmala sundari
CaranamanAtha rakSaki brhannAyakI anAdi guru sampradAya darshaki
sarasIruhAsana su-manOllasanI sarasija nAbha vakSasthaLa vAsinI
shAstra kiraNAdi suragaNa pAlinI sahasra sUrya prakAshini
aharnisha kaivalya nandadAyini manOnmaNi
