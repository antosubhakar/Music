PallavimaitrIM bhajata akhilahRjjEtrIm
AtmavadEva parAnapi paSyata ।
yuddhaM tyajata spardhAM tyajata
tyajata parEshu akramam AkramaNam ॥
jananI pRthivI kAmadughA''stE
