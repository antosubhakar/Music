  Lyrics - English:

Pallavimanuja kulAbharaNam gAndhI mahAntamiha SaraNam vrajEham
Anupallavianujana sEvita adbhuta caraNam ArtiharaNam abhaya karaNam
Caranam 1nirupama guNa kOSam nigadita nigama sadupadESam
parihrta parajana bandhana pASam pUritASam parijanESam
Caranam 2satyAgrahamUlam sutarAmSa madama paripAlam nityA
Srtahari nAmanikhElam nibhrtakAlam niyama nIlam
Caranam 3sundara Subha hAsam satatam sukrta mukha vilAsam
nandana vAGgmayam nirmala bhAsam nija viSvAsam nikhila dAsam
