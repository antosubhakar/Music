AnupallavibhAvuka prada bhavaOga nivAraNa bhAramA nanu brOva bhvatISa sannuta
Caranam 1daSaratha rAjakumAra daSAvatAra daSa mukhAdi danuja samhAra harE
rasana madhuratara raJjita nAma (ika) rArA rArA tyAgarAjAdinuta pAdA
Caranam 2kapaTa nATaka sUtradhArI nIvE gadA kApAduTakidi maJci samayamu
aparimita vaibhavamu bondina nIku Adi varadadAsunipai vAda dayalEda
