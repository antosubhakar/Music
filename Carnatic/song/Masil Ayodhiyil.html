  Lyrics - English:

PallavimAshil ayOddhiyil mannan dasharathan mA tavattAL vanda aiyanE tAlO
kOshikanai vadaitta koDiyavanai azhittu yAgam tanai kAtta dhIranE tAlO
(rAgA: bauLi)
Caranam 2pAdaiyilE oru kalluruvAi ninra pAvaikkaruL sheida rAmanE tAlO
sItai manamagizha shOdanaiyil venra shrIdharanE raghurAmane tAlO
(rAgA : bilahari)
Caranam 3tandai shol kAkkavE tambiyun dAramum toDara vanam shenra rAmane tAlO sundariyai pirindu andippaghalAi alaindu cintai taLarndiTTa aiyane tAlO
(rAgA : kEdAragaula)
Caranam 4vAnara paDaiyuDAn sEtu lankEshanai venravanE jayarAmane tAlO
anbargal AshaiyuDAn arashu muDi taritta Ananda rAmane aruL puri tAlO
(rAgam : SaNmukhapriyA)
