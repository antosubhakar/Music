  Lyrics - English:

PallavinIrajadaLA nayana harE krshNA
Caranam 1nIrajAsanAdi sannuta dvAraka nilaya suguNa bharaNA harE krshNA
Caranam 2gOpIjana jhAla gOvardanoddhAra kanakAmbaradhArA harE krshNA
Caranam 3mAra janaka hari mAdhava sahurE madhusUdana harE harE krshNA
