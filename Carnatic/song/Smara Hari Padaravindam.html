AnupallavitarasA jahi mamatAm dArasutAdiSu guru sajjana sangamamuru puNya samavApyam
Caranam 1yadiha labhasE pada vittam tEna mudamavApnuhi bhAgyAyattam
adhayam samsEvya dhamadirAmattam hanta mA khEdaya cittam ahOmudA
Caranam 2hari mandirE vasa nityam kAmamayi mA mA vada padAsatyam
paramiha bhaja sadA mAdhava bhrtyam pAvanatara nija krtyam ahOmudA
Caranam 3parama puruSam anuvAram bhaja padmanAbham amalamudAram
karuNA pUrNa nayana m asita shubha sharIram kalita kaustubha navahAram ahOmud
