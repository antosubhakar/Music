  Lyrics - English:

pallaviHari hara ninnanu meccisabahudu narara olisuvadu balu kaTina
anupallavisharibandhange mAtanaduva janaru dhareyolagiruvadu balu kaTina.
caraNam 1satiya sangada sukhadindiddare salige kotte tori embuvaru, … nAriuya sanghava bittare napunsaka nI nennuvaru.
caraNam 2nagutaliddare ghatakanemburvaru maunavagiddare mUkanennuvaru, kadehayisu Sri purandaravithalane kaTiNa …nindeyanu
