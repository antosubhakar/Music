  Lyrics - English:

Pallavibhaja bhagavantam ramaNam kAntam paramAtmAnam mAnasa nityam
(rAgA: dEs)
Caranam 1anudita mamatA antam shAntam abhidhita bhEda mahAgama rUpam
mauna vyAkhyA kamudita darpam paramam dattam satata vimuktam
(rAgA: basant)
Caranam 2satyam jagadabhi dEgama pItvam matvA mahA mOhA vEdyam
vishvA jIvA jagatikEtE mutti vikalpA svahi paridEtyE
(rAgA: sindhubhairavi)
Caranam 3dEvOnatvam jIvOnatvam pashya krtantaha kOsti vigjnEham
IshO purarabhi jatmA ramaNam datyAm mUrtim satkaruNaina
