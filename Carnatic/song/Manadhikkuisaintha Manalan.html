PallavimanadiRkisainda maNALan sentil
mAnagar vAzh vaDivElan vaLLi (manadiR)
Anupallavianutinam tuti ceyyum anparkkanpan
iniya tamizh mozhiyil ilaGkiTum inpan vaLLi (manatiR)
