  Lyrics - English:

PallavianAtharakshaka guha araSE nI varuga migha anAthan Erkkumuruga arul peruga
AnupallavimanAtIta mati vadanA vArija daLa nayanA jnAnamuda vacanA enAnavilum
CaranamnarALi veruviya AyirALi mukhan uyir un carALiyAm SaravaNabhava SarabhamE
svara jAlavarALi maruvum kuzhal vaLLi maNavALA guruparA subhakara kavi kuJjaradAsan enaiyALa
