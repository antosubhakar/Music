  Lyrics - English:

PallaviniRaimathi mukamenu moLiyAlE
neRivizhi kaNaiyenu nikarAlE
uRavukoL madavArka LuRavAmO
unathiru vadiyinai yaruLvAyE
maRaipayi larithiru marukOnE
