  Lyrics - English:

Pallavijagat janani sukapANi kalyANi (jagat)
Anupallavisuka svarUpiNi matura vANi
cokkanAtar manam makizhum mInAkshi (jakat)
CaranampANTiya kumAri pavAni ampA civa
paJcami paramEshvari
vENTum varam tara innum manamillaiyO
vEta vEtAnta nAta svarUpiNi (jakat)
