  Lyrics - English:

Pallavidarishana mAyEnu shrI rAmula vAri
Caranam 1darishana mAyEnu dhanyuDanaitini yuramunandu siri merayucunna vAri
Caranam 2vinhu shambhunaku yOgi prabhulaku nainanu abhimukhuDai Alasyamu cUpani vAni
Caranam 3shukamunulaku yOgi prabhulaku daivama abhimukhuDai yAnanamu jUpanivAni
Caranam 4kaNDa krovvuna tanu maracuvAni tala ceNDedanani kOdaNDa mettina vAni
Caranam 5parama bhaktula kila sirulosangedanani karamuna dAna kankaNamu gaTTina vAni
Caranam 6sthiramhga bhadragiri rAmadAsuni arasi brOcedanani birudu gAncina vAni
