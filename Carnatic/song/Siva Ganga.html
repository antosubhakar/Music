  Lyrics - English:

Pallavishivagangaa nagara nivaasini - shreeraajaraajEshwai maamava
Caranam 1abhayavaradE amba maayE niga
maagaNita vibhavE paramashiva jaayE
Caranam 2vadanaruci vijita kamalE - ubhaya
paarshwaviraajita vaaNee kamalE
Caranam 3sadaa raamadaasanutE sharaNaagata
jana baalana saNashupacaritE
