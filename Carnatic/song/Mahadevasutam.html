  Lyrics - English:

pallavimahAdEva sutam madagaja mukham manasA smarAmi aniSam
anupallavimahA vEda vEdyam mangaLakaram maha gaNapatim mAnita dEvam
caraNamrakta varNam praNavAkaram sukta kartikEya sOdaram
bhakta mandAram bharaNi vinutam Sakti hastam mukti dAyakam
