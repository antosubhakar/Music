AnupallavivAhin(I)Sa taraNa daSa vadana sUnu tanu haraNa (pAhi)
Caranam 1ghOr(A)sura vArAn-nidhi kumbha tanaya kRta kArya
pArijAta taru nivAsa pavana tulya vEga (pAhi)
Caranam 2pAda vijita dushTa graha patita lOka pAvana
vEda SAstra nipuNa varya vimala citta satataM mAM (pAhi)
Caranam 3taruN(A)ruNa vadan(A)bja tapana kOTi sankASa
kara dhRta raghuvara su-caraNa kali mal(A)bhra gandha vAha (pAhi)
Caranam 4karuNA rasa paripUrNa kAncan(A)dri sama dEha
parama bhAgavata varENya varada tyAgarAja vinuta (pAhi)
