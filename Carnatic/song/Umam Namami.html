PallaviumAm namAmi haimavatIm bhagavatIm upaniSad prati pAdAdita mahA SaktIm
AnupallaviramA vANyAdi rUpiNIm nArAyaNIm himAmSu vatALi hrt-tOshiNIm phaNi vENIm SiSya pratishTha prakASita nija tatvAm Silpa SAstra viSAradAm Siva dayitAm
CaranamatiSaya mitrAm atiSaya sucaritrAm parAtparAm paGkaja mitrAm bhU kaLatrAm
SirasAlaGkrta candra kalAdhara nIlAm dharaNi suragEyAm himagiri vara bhArAm
para brahmaya prakASa chAyAm paramAdvaita tatva priyAm prakaTita SrI vidyAm
