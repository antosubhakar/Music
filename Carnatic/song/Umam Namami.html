  Lyrics - English:

PallaviumAm namAmi haimavatIm bhagavatIm upanishad prati pAdAdita mahA shaktIm
AnupallaviramA vANyAdi rUpiNIm nArAyaNIm himAmshu vatALi hrt-tOSiNIm phaNi vENIm shishya pratiSTha prakAshita nija tatvAm shilpa shAstra vishAradAm shiva dayitAm
Caranamatishaya mitrAm atishaya sucaritrAm parAtparAm pankaja mitrAm bhU kaLatrAm
shirasAlankrta candra kalAdhara nIlAm dharaNi suragEyAm himagiri vara bhArAm
para brahmaya prakAsha chAyAm paramAdvaita tatva priyAm prakaTita shrI vidyAm
