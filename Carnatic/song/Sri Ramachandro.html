  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI rAmacandrO rakshatu mAm rAkshasAdiharO raghuvarah
AnupallavibharatAgrajah kauSika yAgarakshakah tATakAntakah
CaranammithilA nagara pravESa mahESvara dhanurbhEdakah sItA kalyANa mahOtsava vaibhava yuta citravEshakO mAdhurya gAnAmRta pAna priya guruguha viSvAsO mahAdEvIbhakta paraSurAma garvaharOllAsah
