  Lyrics - English:

PallaviiniyAkilum ninai manamE
inakula tilakanai rAghavanai (iniyAkilum)II
Anupallavipunitha thyAga rAja swamy
kanavil kAkshi tanda gaNNyanai (iniyAkilum) II
