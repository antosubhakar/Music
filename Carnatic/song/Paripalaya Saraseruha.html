  Lyrics - English:

PallaviparipAlaya sarasIruha nayana
AnupallavicaraNam tava kalayE hrdi smara mOhana
Caranam 1dIna jana nikara dEvatara ghana vilasita nijadEha jalajanAbha
mAnitaguNa ripu sancaya mada khaNDana paTubhandana
Caranam 2nanda kavi lasitabahA vibhO pada talanata shuka nArada
mukha ashurEsundara kuruvinda radana suranAyaka sukha dAyaka
Caranam 3bOdhakara bhuvananAtha harE phaNi varashaya su-kapOla rucirahAsa
shrIdhara karuNa nivasana shrta pOSana mrdu bhASaNa
