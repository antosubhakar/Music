  Lyrics - English:

Pallavimarali marali jaya maMgaLamu | soridi niccallunu shuBa maMgaLamu ||
Caranam 1kamalA ramaNiki kamalAkshhunakunu | mamatala jaya jaya maMgaLamu
amara jananikini amaravaMdyunaku | sumuhUrtamutO shuBa maMgaLamu
Caranam 2jaladhikanyakunu jaladhishAyikini | malayucunu shuBa maMgaLamu |
kalimikAMtakA kaliki viBuniki | suLuvulayArati shuBa maMgaLamu ||
Caranam 3cittaju talliki shrI vEMkaTapatiki | mattillina jaya maMgaLamu |
ittala nattala iruvurakaugiTa | jottula ratulaku shuBa maMgaLamu ||
