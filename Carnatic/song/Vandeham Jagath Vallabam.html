  Lyrics - English:

PallavivandEham jagadvallabham durlabham
Anupallavimandaradharam gurum mAdhavam bhUdharam
CaranamrAma nAmam yagya rakshaNam lakshaNam vAmanam kAminam vAsudEvam
srImadAvAsinam sri vEnkatEswaram syAmalam kOmalam sAntha mUrthim harim
