  Lyrics - English:

PallavivAdamEla rAdhA manOhara
sadAnanda hrudaya dayAmaya
AnupallaviAdariJchu shridhara dAmOdara
brundAvana vihAri shauri
CaranamnIlamEgha varNa suvarNa maNi
mAla bhUSaNa samara bhISaNa
lIlA bhASaNa shESashayana sadA
muraLI gAna sudhA rasAsvAda
