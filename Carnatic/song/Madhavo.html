  Lyrics - English:

PallavimAdhavO mAm pAtu matsyAvatArO vEdastEya dushtaharO vEdAdi rakshaNah SrI (Nata)
Caranam 1gOvidam namAmyaham guruguhanutakUrmAvatAram dEvarAjAdi pUjitam divyAmRtapradam (gauLa)
Caranam 2SrIdharENa rakshitOham bhUmipAla sUkarENa hita dEvOpakArENa hayAsurahati nipuNEna (SrI)
Caranam 3narasihmAya namastE prahlAda prArthitAya hiraNya prANaharAya harihayAdi vanditAya (Arabhi)
Caranam 4vAmanAdanyam na jAnEham naravara rUpiNah Sukra balirAjAdi SrutijJAna prabOdhinO (varALi)
Caranam 5paraSurAmasya dAsOham sItApatim SaraNAgatasya kshatriya kula bhIkarasya jamadagni Rshi putrasya (kEdAram)
Caranam 6rAmacandra svAmini bhaktim karOmi daSaratha sukumArAtmani daSavadana bhaJjanAtmani (vasanta)
Caranam 7balarAma mAm kalaya bhOgISAdi sOdara muraLIgAnAnanda mukundAdi sadA lOla (suraTi)
Caranam 8SrI kRshNam bhajarE citta SrI rukmiNIpatim SiSupAla kamsAdiharam pANDavAdi rakshitavaram (saurAshTram)
Caranam 9kaliyuga varavEGkaTESam khala SamUha hata surESam alamElu maGgESam acyutAdim bhajEhamaniSam (madhyamAvati)
