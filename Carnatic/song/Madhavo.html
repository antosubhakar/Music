PallavimAdhavO mAm pAtu matsyAvatArO vEdastEya duStaharO vEdAdi rakSaNah shrI (Nata)
Caranam 1gOvidam namAmyaham guruguhanutakUrmAvatAram dEvarAjAdi pUjitam divyAmRtapradam (gauLa)
Caranam 2shrIdharENa rakSitOham bhUmipAla sUkarENa hita dEvOpakArENa hayAsurahati nipuNEna (shrI)
Caranam 3narasihmAya namastE prahlAda prArthitAya hiraNya prANaharAya harihayAdi vanditAya (Arabhi)
Caranam 4vAmanAdanyam na jAnEham naravara rUpiNah shukra balirAjAdi shrutijHnAna prabOdhinO (varALi)
Caranam 5parashurAmasya dAsOham sItApatim sharaNAgatasya kSatriya kula bhIkarasya jamadagni RSi putrasya (kEdAram)
Caranam 6rAmacandra svAmini bhaktim karOmi dasharatha sukumArAtmani dashavadana bhaHnjanAtmani (vasanta)
Caranam 7balarAma mAm kalaya bhOgIshAdi sOdara muraLIgAnAnanda mukundAdi sadA lOla (suraTi)
Caranam 8shrI kRSNam bhajarE citta shrI rukmiNIpatim shishupAla kamsAdiharam pANDavAdi rakSitavaram (saurASTram)
Caranam 9kaliyuga varavEHNkaTEsham khala shamUha hata surEsham alamElu maHNgEsham acyutAdim bhajEhamanisham (madhyamAvati)
