  Lyrics - English:

PallavimanOnmaNi mandahAsini maJjulavANi mAtE salahau
AnupallavisanakAdi mauni vinutapAdE vanajAyatAkshi vandisuvE tAyE
Caranamtanudhana manamadava kAdi nIDi manadalli ninna manu nenevara
anuvimboreva janani surEndra vinutE harikESa niratE ambA
