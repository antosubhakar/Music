  Lyrics - English:

Pallavivaram idonrE pOdumE vAzhvu kaDaitEra vazhiyAgumE
Anupallavitiruvukkum tiruvAna selvA shrImAn nArasNanE paripUraNanE
Caranamurugi urugi unnai nanikka vENDum uLLattil nilaittuum oLira vENDum
perugum kaNNIrAl kaDal kazhuva vENDum n-irukka vENDum
Madhyamakala sahityamaDiyavar tam uravu koLLa vENdum anavaratamum turai pADa vENDum
arumarai murai paDi naDakka vENDum ayyan un padamalar aDaiya vENDum
