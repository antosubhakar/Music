PallaviSrI kAmakOTi pIThasthitE karuNA kaTAkshi EkAmrapati hrdaya nilayE namAmi SrI kAmAkshi
AnupallavinAgAdISa vinutE himagirisutE SrI kAJcIpurAdhISvari varada sOdari SaGkari
Caranam 1kadambavana nilayE kaJja lOcanE bhavAni madamba vadana
vijita SaSibimbE vara suguNa kadamba lalitAmba
Caranam 2bhaNDa mahisha dAnava mada bhaJjanE niraJjanE bhakta mAnasa-
maNDanAya mAnasa svAntE mauni mAnasAmbhOja jalaja hamsE
Caranam 3sadASiva saGkaTa SamanE surucira saikata SrONi
sadA sammOdini natajanAvani lOka janani nityakalyANi
