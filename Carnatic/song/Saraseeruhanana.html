  Lyrics - English:

PallavisarasIruh(A)nana rAma
samayamu brOva cid-ghana
Anupallavipara bhAmalan(A)Sinci(y)annam(i)Di
pagalu rEyu sarasamADu vArin(o)lla (sara)
CaranambrAhmaNikamu bAya nIcula
bratuk(A)yen(a)di gAka(y)I kalilO
brahmamaina mATala nErcukoni
baragEr(a)yya tyAgarAja nuta (sara)
