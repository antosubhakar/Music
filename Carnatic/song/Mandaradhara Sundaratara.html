  Lyrics - English:

Pallavimandaradhara sundaratara vasudhA bhara nAshana jagadIshvara jaya jaya mArajanaka sArasanAbha vibhO
Anupallavikunda kusuma vAsita cigura mukunda vimala guNa gaNa nivasana
Caranam 1sura ripujana samudaya bhISaNa sujana sharaNa madhu mrdu bhASaNa
karaNa vijita ghana vara bhUSaNa garuDa gamana karivara pOSaNa
caraNa vinata bhaya hrdipara vara karuNArasa varuNAlaya para
Caranam 2nava sarasija sama ruci lOcana namana nirata durita vimOcana
bhava vidhinuta vibhava virOcana bhava madahara suma hita yAcana
vividha vimala nija su-carita vidhu vadanAhatakada nAmrtakara
Caranam 3mama kuru shubhamayi sukhadAyaka madhura muramukha phaNI khaga nAyaka
shamita vimata bhujabala sAyaka sakala bhuvana kushala vidhAyaka
kamala satrusha rucipada yuga mama satatam kuru vitatam sukhamayi
