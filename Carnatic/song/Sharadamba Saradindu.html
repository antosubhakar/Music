  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSAradAmbA Saradindu nibhAnana bhAsita nikhila digantE
pAradE bhavamahAjalarASEH pAvaya mAM vidhikAntE
Caranam 1dantakAnti jita kundasumE vara kuntala nirdhUta bhRGgE
SAnta citta jana santata cintita kOTi candra sadRSAGgE
Caranam 2pAda namra jana vAJchita pUraNa nirjita nandana vallikE
mAdanE
Svasana garva nibarhaNa dakshamanOhara cillikE
Caranam 3RshyaSRGgapura vAsa vilOlE vaSya yantra sadRSAsyE
paSyadaGghri Suka dEvahUti suta kaSyapAdi samupAsyE
