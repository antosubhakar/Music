  Lyrics - English:

pallaviedo theriyamal pochudhe,
ini yenna cheyven,
anupallaviAdi parapara magiya thillai ponnambalavarai,
vIdhi thorum panindu miga magizhnudu nalam pera,
caraNamiravum pagalum paravita idar cheyyum iymporiyal,
aravin vAy chiru therai pol antho manam nondhen,
parivudan nopalakriSnan padi vanangum Kunjitha,
thiruvadiyai dariSanam cheydhu telindu manam urugida
