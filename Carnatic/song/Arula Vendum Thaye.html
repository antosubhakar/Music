  Lyrics - English:

PallaviaruLa vENDum tAyE aGgayaR kaNNi nIyE
enakkaruLa vENDum tAyE... (aruLa)
AnupallaviporuLum pugazhum porundi vAzha
puviyil nAn talai siRandu vAzha (aruLa)
CaranamkalaigaL kaRkavum kaRpanai seyyavum kAlam
kaDavAmal karuttait tiruttavum
ulagilE nalla uNmaigaL pEsavum
unai ninaikkavum uRudiyAy vAzhavum (aruLa)
