  Lyrics - English:

PallavilalitE mAm pAhi dayA kalitE shrtaa kalpalatE lAlita sakala kalA vasatE
Anupallavikula shaila sutE kAmakOTi pITha madhyagatE jaladAyita dAnaratE capalAyita kAyalatE
Caranamghana shObhana guNajAlE karadhrta brahma kapAlE gamana vijita mata marALE nava kambugaLE
munijana mAnasa lOlE mOhAmbuda vartUlE kanakamaya cElE cengalvarAyanuta bAlE
