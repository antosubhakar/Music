  Lyrics - English:

pallaviramA ramaNa nArAyana namOstutE dayAnidhE
anupallavisamAna rahitAdbhuta carita samasta janaka samIra jApta
caraNamvAraNArthi haraNa suva caraNa varijasra sakala mrdu carana
