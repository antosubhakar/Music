anupallaviIshu sakala jagadIshug-jnAnAmbikE su-kALahastIshu nAmamulE
caraNam 1hara hara purahara shankara gangAdhara shashi shEkhara sAmba sadAshiva
kari carmAmbaradhara paramEshvara ciratara mUrtayagu shivanAmamulE
caraNam 2phAla vilOcana pArvatI ramaNA kAlakAla pankEruha caraNa tri-
