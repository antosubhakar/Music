  Lyrics - English:

PallavituNai purindaruL taruNa mAdavA
surargaL nAyakA ulaga nAyakA
AnupallavimaNikkoL mArpanE kamala nApanE
varata nin patam caraNam caraNam
Caranamviti paNintiTum sruti pukazhntiTum
vimalapAta capA puvana mOhanA
patIta pAvanA karuTa vAhanA
parama purusha nArAyaNanE
