Pallavinagavulu nijamani nammEdA voginaDiyAsalu voddanavE
Caranam 1tolliTi karmamu dontala nuNDaga cellabOyika jEsEdA
yella lOkamulu yElETi dEvuDa volla nollanika noddanavE
Caranam 2pOyina janmamu porugulanuNDaga cIyanaka yindu jelagEdA
vEyinAmamula vennuDamAyalu O yayya yinka noddanavE
Caranam 3nali nInAmamu nAlikanuNDaga talakoni yitaramu daDavEdA
balu SrI vEnkaTapati ninnugolici voluku cancalamu loddanavE
