caraNam 1vAridhishayana vAmana shrIdhara | nArasiMha kRushhNa namO namO |
nIrajanABa nigamagOcara | nArAyaNa hari namO namO ||
caraNam 2parama purushha Bava vimOcana- | varada vasudha vadhUvara |
karuNA kAMtA kALiMdI ramaNa | narasaKa shauri namO namO ||
