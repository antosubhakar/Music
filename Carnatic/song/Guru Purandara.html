Pallaviguru purandara dAsarE nimma caraNa kamalava nambidE
Anupallavigarvara hitana mADiyennanu poreva bhAravu nimmadE
Caranam 1ondu ariyada manda mati nAnu indu nimmanu vandipE
indirEshana pAda tOrisO tande mADalu sat-krpE
Caranam 2mAra janakana sannidhAnadi sAragAnava mADuva
nAradare I rUpadindali cAru darushana tOrida
Caranam 3purandarAlaya ghaTTadoLu nI niruta dhanavA ghaLisalu
parama puruSanu vipranandadi karava nIDi yAcisE
Caranam 4parama nirguNa vanavanaritu harige sUreya nIDuta
aritu manadOLu haridu bhavagaLa taruNi sahitA horaTaNe
Caranam 5aja bhavAdigaLa arasanAda vijayaviTTalana dhyAnipa
nija su-jnAnava koDisa bEkendu bhajipenO kELu guruvarA
