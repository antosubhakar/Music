PallavikAkkavenRO kAlai tUkki ninRIrayyA kanakasabhA nAtharE emmai
AnupallaviAkkavum kAkkavum azhikka vallavar nIrE Ekkam tavarttu vinai tAkki dayavuDanE
Caranam 1pArvati dEviyum pakkattil ninRiDa pArttu magizhndu dEvar padineTT-isaiyum pADa
ArvamuDan nandi tALa maddaLam pODa Urdhva tANDavamADi ulagamellAm
Caranam 2pAmbu puliyum pOTRi pAril ponnambalattE pAdam tUkki ADiya paramAnanda naTanE
vEm paNiyum mAran vENDiyapaDi iGgu vEdam tEDiyum kANA pAdam mAriyADiyE
