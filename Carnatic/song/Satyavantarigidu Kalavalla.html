  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSatyavantarigidu kAlavalla
Dushta Janarige subhiksha kAla
Caranam 1Harismarane mAduvage kshyavAguva kAla
Parama pApigaLige subhiksha kAla
stiravAda pativrateya pararu nindipa kAla
dhareyariye jAreyanu kondAduva kAla
Caranam 2upakAra mAdidare apakarisuva kAla
sakalavanu tiLidavage durbhiksha kAla
satisutaru embavara nambalariyada kAla
sateyallavidu bahu viparIta kAla
Caranam 3dharma mAduvava nirmUlavAguva kAla
karmi pAtakarige bahu saukya kAla
nirmalatmaka siri purandaravitalana
marmadoLu bhajisalariyada kAlavayya
