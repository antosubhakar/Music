  Lyrics - English:

pallavisatyavantarigidu kAlavalla
anupallavidushTa janarige subhiksha kAla
caraNam 1hari smaraNe mADuvage kshayavAguva kAla parama pApigaLige subhiksha kAla
sthiravAda pativrateya pararu nindipa kAla dharage jAreyaLa koNDADuva kAla
caraNam 2upakAra mADidare apakarisuva kAla sakalavu tiLidavage durbhiksha kAla
patisutaru embavaru nambalariyada kAla saDeyallavide viparIta kAla
caraNam 3dharmava mADuvage nirmUlavAguva kAla karmi pAtakarige bahu saukhya kAla
nirmalAtmaka siri purandara viTTalana marmadoLu bhajisalariyada kAlavayya
