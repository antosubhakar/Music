  Lyrics - English:

pallavipAhi tarakshu purAlaya santatam pAvana sucarita mAm
anupallavidEhi mE tAvakasEvAm mahanIya guNavAsa
caraNam 1pAlita sura nikara dIna bandhO pAdanata SubhakaraNa
kAlakAla sadA pAhi dEvadEva kanja bhavAdi sEvya caraNa
caraNam 2bAla taTa rAjita nEtra kAmAntaka parijana kAma dAna nipuNa
nIlakaNTha pAlaya mAm nikhila bhuvana SaraNa
caraNam 3pAdanata muni mAnasa samOhanIya bhAsuratara mrduhAsa
vEdAntakAgama vEdyAkhila nirjara vandita pada sArasa
caraNam 4mandara giridhara dEvESa parisala mama hrda vigata tOsha
kundatyuti vilasi taradana vyOmakESa
caraNam 5vAraya pApa nikaram dAsa bhavAdi pATana nirupama lOla
bhUri krpAvAsApAnga gaurIkAnta bAlacandra rucira phAla
caraNam 6pura jaladhara pavana vrshabhayAna parijana vibhuta sAla
sArasanAbha caraNa sEvaka sadAnukUla
