  Lyrics - English:

pallavivINA gAna viSAradE! vara (vINA)
nAnAvidha rasa bhava pradarSita
(vINA)
anupallaviAnandamaya sunAdapriyE! amba
tAnam takita taka layagatylo velayu
(vINA)
caraNamkavi hrudayamanDu udamagu sindhu bhairavi rAga vinOdini!
vivida gatula vinyasamutO I
bhuvilO nI dhari cintincina vara
madhyama kAlam
kovidula celimi kOrina nAdu vinnapammu vinavE talli!
(vINA)
