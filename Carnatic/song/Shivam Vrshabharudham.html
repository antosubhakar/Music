  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSivam vrshabhArUDham SrIkaram vandE
Anupallavibhava pASamOcanam bhavam daralOcanam Sivam
CaranamsAma vEda priyam saGgIta sAram kAmita phalapradam
kAJcana vigraham hEmagiri vAsinam ishham harikESam
