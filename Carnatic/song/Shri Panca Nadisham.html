  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSrI panca nadISam cintayAmyaham aniSam
anupallavitApatrayanala varuNam dArukA vana viharaNam pApa duhina ravikiraNam parAtparam bhavataraNam
caraNamsarvAbhIshTa pradam pancanada kshEtrAdhAram sarvESvaram SrI dharmasamvardhanI manOharam
sarva mangaLakaram jagatgurum mahESvaram sarvArishTAdi vimOcanakaram jalpESvaram
sarva jIvadayAkaram caturdaSa bhuvanESvaram sarvOpaniSad sAram sannuta venkaTESvaram
