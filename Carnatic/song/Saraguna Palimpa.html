  Lyrics - English:

pallavisaraguna pAlimpa samayamurA nIku sari evarunnArurA sarasija nEtrA
anupallavivaraguNa SEshAdri varada venkatESvara vanaja sambhavanuta varamulosagi nannu
caraNamSatadhruti pUjita gajarAjuni moraliDagA anughrahinca lEdA
ati vedajendina parama bhaktuDagu prahlAduni munu nI brOvalEdA
manmata janaka maharshamunaku nIkEttana mahanIya SrInivAsa ninnu
satkathalanuvini ninu vEDitini manasu karagi nIvu nAdu sankaTamula dIrcci
