  Lyrics - English:

PallavidEvi SrI tuLas(a)mma brOcuTak-
(i)dE samayam(a)mma mA(y)amma
AnupallavipAvanI brahm(E)ndr(A)dulu nI
bhakticE vilasillir(a)Ta mahA(dEvi)
CaranamnIvu lEka Sankaru mA ramaNuDu nIraj(A)dula(n)ollar(a)Ta
nIvu lEka tIrthamu sEvincuTa nIr(a)nucu pEr(A)yen(a)Ta
nIvu lEka trAsuna SrI hari sari nilvaka pOyen(a)Ta
nIvu lEka vanamAla(y)ani palikirA
nI sari(y)evvarE tyAgarAja nuta mahA(dEvi)
