  Lyrics - English:

PallavinarmadA kAvErI tIranilayE maNivalayE kalayE
Charanamdharmasamvarddhani dhanini danuja sammarddini janani nirmada guruguha vishvAsini nirmala hRdayAbja nivAsini karmajHnAna yOgarUpiNi dharmAdi puruSArttha dAyini
