  Lyrics - English:

Pallavividulaku mrokkeda sangIta kO(vidulaku)
Anupallavimudamuna Sankara kRta sAma nigama
vidulaku nAd(A)tmaka sapta svara (vidulaku)
CaranamkamalA gaurI vAg-ISvarI vidhi
garuDa dhvaja Siva nAradulu
amar(E)Sa bharata kASyapa caNDISa
AnjanEya guha gaja mukhulu
su-mRkaNDuja kumbhaja tumburu vara
sOmESvara SArnga dEva nandI
pramukhulaku tyAgarAja vandyulaku
brahmAnanda sudh(A)mbudhi marma (vidulaku)
