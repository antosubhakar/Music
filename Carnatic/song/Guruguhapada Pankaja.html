pallaviguruguhapada pankajamati guptamaniSamASrayE niraticaya nijaprakASaka nityasukha phalapradam
nIrajanAbha purandara mArAri vArijasambhava vEditavyam atri SukavasishTha vAmadEvAdi tapOdhana vanditam
