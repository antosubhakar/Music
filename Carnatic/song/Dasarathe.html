  Lyrics - English:

pallavidASarathE dIna dayAnidhE dAnava bhIkara dAmOdara kESava mAmava sItAdhava
kEyUra hAra raghuvIra kOmaLa padAbja kOdaNDarAma SyAmaLa vigraha
