  Lyrics - English:

PallavivElavan tuNai irukka vErenna vENDum vaLLi maNALanai vaNangiDuvOmE
AnupallavimAlavan marugan mayilEru murugan bAlan guNashIlan bhaktarkkanukUlan
CaranammUlapporuL sonna muttukumarayyan jnAlamellAm pugazh jnAna skandan
