  Lyrics - English:

PallaviambA suta gaNapatE SrI
ghambIra vANi priya guNanidhE
(amba)
AnupallavilambOdara SrI lakSmi kara
ambOruha caraNa paGkEruha naLina
(amba)
Caranamsiddhi vinAyaka bhakti pradAyaka mUSika vAhana mOdhakara
(sat) bhAvasthita bhava pASAGkusakara pAvana jana paripAla kripAkara
(amba)
