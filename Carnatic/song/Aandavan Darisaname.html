  Lyrics - English:

PallaviANDavan dariSanamE tyAgESan tANDava dariSanamE
Caranam 1pUNDiDum nAga kuNDalamADa vENDum aDiyAr vinaiyODa tyAgESa dariSanamE
Caranam 2candirak-kalaiyum tazhuvum mAnmazhuvum sundarar nigar anbar kuzhuvum tyAgESan tANDav dariSanamE
Caranam 3maddaLam dArai magizhndiruc-cinnam cittam kaLi kUrum vaNNam tyAgESan tANDava dariSanamE
Caranam 4pApaGgal Oda bhaktargaL kUDa dEvataigaL kaLiyADa tyAgESan tANdava dariSanamE
