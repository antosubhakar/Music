  Lyrics - English:

PallavisahajaguNa rAmchandra dayAnidhE
AnupallavimahanIya raghuvamSa SikhAmaNi guha rAjapriya kOdaNDapANi
Caranamparama bhaktuDaina bharata bhAvameigi pAvanamagutana pAdukala nosagi
varamulanaku abhaya varamiccina sItAvara harikESapura vAsA sukhadA
