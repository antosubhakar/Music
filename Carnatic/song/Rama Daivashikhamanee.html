pallavirAma daivaSikhAmaNi sura rAjamahOjjvala bhUmaNi
tAmarasAksha sudhImaNi bhavya tAraka bhakta cintAmaNi
caraNam 1nADE mimu vEDukoNtigA SaraNAgata birudani viNtigA
vEDukai mimu pogaDa naNTigA nannu digaviDanADa vaddaNDigA
caraNam 2citta sEyaka nEmi lEdugA mundu jEsina gati tappa bOdugA
intaku mikkili rAdugA nE nitarula golicEdi lEdugA
caraNam 3tammuDu nIvokka jaNTanu rAmadAsuni rakshinca kuNTanu
sammati nuncu mAyiNTanu bhadrAcalavAsa nI baNTu baNTanu
