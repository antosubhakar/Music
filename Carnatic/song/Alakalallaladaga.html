  Lyrics - English:

Pallavialakal(a)llalADaga kani(y)-
A rAN-muni(y)eTu pongenO
Anupallaviceluvu mIraganu
mArIcuni madam(a)NacE vELa (alaka)
Caranammuni kanu saiga telisi Siva
dhanuvunu viricE samaya-
muna tyAgarAja
vinutuni mOmuna ranjillu (alaka)
