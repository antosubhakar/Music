  Lyrics - English:

pallavirAmam bhajata manujA! ravikula tilakam SrI
(rAmam)
anupallavikAmam kalikaluSa daLanasaNa
nAmam kala rajanisara nigara
bImam abhinava jaladarasu
syAmam sakala cEtanAtamA
(rAmam)
caraNamdIram janakajA hudaya SrungAram
parikruhIta manujAkAram
ASrita sajjanamandAram
anu vAram vAram
