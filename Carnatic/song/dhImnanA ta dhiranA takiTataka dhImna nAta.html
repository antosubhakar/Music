  Lyrics - English:

pallavidhImnanA ta dhiranA takiTataka dhImna nAta dhiranA dhiranA dhira dhIma nAta dhiranA dhiranA dhira
anupallavidhImna tOmna dhImna tOmna kukundari kiTataka taka dhina tOm taka taka dhina tOm taka dhina tOm taka
caraNamnamkiTataka nomkiTataka takiTa dhimita dhimita takiTa kiTataka jhaNUtaka
dhittaLAngu taka tOm dhittaLAngu taka tOm dhittaLAngu taka
nava nava sundara vilasita SaSimukha kavanAdi lalita kaLA paripUrNa
SravaNAnanda kara muraLI vAdana viSArada nIrada nIla varNa
