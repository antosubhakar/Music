  Lyrics - English:

pallaviambhOjanAbha mAm pAlaya satatam ambhOjAlayAjAnE
anupallavijambhavairi vandita jalaja lOla lOcana kumbha sambhava vinuta kuntIsuta sArathE
caraNam 1bhujanga Sayana hari krshNa bhujanga vairiratha gOpi
bhujanga bhAma sangaranga bhujangavara kAliyadamana
bhujanga lOka vAsinuta bhujanga varada dEva dEva
bhujanga bhUshaNa sannuta lIla bhujanga rUpa sankarshaNa
caraNam 2lOla lOla vanamAla nIla surabhilavAla
lAlasita nija jAla pAlitasura vairi jAla
bAla sUrya sama cEla bAlagOpa dhrtaSaila
pAlita gO gOpAla jAla jita SiSupAla
