  Lyrics - English:

Pallavihim(A)dri sutE pAhi mAM varadE para dEvatE
AnupallavisumEru madhya vAsini SrI kAmAkshi (himAdri)
Caranam 1hEma gAtri pankaja nEtri matang(A)tmajE
sarOja bhava har(I)Sa sura mun(I)ndra nutE (himAdri)
Caranam 2ambuj(A)ri nibha vadanE mauktika maNi
hAra SObhamAna gaLE bhakta kalpa latE (himAdri)
Caranam 3SyAma kRshNa sOdari gauri param(E)Svari
girijE nIla vENi kIra vANi SrI lalitE (himAdri)
