  Lyrics - English:

pallavisadaramava nirupama rAma viracaya Subhamayi vara
anupallavipAda vinata Subhakara SrI padmanAbha su-lalAma virajita
caraNam 1Saradija SaSadhara ruciratara vadana sakala bhuvana bhaya haraNa mrduLa hAsa
karamukha niSicara vidaLana Saradhara kamaladaLa nayana kalusha manajaya
caraNam 2SiSi himakara mada haraNa caNa niTila Satamaka sura parivrDha nuta padayuga
daSa mukha phaNi khagavara guNa nivasana diSa nija padayuga jalaja bhajanamayi
caraNam 3su-lalita maNimaya makuTa parilasita surucira tanu ruci vihasita sumaSura
naLa pavanaja mukha kapi gaNa parivrta vilasa mama hrdi jalanilaya madahara
