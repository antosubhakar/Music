PallavirAmacandra pAhi satatam ravi vamsha dIpa ramaNIya kAlaya
AnupallavisAmajEndragatE bhavasAgara pOtAnata kAmadAna paTutama kAma sundarAnga vibhO
Caranam 1maunijana sEvya caraNa krpArasAbdhE mAruti sannatapAda mAnanIya kIrti
vAsamakhavO pAlAnga kAntE bhAnu himakara nEtra phAla shObhi su-lalAma
Caranam 2kIsha nikara parivrta drtAvanijA kaTAkSa bhAsuradAma nAshitAngri
sEvAlOla nikarAmaya pAsha vAraya mAmaka pApajAtam dinamanu
Caranam 3cAru bAhu shObhi kArmuka sAkEta dhAma jAtarUpa nibha vasana ghOra
raNa taTa bahu dhIra kapi varahara vArirAshi ghana mada vAraNaika paTushara
Caranam 4rAvaNArE kuru kushalam lOkatryApi ranjanIya mrdu hAsa bhAva dukkhESu
krpayA bhavya lIlamAkShipa mAm pAvana shrta shrI padmanAbha rUpadhara
