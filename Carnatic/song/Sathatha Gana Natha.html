pallavishathatha naNanAtha shiddhiyanIva KAryadhali
Mathi Prerisuvalu PArvathi Deviyu
Mukuthi Pathake ManavIva MahA Rudra Deva
caraNam 1Hari Bhakuthi DAyakaLu Bharathi Devi
Yukuthi shhAsthragaLalli Vanaja shambhavanarasi
caraNam 2shathkarmagaLa Nadesi shugnyana Mathi Itthu
nathi PAlisuva Namma PavamAnanu
Chitthadhali Ananda shukhava NIvaLu RamA

shathatha IvaraLu Ninthu EI Kruthiya Nadesuvanu
