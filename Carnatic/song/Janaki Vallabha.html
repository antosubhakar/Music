  Lyrics - English:

PallavijAnakI vallabha namastE jagat paripAlaka namastE
AnupallavibhAnukula tilaka patita pAvana santatam mama hrdayAmbujavAsa
CharanamagaNita guNa dhAman shrI rAmA ahalyA shApa vimOcana bhUman
sugrIvAdi sEvita ghanashyAma sumadhura nAman paripUrNa kAma kumAra sAkEta dhAman
