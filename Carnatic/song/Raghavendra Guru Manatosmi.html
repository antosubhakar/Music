  Lyrics - English:

pallavirAghavEndra guru mAnatOsmi satatam nAgarAja SayanAsyutArccana ratham
anupallavirAga mOhAdi rahitam sucaritamsvAkamapi dAyaka madhva munihitam
caraNamSrI sutIndra guru karAbja samjAtam vAsudEva paramAnugraha bharitam
bhAsura vara tuLasImaNi hArayutam bhUsurAdi vinutam vAtIndranatam
