PallavikailAsapatE mAm pAhi (bhU) kalyANamUrtE sadA namastE
AnupallaviSailAdi rAja kanyApatE kAlAbhra nIlagaLa karuNAnidhE (bhU)
CaranamhariNAnka SEkhara gangAdhara garapurISvara gaja carmAmbara
paripAlita munivara sukumAra para vAsudEva sannuti tatpara nirupama
nija tANDava paritOshita sura muni kinnarAdi bhakta nikara darahAsa
vadanA parama bhakta pArtha varada SambhO Sankara (bhU)
