PallavisArasa mukha sarasijanAbha kuru mudamayi dinamanu
AnupallavivAraNa ghana bhayahara vacanajAla jita sudhA budhAdhihara
Caranam 1amara vara nikara makuTa ruci lasita vimala caraNa parihasita salilaruha
shamala kula jalada pavana mrduLahasa samara nihita rupusamudaya jaya jaya
Caranam 2sarasa ghana sadrsha kamalaja pariNutu vara sari dadhipa shayana janimrti bhayahara
harihaya purahara kamala japa riNutu carita kalikalupa vidaLana jaya jaya
Caranam 3sanaka mukha hrdaya kamala madhupa satata kAlEta dara ratha caraNa varada
kanaka vasana sama bhuvana sharaNa bhava kadana haraNa caNa yaduvara jaya jaya
