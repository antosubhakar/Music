  Lyrics - English:

PallavikripayA pAlaya Saure karuNArasAvAsa
kalushhArttivirAma
AnupallavitapanIyanibhacela tuhinAMSu suvadana
SrIpadmanAbha sarasijalocana
Caranamamaranikara cAru hema mauli rAjita-
tAmarasaghanamadadAraNacaNa pAda
vimalabhaktilolupa samasevakAkhila-
kAmadAnanirata kamanIyatarApAN^ga || ||
kundadyutilasita mandahAsaruci-
nandita nutipara b.rndArakasa~Jcaya
vandArusamudaya mandAra paramAra-
vinda sAyakasama sundarAN^ga bhAsita || ||
kuru me kuSalaM mudA kuruvindanibhadanta
nirupama saMsAra nIradhivara pota
nAradamukha muninikara geyacarita
vAraya mamAkhilapApajAtaM bhagavan || ||
