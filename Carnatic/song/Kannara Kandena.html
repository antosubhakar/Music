  Lyrics - English:

PallavikaNNAre kaNDenacyutana kaJci puNtakOTi karirAja varadana
Caranam 1vara maNi mukuTa mastakana sarva sura sanakAdi vandita pAdayugana
taruNi lakshmI manOharana pItAmbara duDugeyali raJjipa vigrahana
Caranam 2kastUri tilaka nosalana tOra muttina hAra padaka bhUshaNana
ettidA bhaya hastadavana tanna bhaktara stutige hAraisi higguvana
Caranam 3nIla mEgha SyAmala bAla lOla makara kuNDala dharisihana
mUlOkadoLage cennigana umA lOlaja vandya vaikuNTha vallabhana
Caranam 4bhAnu kOTi tEjadavana bhava knana rASige havya vAhanana
dAna varadeya dallaNana muni mAnasa hamsanendenisi kombavana
Caranam 5tuGga caturbhujadavana Subha maGgaLa rEke aGgAlaloppuvana
SrGgAra hAra kandharana dEva gaGgeya pita purandara viTTalana
