pallaviambikE gauru\i kAmAkshi paramAnandavalli viSAlAkshi mInAkshi
anupallavinambinEn nambinEn nambinEnunnaiyE nArAyaNa sOdari dayAkari jagad-
caraNam 1rAjarAja girirAja kumAri SrI jagadISvari Siva sundari jagad-
