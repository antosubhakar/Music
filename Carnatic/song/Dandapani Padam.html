  Lyrics - English:

PallavidaNDapANi padam maravAdavarkku yama daNDanai kiDDaiydu ten pazhani
AnupallaviaNDar munivar aDiyAr tiruk-kUTTamum ANDavanE hara haravena bhajikkum
CaranampAl abhiSEkam vibhUti abhiSEkam pArkka namadu iru kaN pOdumO
KOdaNDapANi raghurAman maruganE shankaran maghanE muruganE guhanE jaya
