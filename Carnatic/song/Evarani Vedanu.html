  Lyrics - English:

Pallavievarani vEDanu evarani pogaDanu bhuvana rakSaka rAma ninnu vinA nEnu
AnupallaviavanijA saumitri bharata shatrughna pavanaja vibhISaNAdi vinuta sucartira
CaranamsAmaja rAjuni dhruvuni prahlAduni prEmajEsi nIvE brOvalEdA munu
shyAmasundara gAtra sarasijadaLa nEtra kAmita phalada shrI para vAsudEva nE
