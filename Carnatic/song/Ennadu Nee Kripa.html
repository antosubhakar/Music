  Lyrics - English:

PallaviennaDu nee krupa kalgunO inakula tilaka nannu rakshim cu Daku ennaDu nee
Anupallavipannaga shayana pannaga shayana patita paavana baanu kula deepa bhavanaashana (ennaDu)
Caranamkanna taNDri naapai karuNimcamani kannavaari
ninna aashrayimcitivi nannu neranammi
yunna vaaDE neetO vinnavimcu koNDi venkatEsha naatO (ennaDu)
