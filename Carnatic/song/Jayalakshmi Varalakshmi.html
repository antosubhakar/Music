  Lyrics - English:

Pallavijayalakshmi varalakshmi saGgrAma vIryalakshmi priyulAlavai hariki berasitivamma
Caranam 1pAla jalanidhilOni pasanaina mIgada melini tamaralOni miJcu vAsana
nIla varNunu ramupai ninDina nidhAnamavai ErEvu lOkamulu mammElamamma amma
Caranam 2canduru tobutina sampadala merugavo kanduva brahmala gAce kalpavallivO
andina gOvinduniki anDane toDu nIdai unDAnanu mA intene unavamma amma
Caranam 3padiyAru vennelato baGgAru patima cedarani vedamulA ciguru bODi
eduTa SrI veGkaTeSu nillalavai nIku nidhula niliche talli nIvAramamma amma
