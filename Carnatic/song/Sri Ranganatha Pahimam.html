  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI raGganAtha pAhimAm krpAlaya SrInivAsa SvEtAdri nilaya apramEya
AnupallavikshIra sAgara Sayana bhava bhayaharaNa kshitijA ramaNa kshitISAdi vandita charaNa garuDa gamana nArAyaNa tritApa haraNa SrI vidyOpAsakAnugraha charaNa
CaranamgajAraNya vAsa SrI vEGkaTESa gajEndra mOkshha tIrtha vAsa dara hAsa
sajjana mitra vasishTAdi stuti pAtra (vasishTha, atri) vijaya jaya SApa vimOchanA sucharitra
aja bhavEndrAdi vinuta anantAnanta guNa bharita kalyANavasanta rAga tOshita
