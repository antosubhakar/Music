  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSankaraharapriyA gurunAthanE ! (Siva Sankara)
ninkaramE enRum abhayakaram ena tigazhndiDum Siva
anupallavipankaja padam tozhum bhaktar tam vAzhvinil
sangaTam nIngiyE mangaLam pongumE
caraNamtattuva vittagarum dinam paNindiDum pAvananum nIyE!
nitya nimala nigamAgama pUraNa guNanidhiyum nIyE!
satya rUpa kAmAkSi annai amsam ena tigazhndAyE!
uttamaneRidanil ulagOr uyndiDa oppilAda tavamEndiya paranE
