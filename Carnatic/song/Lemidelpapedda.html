  Lyrics - English:

PallavilEmi telpa peddal(e)varu lErO
AnupallaviA mahimal(e)lla mAni I mahilO puTTirO (lEmi)
CaranamnI rUpamunu pUni manasu nirmalulai(y)uNDa lEdA
dhArA-dhar(A)bha SarIra tyAgarAja vinuta (lEmi)
