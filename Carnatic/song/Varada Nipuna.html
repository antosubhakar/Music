  Lyrics - English:

Pallavivarada nipuNa naLina caraNE nata janAvanE
AnupallaviSarada sOma bimba vadanE smara dahana vAmAGga sadanE
Caranamkarakalita kAdamba daNDa sImaSara pASAGkuSa-
dhara parama dayAmbudhi mAm pAlaya Sivasati
