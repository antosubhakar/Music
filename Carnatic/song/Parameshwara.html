  Lyrics - English:

PallaviparamESvara pAlaSumAm SAGkari manOhara bhava
Anupallavisura patinuta SUladhara tripurAntaka Siva SaGkara
CaranamhErambha tAta vEdAtIta uragadhara umApatE SaraNAgata
janOpEta SaSidhara kharaharapriya muraLIgAna vinOda
