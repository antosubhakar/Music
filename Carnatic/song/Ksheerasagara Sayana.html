  Lyrics - English:

PallaviKsheera saagara shayana vakshasthala nivaasineem pranamaami
Smara jananeem manonmaneem paapavimochaneem loka jananeem niranjaneem
AnupallaviKshityaadi shivaanda shat-trim-shat tatva swarupineem
Shivamodineem maayaamaalavagoula swarupineem
Kshipra prasaada daayineem vipra vishwaasineem
Swa prakaasha teja prakaashineem chit prakaasha daayineem maanineem
CaranamKankanamani mayaa-bharana yuta maatangeem mechakaangeem
Ankusha chaapa baanekshu hasta mohineem shaila nandineem
Kumbha sambhava lopaamudraarchana toshineem
Kaanchipura vaasineem Kanjadalaaya daaksheem Kaamaaksheem
Antaranga pujaa toshinim taam-brahmaa khyaakaashya tatva shaalineem
Ambaam sri vidya rasaa swaadineem
