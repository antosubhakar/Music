  Lyrics - English:

pallavisundara mUruti mukhya prANa banda namma manege SrI rAma nAma dhvanige
caraNam 1kaNakAlanduge gejje jhaNa jhaNarenuta jhaNaku jhaNakurendu kuNi kuNidADuta
caraNam 2tumburu nAradaru vINe pArisuta vINe pArisuta SrI rAma nAma pADuta
caraNam 3purandara viTTalana nenedu pADutali nenendu pADutali Alingana mADutali
