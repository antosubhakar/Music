  Lyrics - English:

Pallavienna sholli azhaittAl varuvAyO eLiyEn enranai inidu ATkoLLa
Anupallavipannaga shayanA parama dayAkarA padam paNindEn sadgati peravE nAn
Caranamabhayamenra karikkudaviya nAmamO AraNangin mAnam kAttadOr nAmamO
aran manattiDai sadA japittiDum nAmamO avaniyOr uraiyttiDum Ayiram nAmattil
