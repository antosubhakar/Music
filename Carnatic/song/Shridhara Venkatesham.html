  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrIdhara venkaTEsham sadgurum bhajE
AnupallavisAdhu sandOHa sanjIvana dAyinam
satatam shiva nAmAmrta bAyinam
Caranamnigama sAra vidam nija sanyAsinam nikhila jIva karuNAkara hAsinam
jagadIkyAda sahajIpura vAsinam shrIkara pasitha rudrAkSa vibhAsinam
