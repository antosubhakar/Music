  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhO SambhO tavakIna caraNAm bhOja bhaktim dEhimE sadA
AnupallaviISa pApanASa umA mahESa dAsa pOsha hAsa vadana parESa
CaranambhUsurAdi sadbhakta kalpa bhUruha muraLI gAnapriya
bhOgi bhUshaNa vibhUti dhAraNa vibhO sadASiva pAhimAm aniSam

அனுபல்லவிஈஷ பாபனாஷ உமா மஹேஷ தாச போச ஹாச வதன பரேஷ
