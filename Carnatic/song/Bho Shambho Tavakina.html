  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhO shambhO tavakIna caraNAm bhOja bhaktim dEhimE sadA
AnupallaviIsha pApanAsha umA mahEsha dAsa pOSa hAsa vadana parEsha
CaranambhUsurAdi sadbhakta kalpa bhUruha muraLI gAnapriya
bhOgi bhUSaNa vibhUti dhAraNa vibhO sadAshiva pAhimAm anisham

அனுபல்லவிஈச பாபனாச உமா மஹேச தாச போஷ ஹாச வதன பரேச
