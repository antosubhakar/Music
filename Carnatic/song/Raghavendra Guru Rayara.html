pallaviRaghavendra nuru Rayara sevisiro
saukyadi jivisiro ||pa||
anupallavitungathIradi raghuramana pUjisiro
narasinghana bhajakaro ||ap||
caraNam 1srI sudhIndra kara saroja sanjata jagadolage punitA
daSaratiya dasatvava ta vahisi
durmatigala jayisi
I samIra mata samstApakarAgi nindakaranu nIgi
busurarige samsevya sadacharanI
kangolisuva karuni ||||
caraNam 2kundada vara mantralayadalliruva
karadallige baruva brindavanagata mrittike jalapAna
mukutige sopAna
sandaruSanamAtradali mahatApa badidoisalApa
mandabhAgyarige doreyadiruva seva
Saranara sanjIvA ||||
caraNam 3srida vithalana sannidana pAtra
samstutisalu mAtra moda padisutiha tanihaparadalli
itage sariyalli
mediniyolaginnarasalu nA kane pusiyallAne
pAdasmaraneya madadavane papi
na peluve stapi ||||
