  Lyrics - English:

Pallavigauri nAtham kanaka sabESam
Madhyamakala sahityamgauri kamalabhava Sirah-kaNThi santatam Srti bAlE
Anupallavigauri guruguha vinutam vinaSita Damruga SUlam
nartana bAlam pannaga mAlam sannaga pAlam
Madhyamakala sahityambhrGgi mAtaGga tanaGga sanakAdi santatam ati dhrta saGgam
mAtaGga kumari mukhAmbuja mOhana madhuruci tanirasa bhaGgam
