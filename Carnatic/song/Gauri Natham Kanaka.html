  Lyrics - English:

Pallavigauri nAtham kanaka sabEsham
Madhyamakala sahityamgauri kamalabhava shirah-kaNThi santatam shrti bAlE
Anupallavigauri guruguha vinutam vinashita Damruga shUlam
nartana bAlam pannaga mAlam sannaga pAlam
Madhyamakala sahityambhrngi mAtanga tananga sanakAdi santatam ati dhrta sangam
mAtanga kumari mukhAmbuja mOhana madhuruci tanirasa bhangam
