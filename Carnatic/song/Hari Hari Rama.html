  Lyrics - English:

Pallavihari hari rAma rAma nannaramara jUDagu niratamu nI nAma smaraNa Emaranu
AnupallavidaSaratha nandana daSamukha mardana paSupati raJjana pApa vimOcana
CaranammaNimaya bhUshaNa maJjuLa bhAshaNa raNa jaya bhIshaNa raghukula bhUshaNa
patita pAvana nAma bhadrAcaladAma satatamu SrI rAmadAsu nElumA
