PallavikalayE dEva dEvamuru kAruNya nivAsam
Anupallavivalamathana mukha vibudha vandya mAnam aniSam
Caranam 1vimala maNiyuta makuTa vilasitara Sirasam suma vaSita kESabharam sOma bAlabhAlam
kamala bANa cApa sama kamanIya cilliyugaLam ramaNIya kuvalayadaLa rAji nija nayanam
Caranam 2tilaka kusuma vijaya paTu divya tama nAsikam kalita makara kuNDala kAnti dIpta kapOlam
aladhu SONa vara bimba phala surucirAdharam vilasita kundaradanam viSada manda hasitam
Caranam 3aruNa kaustubha maNi hAra bandhura kaNTham puru kaGkaNa lasita prthu bhuja yugala SAlinam
varada mati rucira SrIvatsa virAjita vakshasam harimakhila jagannilayOdaram ASrita vatsalam
Caranam 4caturAnana nilayAyita cAru nAbhi puTama jita mati pitaru cira vasanaAvrta ghanOrubhAgam
ati maJjujaGgAyugama laghumava vArinidhi patita mandarOddharaNa para kUrma padAgram
Caranam 5uraga parivrDha Sayana munnidratanu suSamam dara rathAGga SArGga gadAdyA yudha samsEvitam
dharaNI ramAkAntam agha haraNa caNa caritram bharitakari nAyakam SrI padmanAbhamAdyam
