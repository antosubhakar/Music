  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisundara daSaratha nandana
vandanam(o)narincedarA
Caranam 1pankaja lOcana dharajA-
(y)ankamuna velungaga kani (sundara)
Caranam 2parama dayA-kara Subha-kara
giriSa manOhara Sankara (sundara)
Caranam 3karamuna goDug(i)Dukoni
sOdaru bharatuDu karagaga kani (sundara)
Caranam 4suguNuD(a)nila tanayuDu
gavaya gavAkshulu koluvaga kani (sundara)
Caranam 5ghaTaja vasishTha mRkaNDuja
gautam(A)dulu pogaDa kani (sundara)
Caranam 6akaLanka mukha tyAgarAjunu brOcina
avyAja karuNA sAgara (sundara)
