  Lyrics - English:

PallavipaSupati mOhanakalyANi pAhimAm rAjESvari mahESvari
Anupallavivasumati kAtyAyani SrI hari bhaginI kusuma vara bhUshitEnAda santOshitE
CaranamSiva manOhAriNi kamala nayani sumacani nI karuNAkari tripura sundari
bhava bhaya nivAraNi pApa samhAriNi suvadanE viSva maGgaLakAriNi
