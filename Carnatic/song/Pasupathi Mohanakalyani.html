  Lyrics - English:

Pallavipashupati mOhanakalyANi pAhimAm rAjEshvari mahEshvari
Anupallavivasumati kAtyAyani shrI hari bhaginI kusuma vara bhUSitEnAda santOSitE
Caranamshiva manOhAriNi kamala nayani sumacani nI karuNAkari tripura sundari
bhava bhaya nivAraNi pApa samhAriNi suvadanE vishva mangaLakAriNi
