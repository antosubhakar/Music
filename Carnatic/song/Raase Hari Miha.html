PallavirAse harim iha vihita vilASm
Caranam 1saJcharath adhara sudhA madhuradhvani mukharitha mOhana vamsamchalitha dhrukaJchala saJchala mauLi kapOla volOla vathamsam rAsE harimiha vihitha vilAsamsmarathi manO mama krutha parihAsam
Caranam 2chandraka chAru mayUra sikhaNtakamaNdala valayitha kEsamprachura purandara dhanuranu raJjitha mEdhura mudhira suvESam
Caranam 3gOpa kadamba nithambhavathImukha chumbana lambitha lObhambhandu jIva madhurAdhara pallavakalitha tharasmitha sObham
Caranam 4vipula pulaka bhuja pallava valayitha vallava yuvathi sahasramkaracharaNOrasi maNigaNa bhUSaNa kiraNa vibhinnathamisram
Caranam 5jalatha patala chaladinthu vinindhakachandana bindhu lalAtampIna payOdhara parisara mardhana nirdhaya hrudaya kavAdam
Caranam 6maNimaya makara manOhara kuNtalamaNdithagaNdamudhArampItha vasanam anugatha muni manujasurAsura vara parivAram
Caranam 7visada kadambathalE miLitham kali-kaluSa bhayam samayanthammAmapi kimapi tharaGgadanaGga drusA vapuSA ramayantham
Caranam 8srI jayadEvabhaNitham athisundharamOhana madhuripu rUpamhari charaNa smaraNam prathi samprathipuNyavathAm anurUpam
