pallavigOvinda ghaTaya paramAnadam amrtamiha harE SrI nanda tanaya bahu yOgIndra suravinuta
caraNam 1agaNita guNa grAma aparimita nijakAma nigama paramA rAma nikhila mOhavirAma
nagadhara ghanaSyAma natajana kumuda sOma agha haraNa sarva sama asura maNDala bhIma
caraNam 2parama purushASEshapAla parijanatOsha parihrtAkhila dOsha pataga vAhana SEsha
paryanka mrdubhAsha parama mangaLa vEsha niravadya gOpapuri niyata varamaNi bhUsha
caraNam 3nava mauktika hAra nanda gOpakumAra bhava bandhana vidUra bhadrada sukhAkAra
avanata janAdhAra aparimita SubhAcAra navanItacOra nara nArAyaNAvatAra
caraNam 4Saradindu sama vadana Sata manmatha samAna gurutarA nanda ghana kunda sundara radana
paripanthi gaNa dalana pAlitAkhila bhuvana sarasa nArAyaNa tIrtha satya phaladAna deivam
