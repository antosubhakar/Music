  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviintakann(A)nandam(E)mi O rAma rAma
Anupallavisanta janulak(e)lla sammatiyai(y)uNDu kAni (inta)
Caranam 1ADucu nAdamuna pADucu eduTa rA
vEDucu manasuna kUDi(y)uNDuTa cAlu (inta)
Caranam 2SrI hari kIrtanacE dEh(A)di indriya
samUhamula maraci sOhaM-ainadE cAlu (inta)
