  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisukhi(y)evarO rAma nAma sukhi(y)evarO
Anupallavisukhi(y)evarO sumukhi(y)evarO
akhila sAramagu tAraka nAma (sukhi)
Caranamsatyamu tappaga sakala lOkulaku
bhRtyuDai daiva bhEdamu lEka
nityamaina su-svarapu gAnamutO
nirantaramu tyAgarAja nuta nAma (sukhi)
