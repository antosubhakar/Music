  Lyrics - English:

Pallavihari Bhajane mADO nirantara
paragatigidu nirDhAra nODO
Caranam 1modale tOrutade maDhura vishhaya suKha
kaDeyali duhKha anEka
Caranam 2vEdashhAstragaLa OdidarEnu sADhanakidu nirDhAra
sAravO bahu samsAra vimOcaka sErO shrI hayavadananna
