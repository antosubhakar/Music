  Lyrics - English:

PallaviO raghunandanA rAghava SrI raghunandanA rArA ASrta jana samudaya mandAra
Caranam 1yAdi yuJcumi nAmIda nIkE daya rAvalE gAdA nI
pAdamulaku mrokkeda nAku pratyakshamu kArAdA
Caranam 2nI kAruNyamutOnu nEnu nirvahimpa dalacedanu
nIkE marulukonnAnu niddampu mOmu jUcedanu
Caranam 3sarasija bhavanuta Sauri nI sari daivamu likayEri
SaraNaNTini ninu kOri SarajAla SarAsanadhAri
Caranam 4satatamu nA madilOnu ninu samsmarimpa dalacedanu
gati nIvani namminAnu E gati brOcina nIvEnu
Caranam 5vara bhadrAcalavAsa bhAvaja SatakOTi vilAsa
paramAnanda vikAsa paripAlita SrI rAmadAsa
