  Lyrics - English:

PallaviO raghunandanA rAghava shrI raghunandanA rArA Ashrta jana samudaya mandAra
Caranam 1yAdi yuncumi nAmIda nIkE daya rAvalE gAdA nI
pAdamulaku mrokkeda nAku pratyakSamu kArAdA
Caranam 2nI kAruNyamutOnu nEnu nirvahimpa dalacedanu
nIkE marulukonnAnu niddampu mOmu jUcedanu
Caranam 3sarasija bhavanuta shauri nI sari daivamu likayEri
sharaNaNTini ninu kOri sharajAla sharAsanadhAri
Caranam 4satatamu nA madilOnu ninu samsmarimpa dalacedanu
gati nIvani namminAnu E gati brOcina nIvEnu
Caranam 5vara bhadrAcalavAsa bhAvaja shatakOTi vilAsa
paramAnanda vikAsa paripAlita shrI rAmadAsa
