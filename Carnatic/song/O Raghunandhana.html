Charanam 1yAdi yuncumi nAmIda nIkE daya rAvalE gAdA nI
pAdamulaku mrokkeda nAku pratyakSamu kArAdA
Charanam 2nI kAruNyamutOnu nEnu nirvahimpa dalacedanu
nIkE marulukonnAnu niddampu mOmu jUcedanu
Charanam 3sarasija bhavanuta shauri nI sari daivamu likayEri
sharaNaNTini ninu kOri sharajAla sharAsanadhAri
Charanam 4satatamu nA madilOnu ninu samsmarimpa dalacedanu
gati nIvani namminAnu E gati brOcina nIvEnu

paramAnanda vikAsa paripAlita shrI rAmadAsa
