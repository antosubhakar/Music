(jaya)
Caranam 1jai jai bhavAni jai sharvANi jai tribhuvan sukhadAyI
tErO hi mAyA anant apAr nyArA jAku kOyi nahi pAyi
(jaya)
Caranam 2hastakamal mO bIn virAjE jAmEm sab sur gAyi
dUsarO hAth virAjat pustak vEd shruti upjAyi
(jaya)

padmanAbh prabhu caraN sarUruh sEvak kE man bhAyi
(jaya)
