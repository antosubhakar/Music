  Lyrics - English:

pallavijaya jaya dEvi jai jagajjanani jai jai sarasvati mAyI
(jaya)
caraNam 1jai jai bhavAni jai SarvANi jai tribhuvan sukhadAyI
tErO hi mAyA anant apAr nyArA jAku kOyi nahi pAyi
(jaya)
caraNam 2hastakamal mO bIn virAjE jAmEm sab sur gAyi
dUsarO hAth virAjat pustak vEd Sruti upjAyi
(jaya)
caraNam 3tErO rE rUp bhayO sab vidyA sur nar caran manAyi
padmanAbh prabhu caraN sarUruh sEvak kE man bhAyi
(jaya)
