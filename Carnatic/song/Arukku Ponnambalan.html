  Lyrics - English:

PallaviArukku ponnambalan krpai irukkudO avanE periyavanAm
AnupallavipArukkuL vIDugaL mADugaL ADugaL paNam irundAl avan periyavanAvanO
Caranam 1vEda purANagaL OdinadAlenna vElai SUzhpaNai mAdarAl enna kAriyam
sAdhanaiyAga varAdoru nALum maNNSai peNNASai ponnAshhai pollAdu
Caranam 2bANan madaGgal aDaGgavE Seyda gOpAlakrshNan dinam tozhum ponnambala
vANan enRAdaravAy virumbAdavan vAnavarAghilum tAn avan cinnavan
