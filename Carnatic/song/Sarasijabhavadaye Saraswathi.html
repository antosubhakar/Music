PallaviOh Goddess Saraswati, queen of the Lotus-born Brahma! I bow down to You & always meditate upon Your lotus-feet.
A.pOh Supreme & Eternal Goddess of speech, Bhaarati! Please protect me and bestow upon me That Supreme Bliss, longed for by one & all.
CharanamYour beautiful face, comparable to the autumnal moon, has even the Four-faced Brahma longing for. You are worshipped by the likes of Sanandana; you are holding the Veena in Your hands; you are easily accessible; you bestow unlimited auspiciousness and are like the celestial wish-tree.
