  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviendu kaugalinturA ninn-
(e)ntani varNinturA ninn(endu)
Anupallaviandamaina kunda radana
indirA hRn-mandira ninn(endu)
CaranamnIdu palukE palukurA
nIdu kulukE kulukurA
nIdu taLukE taLukurA
nijamaina tyAgarAja nuta ninn(endu)
