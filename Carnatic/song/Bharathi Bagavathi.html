nannu pAlimpavE SrI sarasvatI iha paramuliyyavE SrI sarasvatI
AnupallavisAramatI bhOgavtI guNavatI kAntimatI sudati lIlAvati
nIrajAsana sati sukrtavati nigamavatI simha madhyamAvati
Madhyamakala sahityam 1vErevaru lEriga nIvE gati vEdapurANAdulu nyAkrti
tOramaina bhaktisEya pUniti duritamulanu tolagiJcuTakiditadi
CaranamnAdarUpamai vEgamE nIvu nAnAlikapaini vasiJcavE
vAta bhEdamulaga kapaTa niyyaga vAkyamulanu pOshiJcavE
mOdamalaranI divya maGgaLa mUrti merayaga naTiJcavE
pItalaina sAdhulapai krpa SObhillu cUpulanu tilakimpavE
