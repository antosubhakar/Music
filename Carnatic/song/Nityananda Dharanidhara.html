  Lyrics - English:

PallavinityAnanda dharaNIdhara ramA ramaNa kAyAyanI stOtra kAma kamalAkSa
Caranam 1aravinda nAbhA jagadAdhArA bhavadUra puruSOttamA namO bhuvanEshA
karuNAsamagra rAkSasa lOka samhAra karaNa kamalAdhIsha karirAja varadA
Caranam 2bhOgIndra shayana paripUrNa pUrNAnanda sAgara nijAvAsa sakalAdhipa
nAgAri gamana nAnAvarNa nijadEha bhAgIrathI janaka parama paramAtmA
Caranam 3pAvana aparAtpara shubhaprada parAtIta kaivalya kAnta shrngAra ramaNa
shrI venkaTEshu dAkSiNya guNanidhi dEvatArAdhya susthira krpAramaNa namO
