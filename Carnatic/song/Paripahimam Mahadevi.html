  Lyrics - English:

PallaviparipAhimAm mahAdEvi mInAkshi pArijAta taruriva mamEStArtam dEhi
AnupallaviparimaLa pushpa mAlini patita pAvani karivadana shaNmukhapriya janani mAnini kAruNya sudhAvAhini kadambavana vAsini kAtyAyani kalyANa guNa SAlini
CaranamanAdi paraSiva sahitE bhaktha janahitE akArAnAkAra rUpayutE lalitE
anAtha paripAlana niratE namastE akArAdhyakshara samUhasthitE girisutE
sunAda vinOdapriyE mahAmAyE apramEyE sanakAdi muni gEyE SrIvidyE
