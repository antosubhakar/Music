AnupallavimaNI gOmEtaka lOhi taganIla marakata vAla vAyuja jAla makuTa
virAjita cigura manOhara mudira samakara kaLEpara kiGkiNi gaNa
Caranammalayaja raJjana yaksha kartama varNakamiSrta anubOdha tilaka makaika sugandha vilEpana
tribhuvana prakaTita pratApa jaladhara nIla samadhyuti bAla svAmi SrI rAjagOpAla lalAma kalOla lalita
