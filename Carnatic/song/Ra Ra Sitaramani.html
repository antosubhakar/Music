  Lyrics - English:

pallavirAra sItA ramaNI manOhara
anupallavinIraja nayana oka
mudd(I)ra dhIra mungala (rAra)
caraNam 1bangAru valvalu nE bAguga kaTTeda mari
SrungArinci sEva jEsi kaugiTa jErceda (rAra)
caraNam 2sAreku nuduTanu kastUri tilakamu peTTeda
sAramaina muktA hAramula diddeda (rAra)
caraNam 3yOgamu nIpai(y)anu rAgamu pADeda
vErE gati evaru SrI tyAgarAja vinuta (rAra)
