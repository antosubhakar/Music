  Lyrics - English:

Pallavi. pasyathi disi disi rahasai bhavanthamthwadhadhara madhura madhooni pibantham NAdha harE jagannAdha harESeedhathi rAdhA vAsa gruhE
. thwathabhisaraNa rabhasEna valantheepathathi padhAni kiyanthi chalanthi
. vihitha visadhabisa kisalaya valayAjeevathi paramiha thavarathi kalayA
. muhurava lOkitha maNdana leelAmadhuripuraham ithi bhAvana seelA
. thwaritham upaithina kadhamapi sAramharirithi vadhathi sakheem anuvAram
. slishyathi chumbathi jaladhara kalpamharirupagatha ithi thimiram analpam
. bhavathi viLambini vigaLitha lajjAvilapathi rOdhithi vAsaka sajjA
. sree jayadEva kavEridhamudhithamrasika janam thanuthAm athimudhitham
