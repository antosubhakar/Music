  Lyrics - English:

PallavivAyu kumAran shrI hanumAn malaraDi paNivOmE jaya vIra
AnupallavitUya anjanai shEyai rAghavan sEvaDi maravA vAnara vIran
CaranamdEvi sItaiyai darishanam sheida pin tennilankaiyai shuTTerittavan
nAvil enrum shrI rAmanAma pArAyaNan kamala caraNan raNadhIra
