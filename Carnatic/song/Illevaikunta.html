Caranam 1AshApAshagaLannu maretu shrIhariya mElAseyannu mADi nele goLisi
vAsuki shayanana dAsanAgi oDane vAsudEvana divya mUrti niTTisuvage
Caranam 2kAma krOdha lObha mada matsaravaLidu kAma janakana kAruNyadinda
nAmAmrtavanuNDu nava vidha bhakti nEmadindacyutana tiLIdu bhajiparige
Caranam 3vara kAvERiya virajA nadiyendu paramapadave ranga kSEtravendu
para vAsudEvane ranganAyakanendu parama bhakutiyinda bhajisuva janarige
Caranam 4snAna dAnadi satkarmava mudadinda shrI naraharige samarpisuva
Ananda vaikuNTHa lakSmIshanaDigaLa sAnurAgadi nitya dhyAnipa janarige
Caranam 5ubhya kAvEri madhyadali pavaDisi abhayavanIyuta dAsarige
