  Lyrics - English:

Pallavijaya jaya gurunAthA jaya hE priya gAyaka nEtA hE
Caranam 1jaya karuNA guNa jaya paripUraNa jaya jaya mrdudhara bhAshaNa
Caranam 2jaya salalita vilasita smita Anana jaya murLIrava mOhana
