PallavigOvinda salahennanu sadAnanda
AnupallavigOvinda salahenna kumuda lOcana ninna sEvakaraDiya sEvakanayya
Caranam 1garuDa gamana gOvinda kinnara siddha garuDa sannuta gOvinda aparimita
karuNA sAgara gOvinda ariyadantha paramjyOtiyE gOvinda gaGgeyapetta
Caranam 2caraNa nirmala gOvinda kOTi surara poreva gOvinda SrI vaikuNTha
puradoLagiha gOvinda acyutAnanta tirupati neleyadi kESava hariyE
