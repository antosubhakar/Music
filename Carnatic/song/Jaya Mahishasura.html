  Lyrics - English:

Pallavijaya mahiSAsura mardhini shrtajana pAlini
Anupallavijaya jayEndra pUjitE jay jaya jaya jaganmAtE
Caranamjaya jaya madhuripu sOdari jaya jaya shrI shAtOdari
jaya gaNesha guhajanani jaya jaya harikEsha bhAmini
