  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSankarAcAryam bhajE mAm pAhi Siva Sankara rUpam
ArUpam advaya nandakaram yativaram guruvaram
anupallavimOdakAdi hrt bhAvita caraNam vidhitAkhila SAstra sudhA jaladhi
mada hita mAyA bhava apavAryam hAram stutisaram vAram vAram
madhyamakAla sAhityamsuSAnta mUrtim hela vicAram ISa khEladi bhAgya vityAsam
vidhyAta parasma nija svarUpam gEyam gEyam gEyam bhayam
