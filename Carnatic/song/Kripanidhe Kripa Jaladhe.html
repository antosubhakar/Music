  Lyrics - English:

PallavikrpAnidhaE krpAjaladhE
AnupallavikapAla hastA skandatAta upamA rahita urAgAbharaNam
CaranamvEda kAraNa vishnupriyakarA bhEda rahitA brhadISa
nAda rUpA namOstutE naTana raJjana SrI harikESa
