  Lyrics - English:

PallavimRdu bhAshaNa nata vibhIshaNa
AnupallavisudurAsa mAnpaga lEni nanu
sukha cittugan(e)varu sEturE (mRdu)
Caranamvara dAnav(A)mbuda samIraNa
varad(A)nagh(A)mara pAlaka
dara hAsa sadguNa bhUsha parAt-
para tyAgarAja kar(A)rcita (mRdu)
