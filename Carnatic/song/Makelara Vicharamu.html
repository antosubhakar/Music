maruk(a)nna SrI rAma candra

sad-bhakta mandAra SrI kara (mAkEla)
Charanamjata kUrci nATaka sUtramunu
jagam(e)lla meccaga karamunan(i)Di
gati tappaka ADincedavu sumI
nata tyAgarAja gir(I)Sa vinuta (mAkEla)
