  Lyrics - English:

Pallavievarani nirNayincirirA ninn-
(e)Tl(A)rAdhincirirA nara varul(evarani)
AnupallaviSivuD(a)nO mAdhavuD(a)nO kamala
bhavuD(a)nO para-brahmam(a)nO ninn(evarani)
CaranamSiva mantramunaku ma jIvamu
mAdhava mantramunaku rA jIvamu(y)I
vivaramu telisina ghanulaku mrokkeda
vitaraNa guNa tyAgarAja vinuta (evarani)
