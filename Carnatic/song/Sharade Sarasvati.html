  Lyrics - English:

PallavishAradE sarasvati vEda mayi vidyAvati
AnupallavivimalapadE kamala sthitE vimalE hrdaya vikasitE
CaranamsangIta sAhitya kalAnidhE svara bahu vidha kalpanAnanda sahitE
vara krtE vINA kara dhrtE vANi manOllAsa kAriNi
