  Lyrics - English:

PallavinI bhakti bhAgya sudhA
nidhin(I)dEdE janmamu
AnupallavibhU-bhAramu kAni sura
bhU surulai janincina (nI)
CaranamvEd(O)ktambau karmamu
veta galgu gat(A)gatamau
nAd(A)tmaka tyAgarAja
nAth(A)pramEya sadA (nI)
