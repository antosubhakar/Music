  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSAradE vINA vAdana viSAradE vandE tavapadEA: nArada jananI catur vadana nAyaki bhukti mukti dAyakinaLina daLa lOcani bhava mOcani hamsavAhini hamsagAmini
caraNamindrAdi sakala bhrndAraka gaNa vandita padAra vindE
indu viTambana manda smitayuta sundara mukhAra vindE
vandAru sujana mandAra dayA sadanE mrdu gatanE
vANi nitya kalyANi varadE rAmadAsa hrdayAlayE SrI
