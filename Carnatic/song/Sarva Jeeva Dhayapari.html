bIja mantrESvari sarvadA sadA tvAmEvanamAmi (sarva)
anupallavisarva samkshObhaNAshTa daLa padma cakrESvari gupta
tara yOgini ananga kusumAdhyashTa dEvI svarUpa mOhini
para mantra tantrESvarIi vyApaka bhaNDAsura mada bhanjani (sarva)
caraNamAnandA karshita sthUla sUkshmamaya bAhyAntara prapanca pAlini
jnAnamaya svaprakASa rUpiNi kAma kalA pradarSini
dIna jana rakshiNi sarvAkarshiNi mahimAtisiddhi natapradAyini

gAna rUpa tantri samanvita vINA dhAriNi nArAyaNi (sarva)
