bIja mantrESvari sarvadA sadA tvAmEvanamAmi (sarva)
Anupallavisarva samkshObhaNAshTa daLa padma cakrESvari gupta
tara yOgini anaGga kusumAdhyashTa dEvI svarUpa mOhini
