PallaviSrI ramA ramaNI manOhara Srtajana mandAra
(SrI)
AnupallavighOra tAnava mada vida caraNamu sukunda varada nandagOpa kuAra
(SrI)
Caranam 1atulita divyaguNAbharaNa sanakAdi munivarya mAnasa sadana
nata janArti haraNa maJjubhAshaNa nava nIla SubhAGga dhrta rathAGga
(SrI)
Caranam 2sanaka sanandanAdi vinuta sarva vEda vEda
svarUpa dinakara kulAbdhi ghambhIra divya nAmAGga nishkaLaGka
(SrI)
Caranam 3mandara giridhara parAtpara-tamAla pallava sadrSa bhAsamAna
sundara purandarAdi vandita suprasanna varada veGkataramaNa
(SrI)
