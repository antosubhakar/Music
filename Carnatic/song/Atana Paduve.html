AnupallaviAavatana kirtiyanu parikshita kele pavananadanu moojagavariya
Caranam 1bhavashuddiyali shukanaaranu pogaluva aavagam prahladanaadavana nenevanayya
Caranam 2shileya baaleya maadida paadavaaradu nalina sambhavanu pettavaraaru
Caranam 3kaliyugada manujarige aaranaamavugati ileya bharavaniluhi salahidaraarayya
Caranam 4drupadana suteya maanalakshakanaaru nrpadharmanige samrakshakanaaru
Caranam 5kripeyinda vidurana maneyalundavanaaru aapatkaaladi gajava salahidaraarayya
Caranam 6saagarana magalige aara naamavegati yogadi naaradanaara bhajipenayya
Caranam 7raagarahita hanumantanodeyanaaru bhagavatara priya pundaravithala
