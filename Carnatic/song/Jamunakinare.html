  Lyrics - English:

PallavijamunA kinArE pyArE kadamatara mOhan bAnsuri bajAvE sakhi kunjabhavan mEn
Caranam 1mOr pinch galE mAlA malarAkrti kuNDal makuTAdhik bhUSaN shObha dEt tan mEn
Caranam 2padmanAbh dInabandhu mErO tAp harO prabhu gOpinAth giridhar rAjO mErE man mEn

  Lyrics - Tamil:
