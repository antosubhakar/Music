  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhAvayAmi gOpAlabAlam manas-sEvitam tatpadam cintayEham sadA
Caranam 1ghaTi ghaTita mEkhalA kacitamaNi khaNDikA paTala nina dEna viprAjamAnam
kuTila pada khaTita samkula simjitE natam caTula naTanA samujvala vilAsam
Caranam 2niratakara kalita navanItam brahmAdi sura nikara bhAvanA shObhita padam
tiruvEnkaTAcala stitam anupam harim parama puruSam gOpAlabAlam
