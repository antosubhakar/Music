PallavipAlay(A)Su mAM para dEvatE
AnupallavikAla kAla vallabhE lalitE ati lalitE (pAlayASu)
Caranam 1gAna vinOdinI niranjanI
dAna pradAyinI ranjanI
mAn(O)nnatiM dEhi mE SivE
mAnavatI hima giri tanayE (pAlayASu)

panc(A)kshara vaibhava muditE
