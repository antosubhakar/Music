  Lyrics - English:

PallavijAnarO I mOhamu sahimpalEnE
AnupallavimAnini jAlamEla mArubAri kOrvalEnE
Caranam 1sarasAngi sAmikEmO marulanu jEsi
bharavashamandu nIvu pOyiramma muddu koDa
Caranam 2sarasija nEtruDaina shrInivAsunipai
karagiyunna nA manasu delipiramma muddu koDa
