  Lyrics - English:

Pallavipannaga shayana pApa vimOcana khinnatA nAshana kEshi niSUdana
Caranam 1kanja vilOcana kALiya damana kunjara gamana kOmaLa vadana
Caranam 2aNDajavAhana Ashrita pAlana pANDava ranjana pauNraka bhanjana
Caranam 3dEvaki nandana daitya niSUdana pAvana caraNa pAlita bhuvana
Caranam 4ghOra sacOraka gOpikA nAyaka vArida mEcaka vAraNa pAlaka
Caranam 5panca janAntaka pAda samsEvaka vanci bhUpAlaka vAncita dAyaka
