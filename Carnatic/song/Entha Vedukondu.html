  Lyrics - English:

Pallavienta vEDukondu rAghava
pantam(E)larA O rAghava
Anupallavicinta tIrcuTak(e)nta mODirA
antarAtma nA centa rAnu nE(nenta)
Charanamcittam(a)ndu ninnu jUcu saukhyamE
uttamamb(a)nucun(u)ppongucunu
satta mAtramA cAla nammitini
sArvabhauma SrI tyAgarAja nuta (enta)
