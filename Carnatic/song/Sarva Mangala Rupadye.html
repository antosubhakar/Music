Pallavisarva maGgaLa rUpADyE sarva saubhAgyapradE
AnupallaviparvatEndra bAlE bhaktajana paripAlE
CaranamdAna darmagaLEdu mariyEnu sahaja nishTaya sAra mariyEnu dInapAl;E nInE gati SrI harikESa sati
