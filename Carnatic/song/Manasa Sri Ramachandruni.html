PallavimanasA SrI rAma candruni
maravakE EmarakE O (manasA)
Anupallavimunu puTTanu puTTina mauni kRtamau
mUDun(n)Ar(a)dhyAyamula jUcukO (manasA)
CaranamsRshTi pushTi nashTi sEyu panulu
nikRshTam(a)nucunu trimUrtulak(o)sagi
tushTuDai paragE sad-bhakta
manObhIshTamul(i)ccE tyAgarAja nutuni (manasA)
