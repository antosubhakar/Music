Anupallavisundara bhairavi suhAsini mandahAsa vadana sukumAri
Charanam 1sugandha puSpAlankrta sundari vasantabhairavi sukumAri kEdAra nAtha priyankari
Charanam 2sAmagAnapriya shyAmaLEshvari samAna rahita sAvitri sOmashEkhara priya sAvEri kOmaLAngi
nIrajAkSi nirmalAkAri nitya hamsanAdOllAsini Ashrta bhadra kannAvara atishaya shrI lalitAmbikA
Charanam 3shankarAbharaNa shakti sakala shAstrArcita cAmuNDEshvari shankari
sankaTa hAriNi pankajAkSi parama pAvani sAramati
unnata umayAmbikA ubhaya lOka rakSaki bAgEshvari
nArAyaNi shAradA dEvi AdhAra nAyaki nara surArcita Anandabhairavi
saha sakalavalli caturbhuja nAyaki vANI sarasvati
kaliyuga varapradAyini hamsadhvani bhUshita niranjani
