  Lyrics - English:

PallavivallabhA nAyakasya bhaktO bhavAmi
Madhyamakala sahityamvAnchitArtha dAyakasya vara mUshika vAhanasya
Samasti caranampallava pada mRdu-tarasya pASAnkuSAdi dharasya
mallikA jAtI campaka hArasya maNi mAlasya
Madhyamakala sahityamvallI vivAha kAraNasya guru guha pUjitasya
kALI kalA mAlinI kamalAkshI sannutasya
