  Lyrics:

PallavivallabhA nAyakasya bhaktO bhavAmi
Madhyama Kala SahityamvAnchitArtha dAyakasya vara mUshika vAhanasya
Samashti Charanampallava pada mRdu-tarasya pASAnkuSAdi dharasya
mallikA jAtI campaka hArasya maNi mAlasya
Madhyama Kala SahityamvallI vivAha kAraNasya guru guha pUjitasya
kALI kalA mAlinI kamalAkshI sannutasya
