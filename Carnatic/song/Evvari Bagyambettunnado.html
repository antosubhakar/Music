  Lyrics - English:

Pallavievvari bhAgyam beTTunnatO davvu cEruvaku tAnE gurutu
Caranam 1parama maGgaLamu bhagavan nAmamu surulaku narulaku Subhakaramu
iravuka nerigina eduTanE unnadi varusala maracinavAriki mAya
Caranam 2vEdAnta sAramu vishNu bhakti iti Adi mUnula matamayinati
sAdhiJcuvAriki sarva sAdhanamu kAdani tolagina gaDu sUnyambu
Caranam 3cEti nidhAnamu SrI vEGkaTapati Etala jUcina nindariki
nItiyu nItiyE nija sEvakulaku pAtakulakunadi bhavasAgaramu
