  Lyrics - English:

pallavimAmavASrta nirjara bhUruha mArakOTi madApaha dEha
anupallavikAmadAyaka parimuditEbha kEvala mOda nivAsa ghanAbha
caraNam 1danta SUka SayAmbuja nEtra dIna janAvana pUtacaraNa
vandakALi hrdayAmbuja mitra vAsavAdi krta stutipAtra
caraNam 2pAdanatAdi samsta nirAsa bhrUvallI nirjita mArasarAsa
dEva gaNAdrta mrdutara hAsa vairi nishUdana sadguNa vAsa
caraNam 3SArada vidhu mukha mauktikahAra SAnta phaNISvara nagara vihAra
parilasa mE hrdi jaladhi gabhIra pankajanAbha bhavArNava pAra
