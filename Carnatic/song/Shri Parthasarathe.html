  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrI pArthasArathE karuNA jalanidhE cittajAri vinutAgha mOcanAravinda lOcana mAm pAhi
Caranam 1gOpAla vasudEva bAla cArushIla pANDavapAla vENugAnalOla
gOvinda mukunda jaganmUla kanda nAgEsha vandya saccidAnanda
Svara sahityamkairaviNI taTa nivEsha shrI tiruvallikkENi nagarIsha bhaktajana pOSa
mArajanaka mandahAsa dEvarAja hrdaya kamala vAsa shrI rukmiNIsha
Caranam 2rAjakumAra rajaka vastrApahAra kubjAgandha lEpana mAlAdhara
sujana rakSaNa kamsANUrAdi haraNa nitya kalyANa bhujaga shayana
bhUsura tOSaNa suraripu haraNa suguNAbharaNa suramuni jIvana
vraja vanitA ramaNa shrImannArAyaNa sadAshiva kavi pOSaNa dhurINa
