  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI pArthasArathE karuNA jalanidhE cittajAri vinutAgha mOcanAravinda lOcana mAm pAhi
Caranam 1gOpAla vasudEva bAla cAruSIla pANDavapAla vENugAnalOla
gOvinda mukunda jaganmUla kanda nAgESa vandya saccidAnanda
Svara sahityamkairaviNI taTa nivESa SrI tiruvallikkENi nagarISa bhaktajana pOsha
mArajanaka mandahAsa dEvarAja hrdaya kamala vAsa SrI rukmiNISa
Caranam 2rAjakumAra rajaka vastrApahAra kubjAgandha lEpana mAlAdhara
sujana rakshaNa kamsANUrAdi haraNa nitya kalyANa bhujaga Sayana
bhUsura tOshaNa suraripu haraNa suguNAbharaNa suramuni jIvana
vraja vanitA ramaNa SrImannArAyaNa sadASiva kavi pOshaNa dhurINa
