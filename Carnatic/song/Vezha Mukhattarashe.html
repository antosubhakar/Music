  Lyrics - English:

PallavivEzha mukhattaraSE vA vA vizhainazhaittEn aruL tA tA
AnupallavivENDum anbar vighnam aghaTRa veLLi ambalattE tigazhum
CaranamdEvI mInAkshi sannidhi mEvi vaDapurattamarndAy mUvulagilum
pUjai mudalvA en kAviya mAlai kazhalaNiya
