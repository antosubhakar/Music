  Lyrics - English:

PallavidEva dEva kalpayAmi sO ahamIsha mAmakam dEha gEhAdikam ca sakalamapitAvakam hi
Caranam 1kuhanA vaTurUpAya ca tE mAhabaliryatA mahati dAna samayE nanu kalitavAnahO tathA
Caranam 2rAjasUya savana karmaNiha cAgraya pUjanE rAjitE yathA krtam ca dharmajEna pAvanE
Caranam 3bhavati tathA krEturEva krIta bharaNa paratA aviratam tavaiva mAmakA vanadUrvahatA
Caranam 4EvamaviduSA param yanmayAtra gaditam sEvakEna padmanAbha tE grahItumucitam
