pallaviSri pArvatI paramEshvarau vandE
madhyamakAla sAhityamcidbimbau lIlA vigrahau
mamAbhISTa siddhayE
samasti caraNamApAda mastakAlankArau
Adi madhyAnta rahitAkArau
sOpAna mArga mukhyAdhArau
sukha pradau gandha rasAdhArau
madhyamakAla sAhityamlOpAmudrEshArcita caraNau

bhaya haraNau bhava taraNau
