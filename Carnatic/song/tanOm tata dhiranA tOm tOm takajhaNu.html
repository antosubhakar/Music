  Lyrics - English:

PallavitanOm tata dhiranA tOm tOm takajhaNu dhIm tajhaNu dhIm jhaNu dhImta
Anupallavi 1tatara tAni dhIm tattatdhIm tataratAni dhIm jhaNudhIm
tataratAni tatanata dhIm dhIm dhimidhimi takadhIm tajhaNuta tOm tOm
jhaNuta dhIm tanana dhIm takaNam takajham tajham taNam tOm
Anupallavi 2jhaNutadhIm tananadhIm takaNam takajham tajham taNam tanananana
Anupallavi 3jhaNutadhIm tananadhIm takaNam takajham tajham taNam tatanata tanananana
CaranamcidAnandE lilitAmbikE nAradAdi muni sannutE jnAna pradAyini durgAdEvi tava pAdAmbOruham sadA bhajAmi
takiTa tOm tatikiTatOm takadhimita tOm tOmta dhIm dhIm tatAm
dha mA ri ma sA ri sa dhA ma dha rI ma ri sA dha sa ma madha dha sa sa rI
tananana nanana tOm tOm tOm tanOm tadhIm
tajhaNu jhaNu jhaNu jham jham jham tanOm tadhIm
tadhimi tadhimita tAm tAm tAm
