pallavijaya jaya vaishnavi durgE jaya jaya kalpitasargE jaya jaya tOshita bhargE jaya jaya kunjita durgE
caraNam 1SrIkara sadguNajAlE sindUra ranjita phAlE pAkaSAsana maNi nIlE prAlEya bhUdhara bAlE
caraNam 2pAlita kisalaya cApE pArvati lOkaika dIpE kAlika kOmala rUpE khaNDita tribhuvana tApE
caraNam 3bhakta janAmara pUjE bhAsila lka samAjE rakta mrdula padAmbhOjE rangaduttunga kshOjE
caraNam 4Sankari satkrpApUrE sambhrta sanmaNihArE sAnkavalipta SarIrE sangatAnga kEyUrE
caraNam 5vINA vinOdini girijE vidruma maNi sannibha girijE mAnita lOka samAjE madana gOpAka sahajE
