PallavirAgi tandIryA bhikshhakE rAgi tandIryA
bhOgyarAgi yOgyarAgi bhAgyavantarAgi nIvu
Caranam 1annadAnava mADuvarAgi anna chatra vaniTTavarAgi
anya vArteya biTTavarAgi anudina bhajaneya mADuvarAgi (_ragi)
Caranam 2Guru gala sevEye mADuvarAgi gurutige bAhOranthavarAgi
Kare kare samsArava nItavarAgi purandara viTTalana sEviparAgi
