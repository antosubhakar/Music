  Lyrics - English:

PallavijatAdhara shankara dEva dEvA shashAnkadhara mangalakara gangAdhara bhujanga-valayitAlankAra
AnupallavinaTanAnanda cidambarAnanda laharI vihAra svacchandha kuTAra pinAka kuranga kOlAhala
tANDava kAlAntaka krIDA kirAda girijA samEta girIsha chitikhaNDa uDDaNDavara
Caranam 1pAlaya lOkam adhunA parama pAvana bhakta janAvana
Caranam 2madamaya tArukA vana muni manOhara nipuNa-magadanA danadhanada
Caranam 3pAmarajana kumuda candra bhAgadEya nIlakaNTha vrSa turanga para
paramEsha bhAsadhra madaharA bahu vara dAyaka madhu murahari sannuta
Caranam 4niravadhi kamanIya ramaNIya ramaNI mukha jhala jhala madhupa mukha
sharaNAgata jana pAlana tAmasAdi hara bhASadhara candra pancamukha
Caranam 5sAmagAna rasa bhUritAja mukha sAdhu mAnasa nirantarA carita
nAgabhOga parivItamadbhuta virAjitAnga vaparAjitAnga vara
pAlaya lOka adhunA parama pAvana bhakta janAvana
kAla kAla kapAla vidhAraNa kailAsa giri sadanA trinayana
