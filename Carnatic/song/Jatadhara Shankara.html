  Lyrics - English:

PallavijatAdhara SaGkara dEva dEvA SaSAGkadhara maGgalakara gaGgAdhara bhujaGga-valayitAlaGkAra
AnupallavinaTanAnanda cidambarAnanda laharI vihAra svacchandha kuTAra pinAka kuraGga kOlAhala
tANDava kAlAntaka krIDA kirAda girijA samEta girISa chitikhaNDa uDDaNDavara
Caranam 1pAlaya lOkam adhunA parama pAvana bhakta janAvana
Caranam 2madamaya tArukA vana muni manOhara nipuNa-magadanA danadhanada
Caranam 3pAmarajana kumuda candra bhAgadEya nIlakaNTha vrsha turaGga para
paramESa bhAsadhra madaharA bahu vara dAyaka madhu murahari sannuta
Caranam 4niravadhi kamanIya ramaNIya ramaNI mukha jhala jhala madhupa mukha
SaraNAgata jana pAlana tAmasAdi hara bhAshadhara candra paJcamukha
Caranam 5sAmagAna rasa bhUritAja mukha sAdhu mAnasa nirantarA carita
nAgabhOga parivItamadbhuta virAjitAGga vaparAjitAGga vara
pAlaya lOka adhunA parama pAvana bhakta janAvana
kAla kAla kapAla vidhAraNa kailAsa giri sadanA trinayana
