  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI gAyatrIdEvI vEda mAtru svarUpiNi mAm pAhi
AnupallaviSrI sAvitrI sarasvatI Saktyadi samsEvitE lalitE SrIkaNThayitE SrIpati pramukha sandyOpAsita mUrtE SrI vidyA mantra rahasya vEditE sujana samprItE
CaranammuktA vidrumAdi kAntiyutE paJca tEjOmaya mukha SObhitE
mumukshu sarva sampatpradanA-SaktE sarasija pIkavAstanE
bhaktAbhaya pradhAna niratE kAmita phala dAna vikhyAtE
bhagavatI daSa bhUjAlaGkrtE kapAlAdi SObhitE
bhArgadEva varEN-yanugrahitE buddhi vikasapracOditE
parabrahma gamana SramahitakAriNi kalyANI
