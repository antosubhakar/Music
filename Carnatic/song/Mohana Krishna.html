  Lyrics - English:

PallavimOhana krshNA jagan
AnupallaviSrIhari pUrNAvatAra SrGgAra SEkhara SrI manOhara
CaranamsAhasikAsura kula nASa karaNa satya guNAbharaNa sarvalOka SaraNa
mOha bhava sAgara taraNa vAhinISa suta vakshasthala dharaNa
