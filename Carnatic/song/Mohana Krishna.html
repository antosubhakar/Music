  Lyrics - English:

PallavimOhana krSNA jagan
AnupallavishrIhari pUrNAvatAra shrngAra shEkhara shrI manOhara
CaranamsAhasikAsura kula nAsha karaNa satya guNAbharaNa sarvalOka sharaNa
mOha bhava sAgara taraNa vAhinIsha suta vakSasthala dharaNa
