  Lyrics - English:

Pallavimarakata simhAsana narasimhA
durita chittagaja simhA
AnupallavimuraLI nAda sudhAsvAdA
varadA yAdagiri nAdhA
CaranamAhlAdamu nI vIkSaNamu
prahlAduniki rakSaNamu
AnandAtmaka abhayAkSatalE
akSayamai dorike nIkSaNamu
