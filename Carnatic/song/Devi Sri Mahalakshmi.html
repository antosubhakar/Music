PallavidEvI SrI mahAlakshmi dInapAlE rakshisenna
AnupallavibhAva bhaktiyinda ninna bhajanegaiva bhAgyavittu
CaranamashTAdaSa nija bhujE amita dayA SAlini dushTAsuramardhini surava paripAlini
SishTA jishTArtadE divya ratna bhUshaNi ashTAGgani vandisuvE harikESapura vAsini
