  Lyrics - English:

Pallavikathaya kathaya mAdhavam hE rAdhE kathaya kathaya mAdhavam
(kathaya)
Anupallaviata sakhi kathaya SrI madhusUdanam aSEksha vidhinA varNita vIryam aparimita vipatam (kathaya)
Caranam 1vana bhuvi rahasi samArAditO bhagavAn anavatya sundarAGgI hE rAdhE
ghana jaganayA tvayA khala nIla kuntaLaSrI vanamAli pItavAsA vasati kvavAjaya gati
(kathaya)
Caranam 2mrgamada tilaka lalita bAlasundaram agaNita suguNamaNi ratnAkaram
vikata mOham akhila vEdabhAgaviditam SrI bhagavantam ASrita bhAvuka vidAdhAram
(kathaya)
Caranam 3sarasa hAsa parihAsa sarasakala muralI miLita gOpa vadUjanam
aravinda lOcana arivarga nishudanam vara nArAyaNa tIrtha vAJcitapala nidAnam
(kathaya)
