Pallavikathaya kathaya mAdhavam hE rAdhE kathaya kathaya mAdhavam
(kathaya)
Anupallaviata sakhi kathaya shrI madhusUdanam ashEkSa vidhinA varNita vIryam aparimita vipatam (kathaya)
Caranam 1vana bhuvi rahasi samArAditO bhagavAn anavatya sundarAngI hE rAdhE
ghana jaganayA tvayA khala nIla kuntaLashrI vanamAli pItavAsA vasati kvavAjaya gati
(kathaya)
Caranam 2mrgamada tilaka lalita bAlasundaram agaNita suguNamaNi ratnAkaram
vikata mOham akhila vEdabhAgaviditam shrI bhagavantam Ashrita bhAvuka vidAdhAram
(kathaya)
Caranam 3sarasa hAsa parihAsa sarasakala muralI miLita gOpa vadUjanam
aravinda lOcana arivarga niSudanam vara nArAyaNa tIrtha vAncitapala nidAnam
(kathaya)
