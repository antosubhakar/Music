  Lyrics - English:

PallaviparAmukham(E)larA rAmayya
AnupallavinirAdaraNa kaladA nIyeDala
nity(A)nanda kalyANa guNa (parA)
Caranamibha rAju nIk(E)mainan(i)ccenA telupu
Subha praduDu nIvE(y)anucu
sundara tyAgarAja nuta (parA)
