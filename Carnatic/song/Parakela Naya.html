  Lyrics - English:

PallaviparAkElanayya rAma parAtparA nIkinta
AnupallavidharAsutA ramaNa sakala carAcaradhara nIku
Caranamparama bhaktitOnu nIdu smaraNa bhajana sEyalEdu
parama durAcArulaina narulajErimaraciti ninnu
caraNa janAdhAruDaina parama puruSa vAsudEva
nirupama guNa nIrajAkSa varamulosagi brOvavayya
