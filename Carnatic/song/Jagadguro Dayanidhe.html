  Lyrics - English:

PallavijagadgurO dayAnidhE SrI SaGkara SivAkrtE
AnupallavinigamAgama sAra suguNa nitya Suddha muktulaina
CaranamilalO manujuDai puTTi iha paramanE nenaru maraci kuladhana nI bhaktilEka unmattuDai
tirigitini calacittamugala nA hrttApamulanu dIrci brOvu calarahita SrI harikESa saccidAnandulaina
