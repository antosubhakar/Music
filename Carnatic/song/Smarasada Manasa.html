  Lyrics - English:

Pallavismara sada manasa balagopalam
Anupallaviduritamaya daruna giri kulisam
Caranamyadukula tilakam, yativara vinutam
madana janakamiha madhu-mura dalanam
sarasija nayanam, samaja varadam
varaguna vasatim, varidhi sayanam
garuda turaGgam, kanaka sucelam
parama purusam sri paṅkajanabham
