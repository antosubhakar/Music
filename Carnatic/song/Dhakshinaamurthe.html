  Lyrics - English:

PallaviDakSinamUrthe, amurthe
shanakadi muni jana hrun mUrthe
AnupallaviAgama sara, paripUrna athma jijnasu manogatha murthe
CaranamPrasIda hrud iSa, Adhi brahma tvaktha,
sarvam yukthamanam Thva charanagatham mAm.
