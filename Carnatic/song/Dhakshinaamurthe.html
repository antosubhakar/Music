  Lyrics - English:

PallaviDakshinamoorthe, amurthe
Sanakadi muni jana hrun moorthe
AnupallaviAgama sara, paripoorna athma jijnasu manogatha murthe
CaranamPraseeda hrud isha, aadhi brahma twaktha,
sarvam yukthamanam Thwa charanagatham maam.
