  Lyrics - English:

PallavinI dayacE rAma nity(A)nanduD(ai)ti
AnupallavinAda brahm(A)nanda ras(A)kRti gala (nI)
Caranamvara mRdu bhAsha su-svara maya bhUsha
vara tyAgarAja vAg-cEl(A)vRta (nI)
