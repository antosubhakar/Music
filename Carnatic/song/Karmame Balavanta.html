  Lyrics - English:

PallavikarmamE balavantamAyA talli
kAy(A)rOhaNa jAyA
Anupallavinirmalamagu nAga purmuna nelakonna
nIl(A)yat(A)kshi sakla lOka sAkshi (karma)
Caranam 1dharanu dhanikula kOrinA nA
paritApamul tIrpa lErairi(y)ani
saraguna nE bayalu-dEri vacci
parama pAvani nI sannidhi jErinA (karma)
Caranam 2vAridhi madi garvinci(y)I
vasudhaku tA rAn(e)nci ninnu
sAreku kani tala vanci(y)uNDu
dhIra tanamu kalgu ninu poDagAncinA (karma)
Caranam 3kAs(A)sa lEni nA madiki nI
karuNayE dhanam(a)ni paliki
niND(A)satO vacci sannidhiki nija
dAsuDaina SrI tyAgarAjuniki (karmamE)
