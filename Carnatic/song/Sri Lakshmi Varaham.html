AnupallavinIlamEgha jaya SyAmaLa gAtram nIlA bhUdEvI stutipAtram nIlakaNTha Siva guruguha mitram nikhila bhaktajana bhayArtti dAtram
CaranammaGgaLAlayAbhOgi nutapadam puGgava budhajana natam vEdanutam SaGkara priyakaram kubEra pratishThitam SaGkha cakra dharam kRpAkaram paGkajAsanapramukha sEvitam paGkajamukha bhArgavI bhAvitam bhaGgahara tAmraparNI tIrastham saGkaTahara sadAnanda sahitam
