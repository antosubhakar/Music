  Lyrics - English:

Pallavi. smara samarOchitha virachitha vEshA galitha kusumapara viluLitha kEsA KApi madhuripuNA vilasathi yuvathirathyadhika guNA
. hari parirambaNa valitha vikArA kucha kala sOpari tharaLitha hArA
. vichalathaLaka lalithAnana chandrA thadhatharapAna rabhasakrutha thanthrA
. chanchala kuNtala lalitha kapOlA mukharitha rasana jagana gathilOlA
. dhayitha vilOkitha lajjitha hasithA bahuvitha koojitha rathirasa rasithA
. vipula puLaka prudhu vEpadhubhangA swasitha nameelitha vikasathanangA
. sramajala gaNapara subaka sareerA paripathithOrasi rathi raNadheerA
. sree jayadEvabhaNitha hari ramitham kali kalusham janayathu parisamitham
