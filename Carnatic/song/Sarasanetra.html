  Lyrics - English:

PallavisArasa nEtr(A)pAra guNa
sAmaja Siksha g(O)ddharaNa
CaranampApa gaN(A)bhra samIraNa pAmara
pAlita pAvana nAma caNa
tApasa mAnasa sArasa su-
bhramar(A)nagha rAghava mA ramaNa
(tisra gati)
yOgi vandya pAhi hE sAgar(A)rti haraNa
rAga lObha dUra SrI tyAgarAja pOshaNa (sA)

(திஸ்ர கதி)

ராக லோப தூர ஶ்ரீ த்யாகராஜ போஷண (சா)
