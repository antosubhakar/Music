  Lyrics - English:

Pallavijaladhi sutA ramaNEna hI sOham sarasamAliGgitO nissandEham
Caranam 1paryaGkE nija dayata sahitE samupa kalitO nava sumalasitE
Caranam 2vAlavyajana virAjita karayA paricaritO nija sadrSa varayA
Caranam 3sarasijanAbha harE bahutAntAm kinnu vadAmi gatO mama kAntam
