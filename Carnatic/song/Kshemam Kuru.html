  Lyrics - English:

PallavikSEmam kuru gOpAla santatam mama
AnupallavikAmam tava padakamala bhramarI bhavatu shrIman mama mAnasam madhusUdana
Caranam 1akSINa karuNAnidhE Ananda ghana prakSINa dOSa vAridhE
shikSitA suragaNa rakSita nijajana kukSisthitAnEka kOTi lOka pAlana
Caranam 2prahlAda bhaya vidUra parayOgi pAvana bhuvanAdhAra
mOha rahita bOdha mauni mAnasa hamsa sArasa jitavairi sanghAta mahOdAra
Caranam 3ajita vijayagOpAla ananta lIla ranjita pada kamala vijaya
dvArakApurI vimala kamalAlOla nija nArAyaNa tIrtha nirmalAnandabAla
