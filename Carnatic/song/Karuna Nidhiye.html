  Lyrics - English:

PallavikaruNAnidhiyE thAyE kadaikkan partarulaye amba
AnupallaviArunodayam kandal agalum
pani irul pol annai undan arul irundal
agandridume pun bavanoi enparama
CaranamUnai marandu en vazhnalil oru kanamum irundariyene
anai marandilam sisu undo avvanam unai nambinen nane
manam ninainda anbarai aala mailai vanda kalpagam neeye
enadu pizhai poruttarulaye ini edu tuani mediniyil madumaiye
