  Lyrics - English:

PallavimahA gaNa patiM manasA smarAmi
Madhyamakala sahityamvasishTha vAma dEvAdi vandita
Samasti caranammahA dEva sutaM guru guha nutaM
mAra kOTi prakASaM SAntaM
Madhyamakala sahityammahA kAvya nATakAdi priyaM
mUshika vAhana mOdaka priyam
