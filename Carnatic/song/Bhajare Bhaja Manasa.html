  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhajarE bhaja mAnasa rAmaM
Anupallaviaja mukha Suka vinutaM Subha caritaM (bhaja)
Caranam 1nirmita lOkaM nirjita SOkaM
pAlita muni janam-adhunA nRpa pAkaM (bhaja)
Caranam 2Sankara mitraM SyAmaLa gAtraM
kinkara jana gaNa tApa traya tamO-mitraM (bhaja)
Caranam 3bhU sama SAntaM bhUjA kAntaM
vAram-akhiladaM tyAgarAja hRd-bhAntaM (bhaja)
