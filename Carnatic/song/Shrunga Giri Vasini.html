  Lyrics - English:

Pallavishrngagiri vAsini amba shrtajana pAlini shrI shAradE pAtu mAm ambA
AnupallavimangaLa shrI cakra madhya nivAsini shankara guru pUjita suvAsini
Madhyamakala sahityampankaja nayani ardha sharIrE amita kalyANa guNa paripUraNi
tungaNa shrIkara nitya vihAriNi duSTa samhAriNi durita nivAriNi
Caranamparama pAvani bhAgya pradAyini pAshu pasha mOcani bhavabhaya nAshini
hara manOranjani Atma niranjani hamsa shOkam ajnapa(?) Anandini
Madhyamakala sahityamsarasija lOcani saundarya vadani sakala niSkaLa rUpa vilAsini
varashikhi vAhana vAraNa janani vandita paTTAbhISTha pradAyini
