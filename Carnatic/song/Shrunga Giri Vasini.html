  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrGgagiri vAsini amba Srtajana pAlini SrI SAradE pAtu mAm ambA
AnupallavimaGgaLa SrI cakra madhya nivAsini SaGkara guru pUjita suvAsini
Madhyamakala sahityampaGkaja nayani ardha SarIrE amita kalyANa guNa paripUraNi
tuGgaNa SrIkara nitya vihAriNi dushTa samhAriNi durita nivAriNi
Caranamparama pAvani bhAgya pradAyini pASu paSa mOcani bhavabhaya nASini
hara manOraJjani Atma niraJjani hamsa SOkam ajnapa(?) Anandini
Madhyamakala sahityamsarasija lOcani saundarya vadani sakala nishkaLa rUpa vilAsini
varaSikhi vAhana vAraNa janani vandita paTTAbhIshTha pradAyini
