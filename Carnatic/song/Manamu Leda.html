  Lyrics - English:

PallavimAnamu lEdA tanavAD(a)ni abhi(mAnamu)
AnupallavikAnamurA nI vale nir-mOhini
gAna ruci teliyu kuSa lava janaka (mAnamu)
CaranamnI samukhamuna ravi tanayuDu ninnu
bAsalu palkaga rOsamutO
siMhAsan(A)dhipati jEsina nIku
dAsuDaina tyAgarAja kar(A)rcita (mAnamu)
