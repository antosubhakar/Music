PallavisarOjanAbha dayArNava mAmava SAradEndu mukha SEshapurAlaya
AnupallavidhArA ramA ramaNAmara pAlaka dAsa lOka bhava bhIti vinASana
Caranam 1phAla SObhita mrgamada tilaka surEndra paripAlaya mukhajita candra
mura rAkshasa sASanakAla kAla vara SApa vibhaJjana mauni lOka hrdayAmbuja khElana
jaladhara samaruci vilasita yaduvara
Caranam 2cAru rUpajita madana manOhara lIla SaraNAgata pAlana lOla
kamalAsana sannuta vAraNEndra bhaya nASana mAdhava
kambu kaNTha vara kaustubha vilasita karivara sukgamana vitara kuSalamyi
Caranam 3vArijAta daLa madahara rucira nayana vara pannagat-anukrta Sayana
vihagAdhipa vAhana pArijAta pushpa lasita kaca bhara
hEma cAru vasanAmita vikrama sura nikara makuTa lasita jalajapada
