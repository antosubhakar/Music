  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhUlOga kumAri hE amrtadhAri
AnupallaviAlOga SrGgAri amrta kalaSa kuSa pArE
kAla bhaya kuDAri kAma vAri kanaga ladA rUba garva timirArE
CaranambAlE rasajAlE bhagavadi prasIda kAlE nIla ratnamaya nEtra viSAlE
nitya yuvadi pada niraja mAlE lIlA jvAlA nirmida vANi nirandarE nikhila
lOgESAni nirubama sundari nitya kalyANi nijam mAm kuru hE manmada rANi
