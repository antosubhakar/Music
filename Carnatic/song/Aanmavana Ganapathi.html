  Lyrics - English:

PallaviAnmA vAna gaNapatiyin
aruLuNTu accam illaiyE
CaranammEnmaip paTuvAy manamE! kEL
viNNin iTi mun vizhuntAlum
pAnmai tavaRi naTuGkAtE
payattAl Etum payanillai
