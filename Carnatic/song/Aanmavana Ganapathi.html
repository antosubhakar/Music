  Lyrics - English:

PallaviAnmA vAna gaNapadiyin
aruLuNDu accam illaiyE
CaranammEnmaip paDuvAy manamE! kEL
viNNin iDi mun vizhundAlum
pAnmai tavaRi naDuGgAdE
payattAl Edum payanillai
yAn mun uraittEn kODi muRai
innum kODi muRai solvEn
