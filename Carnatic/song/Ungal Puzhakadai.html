PallaviuGgaL puzhakkaDai tOTTattu vAviyuL
ceGkazhunIr vAynekizhntu Ampal vAy kUmpina kAN
ceGkal poTikkURai veNpal tavattavar
taGkaL tirukkOyil caGkiTuvAn pOtantAr
