  Lyrics - English:

PallaviuGgaL puzhakkaDai tOTTattu vAviyuL
seGgazhunIr vAynegizhndu Ambal vAy kUmbina kAN
seGgal poDikkURai veNpal tavattavar
taGgaL tirukkOyil saGgiDuvAn pOdandAr
eGgaLai munnam ezhuppuvAn vAypEsum
naGgAy! ezhundirAy nANAdAy nAvuDaiyAy!
saGgoDu sakkaram Endum taDakkaiyan
paGgayak kaNNAnai pADalOr embAvAy.
