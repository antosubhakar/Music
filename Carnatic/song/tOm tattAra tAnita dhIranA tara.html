  Lyrics - English:

pallavitOm tattAra tAnita dhIranA tara tAni
anupallavitOm tattAra tAttara tAni dhitlAm tlAm tam tlAm tAm takadingiNatOm
caraNamSrIkara karuNAkara vara SrIdhara munijana vandita
SrIkari varada durita hara SrI raghuvara mAm pAlaya
jatisvaramtAm tadhimi dhimi tataNata jhamta jham jham takadhimi takaNaka jhaNu
ga* ma* ri*sa* ni pa tataNata ma ga ma tadingiNatOm
