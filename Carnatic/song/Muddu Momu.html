Pallavimuddu mOmu ElAgu celangenO
munul(e)Tla kani mOhincirO
Anupallavikadd(a)nucunu cira kAlamu hRdayamu
karagi karagi nilcu vArik(e)duTa rAmuni (muddu)
Caranammanasu nirmalamagu bhU-sura kRtamau
manci pUjA phalamO tolutaTi tapamO
