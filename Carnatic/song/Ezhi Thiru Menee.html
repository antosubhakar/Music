Pallaviezhil tirumEni kANIr en ayyan
Anupallavitozhudu agam karaindu tUmalar tUvum dAsarkkaruLum SrI gurvAyUr appan
CaranamakhilamuzhudaLanda aDimalar iNaiyum anRu maram sAytta azhagu muzhaGgAlgaLum
arakkanai mAyttiDa iruttiya tuDaigaLum aruLuDan uralil kaTTuNDa tiru vayirum
alaGgaigaL Endum veN saGgam tigiriyum alaGgaLuDan tan tuLabam tavazh mArbum
Madhyamakala sahityampaGgaja nayanamum puriyum punnagaiyum taGgum Siram maguDa oLi mayil pIliyum
kiGgiNi nUburam kaGgaNam kuNDalam kaLabhamuDan tigazh kArmuga vaNNan
