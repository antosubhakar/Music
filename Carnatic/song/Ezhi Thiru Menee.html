Anupallavipozhudu agam karaindu tUmalar tUvum dAsar karudum SrI guuvAyUr appan
Caranamakhlamuzhudum aDimalar iNaiyum anru maram sAitta azhagan kAlgaLum
arakkanai mAittiDa iruttiya tuDaigaLum aruLuDan mudalil kaTTuNDa tiru vaDivam
aGgaigaL Endum veN saGgam tigiriyum anaGgaLuDan tan tulabham kavazh vAzhum (tavazh mArbum)
