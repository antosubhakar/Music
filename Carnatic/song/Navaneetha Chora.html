PallavinavanItacOra namOnamO navamahimArNava namOnamO
Caranam 1harinArAyaNa kESavAcyutakRuShNa narasiMha vAmana namOnamO
murahara padmanABa mukuMda gOviMda naranArAyaNa namOnamO
Caranam 2nigamagOcara viShNu nIrajAkSha vAsudEva nagadhara naMdagOpa namOnamO
