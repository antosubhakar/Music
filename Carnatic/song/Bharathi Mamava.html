  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhArati mAmava krpayA nata janArtti bhAra haraNa niratayA
AnupallaviSArada vidhumaNDala sadrSa manOharamukhi
Caranam 1vAsavAdi suravinutE daraNi Sata bhAsura bhUshaNa lasitE hAsajita kundavitatE
vimala muktA-hAra kaNThi gajEndra gatE dAsa bhUta jana vidyA dAnalOla
paradEvi bhAsura candana mrgamada kusuma su-vAsita gAtri su-pAvana SIlE
Caranam 2nAradAdi manOnilayE bhuvanatraya nAyikE krtAgjnyA nilayE cAru bAhu
dhrta valayE vikaca sArasAkshi tOshita bhUvalayE mAra kArmuka sushamAcOra
cilliyugE vANi vArija bhava dayitE varavINA vAdana lOla karAGguLI jAlE
Caranam 3sakalAgama maya rUpE nikhilalOka janani sudhA madhuralApE akaLaGka
guNa kaLApE karuNArasa hata vivaSa jana vilApE sakalE padmanAbha pada sarasija
sEvakavara Suka sanakAdi munISvara vinutE sOma kalA sadrSAmala phalE
