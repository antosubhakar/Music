  Lyrics - English:

PallaviEmani vEgintunE SrI rAma rAma
AnupallaviEmani vEgintun(e)ntani sairintu
nA muddu dEvuDu nanu bAsen(a)yyayyO (Emani)
Caranam 1pAlinci lAlinci palumAru kaugilinci
tElinci nanu para dESi sEya tOcenO (Emani)
Caranam 2ADina muccaTa nAd(a)ntarangamu niNDan-
(I)Du lEd(a)ni(y)uNTin(i)ndAka sarivArilO (Emani)
Caranam 3eDa bAyaka tyAgarAjun(E)lu SrI harini tolli
baDalikal(A)rci nA ceyi paTTinadi talacucu (Emani)
