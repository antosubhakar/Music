  Lyrics - English:

Pallavienna pizhai seydEnO annaiyE innamum varAdirukka innaruL tarAdirukka nAn
Anupallavicinna vayadil ariyAmal Seydadai annai nI maravAdiruttal azhagO
Caranamnalladu polladu nAn ariyEn un nAmam inRi vEronRum aRindilEn
allum paghalum unnai azhaippadai ariyAyO Adi parASakti akhilANDa nAyaki
