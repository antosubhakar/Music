  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhagavAnai ninaindu pADa vENDum bhaktiyuDan pADa aruL seyya vENDum
AnupallaviiraNDaDi mun nI enniDam vandAl IriraNDaDi mun nAn varuvEn enakkURum
Caranambhartiyil avataritta satya sAy bAbA paramAtma svarUpA nin krupai vENDum
abhayam karattil irundu aruL mazhai pozhiya vENDum Atma Suddhiyai enakku tandiDa vENDum
