  Lyrics - English:

pallavinIkeppuDu daya vaccunO
yaninE gAci yunnAnu rAma
anupallavisAkEta vAsa - mandahAsa
sakala jagannivAsa - nannu brOva
caraNamsamayamu jUci telpu vArevarO
jalajAsana nuta - janakajA nAtha
vimala hRdaya - O sadAshiva nuta
suma shara janaka - sArvabhauma rAma
