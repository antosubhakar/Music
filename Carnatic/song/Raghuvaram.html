Pallaviraghuvaram rAghavam rAmam jalajApadharakaram
AnupallavirAkEndu bhaktam ramaNIya sucaritra pAtram
Caranamdinakarakula kamala nibhAkram dIna sharaNyam varENyam dhIrAgrakanyam
mAnavarUpAm pAlita(mAnita?) muni manOharam
dAnava bhanjana dhrta daNDakAraNyam
Madhyamakala sahityamparandhAmam lOkAbhirAmam pAvana rAmam- shrIkaram shUram bhajEham shrIvatsAngadharam
