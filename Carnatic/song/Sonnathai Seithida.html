  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisonnathai seithida sAhasamA kiLiye
kannanenum murai kandanidalAmA
(rAgA: vandanadhAriNi)
AnupallavimaNNum ponnum indha mAya lOgam ellAm
mAdhavan mayamAgi mathiyay mayakuthadi (sonnathai)
(rAgA: vandanadhAriNi)
Caranam 1thannilai thadumAri tavikkurEnaDi
kannan kannan enRu kadarugirEnaDi
veNilavu vizhum vetta veLi thannil
annavanenakoru Asai thandAradi (sonnathai)
(rAgA: jayantaSrI)
Caranam 3kanni mAdam thannil kavalgalai mIri
enna thunichaludan ennidam vandAradi
kaNmaNiye unnai kANave vandEniGgu
minnoLiye unthan mEni enRAdadi (sonnathai)
(rAgA: dEvamanOhari)
Caranam 3ennenamo solli ennai mayaki vittu
annam pAlum verukka alaiya vaittAraDi
mannavandha varku mainthan gOpAlan
kannam vaithu enthan karuthil pugunthAradi (sonnathai)
(rAgA: mAND)
