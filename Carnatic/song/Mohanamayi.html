  Lyrics - English:

PallavimOhanam-ayi tava muraLI-gAnam ahO! mOdayati kam iha na bhuvanE
AnupallavidEha-vijita -mAra! divya-mAnusha-vEsha !!
Caranam 1nIrasOpi viTapI niSamanEna tarasA cAru kisalayitO jAyatE nava iva
sAraGga-tatirapi SAntOlapa-kabaLA nIrajAksha! bhavati nibhrta citragatEva (mohanam ayi)
Caranam 2matta-vAraNa-vara-mastaka-bhEdanE datta-mati:+api+Ishha tarasA kila kEsarI
cittagata-mOhana cira-vismrta-BhAvOtha! sattama nivasati sAdhur-bata! tatraiva (mohanam ayi)
Caranam 3parama-purusha! jaDa-pAshaNa-nikarOpi paritO dravati hanta! parikarNanEna
taruNI-janamOhakam tadiha samajanIti paramadya kimu citram padmanAbha murArE!(mohanam ayi)
