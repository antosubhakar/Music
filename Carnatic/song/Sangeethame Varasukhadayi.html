  Lyrics - English:

pallavisangItamE varasukhadAyi sa ri ga ma pa dha nI susvara
anupallavimangaLadAyI manasuku hAyI manjuLa nava mOhana muraLI
caraNamrAgamulO anurAgamu dAnamulO varadAnamu
