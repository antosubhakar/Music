  Lyrics - English:

pallaviidi samayamu brOva rAdA yadukula tilaka nI
anupallavisadaya hrdaya sarvESa madana kOTi sundara
caraNamkaruNa jEsi munu nIvE kari rAjuni brOvalEdA murahara mandara giridhara
muraLidhara SrIkara parama purusha vAsudEva garuDa gamana gAnalOla
