PallavisambO mahAdEva saraNam srI kALa hastIsa
AnupallaviampOja campavanum anpAna mAyavanum
aTi muTi kANA neTumalai vANA
akila puvana paripAla cakala varakuNa visAlA (campO)
Caranam 1aRiyEn, cinnaJ ciRiyEn unakkanantam taNTaniTTEn
aparAtaGkaL muzhutum kshamittaruLvAy kai kumpiTTEn
parivAy un col kanavil kaNTu pizhaikkum vazhi toTTEn
pEtaiyAkilum un pAtam paNiyum en perukiya
pavavinai tIrum, kuru paranE karuNaik kaNpArum (campO)
Caranam 2AtiyE oru pEtamillAta pOta nirkuNa rUpA
aTiyavar uLLam kuTikoNTa kuru vaTivAna JAnatIpA
vEta purANa cAttiram ellAm Otum perum pratApA
viNNavar munivar kiGkarar kantarvar
vENTit tozhum tAmarai pAta ANTavanE kALattinAtA
