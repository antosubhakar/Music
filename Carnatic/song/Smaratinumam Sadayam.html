Pallavismarati nu mAm sadayam yadupathi smarati nu mAm sadayam yadupati
Caranam 1bAlyasahAyam krpanamadhIram bhUrivivaSahrdayam yadupati
Caranam 2paSyati kim mAm gatamiha savidhE hAbata madhujidayam yadupati
