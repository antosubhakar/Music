  Lyrics - English:

pallavirAja rAja gOpAla (SrI) bAla vraja rAja tanaya anurAgamudita maya rAsa naTana vraja nArI sahita (mama)
anupallavitEja SyAmaLa vilOla vilOcana tribhuvana prakaTita kAraNa SarIra muraLIdhara karAngulI naTana jAla
caraNamSrI ramaNa vaSAtIta gOvardhana sAnudESa vipina sanchAra ati kOmaLa lOlana vAsantI mAgati mAlati jAti mAlikAdhara
vilAsa vraja jana samAgama naTana mAlasa Ayuta kaiSOra vAsita rallaka rucira kanakamaya vAsa marmara calana gambhIra
