  Lyrics - English:

PallavisArasa lOcana mAmava bhO dayayAmalatam sarasijanAbha
AnupallavinIraja Sara sundara nikhila vibudha muni bhayahara
Caranam 1pApa samUha vibhaJjana pArvika sOma samAnana-mApa nivArita dAnava mAnya guNAti manOhara
Caranam 2bhOgi varENya vAsa vibhO kamalAana pUjita nAga bhayApaha pAvana nAma nikAya murAntaka
Caranam 3SEshapurISvara mAdhava sEvaka maGgaLa dAyaka
bhAshaNa vijita sudhArasa paTala bimba phalAdhara
