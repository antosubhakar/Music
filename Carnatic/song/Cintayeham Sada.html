  Lyrics - English:

PallavicintayEham sadA citsabhAnAyakam cintitArthadAyakam jIvESvara bhEdApaham
cintAmaNi svarUpam tANDavESvaram SAntam munimahitam sa guruguham Sankaram
santatam sAmbhavISam mudA bhAvayE anantakOTi brahmANDa nAtham vimalam
Adi madhyAntavarjam nirAdhAramAkASa nAdAntaatham sundaram sudhAmSumauLim Sivam
bhrAntivAraNa nipuNam bhAnukOTi bhAsvaram danti carmAmbaram dhanadahitam
bhAratIkAnta harinutam karadhrta mrgamEkAnta hrdayE SivakAmasundarIpatim
