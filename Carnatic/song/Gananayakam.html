  Lyrics - English:

PallavigaNa nAyakaM bhajE(a)haM bhajE
kamalESa nutaM kAma ripu sutam
AnupallaviaNimAdi siddhi dAyakaM su-mukham
CaranamvANI ramaNAnandaM varadaM
