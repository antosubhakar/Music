  Lyrics - English:

PallaviaDaikkalam pugundEn aiyanE-ennai
Adarit taruLvAy meyyanE-ninpAl
(aDaikkalam)
AnupallavikaDaikkaN nOkki undan kamalapA danizhal
kiDaittiDavum munvinaip poDi paDavum eNNi
(aDaikkalam)
CaranamkaDantiDa muDiyAda kaDaliDai enai mITkak
kappalAy vandu nI kai koDuppAy
kanaliDai vendu nAn tuDittiDum pOdaGgE
kAr mazhaiyAyp peyden tuyar tuDaippAy
naDantiDa muDiyAmal tavittiDum pOtendan
naTRuNaivan Agi enaiyeDuppAy
nalivuRum paciyAl nAn alaivuRum pOdellAm
valiya vandamu tUTTip pasitavirppAy enRE !!
(aDaikkalam)
