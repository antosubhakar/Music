  Lyrics - English:

pallavisatyamEdi nityamEdi satyamuga delpumA O satya dEvA
anupallavisatyamA mA bhuvi nityamA nIdivi satyamEdO nIvI bhuvini pratyakshamai vivarimpumA
caraNam 1kalla kapaTamu mA jIvitamu ellalEni mA Ipsitamu alla kallOlamau praLayamu ElakO O satya dEvA
caraNam 2andamE mAkAnandamu manda hasita mukhAra vindamu bindugAni muraLIgAnamu andivva mundaDugu vEyumu
