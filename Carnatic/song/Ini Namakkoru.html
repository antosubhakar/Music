  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviini namakkoru kavalaiyum illai enRum inbamE manamE
Anupallavipunita tyAkarAja svAmi nam kanavil vantu kAkshi tantatAl (ini)
Caranam 1kantarva kAnalOla kanasIla tyAkarAja
cuntarar cEvai kaNTa nam cukIrttamE cukIrttam
Caranam 2rAka kavirAja tyAkarAja mahArAjan
vAkatAkavE vantu kantanaruL tantatAlE pAlan pAlE
Caranam 3pAvala kuJcaratAca nAvalan nAm tyAkarAja
kAvalan aruLum peTROmE cEvalaGkoTiyOn aruLAl
