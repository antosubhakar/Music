  Lyrics - English:

Pallavigauri sukumAri Sankara nAri
Madhyamakala sahityammanasi mE vasatu SaSadara kalAdari talOdari
AnupallavimA ramaNa sOdari bhAsvari
dhIra hita kari dhI rahita dari
Madhyamakala sahityamvIra kumAra gaNESvara jananI ghOra danuja sancaya mada haraNI
sArasa vadanA amita madanA hIra maNI ramaNIya susadanA
Caranamkara dhRta Sara jAlA suSIlA
dharaNI dhara bAlA
sura nara kinnara garuDOraga nutA
hara hRdaya vihRti lOlA
Madhyamakala sahityamparamEswarI SaNkari sadguNa vallari sarasa karuNA lahari
