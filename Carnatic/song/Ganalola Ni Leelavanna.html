  Lyrics - English:

PallavigAnalOla nI lIlalenna jAlarA muraLI (gamanAshrama)
AnupallavimAnita suguNAlavAla mAnuta caraNA muraLI
Caranam 1vankalenna mAninA vankagAnarA akaLanka vijita rAkA shashAnka mOhana muraLI (ahIrbhairav)
Caranam 2kEshamu-larpincu bhakta klEshamula nAsimpa jEsi Asincina phalamulannIkai sEyumurA muraLI (AbhOgi)
Caranam 3sapta girulamIda nIvu guptamugA dAginAvu triptitO smarinci nI AptuDaitirA muraLI (mAyAmALavagauLa)
Caranam 4sthAna balimi nIku nI AsthAna balimi nAku dEvasthAna
mAye hrdE sAmagAna mosagitivi dEva dEva dEva dEva muraLI (madhyamAvati)
