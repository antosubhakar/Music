  Lyrics - English:

PallaviaravindAlayE tAyE SaraNu hokkenu kAyE ssiri ramaNana priyE jaganmAtE
Caranam 1kamala sugandhiyE kamaladaLa nEtreye kamala vimala SObhitE
kamanIya hastapAda kamala virAjitE kamalE kAyE ennanu
Caranam 2ninna karuNA kaTAksha vIkshaNadindali tanumanagaLanitte
dhanya virAjitE aja bhavAdigaLa prasannE kAyE ennanu
Caranam 3hari ninna uradalli dharisidanembantha karuvadi mereyadire
nirata ninnaya muddu purandara viTTalana caraNa kamalava tOrise
