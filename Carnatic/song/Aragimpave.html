  Lyrics - English:

PallaviAragimpavE pAl(A)ragimpavE
AnupallaviraghuvIra janakajA kara pavitritamau venna pAl(Ara)
CaranamsAramaina divy(A)nnamu shaD-rasa yuta bhakshaNamulu
dAra sOdar(A)dulatO tyAgarAja vinuta pAl(Ara)
