PallaviSrI madhurAmbikE SrI SivE avAva
AnupallavisOmasundrESvara hRdaya vilAsini kAmagiripIThAdhivAsini kalyANi
CaranamkAdi hAdi sAdi mantra rUpiNi kauLini kaivalya dAyini guruguha janani kadamba kAnana vilAsini kAtyAyani madhura vANi SukapANi aLivEni

  Lyrics - Tamil:
