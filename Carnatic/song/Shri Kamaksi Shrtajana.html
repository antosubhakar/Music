  Lyrics - English:

pallaviSrI kAmAkshi Srtajana rakshaki SrI cakra rAjESvari amba
anupallaviEkAmrESa hrdayESvai gauri muknugraha mukti pradAyini
caraNam 1candraSEkharArdhAnga SarIriNi candra kalAdharE sarva jIvESvari
mantra yOga pITha madhya nivAsini mAdhava sOdari mAmava Subhakari
caraNam 2bhavabhaya hAriNi pApa samhAriNi Siva sati cintAmaNi dvIpa vAsini
navarasa pUrita nAda svarUpiNi nava AvaraNa pUjA (A)nanda manOnmaNi
caraNam 3mrdula kara kamala vINA vAdyE Srutinuta vEda vEdAnta vEdyE
prthvI kshEtra kAnci EkAdhipatiyE krtamidam kIrtanam kincit ArAdyam
