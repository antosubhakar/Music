  Lyrics - English:

PallavipAhi pAhi bAla gaNapatE bhava suta guNanidhE
AnupallavidEhi mudam SRtajana patE sindhura mukha varada sumatE
Caranam 1bhakta cidambara vinuta bAla nayana phaNI sahita
Suddha hRdaya sujana hita sura jana gaNa pApahata
Caranam 2mUshika vAhana catura mOdaka dhara kamala kara
pOshita munijana nikara bhUsura pUjita bhavahara
Caranam 3ghaTa muni vandita caraNa kAruNyAmbudhi karaNa
naTana catura nAgAbharaNa raJjita rajanIca kiraNa
