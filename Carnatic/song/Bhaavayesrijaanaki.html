PallavibhAvayE SrI jAnakI kAntam bhU_ri karuNA sAgaram
Anupallavibhava ghanatara SOka bhaJjana ruciyutam kuvalayadaLa sama kamanIya lOcanam
Caranam 1kOTi kandarpa cAru kAnti sama lasitam niTila taTa bhAsita nirupama lalAmam
