  Lyrics - English:

Pallavishikhi vAhanA dEva sEnApatE
Anupallavisukha rUpa ambikAbAlA mUlA aghanAshiva upadEsha IshA
CaranamskandA shaNmukhA kArtikEyA guhA gaNanAtha sOdarA shrIkarA
Isha sutA harikEshapura vAsA dAsula brOcE bhUsurA varadA
