  Lyrics - English:

PallavidhiranA nAdiranA tanOmtana
AnupallavidhiranA nAdira tillAna diranA tillAna diranA tillAna diranA
Caranamdhru tillAna dhru dhru tillAna tillillAna
(tisram)
tadiGgiNatOm tadiGgiNatOm takatiku tadiGgiNatOm
mayakkum muraLI tillAna iSaikku viyakkum vaNNam tai dhittit tai
nAnADuvEn kaNNA vA nI kaNNA vA vA
