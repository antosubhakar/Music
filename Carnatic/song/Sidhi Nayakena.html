pallavisiddhi nAyakEna saphalI krtO vara
anupallavisiddhE dEvAdi prasiddhanutEna krpAkarE
caraNamsakala suguNAkarENa Sankara priya sutEna a-sahanAdi durmArgacara mardanEna
