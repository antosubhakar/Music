  Lyrics - English:

PallavitA dhirana tana dhirana dhIm tiranA tillAnA tA dhirana tana dhirana tirana titana dhIm
AnupallavinAdhru tani nAdhru tani nAdhru tanidhA jhomtari tajhaNutA jhomtari tajhaNutA jhomtarita jhaNu
CaranamAnanda naTamADum naTarAjan sabhEshan ponnambalat-tarashanai pugazhndu pADuvOm
SA tadingiNatOm pA tadingiNatOm tAkata jhamtari dhA sA tadingiNatOm tadingiNatOm
