  Lyrics - English:

PallavirAdhA samEta krshNa (jaya)
AnupallavinandakumAra navanIta cOra brndAvana gOvinda murArE
CaranamgOpi manOhara gOkula vAsa
SObita murali gAna vilAsA
sundara manmata kOTi prakASA
