  Lyrics - English:

PallaviPaGkaja Lochana PAhi Murantaka
PaGkajAlaya Jiva Natha Vibho Vibho !
AnupallavishhaGkara Vidhivala shhasana Nutha Guna
shhaGkuru shathatham Me shArasa Nabha shhaure!
Caranamshundara Tara _rupa Nandita Bhira NAri
Vrinda Mukha sharasIruha Madhupa
BrindAvanAntha Krutha Vividha shukha VihAra
Kundani Bharatha Govinda Nanda Nandana
