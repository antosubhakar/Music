  Lyrics - English:

PallavinIlakaNtha mahAdEvA nikhila lOka jagat rakSakA
Anupallavinitya kalyANa mangaLa svarUpA nityAnanda svarUpA
CaranamnIlAyatAkSi manOvilAsA nirahankAra rAga kuvalayabharaNA kAla kaNTishvarA
parama krpAkarA panca lingEshvarA jyOtirmaya kAmakOTi pITha kAmAkSi mAmava

  Lyrics - Tamil:
