  Lyrics - English:

pallavirAnidi rAdu sur(A)surulak(ai)na
anupallavipOnidi pOdu bhU-surulak(ai)na (rAnidi)
caraNamdEv(E)ndruniki su-dEhamu pUrva
dEvulak(a)mrutam(a)bhAvamE kAni
