  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhairavipatE pashupatE pAhi mAm parama krpAnidhE
Anupallavishauridhi ninjAdi sannuta caraNa gaurImanOharaNa kanaka sabhAhUna
Caranamharahara shiva shiva jaya jaya shambhO adbhuta tANDava Ananda samba
puraharavara nAgadhara dInabandhO bhuvanAdIshvara bhakta sukha sindhO
