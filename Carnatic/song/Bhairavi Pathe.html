  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhairavipatE paSupatE pAhi mAm parama krpAnidhE
AnupallaviSauridhi niJjAdi sannuta caraNa gaurImanOharaNa kanaka sabhAhUna
Caranamharahara Siva Siva jaya jaya SambhO adbhuta tANDava Ananda samba
puraharavara nAgadhara dInabandhO bhuvanAdISvara bhakta sukha sindhO
