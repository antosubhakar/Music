  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisharavana bhava enum tirumandiram tannai
sada japi yen naavE
(Om sharavaNa bhava)
Anupallavipuram erita paraman neTrikaNNil udita
bOda swaroopan porpaadum tannai paNindu
(sharavaNa bhava yanum)
CaranammaNNmisai kiDanduzhal piravi pinniyai teerkum
maayai agala pErinba neriyil sErkum
tannmaDi nigarkuzhal karuNai nilavu
pozhi priya saDaakshara paavana
(sharavana bhava yanum)
