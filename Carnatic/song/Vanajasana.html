  Lyrics - English:

pallavivanajAsana vinuta nata varadAyaki SrI lalita
anupallavikanakAdri nivAsini nanu kaTAkshincumu kAmAkshi
svara sAhityamtAmasamikanu salupa tagAdu vinumu kAmita phalamu losaguTaku samayamu
purAri ripuniki varamulu birAna nosagitivi yanucu vini nE nI padamula goliciti
caraNam 1kalAbharaNa kalAdhari sakalAgama sannuta amba kalushArti
bhaya vibhanjani ilalOpala nI valanE galadA SrI lalitAmbilkE
caraNam 2parAku salupa rAdikanu parA vinu ninnuvinA parulevvaru
nannu brOcuTaku karudA karuNAkari SrIkari ninnE nera nammiti
caraNam 3sadA dalacina dAsulakunu dAsuDa Sankari madanAri sati
kumAruni muda sampada kAspadamai nadiga nI pada padmamu
