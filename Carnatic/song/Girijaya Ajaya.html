  Lyrics - English:

PallavigirijayA ajayA abhayAmbikayA
girISa jAyayA rakshitOham
AnupallaviharihayAdi devatArAdhitayA
Atma svarUpa prabhOdhitayA
harihara guruguha sammOditayA
AdikshAnta varNa vEditayA
Caranamsamaya viSEsha niryANamukha bIja nirbIja dIkshA vishaya karaNa
samayAcaraNOpayOga pUjA sAmagrI saGgrahAntahkaraNa
samaya mata pratipAdyAnusaraNa sAmAnya viSEsha prajAcaraNa
mamatva nirAkaraNa kriyAyuta sadbhaktAnAM dharmArttha rUpa
samatva vRtti vitaraNa nipuNatara sAmrAjyapradA ruNa caraNayA
