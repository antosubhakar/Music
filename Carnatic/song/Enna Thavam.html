  Lyrics - English:

pallavienna tavam seytanai yasOdA
engum niRai parabirammam ammA enRazhaikka ||
anupallaviIrEzhu puvanankaL paTaittavanai
kaiyilEnti cIrATTi pAluTTi tAlATTa nI ||
caraNam 1canakAtiyar tavayOkam ceytu varunti cAtIttatai
punitamAtE eLitil peRa ||
caraNam 2piramanum intaranum manatil poRAmai koLLa
uralil kaTTi vAypotti kencavaittAy kaNNanai ||
