  Lyrics - English:

PallavichintayAmi sadA gouri sutam
chitrAmbari priyam Ekadantam
Anupallavisiddhiryutam nitya phaladAyakam
ShObhitam amita karunArasa mayam
CaranammUladhAram mOdaka hastam
