  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisakala lOkAdhAra saccitAkAra prakaTitAgama jAla padmAvilOla
Caranam 1agaNitaguNa grAma AtmAbhirAma mrgamadAGkita phAla mitrAnukUla
Caranam 2nagarAjavara dhIra nAnA vihAra khagarAjavAha nirgata bhavamOha
Caranam 3vikaca vArija nEtra vispuradgAtra SakalitAsura jAla SAnyEka mUla
Caranam 4bhagavan bhayApara bhakta mandAra nigama SIrshArAma nityAptakAma
Caranam 5sakala sannutaguNa sAdhu santrANa makaradhvajAkAra maJjuLa hAra
Caranam 6kalita kaustubha SObha kALamEghApa bala bhadra krtakhEla bAlagOpAla
Caranam 7madana gOpAla sanmAnita SIla mudam Ehi dEhi sura muni brnda vandya
Caranam 8sArasAksha mukunda saccidAnanda dhIra nArAyaNa tIrtha sugIta

சரணம் 1அகணிதகுண க்ராம ஆத்மாபிராம ம்ர்கமதாங்கித பால மித்ரானுகூல
