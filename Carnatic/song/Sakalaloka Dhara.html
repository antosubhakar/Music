  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisakala lOkAdhAra saccitAkAra prakaTitAgama jAla padmAvilOla
Caranam 1agaNitaguNa grAma AtmAbhirAma mrgamadAnkita phAla mitrAnukUla
Caranam 2nagarAjavara dhIra nAnA vihAra khagarAjavAha nirgata bhavamOha
Caranam 3vikaca vArija nEtra vispuradgAtra shakalitAsura jAla shAnyEka mUla
Caranam 4bhagavan bhayApara bhakta mandAra nigama shIrSArAma nityAptakAma
Caranam 5sakala sannutaguNa sAdhu santrANa makaradhvajAkAra manjuLa hAra
Caranam 6kalita kaustubha shObha kALamEghApa bala bhadra krtakhEla bAlagOpAla
Caranam 7madana gOpAla sanmAnita shIla mudam Ehi dEhi sura muni brnda vandya
Caranam 8sArasAkSa mukunda saccidAnanda dhIra nArAyaNa tIrtha sugIta

சரணம் 1அகணிதகுண க்ராம ஆத்மாபிராம ம்ர்கமதான்கித பால மித்ரானுகூல
