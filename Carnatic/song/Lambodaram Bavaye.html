  Lyrics - English:

PallavilambOdaram bhAvayE skanda sOdaram sAmbashiva kumAram shubham karam sAdaram
AnupallavimahIta pUrvApaNAkAram vishvAdhAram madhura nivEdyarasa talparam parAtparam
CaranamsarvAnta bharaNa nipuNam sadA sharaNam sadaya varadEkSaNam sAdhu samrakSaNam
vAraNa vadanam paripAlita shivagaNam vana tulsI bhUSaNam vighna nivAraNam
