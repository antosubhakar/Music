caraNam 1aMdariki nitaDepO aMtarAtmuDanucu- | naMduritaDO mariyu navalaDokaDO |
naMdakadharuDu jagannAthuDaccutuDu gO- | viMduDItaDu gAka vErokaDu galaDO ||
caraNam 2tanara niMdariki jaitanyamosagina yAta- | Donara nitaDO mariyu nokaDu galaDO |
dinakarashatatEjuDagu dEvadEvuDu ta- | dvinutuDitaDu gAka vErokaDu galaDO ||
