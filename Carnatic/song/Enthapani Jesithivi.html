Pallavienta pani jEsitivi rAmA ninnmandu sAravabhauma rAma pantamA
AnupallavinAmIda parama pAvana nAmA santOsha mudipitivi sakala sadgaNadhAmA
Caranam 1ninnE daivambanucu nammi rAma tinnaga duhkamula jimmi rAma
kanna dinamani nammi ninnu sEvimpaga nanniTlu naTTEta muJcuterugAka pOti
Caranam 2annanna mATADavera nI kannulanu nanu jUDavera cinnElanniyu
darigiyunna cinnaNNa krannaNNa nanu jUDu manna rAmaNNa
Caranam 3bhadrAdri vAsuDE yanucu mammu nirupadravamuga nundu manucu bhadranuta
karuNA samudrayO SrI rAma bhadra ninnE madini bhadramuga nammitini
