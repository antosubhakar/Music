  Lyrics - English:

Pallavishakti gaNapatim bhajEham sarva saubhAgyapradam
Anupallavirakti bhakti bhAva sahitanija gAna kalAdhAram rasa lalitA trishati nAma kIrtanAnugrahaparam
CaranambhAnu kOTi samkAsham pApaharam paramEsham dAna vakula haraNacaNamsakala vighna
nivAraNam mAnita mUSika vAham vashya mOhana dEham dIna bAAla harikEsha divya sundara kumAram
