  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSakti gaNapatim bhajEham sarva saubhAgyapradam
Anupallavirakti bhakti bhAva sahitanija gAna kalAdhAram rasa lalitA triSati nAma kIrtanAnugrahaparam
CaranambhAnu kOTi samkASam pApaharam paramESam dAna vakula haraNacaNamsakala vighna
nivAraNam mAnita mUshika vAham vaSya mOhana dEham dIna bAAla harikESa divya sundara kumAram
