  Lyrics - English:

pallavijagadISa pancaSara sUdana nigamAgama vEdya paripAlaya mAm
caraNam 1yOgijAta hrdayAbha sadanAdbhuta nAgabhUshaNa paripAlaya mAm
caraNam 2pAda vinata bhavakhEda daLana caNa sAdhu sEvita paripAlaya mAm
caraNam 3pura jalada pavana purandarAdi su-pUjita caraNa paripAlaya mAm
caraNam 4girijA su-hrdaya kuvalaya SaSAnka karuNAvAsa paripAlaya mAm
caraNam 5jalajanAbha pada naLina bhajanaradAkhila Subhada paripAlaya mAm
