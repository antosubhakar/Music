PallavibAlE brhatSrshTi mUlE vimalE kapAlISvara mahAdva lIlE
AnupallavijvalE vicitra mAyAjAlE krpAlavAlE mOkshakari kuNDala mAlE kOmalE
CaranamnamOntAdi divyanAmapUjitE navavarNAntagrtavasaratE bhUmipAlanaratE
