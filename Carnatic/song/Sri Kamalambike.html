  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI kamalAmbikE SrI kamalAmbA nA cinta dIrca (vE amba) vE vEgamE rAvE
AnupallavipAkari mukha suranuta pAda nAtO vAdA
Svara sahityamnA pAdAbjamE gati lOkulaku nikhila sAkshi sutuni brOva ninnu vinA
evarilalOna nijamuga paliki nA nidi nigamAgama nuta rAvE
Caranam 1karuNA jalanidhi dharalO ninnu vin gati evarunnArani caraNamu goliciti nA sadA smaraNamu
jEya kumAruni karuNiJci brOva samayamu niratamuga SrI velayupura nivAsini bhava mOcani gAdA
