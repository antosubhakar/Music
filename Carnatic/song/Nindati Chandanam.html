. aviraLa nipathitha madhana sarAdiva bhavadavanAya visAlamsvahrudhaya marmaNi varmakarOdhisajala naLineedhaLa jAlam
. kusumavisikha sarathalpam analpa vilAsa kalA kamaneeyamvrathamiva thava pariramba sukhAya karOthi kusuma sayaneeyam
. vahathi cha gaLitha vilOchana jaladharam Ananakamalam udhAramvidhumiva vikata vidhunthutha dhantha dhaLana gaLithAmrutha dhAram
. vilikhathi rahasi kurangamadhEna bhavantham asamasara bhoothampraNamathi makaram adhOvinidhAya karE sacharam navachootham
. prathipadham idhamapi nigadhathi mAdhavathava charaNE pathithAhamthvayi vimukhE mayi sapadhi sudhAnidhi:apithanuthE thanu dhAham
. dhyAnalayEna pura: parikalpya bhavantham atheeva dhurApamvilapathi hasathi visheedhathi rOdhithichanchathi munchathi thApam
. sree jayadEvabhaNitham idhamadhikam yadhimanasA nataneeyamhari virahAkula vallava yuvathi sakhee vachanam pataneeyam
