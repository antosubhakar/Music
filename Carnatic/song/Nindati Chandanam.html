  Lyrics - English:

pallavi. nindhathi chandanam indu kiraNam anu-vindathi khEthamadhIramvyALa nilaya miLanEna garaLamiva kalayathi malaya samIram sA virahE thavadInAmAdhava manasija visikha bhayAdiva bhAvanayA thvayi lInA
. aviraLa nipathitha madhana sarAdiva bhavadavanAya visAlamsvahrudhaya marmaNi varmakarOdhisajala naLinIdhaLa jAlam
. kusumavisikha sarathalpam analpa vilAsa kalA kamanIyamvrathamiva thava pariramba sukhAya karOthi kusuma sayanIyam
. vahathi cha gaLitha vilOchana jaladharam Ananakamalam udhAramvidhumiva vikata vidhunthutha dhantha dhaLana gaLithAmrutha dhAram
. vilikhathi rahasi kurangamadhEna bhavantham asamasara bhUthampraNamathi makaram adhOvinidhAya karE sacharam navachUtham
. prathipadham idhamapi nigadhathi mAdhavathava charaNE pathithAhamthvayi vimukhE mayi sapadhi sudhAnidhi:apithanuthE thanu dhAham
. dhyAnalayEna pura: parikalpya bhavantham athIva dhurApamvilapathi hasathi viSIdhathi rOdhithichanchathi munchathi thApam
. srI jayadEvabhaNitham idhamadhikam yadhimanasA natanIyamhari virahAkula vallava yuvathi sakhI vachanam patanIyam
