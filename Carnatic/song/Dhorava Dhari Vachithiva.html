  Lyrics - English:

PallavidoravA dari vachchitivA durita
dUra sogasulUra muddAra
Anupallavivara vitaraNamulO sATi lErOi
karAvalambanamulO dorike hAi
CaranammAnavALi miti marachi charinturu
dhyAna vairAgya dUrulayyEru
hIna bhAvanA niratulu muraLI
gAnamerugaru vArala gAvaga
