  Lyrics - English:

pallaviOra jUpu jUcEdi nyAyamA
O ragh(U)ttama nIvaNTi vAniki
anupallavinIraj(A)ksha munu nI dAsulaku
nIk(E)Ti vAvulu telpavE (Ora)
caraNammAnam(i)ncukaina nIku tOca lEkA
pOyina vainam(E)mi puNya rUpamA
dIna rakshak(A)Srita mAnava
santANa gAna lOla tyAgarAja nuta (Ora)

சந்தாண கான லோல த்யாகராஜ நுத (ஓர)
