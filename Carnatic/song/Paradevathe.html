  Lyrics - English:

PallaviparadEvatE saubhAgyavati parASakti rAjarAjESvari amba
Anupallavisura sEvitE sukumAri gauri aravinda nayani Atma rUpiNi
Caranamkara kamala SUli kaumAra valli kAvEri SUzhum tiru mahishur selvi
sarasa SAnta rUpi cAmuNDi kapAli sarva AparaNi phaNi nAyaki amba
