  Lyrics - English:

Pallavi. mAmiyam chalithA vilOkya vrutham idhUnichayEnasAparAdhathayA mayApi na vArithA athipayEna hari hari hathAdarathayA sA gathA kupithEva
. kim kariSyathi kim vadiSyathi sA chiram virahENakim dhanEna janEna kim mama jIvithEna gruhENa
. chinthayAmi thadhAnanam kutilabru kOpabharENasONa padmamivOpari bramathAkulam bramarENa
. thAmaham hrudhi saGgathAm anisam brusam ramayAmikim vanEnusarAmi thAmiha kimvrudhA vilapAmi
. thanvikhinnam asuyayA hrudhayam thavAkalayAmithanna vEdmi kuthO gathAsi na thEna thEnunayAmi
. dhrusyasE purathO gathAgatham Eva mE vithadhAsikim puravE sasambram parirambaNam na dadAsi
. kSamyathAm aparam kadApi thavEdhrusam na karOmidEhi sundari darsanam mama manmadhEna dhunOmi
. varNitham jayadEvakEna harEridam pravaNenathindhu bilva samudhra sambhava rOhiNi ramaNEna
