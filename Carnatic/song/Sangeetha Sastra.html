  Lyrics - English:

PallavisangIta SAstra jnAnamu
sArUpya saukhyadamE manasA
AnupallaviSRngAra ras(A)d(y)akhila sAra
pUrita rAma kath(A)nand(A)bdhi yuta (sangIta)
CaranamprEma bhakti su-jana vAtsalyamu
SrImad ramA vara kaTAkshamu
nEma nishTha yaSO dhanam(o)sangunE
nErpu kalgu tyAgarAju nErcina (sangIta)
