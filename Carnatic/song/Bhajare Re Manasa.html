  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhajarE rE mAnasa shrI raghuvIram bhukti mukti pradam vAsudEvam harim
Anupallavivrjina vidUram vishvAdhAram sujana mandAram sundarAkAram
CaranamrAvana mathanam rakSita bhuvanam ravi shashi nayanam ravijAri mardanam
ravijAdi vAnara parivrtam naravaram ratnahAra parishObhita kandharam
Madhyamakala sahityamravi shashi kuja budha guru shukra shanIshcara rAhu kEtu nEtAram
rAjakumAram rAmam pavanajAptam avanijA manOharam
