  Lyrics - English:

PallavikAraNam vinA kAryam nOtpadyatE kimapi kaJjanAbha niyatam
AnupallavinIrada nIlam manOhara tara mUrtE dhAritA Srtajana dAruNa vividhArtE
Caranam 1janana kAraNam karama jnAna kAraNam vidyA dhanamATOPa kAraNam taruNI mOha kAraNam anayOhAni kAraNam AdhI SOka kAraNam ghana samrddhi kAraNam nanu dharmao jagadISa
Caranam 2taru kAraNam bIjam dhairyam jaya kAraNam parama dainya kAraNam bhavati hIna yAcanA
karuNA kAraNam sAdhu carita SAlitA lOkE taraLatA nikhilApadAmiha kAraNam
Caranam 3SamatamAdi kAraNam sAdhu SAstra nishEvA samasa syApta kAraNam saravrshTi rEvAhO
timirA bhAva kAraNam dinamaNi ramAkAnta mama Sarmaika kAraNam kamalAksha tava pAdau

चरणम् 2तरु कारणम् बीजम् धैर्यम् जय कारणम् परम दैन्य कारणम् भवति हीन याचना
