  Lyrics - English:

PallavidASarathE pAhimAm dayApayOnidhE suguNanidhE
AnupallaviISa vinuta caraNAravinda ina kulAmbudi candra
ISa cApa mada khaNDana durINA sura munigaNa vinuta caraNa
CaranamkuSa lava gAnalOla kusikAtmaja savapAla viSadi krta jagallIla daSaratha
vara bAla paSimita suragaNa vairijAla bhrSamAna tArti haraNa SIla
SaSi nibha mukha hiraNmaya cEla SrISa vAsudEva dayAlavAla
