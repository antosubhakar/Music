pallavipadmAvati ramaNam jayadEva kavirAja bhOja dEva suta padmapAda smaraNam guru mAnasa
anupallaviyatgOpi vadanEndu pada candra cakOram SrI
caraNamkindu bilva sadanam ati ati divya mangaLam vadanam sundarAnga
Subha SObhita madanam sumukhi ramAdEvi priyakara sutanam
