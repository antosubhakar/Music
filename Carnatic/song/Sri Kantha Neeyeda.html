  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI kAnta nI(y)eDa bal(A)tibala
celanga lEdA vAdA
AnupallavipAk(A)ri nuta nIvAri
bal(A)blamunu teliyaga lEdA (SrI)
CaranamkAka daityun(E)ka Saramunan(E)ya
kanjaj(A)stramai paraga lEdA
SrI kara dvijulai dArin(e)ruga lEni
cinta nIku tOcad(E)mi tyAgarAja nuta (SrI)
