  Lyrics - English:

PallavikailAsapatE paripAlayamAm karuNA jaladhE hE pashupatE
AnupallavishailEsha sutAncita vAmAnga kalayE kaluSa timira patanga
CaranamgangAdhara hara varadApAnga angIkrta shUla Damaru kuranga
sangIta kalA rangAntaranga mangaLadA shrI lalitArdhAnga
