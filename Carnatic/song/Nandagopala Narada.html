  Lyrics - English:

pallavinanda gOpAla nArada gAna vilOla SrI
anupallaviramaNIya vadana rAsa vilAsa jaya madana vadana
caraNamanavarata munigaNa pariguNita guNagaNa Ananda lIlA mAdhava madhura madhuratara
gAnadharaNa nIla suguNa karuNAla gambhIra muralInAda catura
