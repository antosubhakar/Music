  Lyrics:

PallavimarivErE gati nAkevaru I mahilOna ninnuvinA amba
AnupallavikarirAjuni karuNinci brOcina karuNAnidhi sOdari gAdA
CharanamduritApa haraNE bhavataraNE hari vAgIshAdi vinuta caraNE
