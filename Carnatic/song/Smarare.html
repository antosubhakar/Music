  Lyrics - English:

pallavismararE citta SaSivadanam sadA sarvalOka pAlakam suranutam
anupallavidharaNIjA patim dhIra cittam vArija nayanam mArajanakam
caraNamsajjana hrdayAbharaNam sadbhakArcita caraNam
vara gaurI manOharanutam sarasa muraLIgAnamuditam
