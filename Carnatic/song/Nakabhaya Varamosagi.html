  Lyrics - English:

PallavinAkabhaya varamosagi nannu brOvavE amba
AnupallavipAkaSAsanAdi vinuta pAvanE rAkA SaSinibha vadanE rAjIva lOcanE
CaranamrAga tALa rasa bhAva rakti bhakti pUrita nAda rUpa sundari nI
kadalanu vini vini nAga Sayana sOdari ninnE SaraNaNTi
