  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviporumaiyai izhaindEn bhuvanEshvariyE bhU maghaL vANiyum pOTriDum mAyE
Anupallaviorumai manamAghi nin arumaippadam paNivOr varumai aghaTriDum perumai unadallavO
Caranamkarmamum jnAnamum teriyAr shiriyAr marumaiyil varum sukham oruvarum ariyAr
urimaiyuDan unadu uyar tiruvaruLai peruvadarkkum tAyE tiramai illaiyE
