  Lyrics:

PallavishrI rAmam sadA bhajEham shrtajana pAlakam sakala dEva pUjitam
AnupallavishrI ramA nAyakam dhIravara dAyakam suri jana bhAvitam sundara hAsa vadanam
CharanamtaraNi kOTi prakAsham muraLIgAna vilAsam parama
munivara manOharam tyAgarAja sannuta shubhacaritam
