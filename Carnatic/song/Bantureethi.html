  Lyrics - English:

PallavibaNTu rIti koluv(i)yy(av)ayya rAma
AnupallavituNTa viNTi vAni modalaina
mad(A)dula koTTi nEla kUla jEyu nija (baNTu)
CaranamrOmAncam(a)nu ghana kancukamu
rAma bhaktuD(a)nu mudra biLLayu
rAma nAmam(a)nu vara khaDgam(i)vi
rAjillun(a)yya tyAgarAjunikE (baNTu)

ராஜில்லுன்(அ)ய்ய த்யாகராஜுனிகே (பண்டு)
