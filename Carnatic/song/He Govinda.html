  Lyrics - English:

PallavihE gOvinda hE gOpAla rAkhO SaraNa hamare
ab tO jIvana hArE
nIr pivanu hEtu(dharanu) gayO sindhu kE kinArE
sindhu bIcha basatu grAha charaNa dharitha pacArE (hE gOvinda)
dvaruka mem sapatu gayo choru bio bhare
saGgu chakra gatha padma karutale cithare (he govinda)
sUr kahE SyAm sunO SarNE tihArE
ab kI bAr pAr karo nandu kE dulArE (hE gOvinda)
