  Lyrics - English:

PallavidASarathE dayA SaradhE
AnupallaviASara haraNa parASara gEya
kav(I)Sa su-hRdaya nivESa ghRNA-kara (dA)
Caranamsarasija nAbha jala dhara SObha
paripAlit(E)bha vara bhakta lAbha
paricara kISa pApa vinASa
taraNi sankASa tyAgarAj(E)Sa (dA)
