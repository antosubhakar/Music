Anupallavicadivi vEda SAstr(O)panishattula
satta teliya lEnidi padaviyA (padavi)
Caranam 1dhana dAra sut(A)gAra sampadalu
dharaN(I)Sula celimi(y)oka padaviyA (padavi)
Caranam 2japa tap(A)di(y)aNim(A)di siddhulacE
jagamulan(E)cuTa(y)adi padaviyA (padavi)
Caranam 3rAga lObha yuta yajn(A)dulacE
bhOgamul(a)bbuTa(y)adi padaviyA (padavi)

tatvamu teliyanid(o)ka padaviyA (padavi)
