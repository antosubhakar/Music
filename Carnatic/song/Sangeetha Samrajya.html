  Lyrics - English:

PallavisaGgIta sAmrAjya saJcAriNi SrGgAra SrGgEripura vAsini
Anupallaviunnata pANDya kEraLa vAsini sannutaSrI cakra madhya nivAsini
kAlaDi SaGkara hrdaya nivAsini kAla pAlaka brahma viSvAsini
CaranamgAndhAra paJcama dhaivata rUpiNi nishAda madhyama sapta svarUpiNi
mandAra kusum maNimaya tEjO mAdhurya mOhanakalyANi svarUpiNi
