  Lyrics - English:

PallavinIrajAkSi nI caraNamulE gati
mArakOTi karuNA rasa mukhi
AnupallavinArada muni janulu smariJcu
SAradE nanAdariJcu
CaranammAlikAbharaNa bhUSaNi Subha
jAlavarALi rAga mOhini
nIla gagana ganaSyAma sOdharI
kAla haraNa SivakAma sundari!
