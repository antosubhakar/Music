  Lyrics - English:

pallavimadhura gAnam madhukara mandArAnudruta
samAna muraleedhara gAnam
anupallavisukhakara pAnam nAna varNa srI kruishNa khEli prapangyam
madahara njAnam mAdhava gItAsAra rucira prasAdam
caraNammurahara mAnam manamOhana gOpihrdayAbhimAnam
subhavara snAnam nAma sankIrtana nityAkhyAnam
madhyamakAla sAhityamsurapura tAnam nArada tumbura nAdaghOsha pradarzanam
bhavahara prANam advaitAmrta rasa puNya praZamsanam
