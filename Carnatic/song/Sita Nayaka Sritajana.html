  Lyrics - English:

pallavisItA nAyaka Srita jana pOshaka
SrI raghu kula tilaka O rAma
caraNam 1niru-pEda bhaktula karikOta paDa lEka
giri pain(e)kkukoNTivO (SrI vEnkaTa giripai) (sItA)
caraNam 2angalArpu jUci ranga puramuna
celangucu paNDitivO (ranga puramuna) (sItA)
caraNam 3kAcina bhaktula jUci(y)A balinE
yAcinci veDalitivO (A balinE) (sItA)
caraNam 4ASa minci ninn(A)Su jErer(a)ni
kISula jEritivO (jErerani) (sItA)
caraNam 5jAlitO vaccu kucEluni kani gOpI
cElamul(e)ttitivO (kani gOpI) (sItA)
caraNam 6ingitam(e)rigi(y)uppongucu brOcu
bangAru dora(v)aitivO (nannu brOcu) (sItA)
caraNam 7nI guNamula guTTu bAguga telisenu
tyAgarAja vinuta (telisenayya) (sItA)

சரணம் 3காசின பக்துல ஜூசி(ய்)ஆ பலினே
