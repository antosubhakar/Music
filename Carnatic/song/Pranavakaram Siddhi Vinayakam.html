PallavipraNavAkAram siddhi vinAyakam prasanna smita vadanam anubhAvayE
AnupallavigaNanAyakam nikhila bhuvana mangaLa varadAyakam
maNigaNa kiraNa nAnavidha kalpa dharaNam lOka kAraNam
Caranam 1ati samvrta shiva gaNa sura gaNa mahatAnanda naTanam dundubi
DiNDima mridanga Damaruha rava dhrimita dhrimita sanacala caraNam
Caranam 2karuNArasa varSaNa mukha kamalam kAtyAyani phalapradam
nandakumAra laptiva sampradam atishaya lambOdara vikaTarAjam amalam
