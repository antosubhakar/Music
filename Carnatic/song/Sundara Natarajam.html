Pallavisundara naTarAja (SrI) SivakAmasundari hrdayESan santatam cintayamAnavarE
Anupallavinandi dEva caNDISAdi vandita munijana hrdayOnava
induSEkhara kuTilAnakavrta kanakara mukhapada mandahAsam
Madhyamakala sahityamhiraNyamaNI nUpurapada kanakAmaNi nava khalakhala nAda sahAsam
SaraNyacara manOlaya vilAsam saccidAnanda kanakasabESam sOma-
CaranamjnAna pOshaNa karAGguLIyana nava nava salita vilAsam
nata pataJjali pAhinI mana laharI krta suvidArtham
gAnamaya gandha pAlisEvita kAshAkAra naTanESam
kaivalya pada dAyaka parama kAruNya cidambarESam
Madhyamakala sahityamhara raha SaGkara gaurISam mrgadhara mandAkini maulISam
suravara sannuta gaGgESam yati sundari jAgyAnandESan niravadhika
