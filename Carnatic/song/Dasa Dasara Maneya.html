pallavidAsadAsara maneya dAsAnudAsa nAnu SrISa SrIranga nimma maneya dAsa
caraNam 1tALudAsara maneya Alu dAsa nAnayya tILudAsanu nAnu tiriyadAsa
bhALAkshmuntAgi bhajipa dEvara maneya Alina Alina AlinaDidAsa nAnu
caraNam 2pankajanAbhana maneya mankudAsanayya kunkudAsanu nAnu kuruDu dAsa
sankIrtaneya mADi neneva bhaktara meneya bankda bAgida kAyva baDadAsa nAnu
caraNam 3halavu dAsara maneya holedAsa nAnayya kulavillada dAsa kunnidAsa
malahara ranga ninna maneya mAdiga dAsa sale mukti pAlisO AdikESavarAya
