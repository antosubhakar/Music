  Lyrics - English:

pallavibhaja mAnasa SrI vAsudEvam nija bhakta bhavAmbudhi bhavyanAvam
anupallaviaja rudrEndrAdi dEva nAyakam gaja rAjEndra mOkshadAyakam
caraNamsatya bhAmAnvitam satya bhAmanvitam satya pAlana niratam santatam
nitya trpta-mangaLam Srta pArijAtam nrtta gIta sakta cittam satatam
