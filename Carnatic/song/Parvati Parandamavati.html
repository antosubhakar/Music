  Lyrics - English:

PallavipArvati parandAmavati pAlita bhArati bhavathu maGgaLam
AnupallavidaridrAdi duhkha dhvamsini dAnava bhaJjani dvija mAlini
Madhyamakala sahityamdInajanArthi mOchani durgAdyakhila Sakti paripAlini
Caranammanda gajagAmini mahA sati madana janaka mudita kAnthimathi
maNi mANikya hIra maya dhyuti mAtaGgi mahESvara yuvati
Madhyamakala sahityamsAma gAna vilOlini sadaya srIvatsa hrdaya vAsini
nAma rUpa vimarSini rAga tALa bhEda vinOdini
