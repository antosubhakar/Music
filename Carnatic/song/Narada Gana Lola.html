  Lyrics - English:

pallavinArada gAna lOla nata jana paripAla
anupallavinIrada sama nIla nirupama guNa SIla (nArada)
caraNamnIvu lEka(y)E tanuvulu niratamugA naDucunu
nIvu lEka(y)E taruvulu nikkamugA molucunu
nIvu lEka(y)E vAnalu nityamugA kuriyunu
nIvu lEka tyAgarAju nI guNamulan(e)Tu pADunu (nArada)
