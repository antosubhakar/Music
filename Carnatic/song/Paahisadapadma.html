AnupallavidEhi tE pada bhaktim dInarakshaka SaurE vAhinISa nandini vadanAbja madhukara
Caranam 1vArivAhi mEcagAtra vica pArijAta SaGkASa nEtra bhAratISa
SaGkara bhadrasannuti pAtra dAritamadhu mukha dAnavAkhila gOtra
