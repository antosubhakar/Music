  Lyrics - English:

PallavivA vElavA vA mAvalavA
AnupallavivA dEvAdi dEvA mahAnubhAvA
CaranamvA marakata mayUravAhana mOhana mA ghana nAganAthA vAsa sadA muda gIta tAnarUpavA madhura kavi kunjaradaasa vivaasa
