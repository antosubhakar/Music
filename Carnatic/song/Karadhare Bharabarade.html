  Lyrics - English:

Pallavikaredare barabarade guru Raghavendra
Caranam 1vara mantralaya pura mandira tava carana sevakaru karava mugidu || ||
Caranam 2haridasaru su-svara sammeladi paravashadali bayteredu koogi ||||
Caranam 3pusharapita kamalesha vithalana dasagresaru ee samayadi ||||
