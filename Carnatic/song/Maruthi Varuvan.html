  Lyrics - English:

PallavimAruti varuvAn maGgaLam taruvAn
vAridhi tANDiyE vaidEhi tuyar tIrkka
AnupallaviyAr edirttiDilum rAmA enRAl
pAr adanil vandu paghai vilakkiDuvAn
CaranamghOra daSamukhan garvam kaLaindavan
nArada munivan tan ANavam tavirttavan
