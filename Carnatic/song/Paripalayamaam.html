  Lyrics - English:

PallaviparipAlaya mAm parama purusha parandhAma
Anupallavisarasija nayana suranuta caraNa mura samharaNa bhavabhaya vAraNa
Caranamsarasa nATakapriya giridhara kali kalmasha hara
kamalA manOhara karuNAkara muraLIdhara gAnalOla
