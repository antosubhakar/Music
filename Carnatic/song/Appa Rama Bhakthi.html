  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviappa rAma bhakti(y)entO gopparA mA(yappa)
AnupallavitrippaTalanu tIrci kaNTi reppa valenu kAcu(n)A mA(yappa)
Caranam 1lakshmi dEvi valacunA lakshmaNuNDu kolucunA
sUkshma buddhi gala bharatuDu jUci jUci solasunA mA(yappa)
Caranam 2Sabari(y)engil(i)ccunA candra dharuDu meccunA
abala svayamprabhaku daivam(a)cala padavin(i)ccunA mA(yappa)
Caranam 3kapi vAridhi dATunA kaliki rOTa kaTTunA
aparAdhi tyAgarAjuk(A)nandamu heccunA mA(yappa)
