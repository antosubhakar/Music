PallavitulasI bilva mallik(A)di
jalaja sumamula pUjala kaikonavE
Anupallavijalaj(A)sana sanak(A)di kar(A)rcita
jalad(A)bha su-nAbha vibhAkara hRj-
jal(E)Sa hariN(A)nka sugandha (tulasI)
