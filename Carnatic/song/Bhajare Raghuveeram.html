  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhajarE raghuvIram mAnasa bhajarE raghuvIram
AnupallaviambudaDimbha viDambana gAtram ambuda vAhana nandanadAtram
Caranam 1krshika sutArpita kArmuka vEdam vashi hrdayAmbuja bhAskara pAdam
Caranam 2kuNDala maNDan maNDita karNam kuNDali mancakam-adbhuta varNam
Caranam 3daNDita sunda sutAdika vIram maNDitam-anukulamAshraya shIlam
Caranam 4paramahamsam-akhilAgama vEdyam parama vEda makuTI pratipAdyam
