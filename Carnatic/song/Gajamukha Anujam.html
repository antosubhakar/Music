pallavigajamukha anujam nitya kalyANa subrahmanyam nityam bhaja bhaja manasA nityam
anupallavivraja rAja tanaya bhAginEyam vidhinuta caraNam bahulEyam vEda nAda praNavAkarabOdham
viSvarUpam akhila stithi nAdam jAta rUpa gEyUra makuTadhaara SaktyAyuda dhAram pratItam
caraNamArAdhita sajana samAgama Ananda bhAshpamEva dhArA sampAda smititha padAmbujam
abhijAtam kshIra varNa bhasmAnkita pAlam madhyAdyuti kumkuma dharam ati SObham nIrada
sama nava nIla yauvana rOkishajA vallI samEtam nirantara naTana mayUra vAham nuta sura
munigaNa sujana samUham sura ripu hara nava vIra vyUham sundarESa vAmAnag rOham
