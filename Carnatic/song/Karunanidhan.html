PallavikaruNa nidhAn kunjkE vihAri tamhAri bansi lAlA mErO manOhari
Charanam 1is bansi sE sur nar muni mOhE mOh gayI sAri braj kI nAri
Charanam 2jab shyAm sundar kA tan dEkhI janam janam kE main sankaT tArI
Charanam 3mOr mukuT pitAmbar sOhE kuNDal kI chabi mai balihAri
Charanam 4dashamaskanda bhAgavat gAvE nakh par gOvardhan giridhAri
