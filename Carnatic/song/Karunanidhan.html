  Lyrics - English:

PallavikaruNa nidhAn kunjkE vihAri tamhAri bansi lAlA mErO manOhari
Caranam 1is bansi sE sur nar muni mOhE mOh gayI sAri braj kI nAri
Caranam 2jab shyAm sundar kA tan dEkhI janam janam kE main sankaT tArI
Caranam 3mOr mukuT pitAmbar sOhE kuNDal kI chabi mai balihAri
Caranam 4dashamaskanda bhAgavat gAvE nakh par gOvardhan giridhAri
Caranam 5padmanAbh prabhu phaNi parashAyi danuja kula haraNa nAth murAri
