  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviraghu vara nannu marava tagunA
Anupallavinaga dhara bhakta jan(A)gha nivAraNa (raghu)
Caranam 1talli taNDrul(a)nna tammul(u)nnA
polatiki(y)okaDu purushuND(au)nA (raghu)
Caranam 2para daivamulu bAgu sommulu
sura nuta mangaLa sUtramul(au)nA (raghu)
Caranam 3cEsina puNya cayamu brahmaNya
Asan(o)sangiti anupama lAvaNya (raghu)
Caranam 4manasuna nIkE marulu-konnAnu
sajjana hita tyAgarAja nuta Subh(A)kara (raghu)
