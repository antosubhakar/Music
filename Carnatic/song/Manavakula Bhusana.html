  Lyrics - English:

PallavimAnavakula bhUshaNa SrI tyAgarAja asamAna saGgIta sAhitya nipuNAgrESa
AnupallavimAnanIya satta citta bhaktulakunaina gAna vidyA kaivalya mosagunanI jupina
CaranamSrI nAradAmSuDai dayatOnavatariJci mAnApamAna dvandva ripulella jayiJci
jnAnArcilO sakala karmamulanu dehiJci SrI nAthuni ellapuDu manasAra bhajiJci
gAnAmrta tApa maNaSa SrI rAmAmrta pAnArNava nI sudhA drshTicE galgina
Madhyamakala sahityamEna nASana nAma jAnakIpati kAma dIna janahita prEma gAnarasAbdhi sOma
