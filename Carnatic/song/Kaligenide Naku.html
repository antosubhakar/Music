  Lyrics - English:

Pallavikaligenide nAku kaivalyam kalakalaamu harikathA shravaNam
Caranam 1Acintya madbhuta mAnandam pracuram divyam pAvanaM
sucaritram shrutishObhitam acalambidivO harikIrtanam
Caranam 2nityamnikha shakaram duritaharam bhavadUram
paramamangaLam bhAvAtItam karivaradam nijakainkaryam
Caranam 3sulabham sukaram shOkanAshanam phaladam lalitam bhavaharaNam
kalitam shrIvEnkaTEsha sharaNa jalajOdara nityastOtram
