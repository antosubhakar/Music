  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSaNmukham SaNmukham bhaja SaNmukham bhaja SaNmukham
Anupallavisharvakam shikhi vAhanam SaNmukham bhaja SaNmukham
munmOcana pramOcanAya SaNmukham bhaja SaNmukham
CaranamsharavaNabhava vibhavam shrtajana sEvita caraNam
kAmAdi SaDviibhudanam sAmAdi vEda kAvyam bhaja SaNmukham
sarvajana mana ranjakam prajnyam praNavArtha dEshikam
guham guruparam shUram shUladharam parAtanam bhavatArakam
bhU SaNmukham bhaja SaNmukham bhaja SaNmukham bhaja SaNmukham
