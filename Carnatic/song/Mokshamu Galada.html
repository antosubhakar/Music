  Lyrics:

PallavimOkshamu galadA bhuvilO jIvan-
muktulu kAni vAraluku
AnupallavisAkshAtkAra nI sad-bhakti
sangIta jnAna vihInulaku (mOkshamu)
CharanamprAN(A)nala sam-yOgamu valla
praNava nAdamu sapta svaramulai paraga
vINA vAdana lOludau Siva manO-
vidham(e)rugaru tyAgarAja vinuta (mOksha)
