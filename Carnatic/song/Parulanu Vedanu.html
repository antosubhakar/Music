  Lyrics - English:

pallaviparulanu vEDanu patita pAvanuDA
anupallavikari varadA nIvE kAnkshatO brOvum(i)ka (paru)
caraNamamara vara nIdu nAm(A)tiSayambE
jIvanamu sEyuc(u)nnAnu SrI tyAgarAja vinuta (paru)
