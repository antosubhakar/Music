  Lyrics - English:

PallavinArAyaNa naLinAyata lOcana namO namah pAhi pAhi lakSmi
AnupallavivArAmIshvara manjuLa talpa vana mAlAdhara mAdhava lakSmi
CaranammangaLakara karuNAkara shaurE vanaruha padayuga dIna sharaNyA
rangapura ramaNa sAmaja varadA rAmadAsanuta shrIdhara lakSmi
