AnupallavimOhana sundara svarUpiNi SaGkari mOdaka kara guruguha bhaktajanAvana SaGkari
CaranampaJcAnana hRdayESvari paramESvara mOhini sarvESvari sarvAnandamaya cakra vAsini vAJchitArttha phala pradAyini vArijAsanAdinuta caraNa naLina sampradAya kulOttIrNa yOgini
