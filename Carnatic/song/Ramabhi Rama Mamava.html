  Lyrics - English:

pallavirAmAbhi rAma mAmava SrI rAma
anupallavirAma ravikula lalAma rAkshasa kula bhImA rAmacandra
suguNa sAndra SrI mAnasAmbudhi candra
caraNampavanAtmaja sampUjita paramAdbhuta nija carita dEvAdi dEva
mAdhava SrI vAsudEva sArvabhauma satyakAma sarvalOka vandya rAma
