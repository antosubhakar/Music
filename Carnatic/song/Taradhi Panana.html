  Lyrics - English:

PallavitArAdhipAnana daSaratha nandana
tAraka rAma jAyE janaka tanayE
AnupallaviapAra pArAvAra tAraNa kAriNi
dharaNi bhavahAriNi [?] dukha nivAraNi
SrutikIrthi samudAyE Sritajana phaladAyE
SravaNa manana dhyEyE SruGgagiri nilayE
CaranamdanDaka kAnana vAsini suvAsini bhaNDAri bhaJjani prabhAva viSvAsini
praCaNDa nAri parASakti svarUpiNi danDadhara daSagrIva nASini naLini
nirvikalpa kaivalya pradAyini mAnini viCitra maNi mAlini viSva vilAsini
kapISAyudAyE khalahara kharaharapriyE karuNAlayE sadA SrIvatsa sahAyE
