  Lyrics - English:

PallavitAm dhIm tarana tAm dhina takiTa
AnupallavitAm dhIm tarana tAm dhit takiTa tikir takiTa tadhiGgiNatOm tAm dhIm tarana tAm
yamunA taTAka paGkEruhapada sArisa sArisa sArisadaLa nayanAyata sAhasa mOhita jagatiha
CaranamtAmita tajjham taka tajjham takatika tajjham tAm niraja taTAkAdhika
mrdula pada nikaTa taraGgOttuGga tADaGga nirataka nirataka tAm
Caranam 1mata bhujaGga Sira pAda yuga pANi dhrta madhuni nAda vENurava
Caranam 2gOpAGganA kula vrta mAdhava madhusUdana hari sammadana
pada naTana takatakana natajana parivrta sadaya muda hrdaya
Caranam 3nirantarAnanda mukha kamalA ananta naTAGga padayugaLA madhu muraLIdhara
harI laharI marIdhi sagIta manOramaNA vraja durandharA jalada SObhamAna dhara
cigura mukuLa makuTa nIla SikhaNDaka mOhanAGgA kALiGga naTana
Caranam 4mada madhukara madhu padaraLa sama nayana kamaladaLa calana muni
hrdayam api cOra cAturA dayAkarA murAti bhIkarA surapati sannuta mOda
vinAyaka naravara gIyamAna SAsanAdi bhUsha gOpabAla vEsha sahacara
