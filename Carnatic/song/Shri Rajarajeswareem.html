  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrI rAjarAjEshvarim sadA bhajarE rE mAnasa shrI lalitAm mahA tripurasundarIm
AnupallavikSIrAbdi sapta sAgarAvrta shrI cakrapuradIshvarIm kSityAdi shivAnta SaD trimshata tva svarUpiNIm sadyupasita shrI vidyA svarUpiNIm dEvagAndhAri santOSiNIm dahashAyinIm
Caranamparama pavitra yadukula shEkhara lIlAvatAra krSNa sahajAtam padma rAga vaiDUrya marakatAdi mahAmaNi
vinhUSitam suravara sampUjita kOmaLakara pAda pankaja samshObhitam surabhi kAmadhEnu kalpavrkSAdi
dEva vaibhavavirAjitAm padmakara mahApITha shObhitAm abhEdyAnanta suguNa bharitAm
