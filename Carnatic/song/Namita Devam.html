  Lyrics - English:

Pallavinamita dEvam bhajE narasimham sumukha karuNEkshaNam sulabha narasimham
Caranam 1vijaya narasimham vIra narasimham bhujabala parAkrama sphuta nrsimham
rajanicara vidaLana virAjita nrsimham sujana rakshaka mahA SUra narasimham
Caranam 2dAruNa nrsimham pratApa nrsimham cAru kalyANa niScala narasimham
dhIra cittAvAsa divya narasimham sAra yOgAnanda catura narasimham
Caranam 3vimala narasimham vikrama narasimham kamanIya guNa gAna nrsimham
amita su-SrI vEGkaTAdri narasimham ramaNIya bhOshAbhi rAma narasimham
