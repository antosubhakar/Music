  Lyrics - English:

PallavicintayAmi santatam SrI muttusvAmi dIkshitam
AnupallaviantakAri suta mantrOpAsana tatparam AnandAmrta varshaNa kArakam varam
apAra rAga layAbhigjna rAmasvAmi suputram parama pavitram sucaritram
CaranamapUrva paJcaliGga navAvarANAdi daiva parakrti kartAram muraLIrava hitam
nipuNam gAna nipuNam dESIya gAna nipuNam mArga dESIya gAna nipuNam
