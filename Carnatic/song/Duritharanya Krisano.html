PallaviduritAraNya krshanmO mAmava sUnO ramaNIyatAnO
AnupallaviparitApa vraja haraNa kaTAkSa parinAtha shashimukha naLinadaLAkSa

parishuddha kAnana tulasIrada pada parijana shubada kaliyuga varada
