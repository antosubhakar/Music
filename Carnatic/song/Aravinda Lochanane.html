  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviaravinda lOcananE karuNai pozhi
annai maruvum nIla mEniyanE
AnupallavinArAyaNA srIman nArAyaNA enRu
piRavip piNi akaTRum tirunAmam uraittEn
CaranampaTTa ciRumai pOtAtO iniyun
pakkattil azhaikkalAkAtO unnai
viTTup pirinta manam vATAtO patam
toTTu vaNaGkum enaik-kaN pArAtO
