PallavipAlaya SrI paJca nadESvara mAm pAlaya dharmAmbikA manOhara
AnupallavibAla nEtra kamanIya gAtra parama pavitra saccaritra
CaranamtApatryAnala vArivAha dAsa janArti jalada gandhavAha
rUpa vijitAyuta madana janaukha mOkshanAtha tripurAsura samhAra

lApa sudhAkara kalAvatamsa lalita bhAsha pOshitAkhila sumanasa
