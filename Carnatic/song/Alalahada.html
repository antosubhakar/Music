  Lyrics - English:

PallaviALalAgAdA aDiyEnai nAthA nI tAn
AnupallaviALAi nin ponnaDikkaDimai pUNDanudinam Arumukha hara guruguha pannIrukara muruga enruruga tan
Caranamdigambari trishUladhari jagam tarum jaTAdhari hATakAmbari harasakhi hari sOdari sundara taru sutanE
aham tanil ninainda nal varam tara ishainda nAyAsa kavi kunjaradAsa vishvAsanai nEshamoDu
