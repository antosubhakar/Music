  Lyrics - English:

Pallavigiri rAja sutA tanaya sadaya
Anupallavisura nAtha mukh(A)rcita pAda yuga
paripAlaya mAm ibha rAja mukha (giri)
CaranamgaNa nAtha parAtpara Sankar(A)gama
vAri nidhi rajanI-kara
phaNi rAja kankaNa vighna
nivAraNa SAmbhava SrI tyAgarAja nuta (giri)
