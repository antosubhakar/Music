  Lyrics - English:

PallavimAruvakAdi mAlinI shUlinI mAmava kalyANaguNashAlini
CaranamcArusmita mukhAmbuja kapAlini jagajjIvEsha guruguha pAlini vArijanayana vilAsini kauLini vAHnchitArthaphaladAyini hamsini
