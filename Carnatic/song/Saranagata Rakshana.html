PallaviSaraNAgata rakShaNa birudani nE SaraNaNTi gadayyA |
veruvaku mani aBayaM bosaNgavE nanu ganna taNDri vayyA ||
Anupallavikari varadA sirulosaganu daSaratha nanDana rAvayyA |
niratamu nI nAmamu nA jIvaku ruci varamayyA ||
Caranam 1narahari bAluni gAcina SrI jagannAtha vina vayyA |
garuDa vAhanu Davai nA kannula ganupiMpa vayyA ||
Caranam 2Adi dEva nI cittamu BAgyamu bAvam evvariDayyA |
nI dAsulakunu nE dAsuDa daya yuNci yElum ayyA ||
Caranam 3hari nI tOTi samAna maina mA yAptulu yeva rayyA |
karuNA sAgaruDa vani moraliDagA kanikariMca vEmayyA ||
Caranam 4kOriti nA daivamu nIvE yanu koMTini gadayyA |
nEramu leMcaka gAraviMci kRupa nElu nallan ayyA ||
Caranam 5muripemuga SrI rAmadAsuDani muccaTa lADu mayyA |
narulanu brOceDi BadrAcalapati nIvu gAdaTayyA ||
Caranam 6mutuloluga rAmadAsuni brova muccaTa lADu mayyA |
mutuloluga rAmadAsu dani muccaTa lADu mayyA |
baKtulani caer cedi vara BadrAcala pati nIvaen naayyA ||
