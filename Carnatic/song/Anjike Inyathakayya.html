PallaviaJjikin yAtakayya sajjanarige Bhayavu Innyakayya
AnupallavisaJjIva rAyara smaraNe mADida mEle
Caranam 1kanasili manasili kaLavaLa vAdare hanumana nenedare hAri hOgade bIti
Caranam 2rOma rOmake kOTi liGgavu ddurisida bhImana nenedare biTTu hOgade pApa
Caranam 3purandara viTTalana pUjeya mADuva guru madhva rAyara smaraNe mADida mEle
