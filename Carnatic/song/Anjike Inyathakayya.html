  Lyrics - English:

Pallavianjikin yAtakayya sajjanarige Bhayavu Innyakayya
AnupallavisanjIva rAyara smaraNe mADida mEle
Caranam 1kanasili manasili kaLavaLa vAdare hanumana nenedare hAri hOgade beeti
Caranam 2rOma rOmake kOTi lingavu ddurisida bhImana nenedare biTTu hOgade pApa
Caranam 3purandara viTTalana pUjeya mADuva guru madhva rAyara smaraNe mADida mEle
