PallaviparamESvara jagadISvara SaGkarapAhimAm praNatArttihara SrI
Anupallavipurahara mRgadhara sundarESvara dharmasamvarddhanI manOhara
CaranampaJcanadISvara gaGgAdharESvara pannagAbharaNa bhaktajanAvana paJcabrahmahatyAdi pApahara para SivatatvArttha bOdhita catura paJcanadakshEtra prabhAkara pAlita guruguha bhavabhaya hara vIra kshEtrapAlanuta caraNa vicitra yamabhayAdi nivAraNa
