  Lyrics - English:

PallavinamO namO rAghavAya aniSaM
namO namO rAghavAya aniSaM
Caranam 1Suka nutAya dIna bandhavE
sakala lOka dayA sindhavE (namO)
Caranam 2Srita durita tamO bahu ravayE
satata pAlit(A)dbhuta kavayE (namO)
Caranam 3nija sEvaka kalpaka taravE
aja rudr(A)d(y)amara su-guravE (namO)
Caranam 4dIna mAnava gaNa patayE
dAnav(A)ntakAya su-matayE (namO)
Caranam 5Ayur-ArOgya dAyinE
vAyu bhOji bhOgi SAyinE (namO)
Caranam 6nUtana navanIta bhakshiNE
bhU-tal(A)di sarva sAkshiNE (namO)
Caranam 7vara gaja kara tulita bAhavE
Sara jita dAnava subAhavE (namO)
Caranam 8nAga rAja pAlanAya
tyAgarAja sEvitAya (namO)
