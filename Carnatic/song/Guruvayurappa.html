  Lyrics - English:

PallaviguruvAyUrappA kuzhandhAi mukundA viraindAL
Anupallavitiruvurai maNimArbA dEvaki suta sarasija nAbhA jaya jaya
CaranamAdi antamum illAdavanE ananta shayana mAdhavanE yAdava kuladhIpa muraLIdhara rAmadAsan paNi caraNA murahara
