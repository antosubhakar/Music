  Lyrics - English:

PallaviMadhava bhavathu they maGgalam,
Madhu mura hara they maGgalam.
Caranam 1Aravinda lochana , agha brunda mochana,
shura brunda vandhitha maGgalam
Caranam 2Nara vara nandana , naghari kethana,
Marakatha mani nIla MaGgalam
Caranam 3Nanda vara kumara , nava nItha dhadhi chora,
Mandhara giri dhara , maGgalam
Caranam 4Kundha radhanamara kUjitha nUpura,
shundara vadana they maGgalam
Caranam 5Dadhi navanItha chora , tharunI GopikA jara,
Madhu kaidabha samhara , maGgalam
Caranam 6Gaja raja paripala , Ghana thulasI mala,
Nija dasa paripala maGgalam
Caranam 7Vruja shundari vilola , Vibhu thendra paripala,
Vijaya Gopala bala MaGgalam.
