  Lyrics - English:

PallaviMadhava bhavathu they mangalam,
Madhu mura hara they mangalam.
Charanam 1Aravinda lochana , agha brunda mochana,
Sura brunda vandhitha mangalam
Charanam 2Nara vara nandana , naghari kethana,
Marakatha mani neela Mangalam
Charanam 3Nanda vara kumara , nava neetha dhadhi chora,
Mandhara giri dhara , mangalam
Charanam 4Kundha radhanamara koojitha noopura,
Sundara vadana they mangalam
Charanam 5Dadhi navaneetha chora , tharunee Gopikaa jara,
Madhu kaidabha samhara , mangalam
Charanam 6Gaja raja paripala , Ghana thulasee mala,
Nija dasa paripala mangalam
Charanam 7Vruja Sundari vilola , Vibhu thendra paripala,
Vijaya Gopala bala Mangalam.
