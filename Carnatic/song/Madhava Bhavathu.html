  Lyrics - English:

PallaviMadhava bhavathu they mangalam,
Madhu mura hara they mangalam.
Caranam 1Aravinda lochana , agha brunda mochana,
Sura brunda vandhitha mangalam
Caranam 2Nara vara nandana , naghari kethana,
Marakatha mani neela Mangalam
Caranam 3Nanda vara kumara , nava neetha dhadhi chora,
Mandhara giri dhara , mangalam
Caranam 4Kundha radhanamara koojitha noopura,
Sundara vadana they mangalam
Caranam 5Dadhi navaneetha chora , tharunee Gopikaa jara,
Madhu kaidabha samhara , mangalam
Caranam 6Gaja raja paripala , Ghana thulasee mala,
Nija dasa paripala mangalam
Caranam 7Vruja Sundari vilola , Vibhu thendra paripala,
Vijaya Gopala bala Mangalam.
