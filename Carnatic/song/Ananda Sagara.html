PallaviAnanda sAgaram(I)dani dEhamu
bhU bhAramu rAma brahm(Ananda)
AnupallaviSrI nAyak(A)khila naigam(A)Srita
sangIta jnAnam(a)nu brahm(Ananda)
CharanamSrI viSva nAtha SrI kAnta vidhulu

bhajiyincE SrI tyAgarAja nuta (Ananda)
