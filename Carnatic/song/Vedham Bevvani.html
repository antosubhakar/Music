  Lyrics - English:

PallavivEdaM bevvani vedakeDivi | AdEvuni goniyADuDI ||
Caranam 1alarina caitanyAtmaku DevvaDu | kalaDevva DecaTa galaDanina |
talatu revvanini danuviyOgadaSa | yila nAtani BajiyiMcuDI ||
Caranam 2kaDagi sakalarakShaku DiMdevvaDu | vaDi niMtayu nevvanimayamu
piDikiTa tRuptulu pitaru levvanini | daDavina GanuDAtani ganuDu ||
Caranam 3kadasi sakalalOkaMbula vAralu | yidivO koliceda revvanini |
tridaSavaMdyuDagu tiruvEMkaTapati | vedaki vedaki sEviMcuDI ||
