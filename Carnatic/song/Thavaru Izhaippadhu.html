  Lyrics - English:

Pallavitavarizhaippadu en iyalbanRO adai tavarAdu kSamippadum un dayai anRO
Anupallavitava munivOr paNI taNikai nAthanE tava neRi ariyAda pEdai nAnE
CaranamdEvar shirai mITTa dIna dayAparA dEva dEvarkkaruL dEshika guruparA
kAval ilA manam kANbadilellAm AvalAy alaindiDa aRindariyAmal pala
