pallavitrivikramam aham bhajE dEvadEvam su-vigraham haram vAsudEvam
anupallavipavitritO balam prathIta vibhavam ravi prabham patanatAnila bhavam
caraNamdharAtmajArccita sarasija caraNam surAni samadana varagaNa caraNam
