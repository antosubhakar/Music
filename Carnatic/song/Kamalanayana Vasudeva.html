  Lyrics - English:

Pallavikamalanayana vAsudEva karivarada mAm pAhi amala mrdula nalina caraNa acyuta mudam dEhi
Caranam 1jAracOra mEru dhIra sAdhu jana mandAra pAra rahita ghOra kaluSa bhava jaladhi vidUra
Caranam 2nAradAdi gAnalOla nandagOpAla vArijAsanAnukUla mAnita guNashIla
Caranam 3kAma janaka shyAmasundara kanakAmbara dharaNa rAmadAsa vandita shrI rAjIvAdbhuta caraNa
