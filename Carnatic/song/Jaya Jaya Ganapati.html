  Lyrics - English:

Pallavijaya jaya gaNapati mangaLa kArin vighna vinAShaka sankaTa hArin
Caranam 1shambhO nandana kalyANakArin gaurIsha nandana jana hitakArin
Caranam 2kAryEvaiham sakala vihArI jaya ati nAyaka mUlAdhArin
(or) yAtOham tA caraNa samIpam jaya ati nAyaka mUlAdhArin
Caranam 3AyE haiham sharaNa tihAri jaya adhinAyaka mUlAdhAri
Caranam 4siddhi buddhi varam ati sancArin saccidAnandA mahO upakArin
