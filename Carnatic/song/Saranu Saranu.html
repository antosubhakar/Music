garva samhara SaraNu vEGkaTa nAyaka svAmi SrI raghunAyaka SaraNu SaraNu
Caranam 1animiSEndrulu munulu tigbatulu amara kinnara siddhulu ghaNatatO rambAti
kAntalu kASinAreccarikayA ninnu koluSinAreccarikayA SaraNu SaraNu
Caranam 2kamaladharuDunu kamala mitruDu kamala SatruDu kamalaju kramamutO
mimu koluva nippuDu gAcinAreccarikayA
Caranam 3paramapada gOvinda mAdhava padmanAbha janArdana dharaNitaravara garuDa
vAhana daitya bali mata bhaJjana nija dAsa mAnasa koluva vacciri
