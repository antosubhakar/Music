  Lyrics - English:

pallavirAmanAma bhajisidavage uNte bhavada bandhana |
kAmaharana satiyu sadA nemadinda bhajisuva SrI ||
anupallaviSivanu dhanyanAda mauni yuvati pAvaneyAdaLu |
raviyasutanu padavi paDeda dhruvanu divijanenisida SrI ||
caraNamkarivarada prahlAda draupadi varavibhIshaNarellaru |
hariya smaraNe mADi sukhabharitarAgalillavE SrI ||
girije rAmamantradinda paramamangaLeyAdaLu |
varada gopAla viThala nAma duritakAnan pAvaka SrI ||
