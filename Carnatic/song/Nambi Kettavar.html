  Lyrics - English:

Pallavinambi keTTavar illavO raGgaiyyA ninna
nambadE keTTarE kEDalI
(nambi)
Anupallaviambuja nAbana akhila lOkESana
abramEya nAda Adi puruSa ninna
(nambi)
CaranamkarirAjana salahi aJja bEDendu
AdariJcidava dArO
garuDa gamana SrI purandara viTThalanA
caraNa kamala vannu driDa din dhanammirO
(nambi)
