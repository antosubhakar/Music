  Lyrics - English:

Pallavinambi keTTavar illavO rangaiyyaa ninna
nambadE keTTarE kEDalee
(nambi)
Anupallaviambuja naabana akhila lOkEshana
abramEya naada aadi purusha ninna
(nambi)
Caranamkariraajana salahi anja bEDendu
aadarincidava daarO
garuDa gamana shree purandara viTThalanaa
caraNa kamala vannu driDa din dhanammirO
(nambi)
