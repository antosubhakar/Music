  Lyrics - English:

PallavivA vElavA tamiyEnai ALa vaLLi deyvayAnai maNavALA
AnupallavidEvE maikaNNan aruLum tI vaNNanE tImai mALa
CaranamtAdavaiven ciRaiyITTavA taDaikku maRai poruL Inda vA
tA tA varam taralAkAdA tALEn rAman marugA murugA
