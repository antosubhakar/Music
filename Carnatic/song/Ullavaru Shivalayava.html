  Lyrics - English:

Pallaviullavaru Sivalaya mAduvaru nAnena mAdali badavanayya,
enna kAle kamba dehave degula Sirave honna kalaSavayya
KUdala shaGgama Deva kelayya sthavarakkalivuntu jaGgamakalivilla
