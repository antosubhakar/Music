paripari SAstravanOdidarEnu vyarttavAyitu bhakuti
Caranam 1Aru SAstrava Odidarenu mUrAru purANava mugisidarenu
sAdu sajjanara saGghava mADade dhIranendu Ta tirugidarenu || ||
Caranam 2koraLoLu mAleya dharisidarenu beraLoLu japa maNi tiruhidarenu
marLi maraLi Ta horaLi budiyolu marulanandadi tirugidarenu || ||
Caranam 3nAriya bhOga aLisidarenu SArrira sukhavanu biDisidarenu
mAra janaka siri purandara viTTalana sEri koNDu tA paDEyuva tanaka || ||
