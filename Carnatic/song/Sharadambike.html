pallaviSAradAmbikE Samadama dAyikE SatakOTi raviprakASa jagadambikE
anupallavicaraNa paricara pOshiNi purAbhrngI kra bhEda haraNa ghOshiNi Sankara dESika anugAmini cakra Sankha dharAdi dEva pAlini cambara shaNmukhAdi birupAsita Sabdda svarUpiNi SAmbavi SaSAnka viTambini durgA taTasthita vEda ghOshiNi sadA SrngArapura gEha SrngAra vEshiNI sarasijAsana sanaka sanandana samEdini nandana sannuta nAyikE
caraNamSankara vakshasthalaq vihAriNi Sukha pustaka aksha mAlApANi
supra vastAvrta SvEta sumamAlini SaniSEkhari Sankari SAtOdari

manuja jAta SrI suta phaladAyikE
