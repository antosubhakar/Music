Pallavijanani pAhi sadA jagadISa dEvi smarahara vallabhE
Anupallavianavadyatara nava hArAlaGkrtE maJju vana jAyata lOcanaE vAhinI taTavAsE
Caranam 1SailarAja tanayE caNDmuNDa nASini SUla SObhitakarE sundara rUpE
phAla rAji tilakE parama krpAvati pAlita sujana mAlikE surasAla kisalaya cAru nija pAdE
Caranam 2mahita bAhu bala mahishAsurAdi gahana dahanAyitE gatamada sEvyE su-hasitajita kundE
SONaja pAdhari vihara mama hrdi vidhu sadrSamukhi bahuLa malayaja bandhurE lOka
Caranam 3parama pAvana SrI padmanAbha sahOdari nIla cikurE kEsari varavAhE carita durita
bhaktadara nikarE gauri kuru krpAm mayi kuliSa dharamukha pariNutE ghana durita hrdilOla
