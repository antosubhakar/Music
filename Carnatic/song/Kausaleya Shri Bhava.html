PallavikausalEya SrI bhava jaladhi dharaNA jAnaki ramaNa sakala lOkakara sAdhujana smaraNa
Anupallavipannaga SayanA paGkaja nayanA bhadrAdri kshEtra vAsakAtri putrAri dvaja agOkshajAhE
CaranamrAmA pAhimAm parandAmA raviukula soma raghurAja janaka ati Subha paladhara vikula somA
rati khara dUshaNA bhaJjana munijana mana raJjanA Srtajana SrIvatsa nAyaka mukti dAyaka
