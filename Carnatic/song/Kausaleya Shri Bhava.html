  Lyrics - English:

pallavikausalEya SrI bhava jaladhi dharaNA jAnaki ramaNa sakala lOkakara sAdhujana smaraNa
anupallavipannaga SayanA pankaja nayanA bhadrAdri kshEtra vAsakAtri putrAri dvaja agOkshajAhE
caraNamrAmA pAhimAm parandAmA raviukula soma raghurAja janaka ati Subha paladhara vikula somA
rati khara dUshaNA bhanjana munijana mana ranjanA Srtajana SrIvatsa nAyaka mukti dAyaka
