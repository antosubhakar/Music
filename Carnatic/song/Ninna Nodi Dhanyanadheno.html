Pallavininne nOdi danyanAde- |
no he! SrInivAsA ||
ninne nOdi danyanAde- |
no ... ||
AnupallavipakSi vAhana lakSmI ramaNA ||
lakSya vittu nOdO pAndu ||
pakSa sarva daitya sikSa ||
rakSisanna kamalAkSA ||
(ninne)
CaranamdESa dESa tiriginAnu ||
ASa baddha nAde svAmi ||
dAsanenisi enna jaga- ||
dISa kAyO vAsudEvA ||
antaraGga dAsa yannu
antara villa dE pAlisayya
hantakAri purandara viThalana ||
(ninne)
