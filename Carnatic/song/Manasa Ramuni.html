  Lyrics - English:

PallavimanasA rAmuni marakavE O
AnupallavimunupaDi durguNa mulanu viDaciyati vinaya vidhEyuDavai vivEkamutO
Caranam 1bhaktA dhAruDu parama pAvanuDu bhakta vatsaluDu paramAtmuDu
mukti dAyakuDu mOhana rUpuDu bhakti lEni mAnavulaku vimuguDu
Caranam 2dasharatha bAluDu dAnava kAluDu dashamukha daitya samhAruDu dhIruDu
visatamugA bhakta citta vihAruDu rasikula vEgamE rakSincuvADu
Caranam 3shyAmaLa mEgha sundaruTati sUruDu rAmadAsa mAnasa sancAruDu
rAmacandrapurA dhAruDu vEnkaTaramaNa bhAgavata varaduDu gAna
