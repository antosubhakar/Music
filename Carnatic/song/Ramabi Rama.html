  Lyrics - English:

PallavirAm(A)bhirAma manasu ranjilla palka rAdA
AnupallaviI mahini velasinad(E)mi sadA nAtO (rAma)
CaranambangAru mETi pAnpupai bhAmA maNi jAnaki
SRngArincukoni celuvonda ninnu kani
pongucu malle virula pUjincu vELa SrI hari
sangItamu pADum(a)ni svAmi tyAgarAjunitO (rAma)
