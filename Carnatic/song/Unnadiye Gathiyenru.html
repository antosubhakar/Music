  Lyrics - English:

PallaviunnaDiyE gadiyenRaDaindEn tAyE
uNmai nI aRiyAyO ulaka nAyakiyE (unnaDiyE)
Anupallaviponnaiyum pukazhaiyum pUvaiyaraiyum tETi
cinnattanam aTaintu nittamum* mika vATi (unnaTiyE)
CaranammunnamE unniTam colli vaittEnE
