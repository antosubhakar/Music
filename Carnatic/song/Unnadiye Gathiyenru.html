  Lyrics - English:

PallaviunnaDiyE gati enraDaindEn tAyE uNmai nI ariyAyO ulaga nAyakiyE
Anupallaviponnaiyum pugazhaiyum bhUvaraiyum tEDi cinnattanam aDaindu nittamum migha vADi
CaranammunnamE unniDam sholli vaittEnE enna vandAlum nin ponnaDi maravEn
pannaga bhUSaNan parivuDan maruvum minnal koDiyALE innalgaL tIrppAyE
