  Lyrics - English:

pallavibhaktajana pAlakA, bhakti sukha dAyakA,
muktESa dInabaMdhO, krushNa.
yuktiyali ninnaMtha dEvaranu nA kANE,
satyavatIsutanE kAyO, krushNa.
caraNam 1AnaMdatIrtha muniya jAdipada saMga,
AnaMdadali nillisO, krushNa.
caraNam 2dInajana maMdAra nIneMdu naMbidenO,
sAnurAgadali kAyo, krushNa.
caraNam 3keTTa janara saMga ishTu dinavU mADi,
bhrashTanAgi pOdEnO, krushNa.
beTTadoDeyanE ninna muTTi bhajisuva bhAgya,
ishTagaLa enage koDisO, krushNa.
caraNam 4aja janakaraja varadA, bujagaSayananE ninna,
bhajipa bhagyavanE koDisO, krushNa.
caraNam 5nijavAgi ninnoratu salahuvara nA kANE,
vijayaviThSalarEyanE, krushNa.
