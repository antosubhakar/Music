PallavimahAtmulE teliyalEru nI mahA mahima rAma
mahAnubhAvA SrtamandArA Emi pogaDudu nIdu mahima
AnupallavimahIsutA manOharA madana kOTi sundara
mahA prabhO nannu maravakurA krpa jEsi brOvavayya
