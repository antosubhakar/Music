  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrI vAsudEvam shrI ramaNa mAm pAhi
AnupallavinavanIta dadhi cOra nanda gOpakumAra
CaranamsarasIruhAnana vArinidhi vara shayana vara rUpajita madana
kari rAja sharaNa sura vinuta vara caraNa surucirAbharaNa
