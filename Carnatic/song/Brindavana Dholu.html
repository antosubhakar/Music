Caranam 1aruNa pallava padayugaLanE divya marakata maJjuLAbharaNanE
sirivarayadu kula sOmanE inthA paripUrNa kAma nishkAmanE
Caranam 2hArahira guNa dhAranE divya sAra SarIra SrGgArananE
