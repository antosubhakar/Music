  Lyrics - English:

PallavibRndAvana lOla gOvind(A)ravinda nayana
Anupallavisundar(A)nga dhRta rath(A)nga
sujan(A)ri timira patanga (bRndAvana)
CaranammAm-ava SrI ramaNI maNi madan(A)Srita mitra
rAma dAsa dAsa tyAgarAja nuta caritra (bRndAvana)

அனுபல்லவிசுந்தர்(ஆ)ந்க த்ருத ரத்(ஆ)ந்க
