  Lyrics - English:

PallavisAdAramava sarasija daLa su-nayana sakala sujana bhava bhya hara
AnupallavipAdatala nata suramuni samudaya padmaAbha dayAkara mAdhava
Caranam 1gOpahita karatala dhrta girivara kunda vilasita nijarada yadukula
dIpa jaladhara sama madhura vacana dIna janatati Subhakara nirupama
Caranam 2ghOra bhuja bala maghada-nrpati hatikOpa suma gaNa vilasita kaca bhara
SrI ramaNa mrgamada niTila varada sEvaka nikara ghana kalusha Samana
Caranam 3kAma janaka mama vitara murahara kAmita mahivara Sayana nirupama
bhUmi ghana bhara vidaLana samadhika bOdhAkara himakara su-vadana vara
