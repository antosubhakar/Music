  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrI cakrapura vAsini jananI shrta cintAmaNi
AnupallavimEcakAngi mAtangi mEnAtmaja vimalAngi
Caranamniratavu ninna caraNAbjava smaripa bhAgyava nIDu janani suravara vinutE shrI harikEsha pAlini
