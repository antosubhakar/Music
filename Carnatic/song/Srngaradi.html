  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSRGgArAdi navarasAGgI bRhadambAliGgita puGgava dhavaLAGga Sriyam dEhi
CaranamaGgArakAdi vinutAGgaja tripurArE gaGgAdhara vRshabha turaGga sat saGga bhayabhaGga guruguhAntaraGga SrI mahA liGga
