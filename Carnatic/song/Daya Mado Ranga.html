  Lyrics - English:

PallavidayamADO dayamADO dayamADO ranga dayamADO nAninna dAsanendeNisi
Anupallavihalavu kAlavu ninna hambalu enage olidu pAlisabEku vArijanAbha
Caranamihaparadali nInE indira ramaNa bhayavyAkO nIniralu bhaktarabhimAni
karirAja varadanE kandarpa pitanE purandaraviThala sadguNa sArvabhauma
