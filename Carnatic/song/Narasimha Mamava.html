PallaviNarasimha Mamava Bhagavan Nityam
AnupallaviHaraviriJchavasavanutha shauyasindho
Athirabhasanihathasura Dinabandho
Caranam 1Kanaka Kasipu Vanadava Bodhakara Deva Mahanubhava shanakadi Munijana sharana Pavithra Janataribhayagatra sharanetra
Caranam 2Vitathakesarahatha sharabhajala Visvanayaka Bhaktha Varada Athimatravinaya Prahladasamoda Nathapadamalavedatathi GIthabhedha]
Caranam 3Karadritha shitahethiyatha BhUri KaluSavaridha Kulajatha Bharitha Kriparasa Paripalithebha ParamaGgalasobha PaGkajanabha
