  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrI gaNanAyakam saubhAgya dAyakam bhAvayE pUrvApaNESam vinAyakam
AnupallaviAgama sAram aSEsha Subhakaram Anandakaram anantapurAkAram
Caranampadmi mukham nIla padmadaLESaNam pannaga bhUshaNa sutam lOka rakshaNam
pApa viSOshaNam parasukha pOshaNam pAvana kAnana tuLasI vibhUshaNam
