  Lyrics - English:

PallavidEv(A)di dEva sadASiva
dina nAtha sudhAkara dahana nayana
AnupallavidEv(E)Sa pitAmaha mRgya
Sam(A)di guN(A)bharaNa gaurI ramaNa (dEvAdi)
Caranambhava candra kalA dhara nIla gaLa
bhAnu kOTi saMkASa Sr(I)Sa nuta
tava pAda bhaktiM dEhi dIna bandhO
dara hAsa vadana tyAgarAja nuta (dEvAdi)
