  Lyrics - English:

Pallavisujana mAnasa hamsAnandinI sudhA sAgara madhya vAsini amba
AnupallaviajasurEsha sEvita mAlinI nija sukhadAyaki nIrajalOcani
Caranamvijita vAruNi vidhrta yOgi hrda bhajita pAvana padayugaLE
bhujaga shayana haripriya sOdari tyaja mama ghana pApahara shankari
