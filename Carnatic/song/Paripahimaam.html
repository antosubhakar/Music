PallaviparipAhi mAM n.rhare murAre
Anupallavisarasijabhavanuta sAdhulokabhayApaha
CaranamsharaNAgatAdhiharaNa devadevesha shatamanyunuta
caraNa varatApasah.rdayapArijAta kaLahaMsa || ||
danujanikaradamana dhanyakayAdhUtanaya saN^ka.tashamana
janitabhuvanabhIti kanakakashipukAla || ||
shamalashalabha dIpabhakta vIkShaNashamitanistulakopa
kamaladaLanayana kamalanAbha satatam || ||
