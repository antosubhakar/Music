bhava bhava ciraJjIvI bhava ciraJjIvI bhava ciraJjIvI
Caranam 1sakala hrdaya bhuvi sukrta prakASa samudita mAnasa SAnta pradESa
prakaTIkrtapari pUrI tASa param akhila parA nanda nivESa
Caranam 2sarasa guNArNava smarasa kAya sakala janairati sEvanIya
parama kuSalakara hE mahanIya patita sahOdara pAvanOpAya
Caranam 3nirupama karuNA pUrita dEha nikila janAnanda vArivAha
suruciram ahimsA SObhana gEha samarasa viSvam gIta sandOha
