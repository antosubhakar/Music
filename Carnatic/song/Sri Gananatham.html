  Lyrics - English:

PallaviSrIgaNanAtham bhajarE citta parASakti yutam
AnupallavinAga yajJa sUttradharam nAdalayAnanda karam
CaranamAgamAdi sannutam akhila dEva pUjitam yOgaSAli bhAvitam bhOgiSAyi sEvitam rAgadvEshAdi rahita ramaNIya hRdaya viditam SrIguruguha sammuditam cinmUla kamalasthitam

அனுபல்லவிநாக யஞ சூத்த்ரதரம் நாதலயானந்த கரம்
