  Lyrics - English:

Pallavibhava nuta nA hRdayamuna ramimpumu baDalika tIra
Anupallavibhava tAraka nAtO bahu palkina baDalika tIra
kamala sam(bhava)
Caranam 1pavana suta priya tanakai tirigina baDalika tIra
bhavanamu jEri nanu verapincina baDalika tIra
kamala sam(bhava)
Caranam 2varamagu naivEdyamulanu jEyani baDalika tIra
parava lEka sari pOyin(a)TT(A)Dina baDalika tIra
kamala sam(bhava)
Caranam 3prabala jEsi nanu brOcedav(a)nukonna baDalika tIra
prabhu nIvu tyAgarAjuniki baDalika tIra
kamala sam(bhava)
