  Lyrics - English:

PallavikOsalEndra mAmava amita guNa nivAsa bhagavan
AnupallavibhAsamAna mahA maNI maNTapa pari vilasita simhAsana rAjita
Caranam 1bAhu drta sharacApa dHuta bhakta janAkhila tApa
AhavAhata vimatATOpa hAra makuTa kEyUra kalApa
Caranam 2jAnakI lasitAnga jalajAsya vinAshita lanka bhAnu
vamsha payOdhi shashAnkapAda vihata muni taruNI bhanga
Caranam 3nIla jaladhara gAtra vrta nIla naLAruNaputra bAla
lOcana mukha nutipAtra pavanasuta kalita cAru caritra
