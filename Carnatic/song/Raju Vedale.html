PallavirAju veDale jUtAmu rArE kastUri ranga (rAju)
AnupallavitEjin(e)kki samasta rAjul(U)Digamu sEya
tEjarillu nava ratnapu divya bhUshaNamul(i)Di ranga (rAju)
CaranamkAvErI tIramunanu pAvanamagu ranga purini

bhAvinci tyAgarAju pADaga vaibhOga ranga (rAju)
