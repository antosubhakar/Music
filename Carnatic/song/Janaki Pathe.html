  Lyrics - English:

pallavijAnakIpatE jaya kAruNya jaladhE jaya
kausalyAnanda vardhana jagadabirAma paTTAbhirAma
anupallavidIna janAvana dhruta kara kankaNa
daSaratha tanayA aganita guNa gaNa
caraNamguha jaTAyu SabarI pramukha nija bhakta jana mukti dAyakA
jagadahita virATa kabanda niSAcara nikara niSIdana sAyakA sarOruha
caraNa nikida vAli daSavadana suramuni sajjana pAlakA
sakala mahidEndra nIlamaNi nibha SarIra pAhi sAkEta nAyakA SrI
