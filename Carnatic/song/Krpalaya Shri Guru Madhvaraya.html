  Lyrics - English:

PallavikrpAkara SrI guru madhvarAya nrpOttamAgraNi satya vijayapura vibhu SrI tirumala rAjapAla
AnupallavisuparNavAha SrI sItArAma sundarapAda pUjA nirata lOka vinuta divya satya mAnasAnna bhrGga
Caranam 1SrI hariguNa pArAyaNa caNa SObhitadara cakracinna bAhumUla pAhimAm
SrI nandagOpa suta padAnnupaGga bhakta mAnasa
Caranam 2tulasI maNi hAra gaLa racitOpa nishAbdhApyAdi grantha bahuLa
vilasita nArAyaNa mantra paThita rasana vimatadUra suguNAkara
Caranam 3dhrta hEmamaya kAshAyambara payanAvatAra lOkOpakAra
satata sadASiva kavi mAnasacara namAmi bhayahara dayAsAgara
