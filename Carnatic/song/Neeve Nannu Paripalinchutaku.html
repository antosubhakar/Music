PallavinIvE paripAliJcuTaku nityakalyANi amba
AnupallavirAvE SrI mInalOcani amba rAjAdhi rAjESvari aba
Caranamparama lObhulagu narulanu pogaDe paritApamella patita pArvati nIpAda mahima prabhAvamerugagane tirigi tirigi vyarthamaiti bhuvilO trijanana tApa samhAriNi parE kAmitArtha mOkshadAyini parAtpari krshNa gAnamOdini
