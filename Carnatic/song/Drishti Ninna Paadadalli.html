  Lyrics - English:

PallavidrshTi ninna pAdadalli nODo hAge dhare dushTa jana saGgagaLa biDO hAge
Caranam 1keTTa mAtu kiviyinda kELadhAge mana kaTTi sadA ninna dhyAna biDada hAge
Caranam 2diTTanAgi kaiyanetti koDo hAge SrI krshNa ninna pUjeyanu mADO hAge
bhrashTanAgi nAlvaroLu tirugadhAge balu SishTa jana sEveyanu mADO hAge
Caranam 3puTTisida tAyi tandEyallo nInu ondu hoTTrgAgi dainyapaDa bEke nAnu
paTTe paTTAvaLi bEDalilla nAnu enna guTTu abhimAnagaLa kAyo nInu
Caranam 4naTTa nIroLage IsalarO nAnu etti kaTTe sErisa bEkayyA nInu
beTTadantha pApa hottiruve nAnu ada suTTu biDu purandara viTTala nInu
