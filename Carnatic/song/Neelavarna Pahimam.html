  Lyrics - English:

pallavinIlavarNa pAhi mam santatam navanIta krshNa pAhi mAm
anupallavibAlagOpAla pAla krpAlaya ghrta-mAlAkula nilaya nanda gOpanandana navaghana
caraNam 1aLikulam bilalantikAmSu miLita lasa-daLaka nikara calada mala maNi kuNDala
lalita mukha gaLita lAlArUshitAkrrtE gaLa kalita dvIpi nakha bharaNa SrI yaduvara
caraNam 2panka mOcana kELIpatana samaya lagna panka SObhita bhava pankajAsana sEvya
ankurita radana hAsa Sobhi vadana kinkiNI maNigaNa kiraNa ranjita sujaghana
caraNam 3manju nayana yuga manjupari vigaLa bhanjanAncita cEtOhara taramaNi
bandha SinjAna manjIra SrI bhAsura caraNa kanja yugaLa SaurE kamalanAbha jaya jaya
