  Lyrics - English:

Pallavimama hrdayE vihara dayALO krSNa mandaradhara gOvinda mukunda
AnupallavimanthadAma su-virAjita shrI krSNa mandahAsa vadanAravinda nayana
Caranamyadukula vAridhi pUrNa candra vidura vanditapAda guNasAndra
madana janaka shrIkara mahAnubhAva sadaya hrdaya shrI vAsudEva
