  Lyrics - English:

pallaviparipAhi mAm SrI raghupatE sarasIruhAksha sItApatE
anupallavisura vairi rAvaNa hara krpAkara vAsudEva dIna mandAra
caraNamrAkEndu sannibha vadana SrI sAkEta purAdISa tOshitESa
nAkESa mukhAmara pAlaka lOkESa savAndan-dAyakA
