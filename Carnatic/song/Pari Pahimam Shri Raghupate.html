  Lyrics - English:

PallaviparipAhimAm shrI hrSIkEsha kari varada vAsudEva savEmsha
Anupallaviduritaugha shaila kulishEndirEsha paritOSitE sa-rucirAli kEsha
Caranamvara shankha cakra pari shObhitakara sarasIruhAkSa kamsyAdyari hara
kara vIra kunda suma hAra dhIra sura brnda vinuta paramOddAra
