(dhIm)
Anupallavinadhru dru tOm drudru dhIm tatara tAni dhIm tajham jham kiTata tajhaNuta kiTatari
takataNam kiTata takadhimi kiTatari dittillAna drudru tillAna drudru tAna drudrudru
(dhIm)
Caranamninnu kOriyunna nAdupai sannutAnga nI dayayuncarA
pannaga shayana nA vinnapamuvini nannu brOva rAmadAsa sannuta
JatisvaramtAm takiTataka takadhimi risanidha tajhaNu rigapa kiTa dhasariga pA
takiTa sarigarisa tajham jham kiTata ditlAm kiTata pamagari tadhingiNa tOm
(dhIm)
