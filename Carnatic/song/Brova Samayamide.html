  Lyrics - English:

PallavibrOva samayamidE rAmayya
AnupallavikAvumani nE kOriyuNgA nA bhAvamerigi nannAdarimpa vEga (or nannAdariJcu)
Caranamneranammina vAriki niratamu varamu losagE birudu gala
