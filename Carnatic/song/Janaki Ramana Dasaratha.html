  Lyrics - English:

PallavijAnakI ramaNa dasharatha nandana sarvarIsha vadana harE rAma
(jAnakI)
AnupallavidIna vatsala dayA jaladhE (rAma) vara dAna shIla kOdaNDapANE
(jAnakI)
Caranamkaushikamakha samrakSana kara gautamakAnta nija rUpavara
mahita mahEshvara cApa dalLanakara maithila tanaya kara mAlAdhara
(jAnakI)
