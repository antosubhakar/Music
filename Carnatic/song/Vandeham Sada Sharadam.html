  Lyrics - English:

PallavivandEham sadA shAradAm hamsanaTanI rAga priyAm shrI vidyAm
AnupallavimandahAsinIM manOnmanIm manjuLa vAgvilAsinIm brahma mAnasOllAsinIm mAninIm
CaranammUlAdhArAdi SaT chakrAntargatAm mUka vAkpradAna suvikhyAtAm
mUla prakrtyAdi svarUpAm mUrtAmUrta bhavya svarUpAm
kartrtvAdya tIta rUpAm kavi jana sammOhita chidrUpAm
