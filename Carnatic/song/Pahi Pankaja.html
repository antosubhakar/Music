AnupallavidEhi bhaktim tvayi mama dInabandhO dEha kAntijita nIla nIrada shaurE dEha sukham guru pavana purAdhipa dEhi mE vibhO karuNa sindhO
Caranam 1kim nA jAtA dayA gajarAja pAlanatava pannaga shayana harE padmanAbha khinna tarE
mayi krpAm tAvakInAm kuru karNa yugaLa shObhita bhUSa kuNDala varNanIya nija lIla suravara
Caranam 2lIlA lAlita gO gOpi jAla kEshava bahu lAlasita cakra gadA rAnga pankaja lOla tara mAlikAncita
gala bAla gOpa madhu mathana dEva druta shaila sharaNamayi bhava mama bhagavan
