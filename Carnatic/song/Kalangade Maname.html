  Lyrics - English:

PallavikalaGgAdE manamE nI inamE
AnupallavitulaGgezhil vElan padam nidam tudi mati idE gati
Caranamsvara rAgavardhana bhakta paripAlana guhan guNa mAl marugan
vara kavi kuJjara dAsan ninpAl varuvan aruL taruvan
