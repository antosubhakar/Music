  Lyrics - English:

PallavigirijA ramaNa natajana SaraNa karuNArasa pUrNa smara hara nAgAbharaNa
Anupallavipara vAsudEvArAdana durINa kara dhrta hariNa kali mala haraNa
CaranammahA pancAkshari mantra mUrtE mahA bhakta kauntEyanuta kIrtE
mahA gaNapati guha sEvita mUrtE mahAdEva pari hrta dInajanArtE
mahAnandi bhrngyAdi gambhIra nATya pradarSaka kailAsapatE
