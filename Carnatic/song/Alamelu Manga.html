JalajAkSu kannulaku chavulicche vamma ||
Caranam 1Garudachala DhIsu Ghana vakSamuna nundi |
Paramananda sambharita vai ||
Nerathanamulu jUpi nirantharmu nadhuni |
HaruSimpa ga jesiti gadamma ||
Caranam 2shasi kiranamulaku chaluvala chUpulu |
Visadhamuga mIda vedajalluchu ||
_rasikatha pempuna karigiJchi eppudu ni |

Jatti goni yuramuna satha maithivamma ||
