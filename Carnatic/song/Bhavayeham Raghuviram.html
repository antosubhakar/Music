  Lyrics - English:

PallavibhAvayEham raghuvIram SrIkaram sEvakAryi haraNa caturam karuNAkaram
AnupallavijIvanEtAram jAnakI manOharam rAvaNAdyariharam ratipati sundaram
CaranamvAnarAdhipati kula samjAtam bhAsamAna maNihAra SObhitam
bhUsuraukArcitam vAsavAdi sannutam vAsudEvam haram vAtAtmajAvana radam
