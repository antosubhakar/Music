  Lyrics - English:

PallavinI daya rAdA
AnupallavikAd(a)nE vAr(e)varu kalyAna rAma (nI)
Caranam 1nannu brOcuvAr(i)lanu nADE teliya
ina vamSa tilaka nIk(i)nta tAmasamA (nI)
Caranam 2anniTiki(n)adhikAruD(a)ni nE pogaDitE
mannincitE nIdu mahimaku takkuvA (nI)
Caranam 3rAma rAma rAma tyAgarAja hRt-sadana
nA madi tallaDille nyAyamA vEgamE (nI)
