PallaviSrI veGkaTESam bhajAmi satatam Srtajana paripAlam gAnalOlam
AnupallaviSrI bhUmijA ramaNam bhava taraNam nAmAtIta rUpam bhU vaikuNTha patim
Caranamcandra pushkariNi tIra vihAram indrAdi dEvanuta pAdayugam
SrI bhUdEvi sahita nitya kalyANa vasantOtsava priyakaram
SaGkha cakradharam SaGkhanAda priyakaram
vidhi guruguha pUjitAbja padam kaliyuga prasannam
