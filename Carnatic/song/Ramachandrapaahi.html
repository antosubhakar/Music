  Lyrics - English:

PallavirAmacandra pAhi satatam ravi vamSa dIpa ramaNIya kAlaya
AnupallavisAmajEndragatE bhavasAgara pOtAnata kAmadAna paTutama kAma sundarAGga vibhO
Caranam 1maunijana sEvya caraNa krpArasAbdhE mAruti sannatapAda mAnanIya kIrti
vAsamakhavO pAlAGga kAntE bhAnu himakara nEtra phAla SObhi su-lalAma
Caranam 2kISa nikara parivrta drtAvanijA kaTAksha bhAsuradAma nASitAGgri
sEvAlOla nikarAmaya pASa vAraya mAmaka pApajAtam dinamanu
Caranam 3cAru bAhu SObhi kArmuka sAkEta dhAma jAtarUpa nibha vasana ghOra
raNa taTa bahu dhIra kapi varahara vArirASi ghana mada vAraNaika paTuSara
Caranam 4rAvaNArE kuru kuSalam lOkatryApi raJjanIya mrdu hAsa bhAva dukkhEshu
krpayA bhavya lIlamAkshhipa mAm pAvana Srta SrI padmanAbha rUpadhara
