PallavieGgum niraivAna poruLE enadannai karumAri iraGgi magizhndaruLE dEvi
AnupallavimaGgaLamAy ulagil uyar vAzhvu tarum krshNamAri poGgi ezhum anbu tavazh punnakai malarnda dEvi
CaranamvEdapuri ISar tiruvErkkADu tannil vaLarum vEdAnta jyOtiyE nAdAnta jaya SaktiyE
