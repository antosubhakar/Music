  Lyrics - English:

PallavininagyAru sariyillA enaganya gatiyillA ninagU enagU nyAya hELuvarillavO
Caranam 1ondE gUDinoLiddu ondu kshaNava galade endendu ninnu pondiruve bandha
vishaya gaLigenna noppisuvadu indu ninage idu ucitavallavO dEva
Caranam 2parisatigalukaLu parama pApishTanendu paripari narakakke sErisuve
para satiyoLume ninage oppitO dEva dovetana kaJji nA SaraNambe nallade
Caranam 3ninna dhyAnavanu nA ninna prEvaNeyinda ananta karmava nA mADide
innAdaru enna dayadinda nODayya pannaga Sayanane purandara viThala krshNa
