  Lyrics - English:

pallaviadi vyAdihara SrI AnjcanEyA
idu taruNam emai ANDaruL tUyA
(Adi)
anupallavitudisey mArgazhit tingaL uditta
adisaya mAruti pEruruvam taritta
(Adi)
caraNampunida meypporuL aRiyA bhOgi enattirinda
manida manam sIr aDaindiDum marundE
piNi agaRRi Anma veTkai taNindE
anaittuyir nalam peRa rAma nAma pAnam tanda
(adi)
