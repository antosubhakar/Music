  Lyrics - English:

Pallavinagu mOmu gala vAni nA manO-haruni
jagam(E)lu SUruni jAnakI varuni
Caranam 1dEv(A)di dEvuni divya sundaruni
SrI vAsudEvuni sItA rAghavuni (nagu)
Caranam 2su-jnAna nidhini sOma sUrya lOcanuni
ajnAna tamamunu aNacu bhAskaruni (nagu)
Caranam 3nirmal(A)kAruni nikhil(A)gha haruni
dharm(A)di mOkshambu daya cEyu ghanuni (nagu)
Caranam 4bOdhatO palumAru pUjinci nEn-
(A)rAdhintu SrI tyAgarAja sannutuni (nagu)
