  Lyrics - English:

pallavibhajAmi santatam adhOkshajam Subha caritam vAsudEvam
gajEndra pUjita padAmbujam guNabharitam kESavam
anupallaviajAdi nirjara vinutam bhrta nijA SritAkhila yAdavam
vrajAngaNArpita druSam SaSimukham ajAmIla varadam amitam avibhavam
caraNammuralI ninAda parimOhita mrga paSu gaNam munivara Saranam
kara SObhita nirupama marakata mani kankaNam bhavabhaya haraNam
narakAsurAdi suraripu kari kEsariNam rukmiNI ramaNam
vara bhIma pArtha pAnchAli hrdaya tOshaNam dhrta vibhUshaNam

व्रजाङ्गणार्पित द्रुशम् शशिमुखम् अजामील वरदम् अमितम् अविभवम्
