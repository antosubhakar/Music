  Lyrics - English:

PallavisankarshaNa mAm pAlaya pankaja nayana krpAlaya
Anupallavipankaja bhava vinuta caraNa lankAdhipa haraNa nipuNa
CaranambhAsura Sara kArmukhadhara SAsitakara makha nisicarA
kOsala nrpa vara kumAra vAsavAdi pAlana para
SrI samEta pItAmbara dAsavarya mangaLakara
bhAsamAna muraLIdhara vAsudEva dAmOdara
