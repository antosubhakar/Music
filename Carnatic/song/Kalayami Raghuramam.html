  Lyrics - English:

PallavikalayAmi raghurAmam bhUpA lalAmam
AnupallavinIlOtpala sannibha gAtram parama pavitram
Caranam 1dhana dAnuja ghana vana dahanam tapta kanaka nibha vasanam vanajAksham
sanakAdi muni vinuta Subha caritam manasija rUpam anavadyam
Caranam 2karuNA vArinidhim caraNa naLinapUta vara tApa samahilam surapAlam
kuru vinda nibharadam parama purushamiha dharaNi nandinI kEli paramISam
Caranam 3dyumaNi kula tilakam vimala guNam sarva Samala haraNa caNa ramaNIyam
kumudaika bAndhava sama vadanam SrI kamalanAbham ghana SObam
