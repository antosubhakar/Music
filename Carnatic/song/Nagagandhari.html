  Lyrics - English:

PallavinAgagAndhArI rAganutE nagajAnanditE mAmava
AnupallavinAgarAja vinutE surahitE vAgIshAdi guruguha vanditE bhAgavatAdi nuta paradEvatE parama tatvArttha bOdhitE shivE
Caranamparama shAnta svarUpAkArE pAda paHNkajE padma karE niratishaya sukhakarE surucirE sasharacApa pAshAHNkusha dharE
