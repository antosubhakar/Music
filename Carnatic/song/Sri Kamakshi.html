  Lyrics - English:

PallavishrI kAmAkSi nA cinta dIrccammA
Caranam 1dIna mAnava rakSaki
Caranam 2nE nI pAdamulanu nammitini
Caranam 3nA paritApamu kani karuNa jUDavamma
Caranam 4pAvani shrtajana pAlini parAtpari janani krpAkari
Caranam 5nI sari brOcuTaku vErevaru lErani amba niratamuga
gati nIvani nera nammiyunna nAdupai parAku sEyaku
Caranam 6sAma vara gAna lOla shrI pavanajAsana vinuta sarasijamukhi
nI dAsuDanE gadA padAmbujamulanu sadA sharaNaNTini
