  Lyrics - English:

PallaviennaDu jUtunO ina kula tilaka ninn(ennadu)
Anupallavipannaga Sayana bhakta jan(A)vana
punnama canduru pOlu mukhamunu (ennaDu)
CaranamdharaNijA saumitri bharata ripughna
vAnara yUtha pati varuD(A)njanEyuDu
karuNanu okarik(o)karu varNimpan-
(A)daraNanu pilicE ninnu tyAgarAj(A)rcita (ennaDu)
