  Lyrics - English:

PallavipraNamAmyaham SrI prANanAtham nirantaram praNita
janAbhIshhTha phala pradam SrI rAma sEvA dhurandharam
AnupallaviprANApAnavyAnAdyAkhya mukhya prANam
Madhyamakala sahityamprANinAm prANa bhUtam prINitAm SEsha pAtram
bharadvAjAdi vinutam bhAratI kaLatram
CaranamanjanA garbha sambhUta vIrAgrEsaram
niranjana SrI vAsudEva hrdayAkAram
sumanjuLa mukhAravinda jAnakI priyakaram
ripu bhanjana caturam rgvEda suvidita mahimAtaram
Madhyamakala sahityamranjanI rAga tOshitam kunjarAsyAdi vanditam
sanjIvanOjjIvita sumitrA sutam
